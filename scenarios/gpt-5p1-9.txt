BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of limited-overs cricket matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the players’ intrinsic batting and bowling abilities and on how much effort they put into that match.
Teams have multiple players, but the all-rounders Arjun, Bilal, and Cyrus have especially large influence on runs scored and wickets taken.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Arjun’s team (Tigers) beat Bilal’s team (Lions).
In the second match, Bilal’s team (Lions) beat Cyrus’s team (Eagles).
In the third match, Cyrus’s team (Eagles) beat Arjun’s team (Tigers).

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random cricket all-rounders, where do you think Arjun ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Bilal put into the second match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between the Tigers (with Arjun) and the Eagles (with Cyrus), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between the Lions (with Bilal) and the Tigers (with Arjun), who would win and by how much?
