BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes are competing in a series of Ultimate Frisbee games. In each game, the team that wins depends on the score achieved, based on the athletes' intrinsic agility and throwing accuracy modulated by the effort they put into that game.
Athletes compete in fixed teams of 8.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, the team of Cooper, Art, Bill, Cam, Don, Earl, Fred, and Guy lost to the team of Hank, Ian, Jim, Kit, Lou, Moe, Ned, and Otis.
In the second game, the team of Hank, Ian, Jim, Kit, Lou, Moe, Ned, and Otis beat the team of Cooper, Art, Bill, Cam, Don, Earl, Fred, and Guy.
In the third game, the team of Cooper, Art, Bill, Cam, Don, Earl, Fred, and Guy lost to the team of Hank, Ian, Jim, Kit, Lou, Moe, Ned, and Otis.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Cooper ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Hank put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Cooper and Art (Team 1) and Hank and Ian (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Bill and Cam (Team 1) and Jim and Kit (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
