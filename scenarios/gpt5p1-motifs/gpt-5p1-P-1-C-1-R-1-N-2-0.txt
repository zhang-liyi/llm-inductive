BACKGROUND
In this event, the players are competing in a series of half-court basketball games. In each game, the team that wins depends on the players’ intrinsic shooting, ball-handling, and defensive abilities, combined with how much effort they put into that specific game.
Games are played either 1-on-1 or 4-on-4.
Most players stay on fixed 4-player squads (Alicia, Bella, Cara, and Dani on one team; Ethan, Finn, Greg, and Hugo on another), while Marco frequently switches sides.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Alicia, Bella, Cara, and Dani beat Ethan, Finn, Greg, and Hugo.
In the second game, Marco, Ethan, Finn, and Greg lost to Alicia, Bella, Cara, and Dani.
In the third game, Marco, Alicia, Bella, and Cara lost to Ethan, Finn, Greg, and Hugo.
In the fourth game, Marco lost to Alicia.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random basketball players, where do you think Alicia ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Marco put into the fourth game?
Query 3: In a new 4-on-4 game later this same day between Alicia, Bella, Cara, and Dani (Team 1) and Marco, Ethan, Finn, and Greg (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new 1-on-1 game later this same day between Marco (Player 1) and Hugo (Player 2), who would win and by how much?
