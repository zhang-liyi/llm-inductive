BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes compete in Rugby Sevens (adapted here to teams of 8 for training purposes). The winning team is determined by points scored, based on the athletes' intrinsic physicality and game sense, modulated by the effort they commit to the game.
The clubs (Lions, Tigers, Bears) have fixed squads, but Elias is a guest player who rotates into different squads throughout the day.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, the Lions (with Elias) beat the Tigers.
In the second game, the Bears (with Elias) beat the Tigers.
In the third game, the Bears (with Elias) lost to the Lions.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Elias ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think the Lions put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between the Lions (with Elias) and the Bears (without Elias), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between the Tigers (with Elias) and the Bears (without Elias), who would win and by how much?
