BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of indoor volleyball scrimmages. In each match, the team that wins depends on the players’ intrinsic volleyball skills (serving, passing, setting, hitting, and blocking) and how much effort they put into that particular match.
Athletes compete in small teams of three or in pairs.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Lena, Mia, and Jordan beat Chris, Taylor, and Sam.
In the second match, Lena and Chris beat Mia and Taylor.
In the third match, Sam and Taylor beat Jordan and Chris.
In the fourth match, Mia and Sam beat Lena and Jordan.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random volleyball players, where do you think Lena ranks in terms of intrinsic volleyball skill?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Chris put into the second match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Mia and Jordan (Team 1) and Sam and Chris (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Lena, Taylor, and Sam (Team 1) and Jordan, Mia, and Riley (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
