BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Ultimate Frisbee games. The outcome of each game depends on the players’ intrinsic Ultimate skills (such as throwing accuracy, catching ability, and speed) together with how much effort they put in for that game.
Games are played either 1 on 1 throwing contests or between two teams of 9 players on the field.
All games take place on the same day.
Most players stay on fixed teams, but Omar plays for different teams during the event.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Team Horizon (Omar, Alice, Ben, Carla, Diego, Ella, Finn, Grace, and Hugo) beat Team Zenith (Iris, Jack, Kyle, Laura, Max, Nora, Owen, Paige, and Quinn).
In the second game, Team Eclipse (Rosa, Theo, Uma, Victor, Will, Xena, Yara, Zack, and Omar) beat Team Zenith (Iris, Jack, Kyle, Laura, Max, Nora, Owen, Paige, and Quinn).
In the third game, Omar beat Max.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random Ultimate Frisbee players, where do you think Omar ranks in terms of intrinsic Ultimate ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Max put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new 9‑a‑side game later this same day between Omar, Alice, Ben, Carla, Diego, Ella, Finn, Grace, and Hugo (Team 1) and Iris, Jack, Kyle, Laura, Max, Nora, Owen, Paige, and Quinn (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new 9‑a‑side game later this same day between Rosa, Theo, Uma, Victor, Will, Xena, Yara, Zack, and Nora (Team 1, without Omar) and Omar, Iris, Jack, Kyle, Laura, Max, Owen, Paige, and Quinn (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
