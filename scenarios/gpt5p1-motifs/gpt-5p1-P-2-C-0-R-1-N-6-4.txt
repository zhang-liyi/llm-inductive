BACKGROUND
In this ultimate frisbee event, three squads of 8 players each play shortened games. Seven players per side are on the field at a time, with one substitute available. The result of each game depends on the players’ intrinsic throwing accuracy, speed, and field awareness, modulated by how much effort they put in during that game.
Players stay on their original 8-player squad for all games.
Priya plays for Team Zenith in every game with the same seven teammates.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Team Zenith beat Team Cascade.
In the second game, Team Zenith beat Team Nomad.
In the third game, Team Cascade beat Team Zenith.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random ultimate frisbee players, where do you think Priya ranks in terms of intrinsic playing ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Dylan (on Team Cascade) put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Team Zenith and Team Nomad, who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Team Cascade and Team Nomad, who would win and by how much?
