BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of 3-on-3 basketball games. In each game, the team that wins depends on the average shooting accuracy and defensive capability of the athletes, based on their intrinsic skill modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that game.
Athletes compete as a team of three.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Kobe, Shaq, and Pau beat Tim, Manu, and Tony.
In the second game, Kobe, Tim, and Manu beat Shaq, Pau, and Tony.
In the third game, Kobe, Tony, and Manu beat Shaq, Pau, and Tim.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Kobe ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Shaq put into the third race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Kobe and Shaq (Team 1) and Tim and Tony (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Tim and Manu (Team 1) and Shaq and Pau (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
