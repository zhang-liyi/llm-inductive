BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes are playing a series of indoor volleyball matches. In each match, the result depends on their intrinsic volleyball abilities (serving, passing, attacking, blocking) and how much effort they put into that match.
Players compete either individually in skill challenges or in 9‑a‑side team matches.
There are three squads—the Aurora, the Blaze, and the Cyclones—with generally fixed 9‑player lineups. Only one player, Priya, rotates between squads, filling in different lineups as the day goes on.
All matches and challenges take place on the same day.
