BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Volleyball games. In each game, the team that wins depends on the total points scored, based on the athletes' intrinsic jumping height and coordination modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that game.
Athletes compete in teams of 4.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Team Gold (Amy, Jen, Liz, and Sue) beat Team Red (Kim, Deb, Ann) and Casey.
In the second game, Team Blue (Fay, Joy, May, and Kay) beat Team Green (Val, Zoe, Bea) and Casey.
In the third game, Team Red (Kim, Deb, Ann, and Sue) beat Team Gold (Amy, Jen, Liz) and Casey.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Casey ranks in terms of intrinsic jumping height and coordination?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Casey put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Team Red (Kim, Deb, Ann) and Sue (Team 1) and Team Gold (Amy, Jen, Liz) and Casey (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Team Green (Val, Zoe, Bea) and Kay (Team 1) and Team Blue (Fay, Joy, May) and Casey (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
