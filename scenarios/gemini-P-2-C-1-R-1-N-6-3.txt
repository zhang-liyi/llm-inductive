BACKGROUND
In this competition, athletes play Field Hockey in an 8-a-side format. The winner is the team that scores the most goals, a result of the players' intrinsic stick skills and speed, modulated by the effort they put into the match.
The teams (Eagles, Hawks, Falcons) are fixed units, but Zoe is a designated "floater" who plays for different teams in specific rounds.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, the Eagles (with Zoe) beat the Hawks.
In the second match, the Falcons (with Zoe) beat the Hawks.
In the third match, the Falcons (with Zoe) lost to the Eagles.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Zoe ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think the Eagles put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between the Eagles (with Zoe) and the Falcons (without Zoe), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between the Hawks (with Zoe) and the Falcons (without Zoe), who would win and by how much?
