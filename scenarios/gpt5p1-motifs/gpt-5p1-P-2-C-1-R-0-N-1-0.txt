BACKGROUND
In this event, the players are competing in a series of half-court 3v3 basketball games. In each game, the team that wins depends on the players’ intrinsic basketball ability (including shooting accuracy, ball-handling, quickness, and decision-making), modulated by how much effort they put in during that particular game.
Players compete either individually or on three-player teams.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Jordan, Riley, and Sam beat Casey, Morgan, and Taylor.  
In the second game, Jordan, Casey, and Alex beat Riley, Morgan, and Sam.  
In the third game, Jordan lost to Riley.  
In the fourth game, Jordan, Morgan, and Taylor beat Casey, Alex, and Sam.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random basketball players, where do you think Jordan ranks in terms of intrinsic basketball ability?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Riley put into the third game?  
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Jordan, Riley, and Casey (Team 1) and Morgan, Taylor, and Alex (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Jordan, Sam, and Morgan (Team 1) and Riley, Casey, and Taylor (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
