BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes are competing in a field hockey tournament. Teams consist of 9 players on the field. The outcome of each game depends on the team's offensive and defensive coordination, based on the athletes' intrinsic agility and stick skills modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that game.
Athletes compete in fixed teams.
All games take place on the same afternoon.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, The Strikers (featuring Liam) beat The Defenders.
In the second game, The Strikers (featuring Liam) beat The Midfielders.
In the third game, The Strikers (featuring Liam) beat The Goalies.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random field hockey players, where do you think Liam ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think The Goalies put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same afternoon between The Strikers (featuring Liam) and The Defenders, who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same afternoon between The Defenders and The Midfielders, who would win and by how much?
