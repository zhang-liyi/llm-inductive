BACKGROUND  
In this event, the cyclists are competing on a velodrome in a series of track races. Most races are 3‑rider team sprints, and one race is an individual sprint. The outcome of each race depends on each cyclist’s intrinsic power and speed, along with how much effort they put into that particular race.  
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS  
In the first race, Mia, Lara, and Noor lost to Quinn, Rafael, and Samir.  
In the second race, Mia, Quinn, and Talia beat Rafael, Samir, and Noor.  
In the third race, Mia lost to Lara in an individual sprint.

QUERIES  
Query 1: Out of 100 random track cyclists, where do you think Mia ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Rafael put into the second race?  
Query 3: In a new 3‑rider team race later this same day between Mia, Lara, and Samir (Team 1) and Quinn, Noor, and Talia (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new individual sprint later this same day between Rafael and Noor, who would win and by how much?
