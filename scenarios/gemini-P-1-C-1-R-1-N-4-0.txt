BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes compete in a volleyball tournament. The winning team in each match is determined by the collective jumping height, spike power, and defensive coordination of the six players, based on their intrinsic athletic ability modulated by the effort they exert during the set.
Athletes compete in teams of six.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, the team of Leo, Sam, Tom, Ray, Ben, and Dan beat the team of Wes, Ian, Joe, Pat, Cal, and Moe.
In the second match, the team of Wes, Ian, Joe, Pat, Cal, and Moe beat the team of Leo, Sam, Tom, Ray, Ben, and Jaden.
In the third match, the team of Leo, Sam, Tom, Ray, Ben, and Dan beat the team of Wes, Ian, Joe, Pat, Cal, and Jaden.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Leo ranks in terms of intrinsic athletic ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Jaden put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between the team of Leo, Sam, Tom, Ray, Ben, and Dan (Team 1) and the team of Wes, Ian, Joe, Pat, Cal, and Jaden (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between a team of Leo, Sam, Tom, Wes, Ian, and Joe (Team 1) and a team of Ray, Ben, Jaden, Pat, Cal, and Moe (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
