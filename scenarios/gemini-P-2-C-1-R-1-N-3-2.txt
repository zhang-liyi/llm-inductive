BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes are competing in 5-a-side Soccer matches. The outcome of each match is determined by the average ball control and stamina of the team members, based on their intrinsic technical ability modulated by the intensity of effort they apply to that match.
Teams consist of 5 players.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Jamie and the City Squad beat the United Squad.
In the second match, Jamie and the United Squad beat the City Squad.
In the third match, Jamie and the City Squad lost to the United Squad.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random soccer players, where do you think Jamie ranks in terms of intrinsic technical ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Jamie put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Jamie and the United Squad (Team 1) and the City Squad (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between the City Squad (Team 1) and the United Squad (Team 2) without Jamie, who would win and by how much?
