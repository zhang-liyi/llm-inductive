BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Cycling (Road) time trials. In each race, the team that wins depends on the average speed the cyclists maintain, based on their intrinsic power output and aerodynamics modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that race.
Athletes compete as a fixed team of three.
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, Elena, Annemiek, and Demi beat Lotte, Marianne, and Elisa.
In the second race, Elena, Annemiek, and Demi beat Lotte, Marianne, and Elisa.
In the third race, Elena, Annemiek, and Demi lost to Lotte, Marianne, and Elisa.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Elena ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Elena put into the third race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Elena and Lotte (Team 1) and Annemiek and Marianne (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Demi and Elisa (Team 1) and Elena and Lotte (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
