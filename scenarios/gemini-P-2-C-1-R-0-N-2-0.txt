BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Ultimate Frisbee matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the collective scoring ability of the athletes, based on their intrinsic speed and throwing skills modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that match.
Athletes compete as a team of 4.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Jaden, Liam, Noah, and Ethan beat Mason, Lucas, Oliver, and Elijah.
In the second match, Jaden, Mason, Lucas, and Oliver beat Liam, Noah, Ethan, and Elijah.
In the third match, Mason, Lucas, Oliver, and Ethan beat Jaden, Elijah, Liam, and Noah.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Jaden ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Jaden put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Jaden, Liam, Noah, and Ethan (Team 1) and Mason, Lucas, Oliver, and Elijah (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Mason, Lucas, Oliver, and Ethan (Team 1) and Jaden, Elijah, Liam, and Noah (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
