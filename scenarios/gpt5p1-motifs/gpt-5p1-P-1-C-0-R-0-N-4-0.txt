BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of half-court basketball games. In each game, the team that wins depends on how accurately players shoot, how well they handle the ball, and how effectively they defend, all based on their intrinsic basketball ability and how much effort they put into that game.
Athletes compete either individually or in teams.
When competing as a team, each team consists of six players.
All games take place on the same day.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random basketball players, where do you think Alex ranks in terms of intrinsic basketball ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Riley put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Riley, Maya, Theo, Logan, Priya, and Nolan (Team 1) and Alex, Brooke, Cameron, Kai, Sienna, and Val (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
