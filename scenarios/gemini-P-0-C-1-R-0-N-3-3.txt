BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Ultimate Frisbee matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the score reached, based on the athletes' intrinsic agility and throwing precision modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete in teams of 5.
All matches take place on the same day.

In the first match, Brodie, Kurt, Beau, Dylan, and Jimmy beat George, Ken, Matt, Josh, and Andrew.
In the second match, Brodie, George, Ken, Matt, and Josh beat Kurt, Beau, Dylan, Jimmy, and Andrew.
In the third match, Brodie, Andrew, Marques, Nick, and Will beat Ben, Pawel, Rowan, Khalif, and Trent.

QUERIES

Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Kurt put into the second match?
