BACKGROUND  
In this event, three 8‑player softball lineups play a sequence of games. Game outcomes depend on players’ intrinsic hitting power, throwing accuracy, running speed, and fielding instincts, combined with how much effort they put into each at‑bat and defensive play that game.  
All players are on fixed teams for the entire event, and all games happen on the same day.

Team Hawks: Diego, Alicia, Brandon, Carmen, Eli, Fiona, Gabe, Harper  
Team Lions: Ivy, Jason, Kara, Luis, Mona, Neil, Olivia, Peter  
Team Bears: Quinn, Riley, Sara, Tomas, Uma, Victor, Wendy, Xavier  

Diego is on Team Hawks in every game he plays, always with the same seven teammates.

CONDITIONS  
In the first game, Team Hawks beat Team Lions.  
In the second game, Team Hawks beat Team Bears.  
In the third game, Team Lions beat Team Bears.

QUERIES  
Query 1: Out of 100 random softball players, where do you think Diego ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Jason from Team Lions put into the third game?  
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Team Hawks and Team Lions, who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Team Lions and Team Bears, who would win and by how much?
