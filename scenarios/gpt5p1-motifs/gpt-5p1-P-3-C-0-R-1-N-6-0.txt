BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a small-sided soccer tournament. Each team has a fixed squad of 8 players that stays the same in every game. 
The result of a game depends on the players’ intrinsic soccer ability combined with how much focused effort they put into that specific game.
All games take place on the same day.
Diego is on Team Orion in every game he plays.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Team Nova beat Team Orion.
In the second game, Team Eclipse beat Team Orion.
In the third game, Team Orion beat Team Nova.
In the fourth game, Team Nova beat Team Eclipse.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random soccer players, where do you think Diego ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Leo from Team Nova put into the second game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Team Orion and Team Eclipse, who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Team Nova and Team Orion, who would win and by how much?
