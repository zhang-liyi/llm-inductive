BACKGROUND  
In this event, the athletes are playing a series of five-a-side soccer matches. In each match, the outcome depends on the players’ intrinsic speed, ball control, passing vision, finishing ability, and defensive instincts, along with how much effort they put in.  
Matches are either 1-on-1 shootouts or 5-on-5 small-sided games.  
All matches take place on the same day.

In the first match, Liam beat Marco in a 1-on-1 shootout.

QUERIES
