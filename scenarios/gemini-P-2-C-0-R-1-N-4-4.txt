BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes are competing in a series of Handball matches. Each team has 6 players on the court. The outcome depends on the throwing velocity and defensive positioning of the athletes, based on their intrinsic power modulated by the defensive effort they exert during the match.
Teams are fixed for the duration of the event.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Hugo and his 5 teammates beat The Pivots.
In the second match, Hugo and his 5 teammates beat The Wingers.
In the third match, Hugo and his 5 teammates lost to The Keepers.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Hugo ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think the center player of The Keepers put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Hugo’s team (Team 1) and The Pivots (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between The Wingers (Team 1) and The Keepers (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
