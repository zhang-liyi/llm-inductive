BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of small-sided ice hockey games. Each game’s result depends on the players’ intrinsic hockey ability (skating, puck control, shooting, and defensive skill) along with how much effort they put into that game.
Athletes compete either individually (1-on-1 puck control challenges) or in teams of 5 skaters.
All games take place on the same day.

In the fourth game, Sergei, Boris, Henrik, Luca, and Matt lost to Anton, Dylan, Filip, Grant, and Nils.

QUERIES

Query 4: In a new 1-on-1 puck control challenge later this same day between Sergei and Erik, who would win and by how much?
