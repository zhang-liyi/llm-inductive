BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes compete in a 3-on-3 basketball tournament. The winning team is determined by the collective scoring ability and defensive prowess of the trio, based on their intrinsic agility and shooting skills, modulated by the effort they exert during the game.
Athletes compete either individually or as a team of three.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Liam, Noah, and Lucas beat Mason, Ethan, and Ellis.
In the second match, Mason, Ethan, and Lucas beat Liam, Noah, and Ellis.
In the third match, Ellis lost to Liam.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Ellis ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Mason put into the third race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Ellis and Noah (Team 1) and Lucas and Ethan (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Lucas and Mason (Team 1) and Noah and Liam (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
