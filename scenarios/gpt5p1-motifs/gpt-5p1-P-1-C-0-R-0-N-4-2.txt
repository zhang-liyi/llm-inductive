BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of indoor volleyball games. In each game, the team that wins depends on serving accuracy, passing, setting, hitting, and defensive coverage, determined by each player’s intrinsic volleyball skills and the amount of effort they put into that game.
Athletes compete either individually (in skill-based duels) or in teams.
When competing as a team, each team consists of six players.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Caleb lost to Diego.
In the second game, Caleb, Aria, Ben, Lila, Jonah, and Mei lost to Isla, Felix, Grace, Henry, Ivy, and Noah.
In the third game, Caleb, Aria, Ben, Lila, Jonah, and Mei lost to Diego, Grace, Henry, Paige, Rowan, and Sara.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random volleyball players, where do you think Diego ranks in terms of intrinsic volleyball ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Caleb put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Caleb, Aria, Ben, Lila, Jonah, and Mei (Team 1) and Diego, Isla, Grace, Henry, Ivy, and Paige (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Felix, Noah, Rowan, Sara, Yara, and Zack (Team 1) and Diego, Grace, Henry, Ivy, Paige, and Lila (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
