BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Ultimate Frisbee matches. Each team consists of 7 athletes. In each match, the team that wins depends on the average agility and throwing accuracy of the athletes, based on their intrinsic skill modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete in fixed teams.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, the team containing Sarah beat the team containing Rory.
In the second match, the team containing Mike beat the team containing Rory.
In the third match, the team containing Sarah beat the team containing Mike.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Rory ranks in terms of intrinsic skill?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Mike's team put into the second match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between the team containing Rory (Team 1) and the team containing Mike (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between the team containing Sarah (Team 1) and the team containing Mike (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
