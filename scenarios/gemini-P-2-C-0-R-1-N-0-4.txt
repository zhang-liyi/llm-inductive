BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Table Tennis matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the spin control and footwork of the athletes, based on their intrinsic technique modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete as a fixed pair.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Ma and Fan beat Timo and Dimitrij.
In the second match, Ma and Fan beat Timo and Dimitrij.
In the third match, Ma and Fan lost to Timo and Dimitrij.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Ma ranks in terms of intrinsic technique?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Ma put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Ma and Fan (Team 1) and Timo and a new partner, Hugo (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Timo and Dimitrij (Team 1) and Fan and Hugo (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
