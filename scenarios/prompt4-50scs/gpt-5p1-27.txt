BACKGROUND  
In this event, snowboarders are competing in a series of snowboard cross heats. In each heat, the winner is the one or team that finishes ahead, based on intrinsic board control, explosiveness, and decision-making, plus the intensity of effort they bring to that run.  
Some heats are solo, and some involve three-person teams riding together, with the team’s result based on the combined performance of its riders.  
All heats occur on the same day.

CONDITIONS

In the third heat, Harper beat Mira.  
In the fourth heat, Dylan and Harper beat Kai and Zoe.

QUERIES  
Query 1: Out of 100 random snowboarders, where do you think Kai ranks in terms of intrinsic riding ability?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Harper put into the third heat?  
Query 3: In a new heat later this same day between Kai and Mira (Team 1) and Dylan and Harper (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new heat later this same day between Zoe (Team 1) and Liam and Harper (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
