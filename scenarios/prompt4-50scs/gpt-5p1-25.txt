BACKGROUND  
In this event, the skiers are competing in a series of slalom runs in alpine skiing. In each run, the winner is the skier who completes the course in the least time, determined by their intrinsic technical skill, balance, and control, combined with how much effort and focus they put into that particular run.  
All runs take place on the same day, and all skiers compete individually.

CONDITIONS  
In the first run, Lara beat Mika.  
In the second run, Jonas beat Lara.  
In the third run, Mika beat Petra.  
In the fourth run, Jonas lost to Petra.

QUERIES  
Query 1: Out of 100 random alpine skiers, where do you think Lara ranks in terms of intrinsic strength and skill?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Jonas put into the fourth run?  
Query 3: In a new run later this same day between Lara and Petra, who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new run later this same day between Mika and Jonas, who would win and by how much?
