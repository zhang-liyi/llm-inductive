BACKGROUND
In this event, the players are competing in a series of team tennis matchups. For each matchup, the combined results of the four players on each side determine the winning team. Performance in each matchup depends on the players’ intrinsic tennis ability and on how much effort they put into that matchup.  
Players sometimes play singles duels and sometimes play as part of a 4‑player team.  
All matchups take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first matchup, Elena beat Grace.  
In the second matchup, Elena, Marco, Nina, and Felix beat Grace, Oliver, Hannah, and Samir.  
In the third matchup, Grace, Marco, Hannah, and Leo beat Elena, Oliver, Nina, and Felix.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random tennis players, where do you think Elena ranks in terms of intrinsic tennis ability?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Grace put into the third matchup?  
Query 3: In a new matchup later this same day between Elena, Marco, Hannah, and Samir (Team 1) and Grace, Oliver, Nina, and Leo (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new matchup later this same day between Elena, Felix, Oliver, and Grace (Team 1) and Marco, Nina, Leo, and Hannah (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
