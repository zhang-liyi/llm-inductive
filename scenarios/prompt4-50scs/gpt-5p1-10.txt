BACKGROUND  
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of small-sided rugby matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the players’ intrinsic strength, speed, and decision-making, modulated by how much effort they put in during that match.  
Athletes compete either individually (in skill-focused 1-on-1 contests) or in small teams.  
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS  
In the first match, Dana beat Leo.  
In the second match, Dana and Morgan beat Leo and Priya.  
In the third match, Morgan lost to Priya.

QUERIES  
Query 1: Out of 100 random rugby athletes, where do you think Dana ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Morgan put into the third match?  
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Dana and Morgan (Team 1) and Priya and Taylor (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Leo and Priya (Team 1) and Morgan and Casey (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
