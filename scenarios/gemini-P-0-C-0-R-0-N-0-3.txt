BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Pickleball matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on their ability to control the court and score points, based on their intrinsic hand-eye coordination, modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete as a team of two.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Otis and Mae beat Gus and Hank.
In the second match, Otis and Mae beat Hank and Deb.
In the third match, Otis and Mae beat Gus and Deb.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Otis ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Gus put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Otis and Mae (Team 1) and Gus and June (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Hank and Deb (Team 1) and Mae and Saul (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
