BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes are competing in a series of Ultimate Frisbee games. In each game, the team that wins depends on the speed and throwing accuracy of the athletes, based on their intrinsic agility modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that game.
Athletes compete in teams of 5.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, The Gusts beat Morgan, Ash, Elm, Oak, and Fir.
In the second game, The Gales beat Morgan, Red, Blu, Yel, and Grn.
In the third game, Morgan, One, Two, Thr, and Fou beat The Storms.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Morgan ranks in terms of intrinsic agility?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Ash put into the first game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Morgan, Ash, Elm, Oak, and Fir (Team 1) and Morgan, One, Two, Thr, and Fou (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between The Gusts (Team 1) and The Gales (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
