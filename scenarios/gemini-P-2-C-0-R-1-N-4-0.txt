BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes compete in a Volleyball tournament. Each team consists of 6 players. The winning team is determined by the collective jumping height, hitting power, and defensive coordination of the players, based on their intrinsic athleticism modulated by the effort they exert during the match.
Teams are fixed throughout the tournament.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Elena and her 5 teammates beat The Spikers.
In the second match, Elena and her 5 teammates beat The Blockers.
In the third match, Elena and her 5 teammates lost to The Diggers.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Elena ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think the captain of The Diggers put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Elena’s team (Team 1) and The Spikers (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between The Blockers (Team 1) and The Diggers (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
