BACKGROUND  
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of small-sided 7-on-7 soccer matches. In each match, the winning team is the one that is able to create and finish more chances, based on the players’ intrinsic soccer ability and the amount of effort they put into that match.  
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS  
In the first match, Team A (captained by Leo) beat Team B (captained by Sam).  
In the second match, Leo and Jamie joined Team B, and Team B beat Team C (captained by Omar).  
In the third match, Sam and Omar played together on Team D and beat Leo’s new team, Team E.

QUERIES  
Query 1: Out of 100 random soccer players, where do you think Leo ranks in terms of intrinsic soccer ability?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Sam put into the third match?  
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Leo, Jamie, and four randomly selected players (Team 1) and Sam, Omar, and four randomly selected players of similar overall level (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Leo, Sam, and five randomly selected players (Team 1) and Jamie, Omar, and five randomly selected players (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
