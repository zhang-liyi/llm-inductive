BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes are competing in Field Hockey matches adapted for 7 players per side. The winner is determined by the stick-handling speed and tactical vision of the athletes (intrinsic talent), modulated by how much physical effort they exert to control the field.
Athletes compete in fixed teams of 7 and do not swap teammates.
All matches occur on the same turf.
