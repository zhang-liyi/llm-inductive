BACKGROUND  
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of field hockey matches. The winner of each match is determined by the players’ intrinsic stick skills, speed, and coordination, along with how much effort they put into that match.  
Players compete either individually in shooting contests or on teams.  
When teams play, each side uses 9 players. All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS  
In the first match, Serena, Aidan, and Chloe lost to Marcus, Ivy, and Dylan.  
In the second match, Serena, Marcus, and Leah lost to Aidan, Chloe, and Ivy.  
In the third match, Serena beat Dylan in a shooting contest.  
In the fourth match, Chloe, Dylan, and Leah beat Marcus, Aidan, and Nora.

QUERIES  
Query 1: Out of 100 random field hockey players, where do you think Serena ranks in terms of intrinsic field hockey ability?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Marcus put into the second match?  
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Serena, Chloe, and Dylan (Team 1) and Marcus, Aidan, and Ivy (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Serena, Leah, and Nora (Team 1) and Aidan, Chloe, and Ivy (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
