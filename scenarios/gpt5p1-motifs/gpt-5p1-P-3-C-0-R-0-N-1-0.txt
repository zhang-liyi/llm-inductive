BACKGROUND

In each game, the team that wins depends on the players’ intrinsic soccer ability—such as touch, coordination, and speed—combined with how much effort they put into that specific game. All games take place on the same day.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random soccer players, where do you think Diego ranks in terms of intrinsic soccer ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Cole put into the second game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Diego, Ravi, and Leo (Team 1) and Mia, Omar, and Felix (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Tara, Cole, and Nina (Team 1) and Leo, Mia, and Casey (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
