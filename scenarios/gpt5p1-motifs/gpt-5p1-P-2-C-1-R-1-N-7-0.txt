BACKGROUND
In this event, players compete in a series of small-sided soccer contests. In each contest, the outcome depends on each player's intrinsic soccer ability (touch, speed, vision, finishing) and how much effort they put in during that contest.
Players compete either individually or in 9-a-side team matches.
There are several local clubs (the Falcons, the Wolves, and the Rangers) that each have a fixed 9‑player lineup. Only one player, Diego, sometimes fills in for different clubs in place of one of their regulars.
All contests take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first contest, Diego beat Leo.
In the second match, Diego and the Falcons beat the Wolves.
In the third match, Diego and the Rangers beat the Falcons.
In the fourth match, Diego and the Wolves lost to the Rangers.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random small‑sided soccer players, where do you think Diego ranks in terms of intrinsic soccer ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Leo put into the first contest?
Query 3: In a new 9‑a‑side match later this same day between Diego and the Falcons (Team 1) and Leo and the Wolves (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new 9‑a‑side match later this same day between Diego and the Rangers (Team 1) and Marco and the Wolves (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
