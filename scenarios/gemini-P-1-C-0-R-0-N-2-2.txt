BACKGROUND
In this meet, athletes are competing in 4x400m Sprint Track relays. In each race, the winning team is determined by the cumulative speed of the runners, based on their intrinsic sprinting speed modulated by factors such as fatigue and the effort they put into that specific race.
Athletes compete in teams of 4.
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, the team of Owen, Peter, Quinn, and Ryan beat the team of Jaden, Kai, Liam, and Noah.
In the second race, the team of Owen, Peter, Seth, and Toby beat the team of Jaden, Kai, Liam, and Noah.
In the third race, the team of Quinn, Ryan, Seth, and Toby beat the team of Jaden, Kai, Liam, and Noah.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Jaden ranks in terms of intrinsic sprinting speed?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Seth put into the third race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Jaden, Kai, Liam, and Noah (Team 1) and Owen, Quinn, Seth, and Vince (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Owen, Peter, Quinn, and Ryan (Team 1) and Seth, Toby, Vince, and Walt (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
