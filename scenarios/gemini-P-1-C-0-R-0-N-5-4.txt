BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes compete in Field Hockey matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the goals scored, based on the athletes' intrinsic stick work and stamina modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete in teams of 7.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, the team of Rohan, Vikram, and 5 others beat the team of Nina, Priya, and 5 others.
In the second match, the team of Arjun, Kabir, and 5 others beat the team of Nina, Priya, and 5 different players.
In the third match, the team of Rohan, Arjun, and 5 others beat the team of Nina, Priya, and a new mix of 5 players.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Nina ranks in terms of intrinsic stick work and stamina?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Arjun put into the second match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between a team led by Rohan and Arjun (Team 1) and a team led by Nina and Priya (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between a team led by Vikram and Kabir (Team 1) and a team led by Nina and a random player (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
