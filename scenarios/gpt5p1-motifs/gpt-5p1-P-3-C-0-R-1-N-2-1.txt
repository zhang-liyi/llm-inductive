BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in 4×100m freestyle relay swimming races. Each relay is decided by the combined time of the four swimmers, which depends on their intrinsic swimming speed and technique, as well as how much effort they put into that race. All relays take place on the same day. Teams are fixed and always use the same four swimmers.

CONDITIONS
Relay Aurora consists of Marcus, Dylan, Hugo, and Ben.
Relay Comet consists of Oliver, Liam, Theo, and Jack.
Relay Tidal consists of Ryan, Leo, Finn, and Adam.

In the first relay, Relay Comet beat Relay Aurora.
In the second relay, Relay Tidal beat Relay Aurora.
In the third relay, Relay Aurora beat Relay Tidal.
In the fourth relay, Relay Comet beat Relay Tidal.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random competitive swimmers, where do you think Marcus ranks in terms of intrinsic swimming ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Ryan put into the third relay?
Query 3: In a new relay later this same day between Marcus, Dylan, Hugo, and Ben (Team 1) and Oliver, Liam, Theo, and Jack (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new relay later this same day between Ryan, Leo, Finn, and Adam (Team 1) and a new team of four swimmers, Sam, Peter, Owen, and Luke (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
