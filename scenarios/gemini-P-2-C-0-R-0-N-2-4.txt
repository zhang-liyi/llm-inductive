BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of 4x400m sprint track relays. In each race, the team that wins depends on the average velocity the athletes are able to maintain, based on their intrinsic speed and stamina modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that race.
Athletes compete in teams of four.
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, Usain, Asafa, Yohan, and Nesta beat Justin, Tyson, Fred, and Kenny.
In the second race, Usain, Asafa, Yohan, and Nesta lost to Justin, Tyson, Andre, and De.
In the third race, Usain, Asafa, Yohan, and Nesta beat Fred, Kenny, Andre, and De.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Usain ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Usain put into the second race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Usain, Asafa, Yohan, and Nesta (Team 1) and Justin, Tyson, Fred, and Kenny (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Justin, Tyson, Andre, and De (Team 1) and Fred, Kenny, Yohan, and Nesta (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
