BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes compete in a series of mini-rink ice hockey matches. The team that wins depends on the average skating speed and puck-handling accuracy of the athletes, based on their intrinsic coordination modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that match.
Athletes compete either individually or as a team of three.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Jack, Ryan, and Leo beat Finn, Cole, and Sasha.
In the second match, Finn, Cole, and Leo beat Jack, Ryan, and Sasha.
In the third match, Sasha lost to Jack.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Sasha ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Finn put into the third race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Sasha and Ryan (Team 1) and Leo and Cole (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Leo and Finn (Team 1) and Ryan and Jack (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
