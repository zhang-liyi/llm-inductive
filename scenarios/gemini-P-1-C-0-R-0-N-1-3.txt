BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of lacrosse scrimmages. In each scrimmage, the team that wins depends on the stick skills and coordination of the athletes, based on their intrinsic talent modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that scrimmage.
Athletes compete as a team of three.
All scrimmages take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first scrimmage, Ryan, Mark, and John beat Logan, Kyle, and Evan.
In the second scrimmage, Ryan, Peter, and Luke beat Logan, Kyle, and Evan.
In the third scrimmage, Mark, Peter, and Seth beat Logan, Kyle, and Evan.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Logan ranks in terms of intrinsic talent?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Ryan put into the second scrimmage?
Query 3: In a new scrimmage later this same day between Ryan and Mark (Team 1) and Logan and Kyle (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new scrimmage later this same day between Logan and Evan (Team 1) and John and Luke (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
