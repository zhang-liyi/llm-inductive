BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Cycling (Track) team pursuit races. In each race, the team that wins depends on the speed maintained by the paceline, based on their intrinsic wattage and aerodynamics modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that race.
Athletes compete in fixed teams of four.
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, Daniel, Matthew, Samuel, and David beat Joseph, Carter, Wyatt, and John.
In the second race, Daniel, Matthew, Samuel, and David beat Joseph, Carter, Wyatt, and John.
In the third race, Joseph, Carter, Wyatt, and John lost to Daniel, Matthew, Samuel, and David.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Daniel ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Joseph put into the third race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Daniel, Matthew, Samuel, and David (Team 1) and Joseph, Carter, Wyatt, and John (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Daniel, Matthew, Joseph, and Carter (Team 1) and Samuel, David, Wyatt, and John (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
