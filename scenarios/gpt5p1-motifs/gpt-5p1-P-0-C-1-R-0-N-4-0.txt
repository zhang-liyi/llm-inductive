BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of indoor volleyball matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on how powerfully and accurately the athletes can serve, pass, set, and hit, based on their intrinsic volleyball ability and how much effort they put in for that match.
Athletes compete either individually (for serving duels) or in teams of 6 players.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Riley, Jordan, Maya, Leo, Sam, and Brooke beat Devin, Quinn, Harper, Alex, Taylor, and Nico.
In the second match, Riley, Quinn, Harper, Sam, Brooke, and Nico beat Devin, Jordan, Maya, Leo, Alex, and Taylor.
In the third match, Riley beat Devin in a serving duel.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random volleyball players, where do you think Riley ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Devin put into the serving duel in the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Riley, Devin, Jordan, Harper, Alex, and Nico (Team 1) and Maya, Leo, Sam, Brooke, Quinn, and Taylor (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Jordan, Quinn, Maya, Brooke, Alex, and Taylor (Team 1) and Devin, Sam, Leo, Harper, Nico, and Riley (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
