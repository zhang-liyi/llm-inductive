BACKGROUND  
In this event, the athletes are playing a small-sided 3-on-3 soccer tournament.  
For each match, the result depends on the players’ intrinsic soccer abilities (such as touch, speed, and finishing) and how much effort they put in during that match.  
Teams are fixed: each group of three always plays together.  
All matches are played on the same day.

CONDITIONS  
Team River: Maya, Priya, Zoe  
Team Summit: Lucas, Ryan, Emma  
Team Harbor: Jack, Nina, Oscar  

In the first match, Summit beat River.  
In the second match, Harbor beat River.  
In the third match, Summit beat Harbor.

QUERIES  
Query 1: Out of 100 random recreational soccer players, where do you think Lucas ranks in terms of intrinsic soccer ability?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Maya put into the second match?  
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between River and Summit, who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between River and Harbor, who would win and by how much?
