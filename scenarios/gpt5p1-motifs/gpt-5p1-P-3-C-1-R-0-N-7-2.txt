BACKGROUND  
In this event, the players are competing in a series of volleyball games. The outcome of each game depends on players’ intrinsic volleyball abilities such as jumping, timing, and ball control, together with how much effort they put into that game.  
Players compete either individually in serving duels or on teams.  
When teams play, each side has 9 players on the court. All games take place on the same day.

In the fourth game, Lila, Mia, and Stella beat Harper, Owen, and Noah.

QUERIES  
Query 1: Out of 100 random volleyball players, where do you think Jared ranks in terms of intrinsic volleyball ability?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Harper put into the second game?  
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Jared, Lila, and Stella (Team 1) and Harper, Owen, and Quinn (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Jared, Mia, and Noah (Team 1) and Lila, Quinn, and Stella (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
