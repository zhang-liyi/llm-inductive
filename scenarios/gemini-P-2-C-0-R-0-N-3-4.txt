BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Volleyball matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on their set dominance, based on the athletes' intrinsic jump height and spiking power modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete in teams of 5.
All matches take place on the same day.

In the first match, Gabi, She, Tha, Fab, and Dan beat Zhu, Yan, Din, Gon, and Bos.

In the third match, Gabi, She, Tha, Fab, and Dan lost to Din, Gon, Ego, Ogn, and Syll.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Gabi ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Gabi put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Gabi, She, Tha, Fab, and Dan (Team 1) and Zhu, Yan, Din, Ego, and Ogn (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Din, Gon, Ego, Ogn, and Syll (Team 1) and Bos, Zhu, Yan, She, and Tha (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
