BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes compete in 5-on-5 beach ultimate frisbee games. The winner is determined by the team's offensive flow and defensive pressure, based on the athletes' intrinsic throwing accuracy and speed, modulated by how much cutting effort they put into the game.
Sarah plays on a fixed team called "The Huckers" with teammates Emily, Grace, Chloe, and Zoe.
All games are played back-to-back on the same afternoon.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, The Disc Jockeys beat The Huckers.
In the second game, The Sky Walkers beat The Huckers.
In the third game, The Huckers beat The Sand Crabs.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Sarah ranks in terms of intrinsic throwing accuracy and speed?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much cutting effort do you think Sarah put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same afternoon between The Huckers (Team 1) and The Disc Jockeys (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same afternoon between a team comprising Sarah, Emily, and three players from The Sky Walkers (Team 1) and a team comprising Grace, Chloe, and three players from The Sand Crabs (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
