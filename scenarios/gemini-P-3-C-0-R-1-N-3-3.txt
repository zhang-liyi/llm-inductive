<START_SCENARIO>
BACKGROUND
Athletes are competing in a series of 5-on-5 ice hockey scrimmages (excluding goalies for this metric). The winning team is determined by puck possession and shots on goal, derived from the athletes' intrinsic skating speed and stick-handling, modulated by the physical grit and checking effort they apply in that scrimmage.
Ethan is part of a fixed unit called "The Blades" along with teammates Mason, Logan, Aiden, and Lucas.
All scrimmages take place during the same training camp session.

CONDITIONS
In the first scrimmage, The Enforcers beat The Blades.
In the second scrimmage, The Icemen beat The Blades.
In the third scrimmage, The Blades beat The Pucks.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Ethan ranks in terms of intrinsic skating speed and stick-handling?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much physical grit do you think Ethan put into the third scrimmage?
Query 3: In a new scrimmage later this same session between The Blades (Team 1) and The Enforcers (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new scrimmage later this same session between a team formed by Ethan, Mason, and three players from The Icemen (Team 1) and a team formed by Logan, Aiden, and three players from The Pucks (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
