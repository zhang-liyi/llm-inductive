BACKGROUND  
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of squash matches. Each result depends on the players’ intrinsic squash ability—speed, control, and tactical awareness—together with how much effort they put into that match.  
Athletes compete in both singles and doubles formats.  
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS  
In the first match, Sam beat Taylor.  
In the second match, Sam and Jordan lost to Taylor and Morgan.  
In the third match, Jordan beat Morgan.

QUERIES  
Query 1: Out of 100 random squash players, where do you think Sam ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Morgan put into the third match?  
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Sam and Morgan (Team 1) and Taylor and Jordan (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new singles match later this same day between Sam (Player 1) and Morgan (Player 2), who would win and by how much?
