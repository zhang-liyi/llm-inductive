BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes are competing in a series of field hockey matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the goals scored, based on the athletes' intrinsic stick-handling and running speed modulated by the effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete in fixed teams of 8.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, the team of Simon, Ann, Bea, Cat, Dot, Elle, Flo, and Gin lost to the team of Hope, Ida, Jen, Kim, Liz, Mia, Noa, and Pat.
In the second match, the team of Hope, Ida, Jen, Kim, Liz, Mia, Noa, and Pat beat the team of Simon, Ann, Bea, Cat, Dot, Elle, Flo, and Gin.
In the third match, the team of Simon, Ann, Bea, Cat, Dot, Elle, Flo, and Gin lost to the team of Hope, Ida, Jen, Kim, Liz, Mia, Noa, and Pat.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Simon ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Hope put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Simon and Ann (Team 1) and Hope and Ida (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Bea and Cat (Team 1) and Jen and Kim (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
