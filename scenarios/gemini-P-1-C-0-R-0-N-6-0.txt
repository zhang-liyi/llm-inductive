BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Rugby matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the collective power and tactical execution of the athletes, based on their intrinsic strength modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that match.
Athletes compete in teams of 8.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, the team of Liam, Noah, Oliver, Elijah, James, William, Benjamin, and Lucas lost to the team of Henry, Alexander, Michael, Daniel, Jacob, Logan, Jackson, and Sebastian.
In the second match, the team of Liam, Noah, Henry, Alexander, Michael, Daniel, Jacob, and Logan lost to the team of Oliver, Elijah, James, William, Benjamin, Lucas, Jackson, and Sebastian.
In the third match, the team of Liam, Noah, Jackson, Sebastian, Oliver, Elijah, James, and William lost to the team of Henry, Alexander, Michael, Daniel, Jacob, Logan, Benjamin, and Lucas.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Liam ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Henry put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Liam and Noah (Team 1) and Henry and Alexander (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Henry and Oliver (Team 1) and Noah and Jackson (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
