BACKGROUND  
In this event, the rowers are competing in a series of 3‑person sculling races. Each boat’s speed depends on the rowers’ intrinsic rowing strength and technique, together with how much effort they put into that particular race.  
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS  
In the first race, Priya, Lena, and Marco lost to Hugo, Nia, and Victor.  
In the second race, Priya, Hugo, and Zoe lost to Lena, Marco, and Nia.  
In the third race, Priya, Victor, and Nia beat Hugo, Lena, and Zoe.  
In the fourth race, Priya, Marco, and Zoe lost to Victor, Hugo, and Nia.

QUERIES  
Query 1: Out of 100 random rowers, where do you think Priya ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Lena put into the second race?  
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Priya, Hugo, and Nia (Team 1) and Victor, Lena, and Marco (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Priya, Victor, and Zoe (Team 1) and Hugo, Marco, and Nia (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
