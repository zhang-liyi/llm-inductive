BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes are competing in a series of rugby sevens-style scrimmages (adapted for 8 players). In each match, the team that wins depends on the points scored, based on the athletes' intrinsic physicality and tactical skill modulated by the effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete in fixed teams of 8.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, the team of Felix, Ash, Bev, Cal, Deb, Eve, Fay, and Gus lost to the team of Hal, Ivy, Jay, Kay, Lee, Mel, Nan, and Ope.
In the second match, the team of Hal, Ivy, Jay, Kay, Lee, Mel, Nan, and Ope beat the team of Felix, Ash, Bev, Cal, Deb, Eve, Fay, and Gus.
In the third match, the team of Felix, Ash, Bev, Cal, Deb, Eve, Fay, and Gus lost to the team of Hal, Ivy, Jay, Kay, Lee, Mel, Nan, and Ope.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Felix ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Hal put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Felix and Ash (Team 1) and Hal and Ivy (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Bev and Cal (Team 1) and Jay and Kay (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
