BACKGROUND
In this competition, athletes participate in a modified 8-a-side cricket tournament. The winning team is decided by the total runs scored and wickets taken, which depends on the intrinsic batting and bowling abilities of the 8 team members, modulated by their focus and effort during the match.
The teams are fixed, meaning the roster of 8 players does not change.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Rohan's team beat Vikram's team.
In the second match, Rohan's team beat Arjun's team.
In the third match, Rohan's team lost to Kabir's team.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random cricketers, where do you think Rohan ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Rohan's team put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Rohan's team (Team 1) and Vikram's team (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Kabir's team (Team 1) and Arjun's team (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
