BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of 3-on-3 ice hockey games. In each game, the team that wins depends on their offensive production, based on their intrinsic skating speed and puck handling, modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that game.
Athletes compete either individually or as a team of three.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Gordie, Bobby, and Hull beat Guy, Mark, and Paul.
In the second game, Gordie, Guy, and Mark beat Bobby, Hull, and Paul.
In the third game, Gordie beat Guy.
In the fourth game, Gordie, Paul, and Bobby lost to Guy, Mark, and Hull.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Gordie ranks in terms of intrinsic skating speed?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Gordie put into the fourth game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Gordie, Bobby, and Hull (Team 1) and Guy, Mark, and Paul (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Guy, Mark, and Bobby (Team 1) and Gordie, Hull, and Paul (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
