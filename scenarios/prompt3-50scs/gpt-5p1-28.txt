BACKGROUND
In this event, the surfers are competing in a series of heats. In each heat, the winner is the surfer or team whose wave scores are highest, determined by intrinsic paddling strength, positioning, and maneuvering ability, modulated by how much effort and commitment they put into each wave.
Surfers compete either individually or in small teams of two whose scores are combined.
All heats take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first heat, Lina beat Marco.
In the second heat, Lina and Owen beat Marco and Sera.
In the third heat, Sera beat Owen.
In the fourth heat, Marco beat Owen.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random surfers, where do you think Marco ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Lina put into the second heat?
Query 3: In a new heat later this same day between Lina and Sera (Team 1) and Marco and Owen (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new heat later this same day between Marco and Sera (Team 1) and Lina and a new surfer, Theo (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
