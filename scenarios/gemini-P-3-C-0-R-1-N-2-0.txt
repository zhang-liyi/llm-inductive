BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes compete in a series of **Rowing** races. The winning boat is determined by the average power output of the rowers, based on their intrinsic physical strength modulated by the effort they exert during that specific race. Athletes compete either individually (single sculls) or in fixed teams of four (coxless four). All races occur on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, Silas beat Arthur.
In the second race, Team Silas (Silas, Jude, Finn, Ezra) beat Team Arthur (Arthur, Leo, Henry, Jack).
In the third race, Team Arthur (Arthur, Leo, Henry, Jack) beat Team Owen (Owen, Caleb, Isaac, Levi).

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Arthur ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Leo put into the third race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Team Silas and Team Owen, who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between a team of Arthur and Silas (Team 1) and a team of Jude and Leo (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
