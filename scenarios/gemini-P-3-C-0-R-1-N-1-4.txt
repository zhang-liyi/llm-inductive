BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of rowing races (triples). In each race, the team that wins depends on the average power output the athletes can generate, based on their intrinsic strength modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that race.
Athletes compete in fixed teams of three.
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, Kate, Liz, and Mary beat Elena, Sarah, and Jen.
In the second race, Kate, Liz, and Mary beat Elena, Sarah, and Jen.
In the third race, Elena, Sarah, and Jen beat Kate, Liz, and Mary.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Elena ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Elena put into the third race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Elena and Liz (Team 1) and Kate and Sarah (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Jen and Mary (Team 1) and Sarah and Liz (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
