BACKGROUND
This event is a series of small-sided soccer games played 2 versus 2. In each game, the result is determined by the players’ intrinsic soccer skills (like ball control, speed, and awareness) combined with how much effort they put into that particular game. Higher total intrinsic skill and game-specific effort from a team leads to better performance.
All games are played on the same day.
Players always compete in fixed pairs: Carla and Omar form one team, Priya and Ben form another, and Hugo and Lina form a third.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Carla and Omar beat Priya and Ben.
In the second game, Hugo and Lina beat Priya and Ben.
In the third game, Carla and Omar beat Hugo and Lina.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random small-sided soccer players, where do you think Carla ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Priya put into the second game?
Query 3: In a new 2v2 game later this same day between Carla and Omar versus Priya and Ben, who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new 2v2 game later this same day between Hugo and Lina versus Carla and Omar, who would win and by how much?
