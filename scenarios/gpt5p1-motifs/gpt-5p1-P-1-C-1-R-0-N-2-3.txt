BACKGROUND

Players compete either individually or as a team.
All matches take place on the same day.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Serena ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?

Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Yuki, Nadia, Finn, and Logan (Team 1) and Serena, Pavel, Iris, and Omar (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Pavel and Serena (Team 1) and Yuki and Finn (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
