BACKGROUND  
In this event, the players are competing in a series of half-court basketball games. In each game, the team that wins depends on the players’ intrinsic shooting accuracy, ball-handling, and defensive ability, modulated by how much effort they put into that specific game.  
Players compete either 1-on-1 or in 2-on-2 teams.  
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS  
In the first game, Jamal and Owen beat Riley and Marco.  
In the second game, Jamal beat Devin.  
In the third game, Jamal and Owen beat Devin and Riley.

QUERIES  
Query 1: Out of 100 random basketball players, where do you think Jamal ranks in terms of intrinsic skill?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Devin put into the third game?  
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Jamal and Owen (Team 1) and Marco and Devin (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Riley and Marco (Team 1) and Owen and Leo (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
