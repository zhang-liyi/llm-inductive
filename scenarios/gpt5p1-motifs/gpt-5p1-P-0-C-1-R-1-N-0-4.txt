BACKGROUND
In this event, players are competing in a series of pickleball games. The result of each game depends on the players’ intrinsic pickleball skills (shot accuracy, reactions, positioning) along with how much effort they put into that specific game.
Games are usually doubles, and all games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Sofia and James beat Olivia and Chloe.
In the second game, Sofia and Olivia beat James and Ben.
In the third game, in a singles game, Sofia beat Ben.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Sofia ranks in terms of intrinsic pickleball ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Ben put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new doubles game later this same day between Sofia and Chloe (Team 1) and James and Ben (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new doubles game later this same day between Olivia and Chloe (Team 1) and James and Ben (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
