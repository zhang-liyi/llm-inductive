BACKGROUND
In this small-sided soccer event, players compete in 2-on-2 games on a reduced field. The team that wins each game is determined by the players’ intrinsic soccer ability—such as ball control, speed, and finishing—together with the level of effort they choose to put in. Most players keep the same partner, but Sofia frequently plays with different teammates. All games are played on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Sofia and Lucas beat David and Raúl.  
In the second game, Sofia and Carla lost to David and Raúl.  
In the third game, Sofia and Irene beat David and Raúl.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random soccer players, where do you think Sofia ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Carla put into the second game?  
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Sofia and Raúl (Team 1) and David and Lucas (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Carla and Irene (Team 1) and David and Mateo (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
