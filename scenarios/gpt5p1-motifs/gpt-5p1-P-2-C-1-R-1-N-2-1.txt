BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of touch rugby games and possible 1-on-1 drills. In each contest, the result depends on the players’ intrinsic rugby ability (speed, agility, and ball-handling) modulated by how much effort they put into that contest.
Athletes compete either individually or in teams of four.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random touch rugby players, where do you think Nora ranks in terms of intrinsic rugby ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Blake put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new 4-on-4 game later this same day between Nora, Grace, Jordan, and Kai (Team 1) and Blake, Casey, Dylan, and Ethan (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
