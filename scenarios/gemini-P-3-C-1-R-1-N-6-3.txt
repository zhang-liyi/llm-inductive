BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes are competing in field hockey matches using an 8-a-side format. The winning team is determined by goal difference, which relies on the athletes' intrinsic speed and stickwork, modulated by the effort they exert during the match.
Most players are assigned to fixed squads (Squad Alpha and Squad Beta), except for two players who rotate.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Squad Alpha (Amy, Bev, Cat, Deb, Eve, Flo, Gia) and Lou beat Squad Beta (Hana, Ida, Jen, Kim, Liz, Meg, Nan) and **Casey**.
In the second match, Squad Beta and Lou beat Squad Alpha and **Casey**.
In the third match, Squad Alpha and **Casey** beat Squad Beta and Lou.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think **Casey** ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Lou put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Squad Alpha and Lou (Team 1) and Squad Beta and **Casey** (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between a team of Amy, Bev, Cat, Hana, Ida, Jen, Lou, and **Casey** (Team 1) and a team of Deb, Eve, Flo, Gia, Kim, Liz, Meg, and Nan (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
