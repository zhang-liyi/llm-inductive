BACKGROUND  
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of indoor bouldering rounds. In each round, performance depends on the climbers’ intrinsic bouldering ability—power, technique, and problem‑solving—together with how much effort they put into that round.  
Athletes compete individually and in teams where the number of completed problems is combined.  
All rounds take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS  
In the first round, Priya, Quinn, and Ray beat Sasha, Theo, and Uma.  
In the second round, Priya and Sasha lost to Quinn and Theo.  
In the third round, Ray lost to Uma.

QUERIES  
Query 1: Out of 100 random boulderers, where do you think Quinn ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Priya put into the second round?  
Query 3: In a new round later this same day between Priya and Uma (Team 1) and Quinn and Ray (Team 2), whose team would do better and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new round later this same day between Sasha and Theo (Team 1) and Ray and a new climber, Nova (Team 2), whose team would do better and by how much?
