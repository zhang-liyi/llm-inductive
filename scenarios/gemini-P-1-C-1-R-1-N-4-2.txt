BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in Ultimate Frisbee matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the speed, throwing accuracy, and tactical awareness of the six players, based on their intrinsic talent modulated by the intensity of effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete in teams of six.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, the team of Rex, Ty, Oz, Lu, Bo, and Ed beat the team of Al, Cy, Di, Fi, Gi, and Hi.
In the second match, the team of Al, Cy, Di, Fi, Gi, and Hi beat the team of Rex, Ty, Oz, Lu, Bo, and Quinn.
In the third match, the team of Rex, Ty, Oz, Lu, Bo, and Ed beat the team of Al, Cy, Di, Fi, Gi, and Quinn.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Rex ranks in terms of intrinsic talent?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Quinn put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between the team of Rex, Ty, Oz, Lu, Bo, and Ed (Team 1) and the team of Al, Cy, Di, Fi, Gi, and Quinn (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between a team of Rex, Ty, Al, Cy, Oz, and Lu (Team 1) and a team of Bo, Quinn, Di, Fi, Gi, and Hi (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
