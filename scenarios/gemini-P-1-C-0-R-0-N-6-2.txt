BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Soccer matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the collective ball control and stamina of the athletes, based on their intrinsic skill modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that match.
Athletes compete in teams of 8.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, the team of Gabriel, Julian, Mateo, Anthony, Jaxon, Lincoln, Joshua, and Christopher lost to the team of Andrew, Theodore, Caleb, Ryan, Asher, Nathan, Thomas, and Leo.
In the second match, the team of Gabriel, Julian, Andrew, Theodore, Caleb, Ryan, Asher, and Nathan lost to the team of Mateo, Anthony, Jaxon, Lincoln, Joshua, Christopher, Thomas, and Leo.
In the third match, the team of Gabriel, Julian, Thomas, Leo, Mateo, Anthony, Jaxon, and Lincoln lost to the team of Andrew, Theodore, Caleb, Ryan, Asher, Nathan, Joshua, and Christopher.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Gabriel ranks in terms of intrinsic skill?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Andrew put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Gabriel and Julian (Team 1) and Andrew and Theodore (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Andrew and Mateo (Team 1) and Julian and Thomas (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
