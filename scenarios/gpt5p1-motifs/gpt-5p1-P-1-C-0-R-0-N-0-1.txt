BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of half-court 2-on-2 basketball games, plus one individual matchup. In each game, the winning team is determined by the players’ intrinsic shooting accuracy, ball-handling, and endurance, together with how much effort they put into that game.
Athletes compete either individually or in pairs.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Jordan and Tasha beat Marco and Eli.
In the second game, Marco and Eli lost to Ryan and Chloe.
In the third game, Jordan and Ryan beat Marco and Eli.
In a 1-on-1 game, Tasha beat Chloe.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random basketball players, where do you think Jordan ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Eli put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new 2-on-2 game later this same day between Jordan and Chloe (Team 1) and Ryan and Tasha (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new 2-on-2 game later this same day between Marco and Eli (Team 1) and Jordan and Ryan (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
