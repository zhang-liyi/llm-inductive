BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes compete in Beach Volleyball matches. The winning team is determined by the combined agility and power of the players, which stems from their intrinsic athletic ability modulated by the specific amount of effort they exert during that particular match.
Athletes compete as fixed pairs for the duration of the event.
All matches take place on the same afternoon.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Ravi and Sam lost to Leo and Kai.
In the second match, Ravi and Sam beat Leo and Kai.
In the third match, Ravi and Sam lost to Leo and Kai.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Ravi ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Leo put into the second match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Ravi and Leo (Team 1) and Sam and Kai (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Sam and Kai (Team 1) and Ravi and Leo (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
