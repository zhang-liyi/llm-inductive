BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes are competing in a series of 5-a-side Soccer matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the tactical awareness and ball control of the athletes, based on their intrinsic technique modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete in teams of 5.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, City FC beat Alex, Ty, Ky, Ry, and Sy.
In the second match, United FC beat Alex, Bo, Co, Do, and Fo.
In the third match, Alex, Ja, Ka, La, and Ma beat Rovers FC.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Alex ranks in terms of intrinsic technique?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Ty put into the first match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Alex, Ty, Ky, Ry, and Sy (Team 1) and Alex, Ja, Ka, La, and Ma (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between City FC (Team 1) and United FC (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
