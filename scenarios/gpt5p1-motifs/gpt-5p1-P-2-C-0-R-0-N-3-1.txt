BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are playing small-sided 5-a-side soccer matches, plus an occasional 1-on-1 duel. The outcome of each match depends on players’ intrinsic soccer ability—first touch, acceleration, balance, and reading of the play—combined with how hard they choose to work in that match, such as how much they sprint, press, and track back.
All matches take place on the same day.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random soccer players, where do you think Priya ranks in terms of intrinsic soccer ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Leo put into the second match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Priya, Hana, Tariq, Sofia, and Malik (Team 1) and Leo, Clara, Diego, Farid, and Omar (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Jonah, Lila, Sofia, Malik, and Leo (Team 1) and Hana, Tariq, Clara, Diego, and Farid (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
