BACKGROUND  
In this event, surfers are competing in a sequence of heats. In each heat, the winner is the surfer or team that earns higher judging scores, determined by intrinsic wave-reading ability, balance, and maneuver skill, along with how much effort and commitment they put into each ride during that heat.  
Some heats are one-on-one, and some are three-person team heats where combined scores determine the winner.  
All heats take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS  
In the first heat, Isla beat Bruno.  
In the second heat, Bruno beat Talia.  
In the third heat, Isla, Talia, and Noah beat Bruno, Sera, and Max.  

QUERIES  
Query 1: Out of 100 random surfers, where do you think Isla ranks in terms of intrinsic surfing ability?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Bruno put into the second heat?  
Query 3: In a new heat later this same day between Isla and Noah (Team 1) and Bruno and Sera (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new heat later this same day between Talia (Team 1) and Max and Bruno (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
