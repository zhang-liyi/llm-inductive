BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Tennis matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the court coverage and stroke consistency of the athletes, based on their intrinsic talent modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete either individually or as a team of two.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Bjorn beat Andre.
In the second match, Bjorn and Stefan beat Andre and Pete.
In the third match, Bjorn and Stefan lost to Pete and Ivan.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Bjorn ranks in terms of intrinsic talent?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Stefan put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Bjorn and Stefan (Team 1) and Andre and Roger (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Andre and Pete (Team 1) and Stefan and Boris (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
