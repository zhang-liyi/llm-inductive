BACKGROUND  
In this event, the players compete in a series of tennis matches. The outcome of each match depends on how effectively the players can serve, return, and construct points, which is determined by their intrinsic tennis ability combined with the effort they apply in that match.  
Matches are mostly doubles (teams of two), with an occasional singles match.  
Whenever players compete in doubles, they always use the same fixed partner throughout the event.  
All matches occur on the same day.

CONDITIONS  
In the first match, Amira and Chloe beat Tessa and Brooke.  
In the second match, Amira and Chloe beat Nora and Yuki.  
In the third match, Tessa and Brooke beat Amira and Chloe.  
In the fourth match, Amira beat Tessa.

QUERIES  
Query 1: Out of 100 random tennis players, where do you think Amira ranks in terms of intrinsic tennis ability?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Tessa put into the third match?  
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Amira and Chloe (Team 1) and Tessa and Brooke (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Nora and Yuki (Team 1) and Tessa and Brooke (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
