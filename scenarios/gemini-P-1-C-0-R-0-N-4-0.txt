BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes compete in a volleyball tournament. The winner of each set is determined by the collective offensive and defensive capabilities of the six-player team, based on their intrinsic jumping and striking power modulated by the effort they exert during the match.
Athletes compete in fixed teams of six.
All matches take place on the same weekend.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, the Spikers beat the Blockers (consisting of Jared, Tom, Bill, Sam, Gil, and Hal).
In the second match, the Setters beat the Blockers (consisting of Jared, Tom, Bill, Sam, Gil, and Hal).
In the third match, the Blockers (consisting of Jared, Tom, Bill, Sam, Gil, and Hal) lost to the Diggers.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Jared ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think the Diggers put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between the Spikers (Team 1) and the Blockers (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between the Setters (Team 1) and the Diggers (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
