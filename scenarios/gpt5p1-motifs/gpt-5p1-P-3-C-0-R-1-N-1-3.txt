BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are playing 3-on-3 ice hockey on a small rink. The outcome of each game depends on the teams’ overall performance, driven by each player’s intrinsic skating, stick-handling, and shooting ability and the effort they put into that game.
Players compete as part of fixed three-player lines, and occasionally in individual contests.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Mia, Rachel, and Tessa beat Lena, Sofia, and Kat.
In the second game, Erin, Julia, and Harper beat Lena, Sofia, and Kat.
In the third game, Lena, Sofia, and Kat beat Erin, Julia, and Harper.
In the fourth game, Tessa beat Harper.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random ice hockey players, where do you think Lena ranks in terms of intrinsic ice hockey ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Lena put into the second game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Lena, Sofia, and Kat (Line 1) and Mia, Rachel, and Tessa (Line 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Mia, Rachel, and Tessa (Line 1) and Erin, Julia, and Harper (Line 2), who would win and by how much?
