BACKGROUND
In this event, players are competing in a series of handball games. The winner of each game depends on the players’ intrinsic handball ability (throwing power, accuracy, agility, anticipation) combined with how much effort they exert in that game.
Games are played either as 1v1 shooting/goalkeeping drills or as full seven‑a‑side handball matches.
All games take place on the same day.
