BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of team table tennis matches. Most contests are 3-on-3: each team of three players takes turns playing points, and the combined performance determines the winner. There is also the occasional 1-on-1 game. In every contest, the outcome depends on the players’ intrinsic table tennis ability and how much effort they put into that specific match.
All matches take place on the same day.
