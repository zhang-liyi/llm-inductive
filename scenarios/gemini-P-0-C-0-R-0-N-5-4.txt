BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes compete in Field Hockey matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the collective stick handling and endurance of the athletes, based on their intrinsic ability modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete in teams of 7.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Tara, Jen, Kim, Liz, Meg, Sue, and Ann beat Bob, Dan, Gil, Jim, Lee, Moe, and Ned.
In the second match, Tara, Jen, Kim, Liz, Meg, Sue, and Ann beat Bob, Dan, Gil, Pat, Roy, Sam, and Ted.
In the third match, Tara, Jen, Kim, Liz, Meg, Sue, and Ann beat Jim, Lee, Moe, Pat, Roy, Sam, and Ted.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Tara ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Ted put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Tara, Jen, Kim, Liz, Meg, Sue, and Ann (Team 1) and Bob, Dan, Gil, Jim, Lee, Moe, and Pat (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Bob, Dan, Gil, Jim, Lee, Moe, and Ned (Team 1) and Pat, Roy, Sam, Ted, and three new average players (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
