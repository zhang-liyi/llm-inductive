BACKGROUND
In this regatta, the athletes are competing in rowing races. They race either individually in single sculls or as part of large boats with 9 people (eight rowers plus a cox). The boat that wins depends on the rowers’ intrinsic power and technique, combined with how much effort they put into that race.
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, Priya lost to Mei in single sculls.
In the second race, Priya, Lucas and seven others lost to Jordan, Mei and seven others.
In the third race, Priya, Jordan and seven others lost to Lucas, Nina and seven others.
In the fourth race, Priya, Carlos and seven others lost to Mei, Omar and seven others.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random rowers, where do you think Mei ranks in terms of intrinsic rowing strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Priya put into the third race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Priya, Mei and seven others (Team 1) and Lucas, Jordan and seven others (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new single-scull race later this same day between Priya and Lucas, who would win and by how much?
