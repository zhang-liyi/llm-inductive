BACKGROUND
In this event, three field hockey teams are playing a 7-a-side round-robin. Each game is 7-on-7. The outcome of a game depends on the players’ intrinsic stick skills, speed, balance, and aerobic capacity, together with how much effort they choose to exert in that game.
All games take place on the same day.
There are three fixed teams of seven whose rosters do not change:
- Team Redwood: Akira, Mei, Lauren, Jess, Kat, Olivia, Brooke.
- Team Harbor: Nadia, Salma, Priyanka, Iris, Chloe, Dana, Elise.
- Team Summit: Tara, Yuki, Morgan, Aisha, Bella, Carmen, Fiona.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Redwood beat Harbor.
In the second game, Harbor beat Summit.
In the third game, Redwood beat Summit.
In the fourth game, Summit beat Redwood.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random 7-a-side field hockey players, where do you think Akira ranks in terms of intrinsic playing ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Nadia put into the first game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Redwood and Harbor, who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Harbor and Summit, who would win and by how much?
