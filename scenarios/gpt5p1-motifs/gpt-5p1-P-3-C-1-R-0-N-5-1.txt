BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are playing a series of seven-a-side soccer games. The outcome of each game depends on the players’ intrinsic soccer skills and how much effort they choose to exert in that game.
Matches are played on the same day, and players rotate across different teams.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Maya, Sofia, Lucas, Chen, Bruno, Iris, and Mateo beat Elena, Carlos, Hannah, Jamal, Nia, Owen, and Tariq.
In the second game, Carlos, Hannah, Jamal, Nia, Owen, Tariq, and Zoe beat Elena, Maya, Sofia, Lucas, Chen, Bruno, and Rafael.
In the third game, Elena, Carlos, Sofia, Jamal, Nia, Iris, and Rafael beat Maya, Lucas, Chen, Bruno, Owen, Tariq, and Mateo.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random soccer players, where do you think Elena ranks in terms of intrinsic soccer ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Carlos put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Elena, Maya, Lucas, Chen, Bruno, Owen, and Mateo (Team 1) and Carlos, Hannah, Jamal, Nia, Tariq, Iris, and Rafael (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Sofia, Zoe, Tariq, and four other similarly skilled players (Team 1) and Hannah, Jamal, Nia, and four other similarly skilled players (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
