BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of ice hockey matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the average skating speed and puck handling of the athletes, based on their intrinsic strength modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete in fixed teams of 6.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Viktor's team (consisting of Viktor and 5 fixed teammates) lost to Sergei's team.
In the second match, Viktor's team beat Andrei's team.
In the third match, Viktor's team lost to Igor's team.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Viktor ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Viktor's team put into the second match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Viktor's team (Team 1) and Sergei's team (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Andrei's team (Team 1) and Igor's team (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
