BACKGROUND
In this event, three 9-a-side indoor volleyball teams play a round of matches. Each match outcome depends on the players’ intrinsic volleyball capabilities—serving, setting, attacking, blocking, defensive reactions—combined with how much effort they invest in that match.
Teams have fixed rosters of 9 players that never change during the event.
Team Phoenix: Alina, Becca, Cara, Dana, Elise, Fiona, Greta, Holly, Iris.
Team Titans: Jonah, Marco, Stefan, Leo, Victor, Amir, Colin, Reese, Toby.
Team Waves: Marcus, Ben, Oliver, Felix, Hugo, Simon, Derek, Evan, Neil.
All matches are played on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Phoenix beat Waves.
In the second match, Titans beat Waves.
In the third match, Phoenix beat Waves again.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random volleyball players, where do you think Marcus ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Oliver put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Phoenix and Titans, who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Waves and Titans, who would win and by how much?
