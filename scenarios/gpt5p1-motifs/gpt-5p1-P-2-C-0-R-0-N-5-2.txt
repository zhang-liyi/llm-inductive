BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of small-sided soccer matches. For each match, the winning side is determined by the players’ intrinsic soccer ability, such as ball control and positioning, together with how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete either individually or in teams of seven.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Mateo beat Victor.
In the second match, Mateo, Lina, Oscar, Priyanka, Quentin, Sara, and Tomas beat Victor, Wendy, Xavier, Yasmin, Zoe, Aaron, and Bella.
In the third match, Mateo, Lina, Oscar, Priyanka, Quentin, Sara, and Tomas lost to Victor, Wendy, Xavier, Carlos, Diana, Ethan, and Bella.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random soccer players, where do you think Mateo ranks in terms of intrinsic playing ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Victor put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Mateo, Lina, Oscar, Priyanka, Quentin, Sara, and Tomas (Team 1) and Victor, Wendy, Xavier, Yasmin, Zoe, Carlos, and Diana (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Lina, Oscar, Priyanka, Quentin, Sara, Tomas, and Aaron (Team 1) and Victor, Wendy, Xavier, Yasmin, Zoe, Ethan, and Bella (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
