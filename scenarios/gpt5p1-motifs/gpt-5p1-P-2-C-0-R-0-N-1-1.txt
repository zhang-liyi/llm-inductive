BACKGROUND  
In this event, the athletes are competing in distance running, including both solo races and 3-runner team races.  
In each race, the result depends on each runner’s intrinsic running speed and endurance, along with how much effort they put into that particular race.  
For team races, three runners’ performances are combined by their average pace to determine which team is faster.  
All races take place on the same day.
