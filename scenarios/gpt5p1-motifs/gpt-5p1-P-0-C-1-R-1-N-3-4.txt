BACKGROUND
In this event, the players are competing in a series of five-on-five ice hockey scrimmages (with no goalies, just skaters). In each scrimmage, the team that wins depends on the players’ intrinsic skating, puck-handling, and shooting ability, together with how much effort and intensity they put into that scrimmage. Players compete either individually in shootout duels or in teams of five. All scrimmages take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first scrimmage, Tyler beat Nick.
In the second scrimmage, Tyler, Evan, Brad, Sean, and Kyle beat Nick, Ryan, Jake, Cole, and Drew.
In the third scrimmage, Tyler, Nick, Ryan, Jake, and Cole beat Evan, Brad, Sean, Kyle, and Luke.
In the fourth scrimmage, Nick, Ryan, Jake, Cole, and Drew beat Evan, Brad, Sean, Kyle, and Luke.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random ice hockey players, where do you think Tyler ranks in terms of intrinsic hockey ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Evan put into the third scrimmage?
Query 3: In a new scrimmage later this same day between Tyler, Evan, Brad, Sean, and Kyle (Team 1) and Nick, Ryan, Jake, Cole, and Drew (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new scrimmage later this same day between Nick, Ryan, Jake, Cole, and Drew (Team 1) and Evan, Brad, Sean, Kyle, and Luke (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
