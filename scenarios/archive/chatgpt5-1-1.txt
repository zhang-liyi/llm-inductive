BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes participate in a series of 4×100m relay races.
Each runner has an intrinsic sprint speed, and a team’s success depends on the sum of their runners’ speeds plus a handoff-smoothness factor that can vary per race.
All races occur on the same afternoon under identical weather conditions.

CONDITIONS
In the first relay, Team Alpha (Liam, Zara, Noah, Elise) beat Team Beta (Kai, Mira, Jonah, Leah).
In the second relay, Team Beta beat Team Gamma (Ravi, Tessa, Milo, Quinn).
In the third relay, Team Alpha narrowly lost to Team Gamma.

QUERIES
Which team appears to have the strongest average intrinsic speed?
Estimate the likelihood (0–100%) that Team Alpha had unusually poor handoffs in the third race.
In a new relay between Team Alpha and Team Beta, who is favored and by approximately how much time?
Suppose Jonah substitutes into Team Gamma in place of Milo—how might that change Gamma’s expected performance?
