BACKGROUND  
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of track cycling races inside a velodrome. In each race, the result is determined by the riders’ intrinsic sprinting ability and speed endurance, combined with how much effort they put into that particular race.  
Cyclists compete either individually or in two-rider teams.  
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS  
In the first race, Nina beat Oliver.  
In the second race, Nina and Kai beat Oliver and Tess.  
In the third race, Tess beat Kai.  
In the fourth race, Oliver and Tess beat Nina and Rui.

QUERIES  
Query 1: Out of 100 random track cyclists, where do you think Nina ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Tess put into the fourth race?  
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Nina and Kai (Team 1) and Oliver and Rui (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Tess and Nina (Team 1) and Kai and Oliver (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
