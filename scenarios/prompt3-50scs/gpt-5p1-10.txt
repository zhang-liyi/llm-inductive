BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of rugby sevens scrimmages. In each match, the team that wins depends on the players’ intrinsic speed, strength, and ball-handling skills, combined with how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes sometimes play in small-sided teams.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first scrimmage, Riley beat Jordan.
In the second scrimmage, Riley and Sam beat Jordan and Leo.
In the third scrimmage, Riley and Leo beat Sam and Jordan.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random rugby players, where do you think Riley ranks in terms of intrinsic strength and skill?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Jordan put into the first scrimmage against Riley?
Query 3: In a new scrimmage later this same day between Riley and Sam (Team 1) and Jordan and Taylor (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new scrimmage later this same day between Jordan and Leo (Team 1) and Riley and Casey (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
