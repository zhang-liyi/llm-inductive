BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in precision shooting contests (rifle and pistol). In each contest, the winner is the shooter or team whose shots are, on average, more precise and consistent, determined by intrinsic steadiness, aim, and control, modulated by how much effort and focus they put into that contest.
Athletes compete either individually or as a team of two.
All contests take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first contest, Omar beat Nia.
In the second contest, Omar and Priya lost to Nia and Lucas.
In the third contest, Priya beat Lucas.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random shooters, where do you think Nia ranks in terms of intrinsic shooting ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Omar put into the second contest?
Query 3: In a new contest later this same day between Omar and Lucas (Team 1) and Nia and Priya (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new contest later this same day between Omar (Team 1) and Nia and Lucas (Team 2 shooting in an alternating-relay format), who would win and by how much?
