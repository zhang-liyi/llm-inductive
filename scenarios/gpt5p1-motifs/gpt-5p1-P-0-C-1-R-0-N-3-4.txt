BACKGROUND
In this event, the players are competing in a series of small-sided handball matches played 5-on-5. In each match, the winning team is determined by the players’ intrinsic handball ability (throwing power, accuracy, movement, defensive skill) and the effort they put into that match.  
Players compete either individually (in shooting duels) or as members of 5-player teams.  
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Ravi, Arjun, Elias, Jakob, and Milan beat Boris, Conrad, Felix, Hugo, and Leon.  
In the second match, Ravi, Boris, Felix, Nils, and Oskar beat Arjun, Elias, Hugo, Jakob, and Tariq.  
In the third match, Ravi beat Leon in a shooting duel.  
In the fourth match, Ravi, Conrad, Hugo, Tariq, and Milan beat Boris, Arjun, Felix, Jakob, and Nils.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random handball players, where do you think Ravi ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Leon put into the third match?  
Query 3: In a new 5-on-5 match later this same day between Ravi, Arjun, Conrad, Elias, and Tariq (Team 1) and Boris, Felix, Hugo, Jakob, and Milan (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Nils and Oskar (a shooting duel), who would win and by how much?
