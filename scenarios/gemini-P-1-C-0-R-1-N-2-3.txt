BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of 4-on-4 beach volleyball matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the court coverage and striking power of the athletes, based on their intrinsic athleticism modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete in fixed teams of four.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, the team of Sam, Ben, Jon, and Ray beat the team of Toby, Rex, Leo, and Max.
In the second match, the team of Kit, Pat, Vic, and Wes beat the team of Toby, Rex, Leo, and Max.
In the third match, the team of Sam, Ben, Jon, and Ray beat the team of Kit, Pat, Vic, and Wes.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Toby ranks in terms of intrinsic athleticism?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Sam put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between the team of Toby, Rex, Leo, and Max (Team 1) and the team of Sam, Ben, Jon, and Ray (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between the team of Sam, Ben, Jon, and Ray (Team 1) and the team of Kit, Pat, Vic, and Wes (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
