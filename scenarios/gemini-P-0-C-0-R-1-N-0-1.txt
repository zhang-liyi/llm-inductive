BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Tennis matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the average accuracy and speed with which the athletes are able to hit the ball, based on their intrinsic skill modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete either individually or as a fixed team of two.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Emma and Olivia beat Sarah and Jessica.
In the second match, Emma and Olivia beat Ashley and Brittany.
In the third match, Emma beat Sarah.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Emma ranks in terms of intrinsic skill?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Sarah put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Emma and Olivia (Team 1) and Sarah and a new player named Megan (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Jessica and Ashley (Team 1) and Olivia and Brittany (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
