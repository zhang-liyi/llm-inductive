BACKGROUND
In this weightlifting meet, athletes compete in two lifts, and their result in each session is based on the heaviest successful lifts they make. Performance depends on their intrinsic strength and lifting technique, as well as how much effort and commitment they bring to that session.
Athletes lift individually, and some sessions are scored by combining totals from pairs.
All sessions take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first session, Nora beat Felix.
In the second session, Nora and Liam beat Felix and Dana.
In the third session, Dana beat Liam.
In the fourth session, Felix beat Liam.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random weightlifters, where do you think Nora ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Liam put into the third session?
Query 3: In a new session later this same day between Nora and Dana (Team 1) and Felix and Liam (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new session later this same day between Dana (Team 1) and Felix (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
