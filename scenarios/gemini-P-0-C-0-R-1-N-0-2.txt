BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Badminton matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the average reaction time and coordination with which the athletes are able to return the shuttlecock, based on their intrinsic reflexes modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete as a fixed team of two.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Rohan and Vikram beat Neil and Aris.
In the second match, Rohan and Vikram beat Ian and Kyle.
In the third match, Rohan and Vikram beat Neil and Aris.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Rohan ranks in terms of intrinsic reflexes?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Neil put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Rohan and Vikram (Team 1) and Aris and a new player named Sam (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Neil and Ian (Team 1) and Vikram and Kyle (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
