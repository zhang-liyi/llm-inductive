BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of 4-on-4 half‑court basketball games and a few potential 1-on-1 matchups. In each game, the team that wins depends on the players’ intrinsic basketball ability (such as shooting, quickness, and coordination) modulated by how much effort they put into that game.
Athletes compete either individually or in teams of four.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Leo, Amir, Ben, and Carlos beat Devon, Eli, Faisal, and Gabe.
In the second game, Leo, Henry, Ivan, and Jake beat Devon, Eli, Faisal, and Gabe.
In the third game, Leo, Amir, Henry, and Ben lost to Devon, Eli, Faisal, and Gabe.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random basketball players, where do you think Leo ranks in terms of intrinsic basketball ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Gabe put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new 4-on-4 game later this same day between Leo, Carlos, Henry, and Ivan (Team 1) and Devon, Eli, Faisal, and Gabe (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new 4-on-4 game later this same day between Amir, Ben, Jake, and Kyle (Team 1) and Devon, Eli, Faisal, and Gabe (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
