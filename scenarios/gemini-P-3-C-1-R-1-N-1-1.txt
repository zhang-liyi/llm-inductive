BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Ice Hockey matches played on a half-rink. In each match, the team that wins depends on the number of goals scored, based on the athletes' intrinsic skating and puck-handling abilities modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete as a team of three.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Riley, Igor, and Sven lost to Lars, Otto, and Vlad.
In the second match, Riley, Lars, and Otto lost to Igor, Sven, and Vlad.
In the third match, Riley, Vlad, and Erik beat Igor, Sven, and Lars.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Riley ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Vlad put into the third race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Riley and Igor (Team 1) and Lars and Sven (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Lars and Otto (Team 1) and Igor and Vlad (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
