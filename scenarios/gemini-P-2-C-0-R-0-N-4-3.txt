BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Handball matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the throwing velocity and defensive coordination of the athletes, based on their intrinsic power modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete as a team of 6.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Blake, Jude, Omar, Lily, Rex, and Sky beat Paul, May, Gus, Joy, Ted, and Fay.
In the second match, Blake, Jude, Paul, May, Gus, and Joy beat Omar, Lily, Rex, Sky, Ted, and Fay.
In the third match, Blake, Jude, Ted, Fay, Omar, and Lily lost to Rex, Sky, Paul, May, Gus, and Joy.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Blake ranks in terms of intrinsic power?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Blake put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Team 1 (Blake, Jude, Omar, Lily, Rex, Sky) and Team 2 (Paul, May, Gus, Joy, Ted, Fay), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Team 1 (Paul, May, Omar, Lily, Ted, Fay) and Team 2 (Rex, Sky, Gus, Joy, Blake, Jude), who would win and by how much?
