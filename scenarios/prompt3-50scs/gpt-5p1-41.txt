BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of archery rounds. In each round, the winner is the archer or team whose arrows are, on average, closer to the center of the target, determined by their intrinsic accuracy and control, modulated by how much effort and concentration they put into that round.
Athletes compete either individually or as a team of three.
All rounds take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first round, Robin beat Kai.
In the second round, Robin, Leah, and Finn lost to Kai, Mira, and Jonah.
In the third round, Leah beat Mira.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random archers, where do you think Kai ranks in terms of intrinsic accuracy?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Robin put into the second round?
Query 3: In a new round later this same day between Robin and Leah (Team 1) and Kai and Mira (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new round later this same day between Leah, Finn, and Jonah (Team 1) and Robin, Kai, and Mira (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
