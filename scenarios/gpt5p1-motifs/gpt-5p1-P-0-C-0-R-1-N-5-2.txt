BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of seven-a-side ultimate frisbee games. In each game, the winning team is determined by the players’ intrinsic throwing accuracy, speed, and stamina, along with how much effort they put in to that game.
Teams consist of fixed squads of seven players, and each player always plays on the same team whenever they appear.
All games take place on the same day.

Team Sofia: Sofia, Jenna, Tariq, Lila, Arun, Becky, Nolan  
Team Eric: Eric, Dana, Pablo, Quinn, Sara, Victor, Holly  
Team Mateo: Mateo, Kim, Jason, Riley, Oliver, Stella, Grant

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Sofia’s team beat Eric’s team.  
In the second game, Sofia’s team beat Mateo’s team.  
In the third game, Mateo’s team beat Eric’s team.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random ultimate frisbee players, where do you think Sofia ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Eric put into the first game?  
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Sofia’s team (Team 1) and Mateo’s team (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Eric’s team (Team 1) and Mateo’s team (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
