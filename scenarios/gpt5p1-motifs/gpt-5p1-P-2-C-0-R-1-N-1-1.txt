BACKGROUND
In this event, the swimmers are racing in 3x100m freestyle relays. In each relay, the team that wins depends on the swimmers’ intrinsic speed and endurance in the water, combined with how much effort they put into that specific race.
Athletes compete either individually or as part of their fixed 3-swimmer relay team.
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first relay, Lena, Sofia, and Mia beat Harper, Chloe, and Zoe.
In the second relay, Lena, Sofia, and Mia beat Aria, Bella, and Layla.
In the third relay, Harper, Chloe, and Zoe beat Aria, Bella, and Layla.
In the fourth relay, Aria, Bella, and Layla beat Lena, Sofia, and Mia.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random competitive swimmers, where do you think Lena ranks in terms of intrinsic swimming ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Sofia put into the fourth relay?
Query 3: In a new relay later this same day between Lena, Sofia, and Mia (Team 1) and Harper, Chloe, and Zoe (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new relay later this same day between Harper, Chloe, and Zoe (Team 1) and Aria, Bella, and Layla (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
