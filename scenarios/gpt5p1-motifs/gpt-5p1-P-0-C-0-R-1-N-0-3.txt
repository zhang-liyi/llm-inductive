BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are playing a series of small-sided 2-on-2 soccer games on a reduced-size pitch. The winner of each game depends on the players’ intrinsic soccer ability (ball control, speed, finishing, and defensive instincts) and how much effort they put into pressing, moving, and staying focused in that game.  
All games are 2-on-2.  
All games are played on the same day, and only the effort level varies from game to game; intrinsic ability is constant.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Carlos and Diego beat Ben and Oliver.  
In the second game, Carlos and Diego beat Liam and Noah.  
In the third game, Ben and Oliver beat Liam and Noah.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random soccer players, where do you think Carlos ranks in terms of intrinsic skill?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Noah put into the third game?  
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Carlos and Diego (Team 1) and Ben and Oliver (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Ben and Oliver (Team 1) and Liam and Noah (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
