Players are in fixed teams of five for the whole event; no one changes teams.  
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS  
In the first match, Falcons beat Tigers.  
In the second match, Falcons beat Wolves.  
In the third match, Wolves beat Falcons.  

QUERIES  
Query 1: Out of 100 random soccer players, where do you think Diego ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Igor put into the third match?  
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Falcons (Team 1) and Tigers (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Tigers (Team 1) and Wolves (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
