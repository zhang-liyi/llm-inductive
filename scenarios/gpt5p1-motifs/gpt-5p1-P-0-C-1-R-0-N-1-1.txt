BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are playing a series of small-sided Ultimate Frisbee games. Each game is 3-on-3 on a shortened field. The outcome of a game depends on the players’ intrinsic Ultimate skills (speed, throwing accuracy, catching ability, and field awareness), modulated by how much effort they put in during that game.
Players compete either individually or on 3-player teams.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Maya, Tom, and Lina beat Omar, Zoe, and Ben.
In the second game, Maya, Omar, and Kat beat Tom, Zoe, and Lina.
In the third game, Maya, Zoe, and Ben beat Omar, Tom, and Riley.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random Ultimate Frisbee players, where do you think Maya ranks in terms of intrinsic Ultimate ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Omar put into the second game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Maya, Lina, and Ben (Team 1) and Tom, Omar, and Zoe (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Kat, Riley, and Zoe (Team 1) and Maya, Tom, and Lina (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
