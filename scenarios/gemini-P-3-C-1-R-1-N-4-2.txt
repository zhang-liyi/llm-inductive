BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of lacrosse matches using the "sixes" format (6 players per side). In each match, the team that wins depends on the speed and shooting accuracy of the athletes, based on their intrinsic athleticism modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete as part of a team.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Riley and the Harriers lost to the Ospreys.
In the second match, Riley and the Ospreys lost to the Harriers.
In the third match, Riley and the Falcons lost to the Kestrels.
In the fourth match, Riley and the Kestrels beat the Falcons.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Riley ranks in terms of intrinsic athleticism?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think the Kestrels put into the fourth match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between the Harriers (Team 1) and the Falcons (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between the Ospreys (Team 1) and the Kestrels (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
