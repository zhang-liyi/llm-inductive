BACKGROUND
In this regatta, the rowers are competing in a series of rowing races. The speed of each boat depends on the intrinsic power and technique of the rowers, modulated by how much effort they put into that race.
Rowers compete either individually in single sculls (1‑on‑1) or in larger crews of 9.
All races take place on the same day.
