BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Ice Hockey matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the collective skating speed and puck control of the athletes, based on their intrinsic skill modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete in teams of 6 (5 skaters and 1 goalie).
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, the team of Liam, Noah, Oliver, Elijah, James, and William beat the team of Benjamin, Lucas, Henry, Theodore, Jack, and Rory.
In the second match, the team of Benjamin, Lucas, Henry, Theodore, Jack, and William beat the team of Liam, Noah, Oliver, Elijah, James, and Rory.
In the third match, the team of Liam, Noah, Benjamin, Lucas, Oliver, and Henry beat the team of Elijah, James, Theodore, Jack, William, and Rory.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Rory ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think William put into the third race?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Team 1 (Liam, Noah, Oliver, Elijah, James, William) and Team 2 (Benjamin, Lucas, Henry, Theodore, Jack, Rory), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Team 1 (Liam, Benjamin, Oliver, Henry, James, Jack) and Team 2 (Noah, Lucas, Elijah, Theodore, William, Rory), who would win and by how much?
