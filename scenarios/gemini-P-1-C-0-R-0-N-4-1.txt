BACKGROUND
In this event, hockey players are competing in a series of exhibition games. The winning team is determined by the average skating speed and puck-handling skill of the six athletes on the ice, based on their intrinsic coordination modulated by the intensity of their effort during the game.
Athletes compete in teams of six (including the goalie).
All games take place during a single showcase event.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Team Red beat Team Blue (featuring Felix, Oscar, Leo, Max, Gus, and Ian).
In the second game, Team Green beat Team Blue (featuring Felix, Oscar, Leo, Max, Gus, and Ian).
In the third game, Team Blue (featuring Felix, Oscar, Leo, Max, Gus, and Ian) lost to Team White.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Felix ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Team White put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Team Red (Team 1) and Team Blue (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Team Green (Team 1) and Team White (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
