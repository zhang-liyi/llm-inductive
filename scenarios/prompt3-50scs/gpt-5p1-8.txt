BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of softball games. In each game, the result depends on the players’ intrinsic abilities to pitch, hit, and field, and on how much effort they decide to invest in that game.
Teams have several players, but the starting pitchers Mia and Claire have a strong effect on their teams’ success when they play.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Mia pitched for the Storm and Claire pitched for the Blaze; the Storm beat the Blaze.
In the second game, Mia did not pitch; Claire pitched for the Blaze against the Comets, and the Blaze lost to the Comets.
In the third game, Mia pitched for the Storm against the Comets, and the Storm lost to the Comets.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random softball pitchers, where do you think Mia ranks in terms of intrinsic pitching ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Claire put into the second game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day where Mia pitches for the Storm against Claire pitching for the Blaze, who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day where Claire pitches for the Blaze against the Comets (with the same lineup they used before), who would win and by how much?
