BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of seven-a-side basketball games. In each game, the team that wins depends on the players’ intrinsic shooting, ball-handling, and athleticism, together with how much effort they put in to that game.
Teams consist of fixed squads of seven players, and each player always plays on the same team whenever they appear.
All games take place on the same day.

Team Darius: Darius, Malik, Jonah, Eli, Carter, Andre, Shawn  
Team Marco: Marco, Jamal, Owen, Bryce, Tyler, Damon, Keith  
Team Luis: Luis, Aaron, Devin, Marcus, Joel, Ron, Stefan

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Darius’s team beat Marco’s team.  
In the second game, Darius’s team beat Luis’s team.  
In the third game, Luis’s team beat Marco’s team.  
In the fourth game, Darius’s team beat Marco’s team again.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random seven-a-side basketball players, where do you think Darius ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Luis put into the second game?  
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Darius’s team (Team 1) and Luis’s team (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Marco’s team (Team 1) and Luis’s team (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
