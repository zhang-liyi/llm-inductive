BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of tennis matches. In each match, the winning side depends on the players’ ability to serve accurately and control rallies, based on their intrinsic timing, footwork, and shot-making skill, modulated by how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete either individually or as doubles teams.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Nina and Alex lost to Sofia and Marco.
In the second match, Nina and Alex lost to Liam and Sofia.
In the third match, Nina and Alex beat Marco and Tessa.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random tennis players, where do you think Nina ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Sofia put into the second match?
Query 3: In a new doubles match later this same day between Nina and Alex (Team 1) and Liam and Tessa (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new doubles match later this same day between Sofia and Alex (Team 1) and Marco and Liam (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
