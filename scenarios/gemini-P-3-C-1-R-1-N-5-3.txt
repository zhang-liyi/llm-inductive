BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of 7-a-side Soccer matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the number of goals scored, based on the athletes' intrinsic dribbling and shooting skills, modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete in teams of 7.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, The Comets and Mateo lost to The Stars.
In the second match, The Meteors and Mateo lost to The Stars.
In the third match, The Comets and Mateo beat The Meteors.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Mateo ranks in terms of intrinsic dribbling and shooting skills?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think The Stars put into the second match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between The Meteors and Mateo (Team 1) and The Comets (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between The Stars (Team 1) and The Comets and Mateo (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
