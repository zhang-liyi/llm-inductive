BACKGROUND
In this event, three 7-a-side soccer teams are contesting a small tournament. Each game is 7-on-7. The outcome of a game depends on the players’ intrinsic ball control, passing vision, shooting accuracy, and running endurance, together with how much intensity they choose to bring to each game.
All games take place on the same day.
There are three fixed teams of seven whose rosters do not change:
- Team Copper: Marisol, Hana, Becca, Lily, Erica, Jo, Tori.
- Team Slate: Dan, Miguel, Arturo, Nikhil, Farid, Simon, Paul.
- Team Ivory: Renee, Karla, Isla, Naomi, Paige, Kendra, Faith.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Copper beat Slate.
In the second game, Ivory beat Copper.
In the third game, Copper beat Ivory.
In the fourth game, Slate beat Ivory.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random 7-a-side soccer players, where do you think Marisol ranks in terms of intrinsic playing ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Naomi put into the second game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Copper and Slate, who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Slate and Ivory, who would win and by how much?
