BACKGROUND
This event is a rowing regatta on flat water. Crews compete in different boat classes, and the outcome of each race depends on the rowers’ intrinsic rowing strength and technique, as well as the level of effort they choose to apply over the course.
All races happen on the same day. Rowers may compete in singles and in multi-person boats.
