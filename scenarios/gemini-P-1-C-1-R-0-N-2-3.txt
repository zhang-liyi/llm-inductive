BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Rowing races (specifically coxless fours). In each race, the team that wins depends on the average boat speed, based on the athletes' intrinsic power and technique modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that race.
Athletes compete either individually (single scull) or as a team of 4.
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, Bailey lost to Charlie.
In the second race, Charlie, Jude, Skyler, and River beat Bailey, Ellis, Remi, and Sage.
In the third race, Jude, Skyler, Ellis, and Remi beat Bailey, Charlie, River, and Sage.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Bailey ranks in terms of intrinsic power and technique?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Charlie put into the third race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Bailey, Ellis, Remi, and Sage (Team 1) and Charlie, Jude, Skyler, and River (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Bailey, Charlie, Jude, and Skyler (Team 1) and River, Ellis, Remi, and Sage (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
