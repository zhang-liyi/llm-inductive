In each match, the team that wins depends on the average level of volleyball ability of the players on court (such as jumping power, reaction speed, and ball control), combined with how much effort they put into that match.
Players compete either individually in 1-on-1 skill contests or in 6-a-side teams.
All matches take place on the same day.
