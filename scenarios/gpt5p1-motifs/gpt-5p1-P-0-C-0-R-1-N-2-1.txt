BACKGROUND
In this regatta, the rowers are competing in 4-person sculling boats. In each race, the boat that wins depends on the rowers’ intrinsic rowing power and technique, combined with how much effort they put in for that race.
All races take place on the same day.
Each rower is part of a fixed crew of four.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, Elena’s crew (Elena, Mira, Sophie, and Hannah) beat Kat’s crew (Kat, Riley, Dana, and Zoe).
In the second race, Elena’s crew beat Lila’s crew (Lila, Erin, Paige, and Tori).
In the third race, Kat’s crew beat Lila’s crew.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random rowers, where do you think Elena ranks in terms of intrinsic rowing ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Riley put into the third race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Elena’s crew and Kat’s crew, who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Kat’s crew and Lila’s crew, who would win and by how much?
