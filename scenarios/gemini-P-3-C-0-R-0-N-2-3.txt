BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of sand volleyball matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the spiking power and court coverage of the athletes, based on their intrinsic agility modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete as a team of four.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Hana, Yuna, Mina, and Sana lost to Momo, Jihyo, Dahyun, and Chaeyoung.
In the second match, Hana, Yuna, Mina, and Sana lost to Momo, Jihyo, Tzuyu, and Jeongyeon.
In the third match, Hana, Yuna, Mina, and Sana beat Dahyun, Chaeyoung, Tzuyu, and Jeongyeon.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Hana ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Momo put into the second match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Hana, Yuna, Mina, and Sana (Team 1) and Momo, Jihyo, Dahyun, and Chaeyoung (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Momo, Jihyo, Tzuyu, and Jeongyeon (Team 1) and Dahyun, Chaeyoung, Tzuyu, and Jeongyeon (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
