BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Tennis matches. In each match, the winner is determined by the precision and power of their shots, based on their intrinsic technique modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that match.
Athletes compete either individually or as a doubles team.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Maria beat Ana.
In the second match, Maria and Eva beat Mia and Zoe.
In the third match, Maria and Mia lost to Ana and Eva.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Maria ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Maria put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Maria and Eva (Team 1) and Mia and Ana (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Ana and Eva (Team 1) and Mia and Zoe (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
