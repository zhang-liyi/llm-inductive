BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of ice hockey games and individual shootout contests. Results depend on intrinsic skating, puck-handling, and shooting ability, along with how much effort the players put in during each game.
Athletes compete either individually in shootouts or as part of fixed 6-player teams (including goalies) that remain the same in every game.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
Team Glaciers (Omar, Boris, Carl, Denis, Emir, Farid) beat Team Wolves (Gabe, Hugo, Ilya, Jonas, Karl, Liam) in the first game.
In a shootout contest, Omar beat Gabe.
Team Glaciers beat Team Falcons (Milan, Nico, Oliver, Pavel, Quinn, Roman) in the next game.
Later that day, Team Glaciers beat Team Wolves again.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random ice hockey players, where do you think Omar ranks in terms of intrinsic ice hockey ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Gabe put into the shootout contest against Omar?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Team Glaciers and Team Falcons, who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Team Wolves and Team Falcons, who would win and by how much?
