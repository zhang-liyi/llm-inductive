BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Field Hockey matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the number of goals scored, based on the athletes' intrinsic stick work and speed modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that match.
Athletes compete in teams of 7.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Ash, Bev, Cal, Deb, Eve, Fay, and Rhys beat Guy, Hal, Ivy, Jay, Kay, Lee, and Mel.
In the second match, Guy, Hal, Ivy, Jay, Kay, Lee, and Mel beat Ash, Bev, Cal, Deb, Eve, Fay, and Rhys.
In the third match, Guy, Hal, Ivy, Jay, Kay, Lee, and Ash beat Bev, Cal, Deb, Eve, Fay, Mel, and Rhys.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Rhys ranks in terms of intrinsic stick work and speed?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Ash put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Rhys, Bev, Cal, Deb, Eve, Fay, and Mel (Team 1) and Guy, Hal, Ivy, Jay, Kay, Lee, and Ash (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Guy, Hal, Ivy, Jay, Kay, Lee, and Mel (Team 1) and Ash, Bev, Cal, Deb, Eve, Fay, and Rhys (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
