BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of tennis matches. The outcome of each match depends on each player’s intrinsic tennis ability (serving, groundstrokes, net play, tactical decisions) and the amount of effort they put into that particular match.
Most matches are doubles, and all matches are played on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Marco and Dylan beat Felix and Hugo.
In the second match, Marco and Felix beat Dylan and Ethan.
In the third match, Marco and Ivan beat Dylan and Ethan.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Marco ranks in terms of intrinsic tennis ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Dylan put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Marco and Hugo (Team 1) and Dylan and Ethan (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Felix and Hugo (Team 1) and Ivan and Ethan (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
