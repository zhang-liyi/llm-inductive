BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of 4x100m swimming relays. In each race, the team that wins depends on the combined speed of the swimmers, based on their intrinsic technique and power modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that race.
Athletes compete in teams of four.
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, Michael, David, Chris, and Ryan beat Ian, Nathan, Kevin, and Larry.
In the second race, Michael, David, Chris, and Ryan beat Ian, Nathan, Oscar, and Paul.
In the third race, Michael, David, Chris, and Ryan lost to Kevin, Larry, Oscar, and Paul.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Michael ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Michael put into the third race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Michael, David, Chris, and Ryan (Team 1) and Ian, Nathan, Kevin, and Larry (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Kevin, Larry, Oscar, and Paul (Team 1) and Ian, Nathan, Chris, and Ryan (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
