BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes are competing in a series of Field Hockey matches played in a 7-a-side format. The winning team is determined by goals scored, based on the athletes' intrinsic stick-handling and precision, modulated by the effort they put into the game.
A player named Eva rotates between the two competing squads (The Strikers and The Defenders), who otherwise have fixed team members.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Eva and The Strikers beat The Defenders.
In the second match, Eva and The Defenders beat The Strikers.
In the third match, Eva and The Strikers lost to The Defenders.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random field hockey players, where do you think Eva ranks in terms of intrinsic precision?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think The Defenders put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Eva and The Strikers (Team 1) and The Defenders (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between The Strikers (Team 1) and Eva and The Defenders (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
