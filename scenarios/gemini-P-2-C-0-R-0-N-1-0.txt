BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of 3-on-3 basketball games. In each game, the team that wins depends on the average shooting accuracy and defensive skill of the athletes, based on their intrinsic talent modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that game.
Athletes compete as a team of three.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Jordan, Scott, and Dennis beat Magic, Larry, and Isiah.
In the second game, Jordan, Scott, and Dennis beat Larry, Isiah, and Charles.
In the third game, Jordan, Scott, and Dennis lost to Magic, Charles, and Hakeem.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Jordan ranks in terms of intrinsic talent?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Scott put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Jordan, Scott, and Dennis (Team 1) and Magic, Hakeem, and Patrick (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Magic, Larry, and Charles (Team 1) and Isiah, Hakeem, and Patrick (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
