BACKGROUND
This is a 7-a-side handball tournament held over a single day, with full team games and optional individual shooting contests. The outcome of each game is determined by the players’ intrinsic throwing power, agility, and coordination, along with their level of effort in that specific game. Most players are fixed members of the Panthers, Falcons, or Titans, but one skilled backcourt player, Lena, can be temporarily added to different teams. All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Lena and the Panthers beat the Falcons.
In the second match, Lena and the Falcons beat the Titans.
In the third match, Lena and the Titans lost to the Panthers.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random handball players, where do you think Lena ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think the Panthers’ regular players (excluding Lena) put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new 7-a-side match later this same day between Lena and the Panthers (Team 1) and the Titans (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new 7-a-side match later this same day between Lena and the Falcons (Team 1) and the Panthers (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
