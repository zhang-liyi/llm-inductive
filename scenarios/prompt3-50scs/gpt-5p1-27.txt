BACKGROUND
In this event, the snowboarders are competing in a series of slopestyle runs. In each run, the winner is the rider or team with the higher judged performance, which depends on their intrinsic board control, aerial skill, and creativity, modulated by how much effort and risk they commit on that run.
Snowboarders compete either individually or in pairs whose scores are averaged.
All runs take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first run, Kai beat Brooke.
In the second run, Kai and Eli beat Brooke and Finn.
In the third run, Brooke and Harper beat Eli and Finn.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random snowboarders, where do you think Kai ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Brooke put into the third run?
Query 3: In a new run later this same day between Kai and Harper (Team 1) and Brooke and Eli (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new run later this same day between Finn and Brooke (Team 1) and Kai and Finn’s twin, Gray (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
