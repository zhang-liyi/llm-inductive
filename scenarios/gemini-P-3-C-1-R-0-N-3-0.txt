BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Basketball games. In each game, the team that wins depends on the average scoring and defensive efficiency of the athletes, based on their intrinsic skill modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that game.
Athletes compete as a team of 5.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Noah, Liam, Mason, Jacob, and William beat Ethan, James, Alexander, Michael, and Daniel.
In the second game, Noah, Liam, Mason, Ethan, and James beat Jacob, William, Alexander, Michael, and Daniel.
In the third game, Daniel, Noah, Liam, Mason, and Jacob beat William, Ethan, James, Alexander, and Michael.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Daniel ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Daniel put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Daniel, Noah, Liam, Mason, and Ethan (Team 1) and Jacob, William, James, Alexander, and Michael (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Daniel, Jacob, William, Alexander, and Michael (Team 1) and Noah, Liam, Mason, Ethan, and James (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
