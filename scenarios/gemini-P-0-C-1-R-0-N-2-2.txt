BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Volleyball matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the effectiveness of their plays, based on their intrinsic spiking power and reaction time modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete in teams of 4.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Morgan, Alex, Chris, and Pat beat Taylor, Jamie, Drew, and Casey.
In the second match, Morgan, Taylor, Jamie, and Drew beat Alex, Chris, Pat, and Casey.
In the third match, Morgan, Casey, Alex, and Taylor beat Chris, Pat, Jamie, and Drew.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Morgan ranks in terms of intrinsic spiking power and reaction time?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Chris put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Morgan, Alex, Chris, and Pat (Team 1) and Taylor, Jamie, Drew, and Casey (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Alex, Chris, Pat, and Casey (Team 1) and Taylor, Jamie, Drew, and Casey (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
