BACKGROUND
In this small-sided soccer event, three club teams of 8 players each play a short round-robin. The outcome of each match depends on the players’ intrinsic technical ability, awareness, and conditioning, modulated by how much effort they put in for that match.
Players stay on the same team throughout the event.
Diego plays for Team Aurora in every match with the same seven teammates.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Team Aurora beat Team Borealis.
In the second match, Team Aurora beat Team Comet.
In the third match, Team Borealis beat Team Aurora.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random small-sided soccer players, where do you think Diego ranks in terms of intrinsic playing ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Carter (on Team Borealis) put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Team Aurora and Team Comet, who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Team Borealis and Team Comet, who would win and by how much?
