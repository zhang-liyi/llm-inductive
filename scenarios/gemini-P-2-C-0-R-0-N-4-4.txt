BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Lacrosse (Sixes format) matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the stick handling and running endurance of the athletes, based on their intrinsic stamina modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete as a team of 6.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Logan, Quinn, Ash, Eli, Ray, and Val beat Sid, Ken, Mel, Dan, Eve, and Roy.
In the second match, Logan, Quinn, Sid, Ken, Mel, and Dan beat Ash, Eli, Ray, Val, Eve, and Roy.
In the third match, Logan, Quinn, Eve, Roy, Ash, and Eli lost to Ray, Val, Sid, Ken, Mel, and Dan.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Logan ranks in terms of intrinsic stamina?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Logan put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Team 1 (Logan, Quinn, Ash, Eli, Ray, Val) and Team 2 (Sid, Ken, Mel, Dan, Eve, Roy), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Team 1 (Sid, Ken, Ash, Eli, Eve, Roy) and Team 2 (Ray, Val, Mel, Dan, Logan, Quinn), who would win and by how much?
