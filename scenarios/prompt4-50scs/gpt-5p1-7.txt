BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are playing a series of short baseball scrimmages. Each scrimmage is played with small mixed squads. The outcome of each game depends on the players’ intrinsic hitting, pitching, fielding, and base-running ability, along with how much effort they put into that particular scrimmage.
All scrimmages take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first scrimmage, Diego, Marco, Luis, and Owen beat Caleb, Jonah, Eric, and Finn.
In the second scrimmage, Diego, Caleb, and Eric beat Marco, Jonah, and Finn.
In the third scrimmage, Luis and Owen lost to Caleb and Marco in a shortened game.
In the fourth scrimmage, Jonah and Finn beat Diego and Luis.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random baseball players, where do you think Diego ranks in terms of intrinsic baseball skill?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Marco put into the third scrimmage?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Diego, Luis, and Jonah (Team 1) and Caleb, Eric, and Finn (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Marco and Owen (Team 1) and Diego and Caleb (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
