BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of beach volleyball games. In each game, the winning team is determined by how effectively the players can serve, pass, set, and spike, which depends on their intrinsic jumping ability, coordination, and game sense, modulated by how much effort they put into that game.
Athletes compete either individually in simple serving contests or as a team of two.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Hugo and Sam lost to Ben and Carlos.
In the second game, Hugo and Sam beat Diego and Elias.
In the third game, Hugo and Sam lost to Carlos and Diego.
In a serving contest later, Ben beat Elias.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random beach volleyball players, where do you think Hugo ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Sam put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Hugo and Sam (Team 1) and Ben and Diego (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Carlos and Elias (Team 1) and Sam and Ben (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
