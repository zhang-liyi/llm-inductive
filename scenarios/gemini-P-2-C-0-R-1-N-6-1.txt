BACKGROUND
In this tournament, athletes compete in Ultimate Frisbee matches. Each team consists of exactly 8 players on the field at a time. The team that wins is determined by the collective speed, agility, and tactical execution of the players, based on their intrinsic athleticism modulated by the effort they put into that specific match.
Players have fixed teams and do not switch rosters between matches.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Caleb's team beat Julian's team.
In the second match, Caleb's team beat Adrian's team.
In the third match, Caleb's team lost to Dominic's team.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random Ultimate players, where do you think Caleb ranks in terms of intrinsic athleticism?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Caleb's team put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Caleb's team (Team 1) and Julian's team (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Dominic's team (Team 1) and Adrian's team (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
