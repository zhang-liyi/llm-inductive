BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of 3x3 Basketball games. In each game, the team that wins depends on the total points scored, which is a result of the athletes' intrinsic shooting and defensive skills modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that game.
Athletes compete either individually (1v1) or as a fixed team of three.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Marcus, Jay, and Ray beat Steph, Klay, and Dray.
In the second game, Marcus beat Steph.
In the third game, Marcus, Jay, and Ray beat Steph, Klay, and Dray.
In the fourth game, Marcus, Jay, and Ray lost to Steph, Klay, and Dray.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Marcus ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Marcus put into the fourth game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Marcus and Steph (Team 1) and Jay and Klay (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Ray and Dray (Team 1) and Marcus and Steph (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
