BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Rugby Sevens matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the average speed and tackling ability of the athletes, based on their intrinsic strength modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete in teams of 7.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Jamie, Oscar, George, Harry, Charlie, Leo, and Jack beat Archie, Thomas, Freddie, Alfie, Theo, Arthur, and Max.
In the second match, Jamie, Archie, Thomas, Freddie, Alfie, Theo, and Arthur beat Oscar, George, Harry, Charlie, Leo, Jack, and Max.
In the third match, Jamie, Oscar, Archie, George, Thomas, Harry, and Freddie beat Charlie, Leo, Jack, Alfie, Theo, Arthur, and Max.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Jamie ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Max put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Jamie, Oscar, George, Harry, Charlie, Leo, and Jack (Team 1) and Archie, Thomas, Freddie, Alfie, Theo, Arthur, and Max (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Oscar, George, Harry, Charlie, Leo, Jack, and Max (Team 1) and Jamie, Archie, Thomas, Freddie, Alfie, Theo, and Arthur (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
