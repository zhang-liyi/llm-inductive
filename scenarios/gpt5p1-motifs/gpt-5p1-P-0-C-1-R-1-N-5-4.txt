BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are playing a series of small‑sided Field Hockey games. The team that wins depends on the players’ intrinsic stick skills, speed, decision-making, and shooting accuracy, modulated by how much effort they put into that game.
Players compete either individually in 1v1 attack‑versus‑defense drills or in teams of 7.
All games take place on the same day.
Most players stay on fixed squads, but Nico switches between teams.

In the second game, Nico joined Ness, Willow, Harper, Jamie, Morgan, Alex, and Blake and they beat Casey, Max, Taylor, Riley, Sam, Quinn, and Devon.

In the fourth game, without Nico, Casey, Max, Taylor, Riley, Sam, Quinn, and Devon lost to Ness, Willow, Harper, Jamie, Morgan, Alex, and Blake.
