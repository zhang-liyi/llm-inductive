BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes are competing in indoor field hockey games. In each game, the winner is determined by the tactical speed and stick-work of the six players on the court, based on their intrinsic skill level modulated by the physical effort they exert during play.
Athletes compete in teams of six.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Team Alpha beat Team Omega (featuring Hugo, Victor, Charles, Henry, Louis, and George).
In the second game, Team Beta beat Team Omega (featuring Hugo, Victor, Charles, Henry, Louis, and George).
In the third game, Team Omega (featuring Hugo, Victor, Charles, Henry, Louis, and George) lost to Team Gamma.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Hugo ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Team Gamma put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Team Alpha (Team 1) and Team Omega (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Team Beta (Team 1) and Team Gamma (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
