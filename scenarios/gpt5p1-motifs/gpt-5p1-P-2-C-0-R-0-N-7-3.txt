BACKGROUND

All games take place on the same day.
Whenever Diego plays, he is always on the same side with the same eight teammates: Alan, Ben, Chris, Eric, Frank, Greg, Hector, and Isaac.

QUERIES

Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Diego and his usual eight teammates (Team 1) and Jason, Kevin, Luis, Mark, Nate, Oscar, Paul, Ron, and Steve (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Jason, Kevin, Luis, Mark, Nate, Oscar, Tom, Uri, and Vince (Team 1) and Kevin, Luis, Mark, Nate, Oscar, Paul, Ron, Steve, and Vince (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
