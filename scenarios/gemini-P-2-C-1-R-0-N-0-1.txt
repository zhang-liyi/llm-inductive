BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Tennis matches. In each match, the winner is determined by the precision and speed of their returns, based on their intrinsic skill level modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that match.
Athletes compete either individually or as a team.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Rohan beat Kyra.
In the second match, Rohan and Seth beat Kyra and Tara.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Rohan ranks in terms of intrinsic skill level?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Rohan put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Rohan and Seth (Team 1) and Kyra and Tara (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Seth and Kyra (Team 1) and Rohan and Tara (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
