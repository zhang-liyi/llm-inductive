BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are playing team table tennis matches. Each squad has three players, and the overall team result depends on the combined intrinsic table tennis skills (touch, consistency, spin control) of the players and how much effort they put into their individual games during that match.
Teams are of size three.
Most players stay in fixed squads throughout the day, but Nina plays on different squads in different matches.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Nina, Adam, and Bella beat David, Ella, and Felix.
In the second match, Nina, Chris, and Grace beat David, Ella, and Felix.
In the third match, Nina, Henry, and Ivy beat Adam, Bella, and Chris.
In the fourth match, Nina beat Felix.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random table tennis players, where do you think Nina ranks in terms of intrinsic table tennis ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Adam put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Nina, Bella, and Grace (Team 1) and David, Ella, and Chris (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Adam, Bella, and Chris (Team 1) and David, Ella, and Felix (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
