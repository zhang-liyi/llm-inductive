BACKGROUND
In this track cycling meet, riders compete in individual time trials and 4‑rider team pursuit races on an indoor velodrome. The result of each race depends on the riders’ intrinsic power and endurance, together with how much effort they put into that race.
Riders compete either individually or as part of a 4‑rider pursuit team.
Each rider belongs to exactly one fixed 4‑rider team for team pursuits, and these lineups do not change.
All races take place on the same day.
Team Velocity: Clara, Nina, Isabel, Greta.
Team Apex: Marta, Sofia, Elena, Laura.
Team Zenith: Paula, Irene, Lucia, Rosa.

CONDITIONS
In an individual time trial, Clara beat Marta.
In the first team pursuit, Velocity beat Apex.
In the second team pursuit, Velocity lost to Zenith.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random track cyclists, where do you think Clara ranks in terms of intrinsic cycling ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Rosa put into the second team pursuit?
Query 3: In a new team pursuit later this same day between Velocity and Zenith, who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new team pursuit later this same day between Apex and Zenith, who would win and by how much?
