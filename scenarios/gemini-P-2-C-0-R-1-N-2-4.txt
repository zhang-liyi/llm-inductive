BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of cross-country skiing relays. In each race, the team that wins depends on the average speed and endurance of the skiers, based on their intrinsic strength modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that race.
Athletes compete as a fixed team of four.
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, Anders, Nils, Olaf, and Per beat Jon, Tim, Rob, and Gil.
In the second race, Anders, Nils, Olaf, and Per beat Dan, Nic, Pat, and Sal.
In the third race, Anders, Nils, Olaf, and Per lost to Rex, Ty, Vin, and Wes.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Anders ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Rex put into the third race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Anders, Nils, Olaf, and Per (Team 1) and Rex, Ty, Jon, and Tim (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Vin, Wes, Rob, and Gil (Team 1) and Dan, Nic, Pat, and Sal (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
