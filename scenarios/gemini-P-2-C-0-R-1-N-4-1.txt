BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes are competing in a series of Ice Hockey games. Each team consists of 6 players on the ice. The winner is decided by the skating speed and puck handling accuracy of the squad, based on their intrinsic skill modulated by factors such as the physical effort they put into that specific game.
Teams are fixed and do not trade players.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Dmitri and his 5 teammates beat The Glaciers.
In the second game, Dmitri and his 5 teammates lost to The Pucks.
In the third game, Dmitri and his 5 teammates beat The Skaters.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Dmitri ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think the goalie of The Pucks put into the second game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Dmitri’s team (Team 1) and The Glaciers (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between The Pucks (Team 1) and The Skaters (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
