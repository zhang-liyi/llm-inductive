BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of indoor volleyball matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the players’ intrinsic volleyball ability, modulated by how much focused effort they apply during that match.
Players compete either individually (for serving contests) or in teams of eight.
All matches take place on the same day.
