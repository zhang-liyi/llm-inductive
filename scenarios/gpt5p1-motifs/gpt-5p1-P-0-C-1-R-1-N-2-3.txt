BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of 4-on-4 Ultimate Frisbee games. In each game, the team that wins depends on how well the players can run, cut, and throw accurately, based on their intrinsic Ultimate Frisbee ability and endurance, modulated by how much effort they put in to that game.
Athletes compete either individually in 1-on-1 throwing and cutting games, or as a team of four.
All games take place on the same day.
