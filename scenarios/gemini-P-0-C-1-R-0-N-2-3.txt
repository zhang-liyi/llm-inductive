BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Rowing races (Coxless Four). In each race, the boat that wins depends on the average velocity generated, based on their intrinsic stroke power and stamina modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that race.
Athletes compete in teams of 4.
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, Quinn, Sarah, Emily, and Jessica beat Ashley, Amanda, Brittany, and Megan.
In the second race, Quinn, Ashley, Amanda, and Brittany beat Sarah, Emily, Jessica, and Megan.
In the third race, Quinn, Megan, Sarah, and Ashley beat Emily, Jessica, Amanda, and Brittany.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Quinn ranks in terms of intrinsic stroke power and stamina?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Emily put into the third race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Quinn, Sarah, Emily, and Jessica (Team 1) and Ashley, Amanda, Brittany, and Megan (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Sarah, Emily, Jessica, and Megan (Team 1) and Ashley, Amanda, Brittany, and Megan (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
