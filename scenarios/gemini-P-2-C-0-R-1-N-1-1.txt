BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Ice Hockey scrimmages played on a half-rink. In each scrimmage, the team that wins depends on the number of goals scored, based on their intrinsic skating and puck-handling abilities modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that scrimmage.
Athletes compete as a fixed team of three.
All scrimmages take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first scrimmage, Sarah, Kendall, and Brianna beat Hilary, Meghan, and Amanda.
In the second scrimmage, Sarah, Kendall, and Brianna beat Hilary, Meghan, and Amanda.
In the third scrimmage, Sarah, Kendall, and Brianna lost to Hilary, Meghan, and Amanda.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Sarah ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Sarah put into the third scrimmage?
Query 3: In a new scrimmage later this same day between Sarah and Hilary (Team 1) and Kendall and Meghan (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new scrimmage later this same day between Brianna and Amanda (Team 1) and Sarah and Hilary (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
