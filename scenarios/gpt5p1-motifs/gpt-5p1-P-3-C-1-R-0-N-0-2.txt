BACKGROUND  
In this event, the players are competing in a series of small-sided soccer games. Each game is 2-on-2 on a reduced field. Results depend on the players’ intrinsic soccer abilities (ball control, speed, decision-making) along with how much effort they put into each game.  
All games are played on the same day.

CONDITIONS  
In the first game, Riya and Tom lost to Lila and Marco.  
In the second game, Riya and Lila lost to Tom and Yusuf.  
In the third game, Riya and Yusuf beat Emma and Marco.  
In the fourth game, Riya and Emma lost to Tom and Lila.

QUERIES  
Query 1: Out of 100 random small-sided soccer players, where do you think Riya ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Tom put into the fourth game?  
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Riya and Tom (Team 1) and Yusuf and Marco (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Riya and Lila (Team 1) and Emma and Yusuf (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
