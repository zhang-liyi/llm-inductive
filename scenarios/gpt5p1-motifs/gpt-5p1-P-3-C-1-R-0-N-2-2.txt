BACKGROUND
In this event, the players are competing in a series of tennis matches, sometimes in singles and sometimes in a format where four players share one side of the court as a team. Match outcomes depend on each player’s intrinsic tennis skill (serving, strokes, movement) and the effort they put into that match.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Nora lost to Felix.
In the second match, Nora, Liam, Grace, and Omar lost to Felix, Ruby, Caleb, and Dana.
In the third match, Nora, Ruby, Omar, and Theo lost to Felix, Grace, Caleb, and Liam.
In the fourth match, Nora, Caleb, Dana, and Liam beat Felix, Ruby, Omar, and Theo.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random tennis players, where do you think Nora ranks in terms of intrinsic tennis ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Felix put into the fourth match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Nora, Felix, Grace, and Theo (Team 1) and Ruby, Liam, Caleb, and Omar (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Nora, Dana, Ruby, and Liam (Team 1) and Felix, Grace, Caleb, and Theo (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
