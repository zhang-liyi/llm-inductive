BACKGROUND
In this rowing regatta, three eights (boats with 8 rowers each) race multiple times over the same distance. The result of each race depends on the rowers’ intrinsic strength, endurance, and technical rhythm, modulated by how much effort they put into that race.
Each boat keeps the same 8 rowers for all races.
Jonas rows in Boat Orion in every race with the same seven crewmates.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, Boat Orion beat Boat Vega.
In the second race, Boat Orion beat Boat Lynx.
In the third race, Boat Vega beat Boat Orion.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random sweep rowers, where do you think Jonas ranks in terms of intrinsic rowing ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Rafael (in Boat Vega) put into the third race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Boat Orion and Boat Lynx, who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Boat Vega and Boat Lynx, who would win and by how much?
