BACKGROUND

Players compete either individually or as part of an 8‑player team.
All games take place on the same day.

The Red team usually consists of Kai, Liam, Noah, Ava, Maya, Zoe, Ethan, and Lucas.
The Blue team usually consists of Ben, Ivy, Chloe, Aria, Finn, Omar, Luis, and Nora.

Jordan is the only player who moves between teams.

CONDITIONS

In the second game, Jordan joined the Blue team (replacing Nora), and the Blue team beat the Green team.

QUERIES
