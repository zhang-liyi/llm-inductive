BACKGROUND
In this tournament, athletes compete in Handball games. Each team fields 7 players on the court. The winning team is determined by the average throwing power and agility of the athletes (intrinsic strength), adjusted by their defensive coordination and the effort they put into the game.
Players remain on the same fixed teams throughout the tournament.
All games take place in the same arena.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Silas's team beat Team Alpha.
In the second game, Silas's team beat Team Beta.
In the third game, Silas's team beat Team Gamma.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random Handball players, where do you think Silas ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Team Alpha put into the first game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Silas's team and Team Delta, who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Team Alpha and Team Beta, who would win and by how much?
