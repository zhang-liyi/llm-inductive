BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Cross-Country Skiing (Relay) races. In each race, the team that wins depends on the average pace with which the athletes are able to ski, based on their intrinsic endurance modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that race.
Athletes compete as a team of 4.
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, Elin, Anna, Britt, and Cajsa beat Dagny, Ebba, Freja, and Greta.
In the second race, Elin, Dagny, Ebba, and Freja beat Anna, Britt, Cajsa, and Greta.
In the third race, Elin, Anna, Britt, and Cajsa lost to Dagny, Ebba, Freja, and Greta.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Elin ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Anna put into the third race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Elin, Dagny, Anna, and Britt (Team 1) and Ebba, Freja, Cajsa, and Greta (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Anna, Britt, Dagny, and Ebba (Team 1) and Elin, Cajsa, Freja, and Greta (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
