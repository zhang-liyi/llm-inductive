BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of rowing regattas. In each race, the team that wins depends on the boat speed, based on the athletes' intrinsic physical strength and technique, modulated by how much effort they put into that race.
Athletes compete as a fixed team of three.
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, James, Steve, and Matthew beat Josh, Greg, and Peter.
In the second race, James, Steve, and Matthew beat Josh, Greg, and Peter.
In the third race, James, Steve, and Matthew beat Josh, Greg, and Peter.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think James ranks in terms of intrinsic physical strength and technique?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Josh put into the third race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between James, Steve, and Matthew (Team 1) and Josh, Greg, and Peter (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Steve and Matthew (Team 1) and Greg and Peter (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
