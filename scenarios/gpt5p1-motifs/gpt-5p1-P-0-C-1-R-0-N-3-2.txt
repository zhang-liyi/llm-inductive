BACKGROUND
In this event, the players are competing in a series of short indoor volleyball scrimmages played 5-on-5. The outcome of each game depends on the players’ intrinsic volleyball ability (serving, passing, setting, hitting, blocking) and the amount of effort they put into that game.  
Players compete either individually (in serving-and-rally challenges) or on 5-player teams.  
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Diego, Marco, Luis, Bruno, and Alex beat Ethan, Carlo, Yuri, Nico, and Pavel.  
In the second game, Diego, Yuri, Pavel, Simon, and Tomas beat Marco, Bruno, Ethan, Alex, and Carlo.  
In the third game, Diego beat Ethan in a serving-and-rally challenge.  
In the fourth game, Diego, Carlo, Bruno, Luis, and Simon beat Yuri, Marco, Alex, Nico, and Tomas.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random volleyball players, where do you think Diego ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Ethan put into the third game?  
Query 3: In a new 5-on-5 game later this same day between Diego, Marco, Pavel, Nico, and Tomas (Team 1) and Ethan, Bruno, Alex, Yuri, and Luis (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Carlo and Pavel (a 1-on-1 serving-and-rally challenge), who would win and by how much?
