BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of wrestling matches. In each match, the wrestler or team that wins depends on grappling skill, strength, and balance, combined with how much focused effort they put into that match.  
Athletes compete either individually or in tag-team pairs.  
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Liam beat Noah.  
In the second match, Liam and Ava beat Noah and Emma.  
In the third match, Noah and Emma beat Ava and Mia.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random wrestlers, where do you think Liam ranks in terms of intrinsic strength and skill?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Noah put into the first match?  
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Liam and Mia (Team 1) and Noah and Ava (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Emma (Team 1) and Ava (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
