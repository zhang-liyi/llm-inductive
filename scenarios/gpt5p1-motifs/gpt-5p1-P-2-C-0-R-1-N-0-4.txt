BACKGROUND  
In this event, the athletes compete in table tennis. Matches can be doubles, where two players form a team, or occasionally singles. The winner of each match is the side that can produce higher-quality shots and rallies overall, driven by the players’ intrinsic table tennis skill moderated by how much effort they invest in that match.  
Whenever athletes play doubles, they stay with the same partner throughout the event.  
All matches are played on the same day.

CONDITIONS  
In the first match, Priya and Elena beat Jonah and Luis.  
In the second match, Priya and Elena beat Mira and Quinn.  
In the third match, Jonah and Luis beat Priya and Elena.  
In the fourth match, Priya beat Jonah.

QUERIES  
Query 1: Out of 100 random table tennis players, where do you think Priya ranks in terms of intrinsic playing strength?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Jonah put into the third match?  
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Priya and Elena (Team 1) and Jonah and Luis (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Mira and Quinn (Team 1) and Jonah and Luis (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
