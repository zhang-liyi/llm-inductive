BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of half-court 4-on-4 basketball games and occasional 1-on-1 games. In each game, the team that wins depends on the players’ intrinsic basketball ability (shooting, ball-handling, defense, fitness) and on how much effort they put into that specific game.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS

In the fourth game, Lena, Jordan, Ben, and Ava lost to Riley, Marco, Sam, and Priya.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random basketball players, where do you think Lena ranks in terms of intrinsic basketball ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Marco put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Lena, Marco, Riley, and Ava (Team 1) and Tessa, Priya, Sam, and Jordan (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Noah, Ben, Priya, and Jordan (Team 1) and Lena, Marco, Riley, and Sam (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
