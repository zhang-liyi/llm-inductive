BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of baseball scrimmages. Each scrimmage is played between two teams of 9 players. The team that wins depends on the players’ intrinsic batting, pitching, and fielding abilities, combined with how much effort they put into that game.
Athletes sometimes face off individually in pitcher-versus-batter duels, or play full 9‑on‑9 games.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first scrimmage, Dylan and eight others lost to Harper and eight others.
In the second scrimmage, Dylan, Noah and seven others lost to Harper, Sofia and seven others.
In a later pitcher–batter duel, Dylan lost to Harper.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random baseball players, where do you think Harper ranks in terms of intrinsic playing ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Dylan put into the second scrimmage?
Query 3: In a new scrimmage later this same day between Dylan, Sofia and seven others (Team 1) and Harper, Noah and seven others (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new pitcher–batter duel later this same day between Dylan (batter) and Noah (pitcher), who would win and by how much?
