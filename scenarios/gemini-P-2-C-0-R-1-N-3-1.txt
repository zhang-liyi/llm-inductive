BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Ultimate Frisbee matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the number of goals scored, based on the athletes' intrinsic speed and handling skills modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete in fixed teams of five.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Sarah, Jen, Liz, Meg, and Kate beat Alex, Sam, Jo, Nic, and Pat.
In the second match, Sarah, Jen, Liz, Meg, and Kate beat Chris, Mike, Dave, Tom, and Ben.
In the third match, Sarah, Jen, Liz, Meg, and Kate lost to Alex, Sam, Jo, Nic, and Pat.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Sarah ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Alex put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Sarah, Jen, Liz, Meg, and Kate (Team 1) and Chris, Mike, Dave, Tom, and Ben (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Alex, Sam, Jo, Nic, and Pat (Team 1) and Chris, Mike, Dave, Tom, and Ben (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
