BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes compete in a **Basketball** tournament (4v4 format). The winning team is determined by the average scoring ability and defensive prowess of the players, based on their intrinsic skill level modulated by the hustle and effort they display in that game. Athletes compete either in 1-on-1 matchups or in fixed teams of four. All games take place on the same day.
