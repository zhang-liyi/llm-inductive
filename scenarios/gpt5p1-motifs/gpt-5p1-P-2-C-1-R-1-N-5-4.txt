BACKGROUND
In this field hockey exhibition, teams of 7 players compete in short-sided matches, and certain players also enter 1v1 dribbling challenges. Each result is determined by the players’ intrinsic stick skills, speed, and coordination, together with the effort they invest in that particular game. Most athletes remain with their standard lineups (the Lions, Bears, and Wolves), but one standout midfielder, Diego, is allowed to switch sides from match to match. All matches and challenges take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Diego playing for the Lions beat the Bears.
In the second match, Diego playing for the Bears beat the Wolves.
In the third contest, Diego beat Noah in a 1v1 dribbling challenge.
In the fourth match, Diego playing for the Wolves lost to the Lions.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random field hockey players, where do you think Diego ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Noah put into the third contest?
Query 3: In a new 7-a-side match later this same day between Diego and the Lions (Team 1) and the Wolves (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new 7-a-side match later this same day between Diego and the Bears (Team 1) and the Lions (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
