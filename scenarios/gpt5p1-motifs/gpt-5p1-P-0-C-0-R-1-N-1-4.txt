BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in track cycling team sprint races. Each team has three riders, and the outcome of each race depends on the riders’ intrinsic power, aerodynamics, pacing sense, and bike handling, combined with how much effort they put into that race.
Riders compete only in fixed three-rider teams.
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS

QUERIES

Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Karin put into the third race?  
Query 3: In a new team sprint race later this same day between Eva, Marta, and Sofia (Team 1) and Chloe, Isabelle, and Nina (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
