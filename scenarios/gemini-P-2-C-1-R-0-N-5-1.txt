BACKGROUND

Athletes compete in teams of 7.
All games take place on the same day.

In the third game, Sienna, Liam, Lucas, Noah, Henry, Eli, and Alex lost to James, Mason, Will, Mike, Ben, Ethan, and Dan.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Sienna ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Sienna put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Sienna, Liam, Noah, Eli, James, Will, and Ben (Team 1) and Lucas, Henry, Alex, Mason, Mike, Ethan, and Dan (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
