BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Pickleball matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the average shot placement and court coverage of the athletes, based on their intrinsic strategy modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that match.
Athletes compete as a fixed pair.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Ben and Collin beat Gary and Phil.
In the second match, Gary and Phil lost to Ben and Collin.
In the third match, Ben and Collin beat Gary and Phil.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Ben ranks in terms of intrinsic strategy?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Gary put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Ben and Collin (Team 1) and Gary and Phil (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Gary and Collin (Team 1) and Ben and a new player named Jack (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
