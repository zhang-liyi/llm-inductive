BACKGROUND
In this event, the rowers are competing in single sculls and 4-person boats. The outcome of each race depends on the rowers’ intrinsic rowing power and technique, combined with how much effort they put into that specific race.
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, Diego beat Harper.
In the second race, Diego, Lila, Owen, and Grace beat Harper, Quinn, Miles, and Sara.
In the third race, Diego, Miles, Sara, and Jonah beat Harper, Lila, Owen, and Quinn.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random rowers, where do you think Diego ranks in terms of intrinsic rowing ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Harper put into the third race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Diego, Lila, Miles, and Quinn (Team 1) and Harper, Owen, Sara, and Jonah (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Lila, Owen, Miles, and Grace (Team 1) and Harper, Quinn, Sara, and Jonah (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
