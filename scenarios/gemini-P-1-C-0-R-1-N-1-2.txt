BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of 3-on-3 ice hockey scrimmages. In each scrimmage, the team that wins depends on the number of goals scored, based on the athletes' skating speed and puck handling, modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that scrimmage.
Athletes compete either individually (shootout drill) or as a fixed team of three.
All scrimmages take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first scrimmage, The Jets (Sidney, Evgeni, and Kris) beat The Sharks (Sven, Lars, and Erik).
In the second scrimmage, The Oilers (Connor, Leon, and Ryan) beat The Sharks (Sven, Lars, and Erik).
In the third scrimmage, Sidney beat Sven.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Sven ranks in terms of intrinsic skating speed and puck handling?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Sven put into the third scrimmage?
Query 3: In a new scrimmage later this same day between Sidney and Connor (Team 1) and Sven and Lars (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new scrimmage later this same day between Evgeni and Leon (Team 1) and Erik and Ryan (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
