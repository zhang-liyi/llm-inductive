BACKGROUND
In this event, the players are competing in a series of small-sided ultimate frisbee games played 5-on-5. Each game’s result depends on the players’ intrinsic ultimate skills (throwing accuracy, speed, catching, cutting) and the effort they put into that game.  
Players compete either individually (in throwing accuracy duels) or as part of a 5-player team.  
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Lena, Harper, Jonah, Miles, and Zoe beat Caleb, Dina, Iris, Nolan, and Paige.  
In the second game, Lena, Caleb, Iris, Owen, and Theo beat Harper, Jonah, Dina, Miles, and Quinn.  
In the third game, Lena beat Quinn in a throwing accuracy duel.  
In the fourth game, Lena, Dina, Nolan, Paige, and Theo beat Harper, Caleb, Iris, Jonah, and Zoe.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random ultimate frisbee players, where do you think Lena ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Quinn put into the third game?  
Query 3: In a new 5-on-5 game later this same day between Lena, Harper, Quinn, Nolan, and Miles (Team 1) and Caleb, Dina, Jonah, Owen, and Zoe (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Iris and Paige (a throwing accuracy duel), who would win and by how much?
