BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Beach Volleyball matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the average agility and power with which the athletes are able to play, based on their intrinsic athleticism modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete as a fixed team of two.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Lucas and Mateo beat Alex and Ryan.
In the second match, Lucas and Mateo beat Seth and Cole.
In the third match, Lucas and Mateo beat Alex and Ryan.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Lucas ranks in terms of intrinsic athleticism?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Alex put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Lucas and Mateo (Team 1) and Ryan and a new player named Jack (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Alex and Seth (Team 1) and Mateo and Cole (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
