BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Table Tennis matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the average hand-eye coordination and spin control of the athletes, based on their intrinsic skill modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that match.
Athletes compete either individually or as a fixed pair.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Xu beat Tim.
In the second match, Xu and Wang beat Tim and Rob.
In the third match, Tim and Rob lost to Xu and Wang.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Xu ranks in terms of intrinsic skill?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Tim put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Xu and Wang (Team 1) and Tim and Rob (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Tim and Wang (Team 1) and Xu and a new player named Lin (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
