BACKGROUND
In this tournament, athletes compete in Ultimate Frisbee matches. Each side fields a team of 8 players. The winning team is decided by the score, which reflects the players' intrinsic agility and tactical skill, modulated by the intensity of effort they put into the match.
The teams (Alpha, Beta, Gamma, Delta) have fixed rosters, except for Maya, who is a wildcard player assigned to different teams for different matches.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Team Alpha (with Maya) beat Team Beta.
In the second match, Team Gamma (with Maya) beat Team Beta.
In the third match, Team Gamma (with Maya) beat Team Delta.
In the fourth match, Team Gamma (with Maya) lost to Team Alpha.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Maya ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Team Alpha put into the fourth match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Team Alpha (with Maya) and Team Gamma (without Maya), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Team Beta (with Maya) and Team Delta (without Maya), who would win and by how much?
