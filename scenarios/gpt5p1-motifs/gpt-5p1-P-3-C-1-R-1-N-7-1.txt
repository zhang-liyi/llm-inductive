BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of rugby games and goal‑kicking contests. In each game, the team that wins is the one whose players can run, pass, tackle, and kick more effectively, based on their intrinsic strength, speed, coordination, and how much effort they put into that game.
Athletes compete either individually or as a team of 9 players.
All games and contests take place on the same day. Most players remain with fixed 9‑player squads, but one player, Priya, is moved between different teams over the course of the event.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Priya, Ethan, and seven other Stags lost to Noah, Liam, and six other Wolves.
In the second game, Priya, Noah, and seven other Wolves lost to Hana, Omar, and six other Hawks.
In a drop‑goal kicking contest later that day, Priya beat Ethan.
In the final game, Priya, Hana, and seven other Hawks lost to Liam, Omar, and seven other Wolves.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random rugby players, where do you think Priya ranks in terms of intrinsic rugby ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Ethan put into the first game?
Query 3: In a new rugby game later this same day between Priya, Ethan, and seven other Stags (Team 1) and Noah, Liam, and seven other Wolves (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new rugby game later this same day between Priya, Hana, and seven other Hawks (Team 1) and Noah, Omar, and seven other Wolves (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
