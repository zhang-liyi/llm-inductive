BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes are competing in Ultimate Frisbee games played with 6 players per team. The winning team depends on the average running speed and throwing accuracy of the squad, based on intrinsic endurance modulated by the effort put into the game.
The tournament features fixed teams: The Hucks, The Cuts, and The Dumps. One premier handler, Parker, switches teams between rounds.
All games happen on the same afternoon.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, The Hucks (featuring Parker) beat The Cuts (led by Drew).
In the second game, The Dumps (featuring Parker) beat The Hucks (led by Reese).
In the third game, The Cuts (featuring Parker) beat The Dumps (led by Cameron).

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random ultimate players, where do you think Parker ranks in terms of intrinsic endurance?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Cameron put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between The Hucks (featuring Parker) and The Dumps (led by Cameron), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between The Cuts (led by Drew) and The Hucks (led by Reese), who would win and by how much?
