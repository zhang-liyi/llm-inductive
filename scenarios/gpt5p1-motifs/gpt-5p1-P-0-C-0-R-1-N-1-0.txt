BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of 3-on-3 half-court basketball games. In each game, the team that wins depends on the players’ intrinsic shooting accuracy, ball-handling, speed, and court vision, modulated by how much effort they put into that specific game.
Players compete either in fixed three-player squads or, in one case, individually.
All games take place on the same day.

In the fourth game, Jordan beat Leo in a 1v1 game.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random basketball players, where do you think Jordan ranks in terms of intrinsic basketball ability?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Leo put into the third game?  
Query 3: In a new 3-on-3 game later this same day between Jordan, Malik, and Owen (Team 1) and Leo, Sam, and Tyler (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new 3-on-3 game later this same day between Victor, Ryan, and Cole (Team 1) and a new team of Noah, Elias, and Grant (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
