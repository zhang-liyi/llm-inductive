BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Ultimate Frisbee games. In each game, the team that wins depends on the score reached, based on the athletes' intrinsic agility and throwing accuracy modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that game.
Athletes compete in teams of 5.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Sarah, Jen, Meg, Liz, and Amy beat Kim, Pat, Sue, Deb, and Val.
In the second game, Sarah, Jen, Meg, Liz, and Amy beat Kim, Pat, Flo, Bea, and Dot.
In the third game, Sarah, Jen, Meg, Liz, and Amy lost to Sue, Deb, Flo, Bea, and Dot.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Sarah ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Sarah put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Sarah, Jen, Meg, Liz, and Amy (Team 1) and Kim, Pat, Sue, Flo, and Bea (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Sue, Deb, Flo, Bea, and Dot (Team 1) and Val, Kim, Pat, Jen, and Meg (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
