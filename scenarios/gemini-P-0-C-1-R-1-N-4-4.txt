BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes compete in Indoor Field Hockey matches. Each side fields 6 players. The outcome is determined by the team's collective tactical awareness and dribbling speed, based on intrinsic talent modulated by the mental and physical effort exerted.
There are three fixed squads: Red Squad, Blue Squad, and Green Squad. A highly skilled player, Dakota, rotates into the lineups.
All matches are held consecutively.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Red Squad (featuring Dakota) beat Blue Squad (led by Pat).
In the second match, Green Squad (featuring Dakota) beat Red Squad (led by Lou).
In the third match, Blue Squad (featuring Dakota) beat Green Squad (led by Sam).

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random field hockey players, where do you think Dakota ranks in terms of intrinsic talent?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Sam put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Red Squad (featuring Dakota) and Green Squad (led by Sam), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Blue Squad (led by Pat) and Red Squad (led by Lou), who would win and by how much?
