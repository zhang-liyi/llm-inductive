BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Ultimate Frisbee matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the collective scoring ability of the athletes, based on their intrinsic speed and handling skills modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete in teams of 4.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Jordan, Ben, Liam, and Noah beat Lucas, Mason, Ethan, and Oliver.
In the second match, Jordan, Lucas, Mason, and Ethan beat Ben, Liam, Noah, and Oliver.
In the third match, Jordan, Oliver, Ben, and Lucas beat Mason, Ethan, Liam, and Noah.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Jordan ranks in terms of intrinsic speed and handling skills?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Lucas put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Jordan, Ben, Liam, and Noah (Team 1) and Lucas, Mason, Ethan, and Oliver (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Ben, Liam, Noah, and Oliver (Team 1) and Lucas, Mason, Ethan, and Ethan (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
