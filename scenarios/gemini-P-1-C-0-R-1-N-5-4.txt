BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Lacrosse matches. Each team consists of 7 athletes. In each match, the team that wins depends on the average stick skills and running speed of the athletes, based on their intrinsic capability modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete in fixed teams.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, the Hawks (containing Arlo) lost to the Wolves.
In the second match, the Eagles beat the Hawks (containing Arlo).
In the third match, the Wolves beat the Eagles.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Arlo ranks in terms of intrinsic capability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think the Wolves put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between the Hawks (Team 1) and the Eagles (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between the Wolves (Team 1) and the Eagles (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
