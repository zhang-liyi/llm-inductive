BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Beach Volleyball matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the combined agility and coordination of the athletes, based on their intrinsic athleticism modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete as a team of two.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Javier and Tom beat Sam and Ben.
In the second match, Javier and Ben beat Tom and Sam.
In the third match, Javier and Sam beat Tom and Ben.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Javier ranks in terms of intrinsic athleticism?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Tom put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Javier and Tom (Team 1) and Sam and Ben (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Tom and Ben (Team 1) and Sam and a new player named Chris (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
