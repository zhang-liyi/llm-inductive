BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Ice Hockey scrimmages. In each scrimmage, the team that wins depends on the net goals scored, based on the athletes' intrinsic skating and puck-handling ability modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that scrimmage.
Athletes compete in teams of 5.
All scrimmages take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first scrimmage, Wayne, Mark, Paul, Ray, and Grant beat Mario, Jaromir, Ron, Bryan, and Kevin.
In the second scrimmage, Wayne, Mario, Jaromir, Ron, and Bryan beat Mark, Paul, Ray, Grant, and Kevin.
In the third scrimmage, Wayne, Kevin, Brett, Chris, and Steve beat Joe, Peter, Mike, Dale, and Adam.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Wayne ranks in terms of intrinsic skating and puck-handling ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Mario put into the second scrimmage?
Query 3: In a new scrimmage later this same day between Wayne, Mark, Paul, Ray, and Grant (Team 1) and Mario, Jaromir, Ron, Bryan, and Kevin (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new scrimmage later this same day between Mark, Paul, Ray, Grant, and Kevin (Team 1) and Mario, Jaromir, Ron, Bryan, and Brett (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
