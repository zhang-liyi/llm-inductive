BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Basketball games. In each game, the team that wins depends on the average shooting accuracy and defensive capability of the athletes, based on their intrinsic skill modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that game.
Athletes compete as a team of 5.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Jordan, Scottie, Dennis, Steve, and Luc beat Clyde, Terry, Cliff, Jerome, and Buck.
In the second game, Jordan, Scottie, Dennis, Steve, and Luc beat Clyde, Terry, Cliff, Horace, and John.
In the third game, Jordan, Scottie, Dennis, Steve, and Luc beat Horace, John, Jerome, Buck, and Clyde.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Jordan ranks in terms of intrinsic skill?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Clyde put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Jordan, Scottie, Dennis, Steve, and Luc (Team 1) and Clyde, Terry, Cliff, Jerome, and Buck (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Jordan, Clyde, Horace, Steve, and Buck (Team 1) and Scottie, Terry, Dennis, Cliff, and Luc (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
