BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of track cycling team pursuits. In each race, the team that wins depends on the average speed and aerodynamic efficiency of the cyclists, based on their intrinsic strength modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that race.
Athletes compete as a fixed team of four.
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, Jada, Ken, Leo, and Mari beat Sam, Tom, Ben, and Dan.
In the second race, Jada, Ken, Leo, and Mari lost to Rex, Ty, Vin, and Wes.
In the third race, Jada, Ken, Leo, and Mari beat Ali, Mo, Zak, and Ray.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Jada ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Rex put into the second race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Jada, Ken, Leo, and Mari (Team 1) and Rex, Ty, Sam, and Tom (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Ben, Dan, Ali, and Mo (Team 1) and Vin, Wes, Zak, and Ray (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
