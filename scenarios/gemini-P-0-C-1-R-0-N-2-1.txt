BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of half-court Basketball games. In each game, the team that wins depends on the total points scored, based on their intrinsic shooting accuracy and defense modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that game.
Athletes compete in teams of 4.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Riley, Jay, Kai, and Poe beat Ty, Lou, Sam, and Ray.
In the second game, Riley, Ty, Lou, and Sam beat Jay, Kai, Poe, and Ray.
In the third game, Riley, Ray, Jay, and Ty beat Kai, Poe, Lou, and Sam.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Riley ranks in terms of intrinsic shooting accuracy and defense?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Kai put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Riley, Jay, Kai, and Poe (Team 1) and Ty, Lou, Sam, and Ray (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Jay, Kai, Poe, and Ray (Team 1) and Ty, Lou, Sam, and Ray (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
