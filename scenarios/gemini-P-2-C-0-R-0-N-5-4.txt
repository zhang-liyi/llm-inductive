BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes compete in a series of 7-on-7 flag football games. In each game, the team that wins depends on the collective route-running and catching ability of the athletes, based on their intrinsic talent modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that game.
Athletes compete in teams of 7.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Dax, Eli, Fay, Gil, Hal, Ian, and Jon beat Ken, Leo, Mac, Ned, Obe, Pat, and Ray.
In the second game, Dax, Eli, Ken, Leo, Mac, Ned, and Obe beat Sal, Tim, Uli, Van, Wes, Xab, and Ygo.
In the third game, Dax, Eli, Sal, Tim, Uli, Van, and Wes lost to Fay, Gil, Hal, Ian, Jon, Pat, and Ray.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Dax ranks in terms of intrinsic talent?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Dax put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Team 1 (Dax, Eli, Fay, Gil, Hal, Ian, Jon) and Team 2 (Sal, Tim, Uli, Van, Wes, Xab, Ygo), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Team 1 (Ken, Leo, Mac, Ned, Obe, Pat, Ray) and Team 2 (Sal, Tim, Uli, Van, Wes, Xab, Ygo), who would win and by how much?
