BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Pickleball matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the average precision and court coverage with which the athletes are able to play, based on their intrinsic hand-eye coordination modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete either individually or as a fixed team of two.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Dale and Chip beat Hank and Bill.
In the second match, Dale and Chip beat Jeff and Larry.
In the third match, Dale beat Hank.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Dale ranks in terms of intrinsic hand-eye coordination?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Hank put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Dale and Chip (Team 1) and Bill and a new player named Ross (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Hank and Jeff (Team 1) and Chip and Larry (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
