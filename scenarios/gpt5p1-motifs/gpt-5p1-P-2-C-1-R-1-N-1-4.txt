BACKGROUND
In this table tennis event, players are competing in team matches where three players on each side alternate singles games within a team format, and there is also the occasional standalone singles match. The outcome of each team match depends on the players’ intrinsic racket control and reaction speed, combined with how much focused effort they put into each point on that day.
Teams are usually fixed groups of three, but today Hiro is being moved between different trios while the other players mostly stick with their regular teams.
All matches take place on the same day.

In the fourth match, Hiro beat Akira.
