BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of small-sided soccer games. The result of each game depends on the players’ intrinsic soccer ability (ball control, passing, shooting, and movement) along with how much effort they put in during that game.
Athletes compete either individually (in 1-on-1 duels) or in teams of 5.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Marco, Anna, Carlos, Diego, and Elena lost to Bella, Farid, Hugo, Ivan, and Jia.
In the second game, Marco, Bella, Diego, Hugo, and Karim beat Anna, Carlos, Elena, Farid, and Lara.
In the third game, Marco, Ivan, Jia, Karim, and Mateo lost to Bella, Carlos, Diego, Elena, and Nina.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random soccer players, where do you think Marco ranks in terms of intrinsic soccer ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Bella put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Marco, Bella, Diego, Hugo, and Karim (Team 1) and Anna, Carlos, Elena, Farid, and Nina (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new 1-on-1 duel later this same day between Marco and Carlos, who would win and by how much?
