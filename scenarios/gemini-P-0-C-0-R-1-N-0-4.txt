BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Table Tennis matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the average spin control and speed with which the athletes are able to strike the ball, based on their intrinsic technique modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete as a fixed team of two.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Jin and Tao beat Fritz and Hans.
In the second match, Jin and Tao beat Lars and Sven.
In the third match, Jin and Tao beat Fritz and Hans.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Jin ranks in terms of intrinsic technique?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Fritz put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Jin and Tao (Team 1) and Hans and a new player named Otto (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Fritz and Lars (Team 1) and Tao and Sven (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
