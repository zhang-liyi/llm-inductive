BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes are competing in Ultimate Frisbee matches. The team that wins depends on the collective throwing accuracy and agility of the athletes, based on their intrinsic skill modulated by the hustle and effort they put into that match.
Teams consist of 5 players.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Reese and the Flyers beat the Catchers.
In the second match, Reese and the Catchers beat the Flyers.
In the third match, Reese and the Flyers lost to the Catchers.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random ultimate frisbee players, where do you think Reese ranks in terms of intrinsic skill?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Reese put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Reese and the Catchers (Team 1) and the Flyers (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between the Flyers (Team 1) and the Catchers (Team 2) without Reese, who would win and by how much?
