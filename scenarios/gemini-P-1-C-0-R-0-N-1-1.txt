BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of mini ice hockey matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the average skating speed and puck handling of the athletes, based on their intrinsic agility modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete as a team of three.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Chris, Jordan, and Taylor beat Jamie, Alex, and Pat.
In the second match, Chris, Morgan, and Riley beat Jamie, Alex, and Pat.
In the third match, Jordan, Morgan, and Casey beat Jamie, Alex, and Pat.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Jamie ranks in terms of intrinsic agility?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Chris put into the second match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Chris and Jordan (Team 1) and Jamie and Alex (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Jamie and Pat (Team 1) and Taylor and Riley (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
