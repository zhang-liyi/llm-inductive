BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes participate in Rugby Sevens variations adapted for 8 players per side. The team that wins depends on the combined physical power and speed of the athletes, based on their intrinsic strength modulated by how much effort they put into the scrum and sprints.
There are fixed teams (The Lions, The Tigers, and The Bears), but one elite player, Sam, switches sides to balance the matches.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Sam and The Lions beat The Tigers.
In the second match, Sam and The Tigers beat The Lions.
In the third match, The Bears lost to Sam and The Tigers.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Sam ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think The Bears put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Sam and The Bears (Team 1) and The Lions (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between The Lions (Team 1) and The Tigers (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
