BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of beach volleyball matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the players’ intrinsic volleyball ability (including serving, passing, and hitting) and how much focused effort they put into that match.  
All matches are played as 2-on-2 games.  
All matches take place on the same day, and each athlete’s intrinsic ability is fixed; only their effort varies from match to match.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Riley and Jordan beat Sam and Leo.  
In the second match, Riley and Jordan beat Casey and Morgan.  
In the third match, Sam and Leo beat Casey and Morgan.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random beach volleyball players, where do you think Riley ranks in terms of intrinsic skill?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Leo put into the third match?  
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Riley and Jordan (Team 1) and Sam and Leo (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Sam and Leo (Team 1) and Casey and Morgan (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
