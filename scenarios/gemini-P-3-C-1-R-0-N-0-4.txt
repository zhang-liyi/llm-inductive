BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of pickleball games. In each game, the team that wins depends on the combined placement accuracy and patience of the athletes, based on their intrinsic capability modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that game.
Athletes compete either individually or as a team.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Oscar and Dan lost to Eve and Fay.
In the second game, Oscar and Eve beat Dan and Fay.
In the third game, Oscar and Fay lost to Dan and Eve.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Oscar ranks in terms of intrinsic capability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Eve put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Oscar and Dan (Team 1) and Eve and Fay (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Oscar and Fay (Team 1) and Dan and Eve (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
