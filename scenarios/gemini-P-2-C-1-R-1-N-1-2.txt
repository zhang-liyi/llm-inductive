BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of small-sided soccer matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the number of goals scored, based on the athletes' intrinsic ball control and tactical awareness, modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete as a team of three.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Diego, Pele, and Zico beat Romario, Rivaldo, and Cafu.
In the second match, Diego, Romario, and Rivaldo beat Pele, Zico, and Cafu.
In the third match, Diego, Cafu, and Pele lost to Romario, Rivaldo, and Zico.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Diego ranks in terms of intrinsic ball control?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Diego put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Diego, Pele, and Zico (Team 1) and Romario, Rivaldo, and Cafu (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Romario, Rivaldo, and Pele (Team 1) and Diego, Zico, and Cafu (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
