BACKGROUND
In this event, three fixed teams of five players each are competing in a series of full-court basketball games. Outcomes depend on each player's intrinsic shooting accuracy, speed, coordination, and decision-making, modulated by how much effort they put into each game that day. The same five players always play together on the same team.
Team Orion: Jordan, Malik, Theo, Ryan, Luis.
Team Nova: Carlos, Nate, Omar, Victor, Henry.
Team Titan: Ethan, Pavel, Samir, Leo, Marco.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Team Orion beat Team Nova.
In the second game, Team Orion beat Team Titan.
In the third game, Team Nova beat Team Titan.
In the fourth game, Team Orion beat Team Nova again.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random basketball players, where do you think Jordan ranks in terms of intrinsic basketball ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Samir put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Team Orion (Jordan, Malik, Theo, Ryan, Luis) and Team Titan (Ethan, Pavel, Samir, Leo, Marco), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Team Nova (Carlos, Nate, Omar, Victor, Henry) and Team Titan (Ethan, Pavel, Samir, Leo, Marco), who would win and by how much?
