BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of small-sided soccer matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the goal differential, which is a result of the athletes' ball control and tactical awareness, based on their intrinsic talent modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete as a fixed team of three.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Team Red (Leo, Cristiano, and Neymar) beat Team Blue (Hugo, Louis, and Gabriel).
In the second match, Team Green (Kylian, Erling, and Kevin) beat Team Blue (Hugo, Louis, and Gabriel).
In the third match, Team Yellow (Luka, Karim, and Gareth) beat Team Blue (Hugo, Louis, and Gabriel).

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Hugo ranks in terms of intrinsic ball control and tactical awareness?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Hugo put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Leo and Kylian (Team 1) and Hugo and Louis (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Cristiano and Erling (Team 1) and Gabriel and Kevin (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
