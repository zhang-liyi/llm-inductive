BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a Handball tournament. Each team fields 7 players. The winner is decided by the team's total goal count, which depends on the players' intrinsic throwing power and tactical intelligence, modulated by the effort they put into the game.
Players are generally fixed to specific clubs (North Squad, South Squad, East Squad, West Squad), except for Quinn, who plays as a guest for different clubs.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, North Squad (with Quinn) beat South Squad.
In the second game, East Squad (with Quinn) beat West Squad.
In the third game, South Squad (with Quinn) beat North Squad.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Quinn ranks in terms of intrinsic throwing power?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think North Squad put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between East Squad (with Quinn) and South Squad, who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between North Squad and West Squad, who would win and by how much?
