BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in rowing races using double sculls. In each race, the winning boat depends on how powerfully and smoothly the rowers can drive each stroke, based on their intrinsic endurance, strength, and rhythm, modulated by how much effort they put into that race.
Athletes compete as teams of two.
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, Omar and Felix lost to Clara and Hugo.
In the second race, Omar and Felix beat Ivan and Clara.
In the third race, Omar and Felix lost to Hugo and Lila.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random rowers, where do you think Omar ranks in terms of intrinsic strength and rowing ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Felix put into the third race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Omar and Felix (Boat 1) and Hugo and Ivan (Boat 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Clara and Lila (Boat 1) and Felix and Hugo (Boat 2), who would win and by how much?
