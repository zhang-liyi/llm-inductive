BACKGROUND
In this event, the players are taking part in a series of Ultimate Frisbee scrimmages with 5 players per side, as well as 1-on-1 cutting-and-throwing drills scored as duels. Each result depends on intrinsic Ultimate abilities (speed, cutting, throwing accuracy, catching) and how much effort players put in on each point.
Players compete either in 1-on-1 drills or in 5-player teams.
All games and drills take place on the same day.
