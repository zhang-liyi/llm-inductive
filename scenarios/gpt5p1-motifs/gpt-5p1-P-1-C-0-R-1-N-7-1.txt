BACKGROUND
In this event, three 9-player baseball teams play a set of games. Each game’s result depends on the players’ intrinsic batting, pitching, and fielding talent, together with how much effort they apply during that game.
Teams have fixed rosters of 9 players that stay the same for every game.
Team Thunder: Alex, Jordan, Chris, Devin, Malik, Connor, Sean, Blake, Tyler.
Team Royals: Priya, Anya, Rohan, Vikram, Meera, Nikhil, Tara, Kunal, Sanya.
Team Mariners: Cole, Dylan, Parker, Austin, Bryce, Jared, Logan, Spencer, Grant.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Thunder beat Royals.
In the second game, Mariners beat Royals.
In the third game, Thunder beat Royals again.
In the fourth game, Mariners beat Royals again.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random baseball players, where do you think Priya ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Anya put into the fourth game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Thunder and Mariners, who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Royals and Mariners, who would win and by how much?
