BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Cycling (Track) team pursuit races. In each race, the team that wins depends on the average speed and aerodynamic efficiency the riders can maintain, based on their intrinsic cycling power modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that race.
Athletes compete as a team of four.
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, Freya, Isla, Ivy, and Ruby beat Alice, Clara, Daisy, and Elsie.
In the second race, Freya, Isla, Ivy, and Ruby beat Alice, Clara, Grace, and Heidi.
In the third race, Freya, Isla, Ivy, and Ruby beat Daisy, Elsie, Grace, and Heidi.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Freya ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Alice put into the second race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Freya, Isla, Ivy, and Ruby (Team 1) and Alice, Daisy, Grace, and Heidi (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Clara, Elsie, Grace, and Heidi (Team 1) and Daisy, Alice, Sophie, and Mia (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
