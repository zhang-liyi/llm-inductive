BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Sprint Track (4x100m Relay) races. In each race, the team that wins depends on the average velocity with which the athletes are able to run, based on their intrinsic explosiveness modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that race.
Athletes compete as a team of 4.
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, Andre, Bill, Cam, and Dan beat Ed, Fred, Gil, and Hal.
In the second race, Andre, Ed, Fred, and Gil beat Bill, Cam, Dan, and Hal.
In the third race, Andre, Bill, Cam, and Dan lost to Ed, Fred, Gil, and Hal.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Andre ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Andre put into the third race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Andre, Ed, Bill, and Cam (Team 1) and Fred, Gil, Dan, and Hal (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Bill, Cam, Ed, and Fred (Team 1) and Andre, Dan, Gil, and Hal (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
