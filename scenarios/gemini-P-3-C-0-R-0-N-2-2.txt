BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Ultimate Frisbee matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the speed and throwing accuracy of the athletes, based on their intrinsic athleticism modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete as a team of four.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Mike, Chris, Dave, and Steve lost to Paul, Mark, Luke, and John.
In the second match, Mike, Chris, Dave, and Steve beat Paul, Mark, Peter, and Simon.
In the third match, Mike, Chris, Dave, and Steve lost to Luke, John, Peter, and Simon.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Mike ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Peter put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Mike, Chris, Dave, and Steve (Team 1) and Luke, John, Peter, and Simon (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Paul, Mark, Luke, and John (Team 1) and Paul, Mark, Peter, and Simon (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
