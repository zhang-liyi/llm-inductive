BACKGROUND  
In this event, the athletes are playing a series of small-sided 2-on-2 soccer games. In each game, the result depends on the players’ intrinsic ball control, speed, and finishing ability, combined with how much effort they put into that particular game.  
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS  
In the first game, Marco and Luis beat Nina and Omar.  
In the second game, Marco and Luis beat Jade and Pavel.  
In the third game, Nina and Pavel beat Marco and Luis.

QUERIES  
Query 1: Out of 100 random soccer players, where do you think Marco ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Pavel put into the third game?  
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Marco and Luis (Team 1) and Nina and Pavel (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Jade and Omar (Team 1) and Nina and Pavel (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
