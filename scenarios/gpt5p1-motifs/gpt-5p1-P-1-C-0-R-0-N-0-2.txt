BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in rowing races using double sculls and single sculls. In each race, the winning boat depends on the rowers’ intrinsic power, technique, and endurance, combined with how much effort they put into that race.
Athletes compete either individually or in pairs.
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, Carla and Dan beat Yuki and Ben.
In the second race, Ben and Yuki lost to Emma and Leo.
In a single-scull race, Dan beat Leo.
In the third race, Carla and Emma beat Yuki and Ben.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random rowers, where do you think Emma ranks in terms of intrinsic strength and technique?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Yuki put into the third race?
Query 3: In a new double-scull race later this same day between Dan and Emma (Boat 1) and Leo and Carla (Boat 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new double-scull race later this same day between Yuki and Ben (Boat 1) and Dan and Leo (Boat 2), who would win and by how much?
