BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes are competing in a series of Handball games. In each game, the team that wins depends on the average throwing power and tactical coordination of the players, based on their intrinsic skill modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that game.
Athletes compete in teams of 7.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, the team of Luka, Miko, Nils, Otto, Paul, Quig, and Rob lost to the team of Stan, Ted, Ugo, Vin, Walt, Xavi, and Yul.
In the second game, the team of Luka, Miko, Nils, Otto, Paul, Quig, and Rob lost to the team of Stan, Ted, Ugo, Zade, Arn, Bob, and Cal.
In the third game, the team of Luka, Miko, Nils, Otto, Paul, Quig, and Rob beat the team of Vin, Walt, Xavi, Zade, Arn, Bob, and Cal.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Luka ranks in terms of intrinsic skill?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Luka put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between the team of Luka, Miko, Nils, Otto, Paul, Quig, and Rob (Team 1) and the team of Stan, Ted, Ugo, Vin, Walt, Xavi, and Yul (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between the team of Vin, Walt, Xavi, Zade, Arn, Bob, and Cal (Team 1) and the team of Luka, Miko, Nils, Otto, Paul, Quig, and Rob (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
