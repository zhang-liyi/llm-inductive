BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes compete in a series of Rugby Sevens matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the combined strength and speed of the athletes, based on their intrinsic physical capability modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete in teams of 7.
All matches take place on the same day.
