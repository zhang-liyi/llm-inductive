BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of road cycling time trials. In each race, the team that wins depends on the average speed maintained, based on the athletes' intrinsic power output and endurance, modulated by how much effort they put into that race.
Athletes compete either individually or as a fixed team of three.
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, Tadej beat Jonas.
In the second race, Tadej, Primoz, and Matej beat Jonas, Wout, and Sepp.
In the third race, Tadej, Primoz, and Matej beat Jonas, Wout, and Sepp.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Tadej ranks in terms of intrinsic power output and endurance?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Jonas put into the third race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Tadej, Primoz, and Matej (Team 1) and Jonas, Wout, and Sepp (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Primoz and Matej (Team 1) and Wout and Sepp (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
