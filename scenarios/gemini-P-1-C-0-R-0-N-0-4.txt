BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Pickleball matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on their ability to control the kitchen line, based on their intrinsic hand-eye coordination and reaction time modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that match.
Athletes compete either individually or as a team of two.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Mark and Sophie beat Gary and Linda.
In the second match, Sophie and Dan beat Gary and Linda.
In the third match, Mark beat Gary.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Mark ranks in terms of intrinsic hand-eye coordination and reaction time?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Gary put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Mark and Sophie (Team 1) and Gary and a new player named Bill (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Gary and Linda (Team 1) and Dan and a new player named Kate (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
