BACKGROUND
In this event, disc golfers are competing in a series of rounds on the same course. Performance in each round depends on how accurately and powerfully they can throw, and how well they control their approaches and putts, which is determined by their intrinsic disc golf skill, modulated by how much effort and focus they devote to that round.
Players compete both individually and in three-person teams using a format where the team’s result reflects the overall quality of all three players.
All rounds take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first round, Tessa beat Marco.
In the second round, Marco beat Lina.
In the third round, Tessa, Lina, and Quinn beat Marco, Noah, and Riley.
In the fourth round, Marco, Lina, and Riley beat Tessa, Noah, and Quinn.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random disc golfers, where do you think Tessa ranks in terms of intrinsic disc golf skill?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Lina put into the fourth round?
Query 3: In a new team round later this same day between Tessa, Lina, and Quinn (Team 1) and Marco, Noah, and Riley (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new team round later this same day between Tessa and Marco (Team 1) and Lina and Quinn (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
