BACKGROUND
In this event, the players are competing in a series of small-sided 7-a-side soccer games and short 1v1 skill contests. In each game, the outcome depends on the players’ intrinsic soccer ability (such as speed, control, and finishing) combined with how much effort they put in during that game. Most players stay with their usual club teams (the Lions, Wolves, and Eagles), but one standout player, Jordan, can be loaned to different teams from match to match. All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first contest, Jordan beat Riley in a 1v1 skills game.
In the second game, Jordan and the Lions beat the Wolves.
In the third game, Jordan and the Wolves beat the Eagles.
In the fourth game, Jordan and the Eagles lost to the Lions.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random soccer players, where do you think Jordan ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Riley put into the first contest?
Query 3: In a new 7-a-side game later this same day between Jordan and the Lions (Team 1) and the Eagles (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new 7-a-side game later this same day between Jordan and the Wolves (Team 1) and the Lions (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
