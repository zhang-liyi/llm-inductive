BACKGROUND
In this event, the players are competing in a series of volleyball contests in an indoor gym. In each contest, the outcome depends on each player's intrinsic volleyball skill, combined with how much effort they put into that contest.
Players compete either individually in serving duels or in teams of four.
Each player belongs to exactly one fixed 4‑player team for all team matches, and they always play with the same teammates.
All contests take place on the same day.
Team Waves: Lena, Marco, Yasmin, Ethan.
Team Blaze: Nora, Tom, Priyanka, Leo.
Team Storm: Hannah, Victor, Sami, Carlos.

CONDITIONS
In a morning serving duel, Lena beat Tom.
In the first team match, Waves beat Blaze.
In the second team match, Waves lost to Storm.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random amateur volleyball players, where do you think Lena ranks in terms of intrinsic skill?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Carlos put into the second team match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Waves and Storm, who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Blaze and Storm, who would win and by how much?
