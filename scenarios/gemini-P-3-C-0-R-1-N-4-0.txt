BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of volleyball matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the average jumping and hitting ability of the athletes, based on their intrinsic strength modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete in fixed teams of 6.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Sofia's team (consisting of Sofia and 5 fixed teammates) lost to Gina's team.
In the second match, Sofia's team lost to Mia's team.
In the third match, Sofia's team beat Sam's team.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Sofia ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Sofia's team put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Sofia's team (Team 1) and Mia's team (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Gina's team (Team 1) and Sam's team (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
