BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes are competing in an Ultimate Frisbee tournament played with 9 players per side. In each game, the team that wins depends on the speed and tactical efficiency of the squad, based on their intrinsic athleticism modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that game.
Teams are fixed and do not change rosters.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, The Skywalkers (with Zoe) beat The Windrunners.
In the second game, The Skywalkers (with Zoe) beat The Disc Jockeys.
In the third game, The Skywalkers (with Zoe) beat The Zone Breakers.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random Ultimate Frisbee players, where do you think Zoe ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think The Zone Breakers put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between The Skywalkers (with Zoe) and The Windrunners, who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between The Windrunners and The Disc Jockeys, who would win and by how much?
