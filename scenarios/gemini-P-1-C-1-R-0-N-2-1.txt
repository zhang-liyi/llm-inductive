BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of half-court Basketball games. In each game, the team that wins depends on the total points scored, based on the athletes' intrinsic shooting and defensive skills modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that game.
Athletes compete either individually (1 on 1) or as a team of 4.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Reese lost to Morgan.
In the second game, Morgan, Taylor, Drew, and Casey beat Reese, Avery, Blake, and Zion.
In the third game, Taylor, Drew, Avery, and Blake beat Reese, Morgan, Casey, and Zion.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Reese ranks in terms of intrinsic shooting and defensive skills?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Morgan put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Reese, Avery, Blake, and Zion (Team 1) and Morgan, Taylor, Drew, and Casey (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Reese, Morgan, Taylor, and Drew (Team 1) and Casey, Avery, Blake, and Zion (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
