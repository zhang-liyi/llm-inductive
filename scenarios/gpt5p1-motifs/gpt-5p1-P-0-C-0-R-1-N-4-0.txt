BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of half-court basketball games. Outcomes depend on each player’s intrinsic basketball ability (shooting, ball-handling, awareness) together with how much effort they put in on this particular day.
Athletes compete either individually or as part of fixed 6-player teams that stay the same across all games.
All games take place on the same day.

Team Rockets beat Team Storm (Owen, Paul, Quinn, Ryan, Sam, TJ) in the next game.
Later that day, Team Rockets beat Team Thunder again.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random basketball players, where do you think Jordan ranks in terms of intrinsic basketball ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Caleb put into the 1v1 game against Jordan?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Team Rockets and Team Thunder, who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new 1v1 game later this same day between Ben and Finn, who would win and by how much?
