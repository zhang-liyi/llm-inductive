BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes compete in Ultimate Frisbee matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the collective scoring ability of the players, based on their intrinsic agility and handling skills modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that specific match.
Athletes compete in teams of 7.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, the team of Liam, Ava, and 5 others beat the team of Noah, Emma, and 5 others.
In the second match, the team of Mason, Harper, and 5 others beat the team of Noah, Emma, and 5 different players.
In the third match, the team of Liam, Mason, and 5 others beat the team of Noah, Emma, and a new set of 5 players.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Noah ranks in terms of intrinsic agility and handling?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Mason put into the second match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between a team led by Liam and Mason (Team 1) and a team led by Noah and Emma (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between a team led by Harper and Ava (Team 1) and a team led by Noah and a random player (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
