BACKGROUND  
In this event, crews are competing in a series of sailing races in small keelboats. Each boat’s performance depends on the crew’s intrinsic tactical sense, boat-handling skill, and coordination, as well as how much focused effort they put into trimming sails, steering, and maneuvers in each race.  
Some boats have two sailors, and some have three.  
All races are held on the same day.

CONDITIONS  
In the first race, Ava and Finn beat Chloe and Ethan.  
In the second race, Chloe, Ethan, and Lucas beat Ava, Finn, and Maya.  
In the third race, Maya and Finn beat Lucas and Noah.  
In the fourth race, Noah and Chloe beat Ava and Lucas.

QUERIES  
Query 1: Out of 100 random sailors, where do you think Ava ranks in terms of intrinsic sailing skill?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Lucas put into the second race?  
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Ava, Finn, and Maya (Boat 1) and Chloe, Ethan, and Noah (Boat 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Lucas and Noah (Boat 1) and Ava and Chloe (Boat 2), who would win and by how much?
