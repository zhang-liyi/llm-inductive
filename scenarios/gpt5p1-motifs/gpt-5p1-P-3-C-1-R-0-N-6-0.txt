BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of small-sided soccer matches. In each match, performance depends on the players’ intrinsic soccer ability together with how much effort they put into that specific match. Athletes sometimes play individually and sometimes as part of 8‑player teams.

QUERIES
