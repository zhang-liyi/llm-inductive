BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are playing a series of 4-a-side small-field soccer matches. Each match outcome depends on passing, ball control, and finishing, which are determined by the players’ intrinsic soccer ability and how much effort they put into that match. All matches take place on the same day. Teams are fixed and always use the same four players.

CONDITIONS
Team Redwood consists of Sofia, Elena, Marco, and Yusuf.
Team Harbor consists of Chloe, Daniel, Amir, and Lucas.
Team Summit consists of Isla, Henry, Tomas, and Kevin.

In the first match, Team Harbor beat Team Redwood.
In the second match, Team Summit beat Team Redwood.
In the third match, Team Redwood beat Team Harbor.
In the fourth match, Team Summit beat Team Harbor.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random soccer players, where do you think Sofia ranks in terms of intrinsic soccer ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Chloe put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Sofia, Elena, Marco, and Yusuf (Team 1) and Isla, Henry, Tomas, and Kevin (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Chloe, Daniel, Amir, and Lucas (Team 1) and a new team of four players, Lara, Bruno, Ahmed, and Stefan (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
