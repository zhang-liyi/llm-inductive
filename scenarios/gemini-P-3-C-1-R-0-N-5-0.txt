BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Ultimate Frisbee matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the collective scoring ability of the athletes, based on their intrinsic speed and handling skills modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that match.
Athletes compete in teams of 7.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Ben, Dan, Gil, Hal, Jon, Ken, and Leo beat Max, Ned, Pat, Ray, Sam, Tom, and Caleb.
In the second match, Ben, Dan, Gil, Hal, Jon, Max, and Leo beat Ken, Ned, Pat, Ray, Sam, Tom, and Caleb.
In the third match, Caleb, Ben, Dan, Gil, Hal, Jon, and Ned beat Ken, Max, Pat, Ray, Sam, Tom, and Leo.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Caleb ranks in terms of intrinsic speed and handling skills?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Leo put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Caleb, Ned, Pat, Ray, Sam, Tom, and Wes (Team 1) and Ben, Dan, Gil, Hal, Jon, Ken, and Leo (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Max, Ned, Pat, Ray, Sam, Tom, and Caleb (Team 1) and Ben, Dan, Gil, Hal, Jon, Ken, and Leo (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
