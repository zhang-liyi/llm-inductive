BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of 7-a-side Soccer matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the goal differential, based on the athletes' intrinsic dribbling and passing skills modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that match.
Athletes compete in teams of 7.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Kit, Lou, Mat, Nat, Oz, Pat, and Milo beat Ron, Sal, Ted, Val, Will, Xav, and Yan.
In the second match, Ron, Sal, Ted, Val, Will, Xav, and Yan beat Kit, Lou, Mat, Nat, Oz, Pat, and Milo.
In the third match, Ron, Sal, Ted, Val, Will, Xav, and Kit beat Lou, Mat, Nat, Oz, Pat, Yan, and Milo.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Milo ranks in terms of intrinsic dribbling and passing skills?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Kit put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Milo, Lou, Mat, Nat, Oz, Pat, and Yan (Team 1) and Ron, Sal, Ted, Val, Will, Xav, and Kit (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Ron, Sal, Ted, Val, Will, Xav, and Yan (Team 1) and Kit, Lou, Mat, Nat, Oz, Pat, and Milo (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
