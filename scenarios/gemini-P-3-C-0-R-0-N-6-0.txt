BACKGROUND
In this tournament, athletes compete in 8-on-8 Ultimate Frisbee matches. The winning team is determined by the collective agility and throwing accuracy of the players, based on their intrinsic athleticism modulated by the effort they exert during the specific match.
One specific group of players decided to stick together as a fixed squad throughout the day, while other players rotated to form opposing teams.
All matches occur on the same day.
