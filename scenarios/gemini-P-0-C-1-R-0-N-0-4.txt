BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Table Tennis matches. In each match, the winner depends on the spin control and hand-eye coordination of the athletes, based on their intrinsic reflexes modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete either individually or as a team.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Hana beat Yuto.
In the second match, Hana and Ken beat Yuto and Ren.
In the third match, Hana and Ren beat Yuto and Ken.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Hana ranks in terms of intrinsic reflexes?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Yuto put into the first match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Hana and Ken (Team 1) and Yuto and Ren (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Yuto and Ken (Team 1) and Ren and a new player named Suki (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
