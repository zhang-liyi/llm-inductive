BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes compete in **Swimming** heats. The winner is determined by the average hydrodynamic efficiency and propulsion the swimmers generate, based on their intrinsic technique and fitness modulated by the effort they put into the pool for that race. Athletes compete either individually or in fixed teams of four. All races take place on the same day.
