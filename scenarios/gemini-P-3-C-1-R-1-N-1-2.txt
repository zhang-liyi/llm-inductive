BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of small-sided Soccer matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the goal differential, determined by the athletes' intrinsic ball control and tactical awareness modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete as a team of three.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Sam, Leo, and Ney lost to Kylian, Luka, and Gareth.
In the second match, Sam, Kylian, and Luka lost to Leo, Ney, and Gareth.
In the third match, Sam, Gareth, and Paul beat Kylian, Luka, and Leo.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Sam ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Gareth put into the third race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Sam and Leo (Team 1) and Kylian and Ney (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Kylian and Luka (Team 1) and Leo and Gareth (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
