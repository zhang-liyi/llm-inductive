BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Volleyball matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the collective vertical reach and hitting power of the athletes, based on their intrinsic athleticism modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete as a team of 6.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Rowan, Sage, Kai, Elara, Finn, and Bea beat Leo, Ivy, Dax, Mia, Cole, and Una.
In the second match, Rowan, Sage, Leo, Ivy, Dax, and Mia beat Kai, Elara, Finn, Bea, Cole, and Una.
In the third match, Rowan, Sage, Cole, Una, Kai, and Elara lost to Finn, Bea, Leo, Ivy, Dax, and Mia.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Rowan ranks in terms of intrinsic athleticism?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Rowan put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Team 1 (Rowan, Sage, Kai, Elara, Finn, Bea) and Team 2 (Leo, Ivy, Dax, Mia, Cole, Una), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Team 1 (Leo, Ivy, Kai, Elara, Cole, Una) and Team 2 (Finn, Bea, Dax, Mia, Rowan, Sage), who would win and by how much?
