BACKGROUND  
In this event, the athletes are playing a series of small-sided soccer matches. In each match, the result depends on players’ intrinsic soccer skill—such as ball control, speed, and positioning—combined with how much effort they put into that match.  
Players compete either individually in skill contests or on teams.  
When teams play, each side fields 9 players. All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS  
In the first match, Mila, Jonas, and Farah lost to Lucas, Hana, and Tariq.  
In the second match, Mila, Hana, and Rosa lost to Jonas, Tariq, and Eli.  
In the third match, Mila beat Rosa in a one-on-one skills contest.  
In the fourth match, Hana, Eli, and Rosa beat Lucas, Jonas, and Farah.

QUERIES  
Query 1: Out of 100 random soccer players, where do you think Mila ranks in terms of intrinsic soccer ability?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Jonas put into the second match?  
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Mila, Lucas, and Eli (Team 1) and Hana, Jonas, and Rosa (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Mila, Farah, and Tariq (Team 1) and Lucas, Hana, and Eli (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
