BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of 7-a-side Soccer matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the average dribbling and shooting capability of the athletes, based on their intrinsic talent modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete in teams of 7.
All matches take place on the same day.

In the first match, Dakota, Lucas, Oliver, Ryan, Nathan, Caleb, and Josh beat Andrew, Connor, Dylan, Luke, Isaac, Owen, and Seth.
In the second match, Dakota, Andrew, Connor, Dylan, Luke, Isaac, and Owen beat Lucas, Oliver, Ryan, Nathan, Caleb, Josh, and Seth.
In the third match, Dakota, Lucas, Andrew, Oliver, Connor, Ryan, and Dylan beat Nathan, Caleb, Josh, Luke, Isaac, Owen, and Seth.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Dakota ranks in terms of intrinsic talent?
