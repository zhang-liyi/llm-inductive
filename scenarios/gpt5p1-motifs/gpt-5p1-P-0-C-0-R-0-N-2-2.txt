BACKGROUND
In this event, the players are competing in a series of small-sided 2-on-2 soccer games on a short field, with one optional 1-on-1 game. The results depend on the players’ intrinsic soccer ability—such as ball control, passing, finishing, and defending—combined with how much effort they put into each game.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Diego and Mateo beat Luca and Omar.
In the second game, Diego and Mateo beat Noah and Ethan.
In the third game, Luca and Noah beat Omar and Ethan.
In the fourth game, Diego beat Luca.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random players, where do you think Diego ranks in terms of intrinsic soccer ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Luca put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Diego and Mateo (Team 1) and Luca and Noah (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Omar and Ethan (Team 1) and Luca and Noah (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
