BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Badminton games. In each game, the winner is determined by the speed and reaction time of the athletes, based on their intrinsic skill level modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that game.
Athletes compete either individually or as a team.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Mei beat Lin.
In the second game, Mei and Chen beat Lin and Tao.
In the third game, Mei and Tao beat Lin and Chen.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Mei ranks in terms of intrinsic skill level?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Lin put into the first game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Mei and Chen (Team 1) and Lin and Tao (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Lin and Chen (Team 1) and Tao and a new player named Wei (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
