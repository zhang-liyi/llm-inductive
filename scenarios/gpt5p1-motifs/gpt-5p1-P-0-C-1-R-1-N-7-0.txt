BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of small‑sided soccer matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on how well the players perform, based on their intrinsic soccer ability (such as touch, speed, and awareness) modulated by how much effort they put in to that match.
Matches are played either 1 on 1 or between two teams of 9 players.
All matches take place on the same day.
Most players stay on the same team throughout the day, but Lena sometimes guest‑plays for different teams.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Team Aurora (Lena, Diego, Mateo, Sofia, Raul, Carmen, Hugo, Pilar, and Luis) beat Team Blaze (Noah, Emma, Lucas, Ava, Marco, Julia, Leo, Isabel, and Victor).
In the second match, Team Cosmos (Ethan, Mia, Oliver, Chloe, Daniel, Sara, Adrian, Nora, and Lena) beat Team Blaze (Noah, Emma, Lucas, Ava, Marco, Julia, Leo, Isabel, and Victor).
In the third match, Lena beat Noah.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random soccer players, where do you think Lena ranks in terms of intrinsic soccer ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Noah put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new 9‑a‑side match later this same day between Lena, Diego, Mateo, Sofia, Raul, Carmen, Hugo, Pilar, and Nora (Team 1) and Noah, Emma, Lucas, Ava, Marco, Julia, Leo, Isabel, and Victor (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new 9‑a‑side match later this same day between Ethan, Mia, Oliver, Chloe, Daniel, Sara, Adrian, Nora, and Javier (Team 1, without Lena) and Lena, Diego, Mateo, Sofia, Raul, Carmen, Hugo, Pilar, and Luis (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
