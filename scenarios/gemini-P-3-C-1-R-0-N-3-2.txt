BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Ultimate Frisbee matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the average speed and throwing accuracy of the athletes, based on their intrinsic athleticism modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete as a team of 5.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Lucas, Leo, Ryan, Jaxon, and Adam beat Ian, Cole, Seth, Owen, and Caleb.
In the second match, Lucas, Leo, Ryan, Ian, and Cole beat Jaxon, Adam, Seth, Owen, and Caleb.
In the third match, Caleb, Lucas, Leo, Ryan, and Jaxon beat Adam, Ian, Cole, Seth, and Owen.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Caleb ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Caleb put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Caleb, Lucas, Leo, Ryan, and Ian (Team 1) and Jaxon, Adam, Cole, Seth, and Owen (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Caleb, Jaxon, Adam, Seth, and Owen (Team 1) and Lucas, Leo, Ryan, Ian, and Cole (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
