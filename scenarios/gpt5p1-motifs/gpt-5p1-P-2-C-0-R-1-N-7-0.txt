BACKGROUND
In this event, three 9‑a‑side soccer teams play a short round‑robin. Each team’s performance in a match depends on the players’ intrinsic soccer ability and how much effort they put in during that match.  
Players are assigned to fixed squads of 9 that do not change throughout the day.  
Lena plays for Team Aurora. Team Blaze and Team Comet are the other two teams.  
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Team Aurora beat Team Blaze.  
In the second match, Team Aurora beat Team Comet.  
In the third match, Team Comet beat Team Aurora.  
In the fourth match, Team Blaze beat Team Comet.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random soccer players, where do you think Lena ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Marco from Team Blaze put into the first match?  
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Team Aurora and Team Blaze, who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Team Aurora and Team Comet, who would win and by how much?
