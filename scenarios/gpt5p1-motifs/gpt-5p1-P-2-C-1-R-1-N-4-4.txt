BACKGROUND

In each scrimmage, the winning team is determined by the average level of field-hockey ability of the players (such as stick control, acceleration, and shooting accuracy), combined with how much effort they put into that scrimmage.
Players compete either individually in 1-on-1 skill contests or in 6-a-side teams.
All scrimmages take place on the same day.
