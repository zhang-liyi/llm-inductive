BACKGROUND
In this regatta, athletes are competing in Rowing races using coxless fours. The boat that wins depends on the synchronized power output of the rowers, based on their intrinsic endurance and strength modulated by the effort they commit to that specific race.
Athletes compete in teams of 4.
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, the team of Evan, Frank, George, and Harry beat the team of Arthur, Bill, Charlie, and Dave.
In the second race, the team of Evan, Frank, Ian, and Jack beat the team of Arthur, Bill, Charlie, and Dave.
In the third race, the team of George, Harry, Ian, and Jack beat the team of Arthur, Bill, Charlie, and Dave.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Arthur ranks in terms of intrinsic endurance and strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Ian put into the third race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Arthur, Bill, Charlie, and Dave (Team 1) and Evan, George, Ian, and Kyle (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Evan, Frank, George, and Harry (Team 1) and Ian, Jack, Kyle, and Liam (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
