BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of 7-a-side Soccer matches. Each team consists of 7 athletes. In each match, the team that wins depends on the average ball control and stamina of the athletes, based on their intrinsic talent modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete in fixed teams.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, the Blue Squad beat the Red Squad (containing Felix).
In the second match, the Green Squad beat the Red Squad (containing Felix).
In the third match, the Blue Squad lost to the Green Squad.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Felix ranks in terms of intrinsic talent?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think the Green Squad put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between the Red Squad (Team 1) and the Green Squad (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between the Blue Squad (Team 1) and the Green Squad (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
