BACKGROUND  
In this event, the athletes are competing in table tennis, sometimes in singles and sometimes as part of 4-player team matchups. In each match, the outcome depends on the players’ intrinsic table tennis ability (spin control, reflexes, placement) and the amount of effort they put into that match.  
All matches take place on the same day.

QUERIES

Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Priya put into the third match?  
Query 3: In a new 4-on-4 team match later this same day between Hana, Mei, Noah, and Ravi (Team 1) and Priya, Isha, Kara, and Lina (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
