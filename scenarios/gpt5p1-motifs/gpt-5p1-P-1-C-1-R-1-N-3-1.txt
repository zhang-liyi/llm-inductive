BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of 5-a-side soccer matches. In each match, the winning team is determined by the players’ intrinsic soccer ability (such as ball control, passing vision, and finishing) together with the effort they put into that match.
Teams are generally fixed groups of five players, except for one player, Nora, who joins different lineups.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Adam, Bruno, Carlos, Diego, and Nora lost to Fabio, Gabriel, Hugo, Ivan, and Jonas.
In the second match, Adam, Bruno, Carlos, Elias, and Nora lost to Fabio, Gabriel, Hugo, Ivan, and Jonas.
In the third match, Bruno, Carlos, Diego, Elias, and Nora lost to Fabio, Gabriel, Hugo, Ivan, and Jonas.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random soccer players, where do you think Nora ranks in terms of intrinsic soccer ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Nora put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Adam, Bruno, Carlos, Diego, and Elias (Team 1) and Fabio, Gabriel, Hugo, Ivan, and Jonas (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Bruno, Carlos, Diego, Elias, and Nora (Team 1) and Fabio, Gabriel, Hugo, Ivan, and Marco (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
