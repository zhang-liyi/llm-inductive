BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Table Tennis matches. In each match, the winner is determined by the spin and speed they can impart on the ball, based on their intrinsic hand-eye coordination modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that match.
Athletes compete either individually or as a doubles team.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Deng beat Wei.
In the second match, Deng and Hao beat Lu and Bo.
In the third match, Deng and Lu lost to Wei and Hao.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Deng ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Deng put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Deng and Hao (Team 1) and Lu and Wei (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Wei and Hao (Team 1) and Lu and Bo (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
