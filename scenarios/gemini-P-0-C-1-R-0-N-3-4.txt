BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Volleyball games. In each game, the team that wins depends on the points scored, based on the athletes' intrinsic spiking power and defensive reflexes modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that game.
Athletes compete in teams of 5.
All games take place on the same day.

In the first game, Gabi, Sheilla, Thaisa, Fabiana, and Dani beat Logan, Jordan, Foluke, Destinee, and Christa.

In the third game, Gabi, Christa, Zhu, Yuan, and Zhang beat Kim, Han, Yang, Lee, and Park.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Gabi ranks in terms of intrinsic spiking power and defensive reflexes?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Logan put into the second game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Gabi, Sheilla, Thaisa, Fabiana, and Dani (Team 1) and Logan, Jordan, Foluke, Destinee, and Christa (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Sheilla, Thaisa, Fabiana, Dani, and Christa (Team 1) and Logan, Jordan, Foluke, Destinee, and Zhu (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
