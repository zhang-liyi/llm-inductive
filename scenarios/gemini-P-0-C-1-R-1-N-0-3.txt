BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Pickleball matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the accuracy of their dinks and volleys, based on their intrinsic hand-eye coordination modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that match.
Athletes compete as a team of two.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Quinn and Avery beat Reese and Logan.
In the second match, Quinn and Reese beat Avery and Logan.
In the third match, Quinn and Logan beat Avery and Reese.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Quinn ranks in terms of intrinsic hand-eye coordination?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Avery put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Quinn and Avery (Team 1) and Reese and Logan (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Avery and Reese (Team 1) and Logan and a new player, Parker (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
