BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Pickleball matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the shot placement and net control the athletes are able to execute, based on their intrinsic hand-eye coordination modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that match.
Athletes compete as a team of two.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Chris and Jo beat Morgan and Pat.
In the second match, Pat and Lou beat Morgan and Chris.
In the third match, Chris and Jo beat Morgan and Lou.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Morgan ranks in terms of intrinsic hand-eye coordination?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Morgan put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Morgan and Pat (Team 1) and Chris and Lou (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Pat and Jo (Team 1) and Chris and Lou (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
