BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Table Tennis matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the points accumulated through spin and speed, based on the athletes' intrinsic technique, modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete either individually or as a team.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Wei beat Han.
In the second match, Wei and Jun beat Han and Park.
In the third match, Wei and Jun beat Park and Lee.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Wei ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Park put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Wei and Jun (Team 1) and Han and Chen (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Park and Lee (Team 1) and Jun and Ming (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
