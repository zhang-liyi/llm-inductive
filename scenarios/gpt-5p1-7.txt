BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of baseball games. In each game, the team that wins depends on the players’ intrinsic abilities to pitch, hit, field, and run, combined with how much focused effort they put into that game.
Teams consist of multiple players, but the starting pitchers Jake and Ryan have especially large influence on the outcomes when they are used.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, with Jake pitching for the Falcons and Ryan pitching for the Wolves, the Falcons beat the Wolves.
In the second game, Ryan did not pitch; Jake pitched again for the Falcons, and the Falcons lost to the Bears.
In the third game, Ryan pitched for the Wolves against the Bears, and the Wolves beat the Bears.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random baseball players in similar roles, where do you think Jake ranks in terms of intrinsic pitching ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Ryan put into the first game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day where Jake starts for the Falcons against Ryan starting for the Bears, who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day where Ryan starts for the Wolves against a Falcons team that does not use Jake, who would win and by how much?
