BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in rowing races. Performance in each race depends on the rowers’ intrinsic power and endurance, together with how much effort they put in during that race.
Athletes compete either individually (on single sculls) or in boats of 6 rowers.
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, Sienna, Rowan, Caleb, Tessa, Grant, and Mei beat Oliver, Jade, Henry, Lila, Marco, and Evan.
In the second race, Sienna, Oliver, Jade, Henry, Grant, and Evan beat Rowan, Caleb, Tessa, Lila, Marco, and Mei.
In the third race, Sienna beat Marco in single sculls.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random rowers, where do you think Sienna ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Marco put into the single-scull race in the third race?
