BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of 3x3 Basketball games. In each game, the team that wins depends on the total points scored, which is a result of the athletes' intrinsic shooting and defensive skills modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that game.
Athletes compete either individually or as a team of three.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Jamie, Marcus, and Tyrell lost to David, Chris, and Alan.
In the second game, Jamie, David, and Chris lost to Marcus, Tyrell, and Alan.
In the third game, Jamie beat Alan.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Jamie ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Alan put into the third race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Jamie and Marcus (Team 1) and David and Tyrell (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between David and Chris (Team 1) and Marcus and Alan (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
