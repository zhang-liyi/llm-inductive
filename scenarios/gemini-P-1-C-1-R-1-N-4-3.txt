BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a Lacrosse (Sixes format) tournament. In each game, the team that wins depends on the agility and stick-handling proficiency of the six athletes, based on their intrinsic coordination modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that game.
Athletes compete in teams of six.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, the team of Ash, Bev, Cat, Deb, Eve, and Fay beat the team of Gin, Hop, Ivy, Joy, Kim, and Liz.
In the second game, the team of Gin, Hop, Ivy, Joy, Kim, and Liz beat the team of Ash, Bev, Cat, Deb, Eve, and Morgan.
In the third game, the team of Ash, Bev, Cat, Deb, Eve, and Fay beat the team of Gin, Hop, Ivy, Joy, Kim, and Morgan.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Ash ranks in terms of intrinsic coordination?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Morgan put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between the team of Ash, Bev, Cat, Deb, Eve, and Fay (Team 1) and the team of Gin, Hop, Ivy, Joy, Kim, and Liz (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between a team of Ash, Bev, Gin, Hop, Cat, and Deb (Team 1) and a team of Eve, Morgan, Ivy, Joy, Kim, and Liz (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
