BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of ice hockey scrimmages. In each game, the outcome depends on the players’ intrinsic skating speed, puck handling, shooting accuracy, and physical strength, along with how hard they choose to skate, check, and stay engaged during that game.
Athletes compete in teams of six.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Frank, Greg, Harry, Ian, Jack, and Kevin beat Lucas, Adam, Ben, Chris, David, and Eric.
In the second game, Lucas, Adam, Ben, Chris, David, and Eric lost to Harry, Leo, Mark, Nate, Owen, and Paul.
In the third game, Lucas, Adam, Ben, Chris, David, and Eric beat Frank, Greg, Jack, Kevin, Mark, and Quinn.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random ice hockey players, where do you think Lucas ranks in terms of intrinsic ice hockey ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Harry put into the second game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Lucas, Adam, Ben, Chris, David, and Eric (Team 1) and Frank, Greg, Harry, Ian, Jack, and Kevin (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Leo, Mark, Nate, Owen, Paul, and Quinn (Team 1) and Frank, Greg, Jack, Kevin, Ian, and Harry (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
