BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Ultimate Frisbee games played with 6 players per team. In each game, the team that wins depends on the field coverage and throwing precision of the athletes, based on their intrinsic stamina modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that game.
Athletes compete as part of a team.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Quinn and the Huckers lost to the Cutters.
In the second game, Quinn and the Cutters lost to the Huckers.
In the third game, Quinn and the Handlers beat the Stackers.
In the fourth game, Quinn and the Stackers lost to the Handlers.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Quinn ranks in terms of intrinsic stamina?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think the Handlers put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between the Huckers (Team 1) and the Stackers (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between the Cutters (Team 1) and the Handlers (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
