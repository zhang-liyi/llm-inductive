BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes compete in rugby training matches with 8 players per side (a full forward pack). The winning pack is determined by their ability to push back the opposition, based on intrinsic strength and technique, modulated by the effort they exert.
Players are shuffled into different packs for each match.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, the pack of (Huck, Ike, Jax, Kit, Lou, Mac, Nic, Obe) beat the pack of (Jett, Ace, Bud, Cy, Dax, Emo, Fly, Gil).
In the second match, the pack of (Huck, Ike, Jax, Kit, Lou, Mac, Nic, Obe) beat the pack of (Jett, Ace, Bud, Cy, Dax, Emo, Fly, Gil).
In the third match, the pack of (Jett, Huck, Ike, Jax, Kit, Lou, Mac, Nic) beat the pack of (Ace, Bud, Cy, Dax, Emo, Fly, Gil, Obe).

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Jett ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Ace put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between the pack of (Jett, Huck, Ike, Jax, Kit, Lou, Mac, Nic) (Team 1) and the pack of (Ace, Bud, Cy, Dax, Emo, Fly, Gil, Obe) (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between the pack of (Jett, Ace, Bud, Cy, Dax, Emo, Fly, Gil) (Team 1) and the pack of (Huck, Ike, Jax, Kit, Lou, Mac, Nic, Obe) (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
