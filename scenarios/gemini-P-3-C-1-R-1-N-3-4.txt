BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes are competing in a series of Lacrosse matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the stick skills and defensive positioning of the athletes, based on their intrinsic strength modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete in teams of 5.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, The Sharks beat Taylor, Ann, Bea, Cat, and Deb.
In the second match, The Jets beat Taylor, Eve, Flo, Gem, and Hop.
In the third match, Taylor, Ina, Jen, Kim, and Liz beat The Giants.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Taylor ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Ann put into the first match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Taylor, Ann, Bea, Cat, and Deb (Team 1) and Taylor, Ina, Jen, Kim, and Liz (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between The Sharks (Team 1) and The Jets (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
