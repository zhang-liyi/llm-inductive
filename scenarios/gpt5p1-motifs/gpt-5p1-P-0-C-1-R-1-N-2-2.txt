BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of swimming races, both individual and 4-person relays. In each race, the winner is determined by how fast the swimmer or relay can cover the distance, based on their intrinsic swimming ability and conditioning, modulated by how much effort they put in to that race.
Athletes compete either individually or as part of a 4-swimmer relay team.
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, Selene beat Chloe in an individual event.
In the second race, Selene, Chloe, Erin, and Grace beat Paige, Riley, Brooke, and Dana in a relay.
In the third race, Selene, Paige, Riley, and Brooke beat Chloe, Erin, Grace, and Hannah in a relay.
In the fourth race, Paige, Riley, Brooke, and Dana beat Chloe, Erin, Grace, and Hannah in a relay without Selene.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random swimmers, where do you think Selene ranks in terms of intrinsic swimming ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Chloe put into the second race?
Query 3: In a new relay later this same day between Selene, Paige, Riley, and Dana (Team 1) and Chloe, Erin, Grace, and Hannah (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new relay later this same day between Paige, Riley, Brooke, and Dana (Team 1) and Chloe, Erin, Grace, and Hannah (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
