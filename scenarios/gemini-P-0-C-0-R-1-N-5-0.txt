BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes compete in Ultimate Frisbee matches. Each team consists of 7 players. The team that wins depends on the collective speed, handling, and tactical awareness of the players (intrinsic ability), modulated by the intensity and effort they exert during the match.
The teams are fixed for the duration of the event.
All matches occur on the same weekend.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Caleb's team beat the Falcons.
In the second match, Caleb's team beat the Vipers.
In the third match, Caleb's team beat the Badgers.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random Ultimate Frisbee players, where do you think Caleb ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think the Falcons put into the first match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same weekend between Caleb's team and the Hornets (a new team), who would win and by how much?
