BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes are competing in a Rugby Sevens tournament (modified to 8 players per side for this specific league). In each match, the team that wins depends on the tactical execution and physicality of the players, based on their intrinsic skill modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Players have fixed teams of 8.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Marcus's Team lost to the Highlanders.
In the second match, Marcus's Team lost to the Islanders.
In the third match, Marcus's Team beat the Lowlanders.
In the fourth match, Marcus's Team lost to the Borderers.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random rugby players, where do you think Marcus ranks in terms of intrinsic skill?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Marcus's Team put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Marcus's Team (Team 1) and the Highlanders (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between the Islanders (Team 1) and the Lowlanders (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
