BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in Field Hockey matches. Each team consists of 8 players. In each match, the team that wins depends on the coordination and stick-handling speed of the athletes, based on their intrinsic talent modulated by the intensity of effort they put into that match.
The league has fixed teams (The Strikers, The Defenders, and The Midfielders), but a top-tier player, Riley, joins different teams for specific matches.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Riley and The Strikers beat The Defenders.
In the second match, Riley and The Defenders beat The Strikers.
In the third match, The Midfielders lost to Riley and The Defenders.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Riley ranks in terms of intrinsic talent?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think The Midfielders put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Riley and The Midfielders (Team 1) and The Strikers (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between The Strikers (Team 1) and The Defenders (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
