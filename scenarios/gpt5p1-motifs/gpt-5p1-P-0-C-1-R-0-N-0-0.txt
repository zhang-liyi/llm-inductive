BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of beach volleyball matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the players’ intrinsic volleyball ability, such as their coordination, jumping ability, and ball control, combined with how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete mostly in teams of two, with an occasional 1v1 practice game.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Lena and Tia beat Marco and Noah.
In the second match, Lena and Marco beat Tia and Ravi.
In the third match, Lena beat Zoe.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random beach volleyball players, where do you think Lena ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Marco put into the second match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Lena and Ravi (Team 1) and Tia and Zoe (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Marco and Noah (Team 1) and Ravi and Zoe (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
