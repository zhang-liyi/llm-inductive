BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of field hockey games. In each game, the team that wins depends on the dribbling speed and tactical awareness of the athletes, based on their intrinsic ability modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that game.
Athletes compete as a team of three.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Jo, Mel, and Pip beat Sasha, Kim, and Lee.
In the second game, Jo, Kit, and Sal beat Sasha, Kim, and Lee.
In the third game, Mel, Kit, and Ray beat Sasha, Kim, and Lee.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Sasha ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Jo put into the second game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Jo and Mel (Team 1) and Sasha and Kim (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Sasha and Lee (Team 1) and Pip and Sal (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
