BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of flag football games. In each game, the team that wins depends on the score achieved, based on the athletes' intrinsic athleticism and strategy modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that game.
Athletes compete in fixed teams of five.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Brett, Aaron, Jordy, Davante, and Clay beat Peyton, Marvin, Reggie, Edgerrin, and Dallas.
In the second game, Brett, Aaron, Jordy, Davante, and Clay beat Eli, Odell, Saquon, Victor, and Plaxico.
In the third game, Brett, Aaron, Jordy, Davante, and Clay lost to Peyton, Marvin, Reggie, Edgerrin, and Dallas.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Brett ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Peyton put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Brett, Aaron, Jordy, Davante, and Clay (Team 1) and Eli, Odell, Saquon, Victor, and Plaxico (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Peyton, Marvin, Reggie, Edgerrin, and Dallas (Team 1) and Eli, Odell, Saquon, Victor, and Plaxico (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
