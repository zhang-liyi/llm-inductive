BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Beach Volleyball matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the combined agility and power of the athletes, based on their intrinsic athleticism modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that specific match.
Athletes compete as a fixed pair.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Sarah and Jen beat Mia and Zoe.
In the second match, Sarah and Jen beat Mia and Zoe.
In the third match, Sarah and Jen lost to Mia and Zoe.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Sarah ranks in terms of intrinsic athleticism?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Sarah put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Sarah and Jen (Team 1) and Mia and a new partner, Ava (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Mia and Zoe (Team 1) and Jen and Ava (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
