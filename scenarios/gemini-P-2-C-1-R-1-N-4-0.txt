BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes are competing in a series of 6-on-6 Volleyball matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the collective vertical leap and hitting power of the athletes, based on their intrinsic athleticism modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that specific match.
Athletes compete as part of fixed squads (Team Red, Team Blue, Team Green), but one athlete, Elena, rotates between them as a guest spiker.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Team Red (with Elena) beat Team Blue.
In the second match, Team Green (with Elena) beat Team Red.
In the third match, Team Blue (with Elena) lost to Team Green.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Elena ranks in terms of intrinsic athleticism?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Elena put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Team Red (with Elena) and Team Green, who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Team Blue (without Elena) and Team Red (without Elena), who would win and by how much?
