BACKGROUND  
In this event, athletes are playing a series of small-sided handball matches. The outcome of each match depends on how powerfully and accurately they can throw, how quickly they can move, and how well they coordinate with teammates. These are driven by their intrinsic athletic ability and how much effort they put into each match.  
Teams are either 2-on-2 or 3-on-3.  
All matches take place on the same afternoon.

CONDITIONS

In the second match, Jordan and Mia beat Leo and Sam.  
In the third match, Leo and Nora beat Jordan and Sam.

QUERIES  
Query 1: Out of 100 random handball players, where do you think Jordan ranks in terms of intrinsic strength and skill?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Leo put into the first match?  
Query 3: In a new 3-on-3 match later this same afternoon between Jordan, Mia, and Nora (Team 1) and Leo, Sam, and Riley (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new 2-on-2 match later this same afternoon between Jordan and Sam (Team 1) and Leo and Mia (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
