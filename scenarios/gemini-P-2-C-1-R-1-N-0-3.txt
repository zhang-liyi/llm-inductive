BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Pickleball matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the strategic placement and consistency of their shots, based on their intrinsic court sense modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that match.
Athletes compete as a team of two.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Zane and Kyle beat Jim and Tim.
In the second match, Zane and Jim beat Kyle and Rob.
In the third match, Zane and Rob lost to Jim and Tim.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Zane ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Zane put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Zane and Kyle (Team 1) and Jim and Rob (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Jim and Tim (Team 1) and Kyle and Rob (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
