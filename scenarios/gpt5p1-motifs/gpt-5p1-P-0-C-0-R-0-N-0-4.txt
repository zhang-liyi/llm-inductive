BACKGROUND  
In this event, the players are competing in a series of small-sided soccer games on a reduced field. Each game’s outcome is determined by the players’ intrinsic ball control, speed, and finishing ability, along with how much effort they put into that specific game.  
All games are played 2-on-2, and all games occur on the same day.

CONDITIONS  
In the first game, Luca and Noah beat Amir and Ben.  
In the second game, Luca and Noah beat Ben and Kai.  
In the third game, Luca and Noah beat Kai and Amir.

QUERIES  
Query 1: Out of 100 random soccer players, where do you think Luca ranks in terms of intrinsic skill?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Amir put into the third game?  
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Luca and Noah (Team 1) and Ben and Amir (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Kai and Ben (Team 1) and Noah and Amir (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
