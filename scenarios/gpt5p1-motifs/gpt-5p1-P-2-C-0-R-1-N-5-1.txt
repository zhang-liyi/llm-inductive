BACKGROUND
In this event, three rugby sevens teams are playing a series of short matches. Each match is 7-on-7. The result of a match depends on the players’ intrinsic speed, tackling power, evasiveness, and stamina, along with how hard they choose to push themselves in that match.
All matches take place on the same day.
There are three fixed teams of seven whose rosters do not change:
- Team Falcons: Darius, Toby, Jamal, Stefan, Luis, Brant, Ewan.
- Team Titans: Cole, Andre, Malik, Hugo, Rafael, Shane, Victor.
- Team Lynx: Owen, Bryce, Tariq, Milo, Jonas, Eli, Carter.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Lynx beat Falcons.
In the second match, Falcons beat Titans.
In the third match, Falcons beat Lynx.
In the fourth match, Titans beat Lynx.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random rugby sevens players, where do you think Darius ranks in terms of intrinsic playing ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Owen put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Falcons and Titans, who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Falcons and Lynx, who would win and by how much?
