BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in three-person rowing races (triple sculls). The crew that wins each race is determined by the rowers’ intrinsic power and endurance, together with how much effort they put into that particular race. All races happen on the same day.

CONDITIONS

In the second race, Sasha, Dana, and Brooke lost to Liam, Maya, and Jules.
In the third race, Sasha, Dana, and Brooke beat Colin, Jules, and Hana.
In the fourth race, Eric beat Liam.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random rowers, where do you think Sasha ranks in terms of intrinsic rowing strength and endurance?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Maya put into the second race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Sasha, Dana, and Brooke (Crew 1) and Colin, Eric, and Hana (Crew 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Liam, Maya, and Jules (Crew 1) and Dana, Brooke, and Hana (Crew 2), who would win and by how much?
