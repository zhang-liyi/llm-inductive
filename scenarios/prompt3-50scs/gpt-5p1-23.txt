BACKGROUND
In this event, the skaters are competing in a series of speed skating races on an indoor oval. In each race, the winning skater or team is determined by finishing time, which depends on each skater’s intrinsic speed and endurance, combined with how much effort they put into that specific race.
Skaters compete either individually or in teams.
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, Hannah beat Kai.
In the second race, Hannah and Liam lost to Kai and Mia.
In the third race, Hannah, Mia, and Noah beat Kai, Liam, and Owen.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random speed skaters, where do you think Hannah ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Kai put into the second race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Hannah and Kai (Team 1) and Liam and Mia (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Kai and Noah (Team 1) and Hannah and Owen (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
