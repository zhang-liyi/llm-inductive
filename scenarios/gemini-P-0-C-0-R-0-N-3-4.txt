BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Lacrosse games. In each game, the team that wins depends on the average stick skills and agility of the athletes, based on their intrinsic prowess modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that game.
Athletes compete as a team of 5.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Paul, Tom, Rob, Kyle, and Mike beat Lyle, Miles, Jeremy, Thompson, and Randy.
In the second game, Paul, Tom, Rob, Kyle, and Mike beat Lyle, Miles, Jeremy, Connor, and Brendan.
In the third game, Paul, Tom, Rob, Kyle, and Mike beat Connor, Brendan, Thompson, Randy, and Lyle.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Paul ranks in terms of intrinsic prowess?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Lyle put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Paul, Tom, Rob, Kyle, and Mike (Team 1) and Lyle, Miles, Jeremy, Thompson, and Randy (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Paul, Lyle, Connor, Kyle, and Randy (Team 1) and Tom, Miles, Rob, Jeremy, and Mike (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
