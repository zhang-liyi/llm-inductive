BACKGROUND
In this event, three 9‑player basketball teams compete in a series of half‑court games. Game outcomes depend on the players’ intrinsic basketball skill and their effort level in that game.  
Each team has a fixed roster of 9 players that stays the same all day.  
Malik plays for Team Storm. The other teams are Team Ridge and Team Harbor.  
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Team Storm beat Team Ridge.  
In the second game, Team Storm beat Team Harbor.  
In the third game, Team Harbor beat Team Storm.  
In the fourth game, Team Ridge beat Team Harbor.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random basketball players, where do you think Malik ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Jordan from Team Ridge put into the first game?  
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Team Storm and Team Harbor, who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Team Ridge and Team Storm, who would win and by how much?
