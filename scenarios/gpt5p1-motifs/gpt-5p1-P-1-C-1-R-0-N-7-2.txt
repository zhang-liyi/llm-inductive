BACKGROUND
In this academy tournament, the athletes are competing in small-sided soccer matches. Full matches are played 9‑a‑side, and occasionally players do 1‑on‑1 attacking drills. The side that wins depends on the players’ intrinsic technical skill, speed, and stamina, combined with how much effort they put into that match.
All matches and drills take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Marco, Ben and seven others lost to Ella, Sara and seven others.
In the second match, Marco, Ella and seven others lost to Ben, Mia and seven others.
In a 1‑on‑1 finishing drill, Marco lost to Chloe.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random soccer players, where do you think Ella ranks in terms of intrinsic playing ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Marco put into the second match?
Query 3: In a new 9‑a‑side match later this same day between Marco, Sara and seven others (Team 1) and Ben, Ella and seven others (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new 1‑on‑1 finishing drill later this same day between Marco and Ben, who would win and by how much?
