BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in small-sided ultimate frisbee games. In each game, the winning team is determined by their throwing accuracy, catching consistency, cutting speed, and defensive positioning, which depend on their intrinsic ultimate ability and how much effort they put into that game.
Players compete either individually or as teams of four.
All games take place on the same day.
