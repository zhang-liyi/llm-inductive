BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Table Tennis matches. In each match, the winner is determined by the speed and spin they can generate and handle, based on their intrinsic reflexes and hand-eye coordination modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete either individually or as a team of two.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Zara lost to Leo.
In the second match, Zara and Ben lost to Leo and Sam.
In the third match, Zara and Ben beat Sam and Tom.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Zara ranks in terms of intrinsic reflexes and hand-eye coordination?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Zara put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Zara and Ben (Team 1) and Leo and Chloe (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Leo and Sam (Team 1) and Ben and Tom (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
