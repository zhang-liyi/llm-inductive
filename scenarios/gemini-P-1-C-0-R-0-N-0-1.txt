<START_SCENARIO>
BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Beach Volleyball games. In each game, the team that wins depends on the effectiveness of their defense and attack, based on their intrinsic vertical jump and coordination modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that game.
Athletes compete as a team of two.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Ben and Nick beat Koji and Sam.
In the second game, Nick and Tom beat Koji and Sam.
In the third game, Ben and Tom beat Koji and Sam.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Ben ranks in terms of intrinsic vertical jump and coordination?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Koji put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Ben and Nick (Team 1) and Koji and a new player named Alex (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Koji and Sam (Team 1) and Tom and a new player named Eric (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
