BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes are competing in a series of Ultimate Frisbee games. In each game, the team that wins depends on the speed and catching ability of the players, based on their intrinsic athleticism modulated by the effort they put into cutting and defending.
Athletes compete in fixed teams of eight.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Mike, Al, Bo, Cy, Dom, Eli, Fin, and Gus beat Hal, Ike, Jay, Kai, Lee, Moe, Noa, and Oz.
In the second game, Mike, Al, Bo, Cy, Dom, Eli, Fin, and Gus beat Pax, Quin, Rob, Sol, Ted, Uli, Van, and Wes.
In the third game, Hal, Ike, Jay, Kai, Lee, Moe, Noa, and Oz lost to Mike, Al, Bo, Cy, Dom, Eli, Fin, and Gus.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Mike ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Hal put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between the team of Mike, Al, Bo, Cy, Dom, Eli, Fin, and Gus (Team 1) and the team of Pax, Quin, Rob, Sol, Ted, Uli, Van, and Wes (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between the team of Hal, Ike, Jay, Kai, Lee, Moe, Noa, and Oz (Team 1) and the team of Pax, Quin, Rob, Sol, Ted, Uli, Van, and Wes (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
