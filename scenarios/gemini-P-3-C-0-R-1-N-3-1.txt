BACKGROUND
Athletes are competing in a 5-a-side soccer tournament. The team that wins is decided by the total goals scored, which is a function of the players' intrinsic ball control and tactical awareness, modulated by the physical running intensity they contribute to that specific match.
Liam competes on a fixed team known as "United" with teammates Noah, Oliver, Elijah, and James.
All matches occur consecutively on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, City beat United.
In the second match, Rovers beat United.
In the third match, United beat Athletic.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Liam ranks in terms of intrinsic ball control and tactical awareness?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much running intensity do you think Liam put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between United (Team 1) and City (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between a team consisting of Liam, Noah, and three players from Rovers (Team 1) and a team consisting of Oliver, Elijah, and three players from Athletic (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
