BACKGROUND  
This is a small indoor volleyball event. In each match, performance depends on each player’s underlying volleyball ability (serving, passing, spiking, and reacting) and on how much effort they put in during that match.  
Players sometimes compete individually (1v1 serving/receiving contests) or in 6‑player teams. All matches happen on the same day.

- Aurora core: Priya, Zoe, Hannah, Erika, Kim
