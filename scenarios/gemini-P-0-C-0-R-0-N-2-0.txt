BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Rowing (Coxless Four) races. In each race, the team that wins depends on the combined power output and synchronization of the four rowers, based on their intrinsic rowing ability modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that race.
Athletes compete as a team of four.
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, Elias, Julian, Gabriel, and Mateo beat Adrian, Dominic, Xavier, and Miles.
In the second race, Elias, Julian, Gabriel, and Mateo beat Adrian, Dominic, Nolan, and Roman.
In the third race, Elias, Julian, Gabriel, and Mateo beat Xavier, Miles, Nolan, and Roman.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Elias ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Adrian put into the second race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Elias, Julian, Gabriel, and Mateo (Team 1) and Adrian, Xavier, Nolan, and Roman (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Dominic, Miles, Nolan, and Roman (Team 1) and Xavier, Adrian, Caleb, and Silas (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
