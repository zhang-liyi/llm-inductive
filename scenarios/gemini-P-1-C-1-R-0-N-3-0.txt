BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Basketball games. In each game, the team that wins depends on the collective shooting accuracy and defensive coordination the athletes are able to maintain, based on their intrinsic skill modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that game.
Athletes compete as a team of 5.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, James, Curry, Harden, Davis, and Durant beat Paul, Westbrook, George, Anthony, and Noah.
In the second game, Leonard, Butler, Thompson, Green, and Cousins beat Irving, Lillard, DeRozan, Wall, and Noah.
In the third game, Noah, Lowry, Walker, Beal, and Love lost to James, Curry, Leonard, Butler, and Thompson.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Noah ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Noah put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between James, Curry, Leonard, Butler, and Thompson (Team 1) and Paul, Westbrook, George, Anthony, and Irving (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Noah, DeRozan, Wall, Lowry, and Walker (Team 1) and Harden, Davis, Durant, Green, and Cousins (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
