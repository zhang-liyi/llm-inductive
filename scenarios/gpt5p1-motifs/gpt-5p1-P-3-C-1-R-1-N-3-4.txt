BACKGROUND  
In this event, the players are competing in team table tennis ties using squads of five, along with individual 1-on-1 singles matches. Results depend on each player’s intrinsic table tennis skills such as spin control, reaction speed, and consistency, as well as how much effort they exert in each tie or match.  
All ties and matches are held on the same day.
