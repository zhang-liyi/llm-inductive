All matches take place on the same day.

Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Luca put into the fourth match?  
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Aisha, Tom, and Ren (Team 1) and Luca, Sophia, and Jonah (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Mei, Kai, and Jonah (Team 1) and Sophia, Luca, and Tom (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
