BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in rowing races using 6-person boats, along with some individual ergometer time trials. In each race, the boat that wins depends on the rowers’ intrinsic rowing ability (strength, endurance, technical efficiency) averaged across the crew, together with how much effort they put in that race.
Athletes compete either individually or in 6-person boats.
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, Sofia, Alan, Becca, Carlos, Dani, and Ethan beat Iris, Jack, Lila, Marcus, Nate, and Olivia.
In the second race, Sofia, Alan, Becca, Carlos, Dani, and Ethan beat Priya, Quinn, Ryan, Sara, Theo, and Vincent.
In the third race, Iris, Jack, Priya, Quinn, Ryan, and Sara beat Lila, Marcus, Nate, Olivia, Theo, and Vincent.
Later that day, Sofia beat Iris.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random rowers, where do you think Sofia ranks in terms of intrinsic rowing ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Iris put into the third race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Sofia, Alan, Becca, Carlos, Dani, and Ethan (Boat 1) and Iris, Jack, Priya, Quinn, Ryan, and Sara (Boat 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Alan, Iris, Lila, Marcus, Nate, and Theo (Boat 1) and Jack, Priya, Olivia, Quinn, Ryan, and Vincent (Boat 2), who would win and by how much?
