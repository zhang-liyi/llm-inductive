BACKGROUND  
In this swimming meet, athletes compete in individual races and relay teams. The result of each race depends on their intrinsic swimming ability—stroke efficiency, power, and endurance—combined with how much effort they put into that race.  
Athletes swim both individual events and 4-person relays.  
All races take place in the same session.

CONDITIONS  
In the first race, a 200m freestyle, Alex beat Priya.

In the fourth race, a 4×100m medley relay, Alex, Dana, Finn, and Gia beat Priya, Blake, Cara, and Ethan.

Query 1: Out of 100 random competitive swimmers, where do you think Alex ranks in terms of intrinsic swimming ability?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Priya put into the third race?
