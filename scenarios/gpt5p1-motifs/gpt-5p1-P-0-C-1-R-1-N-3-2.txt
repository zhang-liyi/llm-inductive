BACKGROUND
In this event, the players are competing in a series of small-sided indoor volleyball games. In each game, the winning team is determined by the players’ intrinsic serving, passing, and attacking ability, along with how much effort and concentration they apply to that game. Players compete either individually in skill duels or in teams of five. All games take place on the same day.
