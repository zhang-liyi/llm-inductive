BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes are competing in a series of Ultimate Frisbee matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the collective athleticism and disc-handling skills of the players, based on their intrinsic agility modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that specific match.
Athletes compete in teams of 7.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, the team of Rory, Sam, Tom, Uma, Val, Will, and Xin lost to the team of Yara, Zack, Ben, Cole, Dan, Eli, and Fay.
In the second match, the team of Rory, Sam, Tom, Uma, Val, Will, and Xin lost to the team of Yara, Zack, Ben, Gil, Hal, Ian, and Jon.
In the third match, the team of Rory, Sam, Tom, Uma, Val, Will, and Xin beat the team of Cole, Dan, Eli, Gil, Hal, Ian, and Jon.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Rory ranks in terms of intrinsic agility?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Rory put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between the team of Rory, Sam, Tom, Uma, Val, Will, and Xin (Team 1) and the team of Yara, Zack, Ben, Cole, Dan, Eli, and Fay (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between the team of Gil, Hal, Ian, Jon, Cole, Dan, and Eli (Team 1) and the team of Rory, Sam, Tom, Uma, Val, Will, and Xin (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
