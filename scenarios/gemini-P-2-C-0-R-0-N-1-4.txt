BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of 3-on-3 ice hockey scrimmages. In each scrimmage, the team that wins depends on the average skating speed and puck handling of the athletes, based on their intrinsic talent modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that scrimmage.
Athletes compete as a team of three.
All scrimmages take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first scrimmage, Wayne, Mario, and Brett beat Sidney, Alex, and Connor.
In the second scrimmage, Wayne, Mario, and Brett beat Alex, Connor, and Mark.
In the third scrimmage, Wayne, Mario, and Brett lost to Sidney, Mark, and Gordie.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Wayne ranks in terms of intrinsic talent?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Brett put into the third scrimmage?
Query 3: In a new scrimmage later this same day between Wayne, Mario, and Brett (Team 1) and Sidney, Gordie, and Bobby (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new scrimmage later this same day between Sidney, Alex, and Mark (Team 1) and Connor, Gordie, and Bobby (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
