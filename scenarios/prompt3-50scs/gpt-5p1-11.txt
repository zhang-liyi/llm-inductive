BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are taking part in a series of small-sided American football games (two-way, no special teams). In each game, the winning side is determined by the players’ intrinsic athleticism, throwing and catching ability, and decision-making, along with how much effort they put into that game.
Athletes play in short, small-team formats.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Alex beat Devon.
In the second game, Alex and Morgan beat Devon and Chris.
In the third game, Devon and Chris beat Morgan and Jamie.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random American football players, where do you think Alex ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Chris put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Alex and Jamie (Team 1) and Devon and Morgan (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Alex and Chris (Team 1) and Devon and Taylor (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
