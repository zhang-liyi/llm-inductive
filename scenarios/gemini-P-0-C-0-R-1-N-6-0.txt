BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes compete in a series of Rowing races (Eights). The winning boat is determined by the average power output and synchronization of the crew, based on their intrinsic rowing ability modulated by the effort they exert during the race.
Athletes compete in fixed crews of eight.
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, the crew of Elias, Ben, Cam, Dan, Ed, Fred, Greg, and Hal beat the crew of Ian, Jon, Ken, Leo, Mat, Ned, Oly, and Pat.
In the second race, the crew of Elias, Ben, Cam, Dan, Ed, Fred, Greg, and Hal beat the crew of Ray, Sam, Tim, Ugo, Vic, Will, Xav, and Yan.
In the third race, the crew of Ian, Jon, Ken, Leo, Mat, Ned, Oly, and Pat lost to the crew of Elias, Ben, Cam, Dan, Ed, Fred, Greg, and Hal.
