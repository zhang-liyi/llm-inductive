BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes compete in **Volleyball** matches (4v4 format). The winning team is determined by the average vertical leap and ball control of the players, based on their intrinsic athleticism modulated by the effort and communication they utilize in that match. Athletes compete either in 1-on-1 drills or in fixed teams of four. All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Kageyama beat Hinata.
In the second match, Team Kageyama (Kageyama, Tsukishima, Daichi, Asahi) beat Team Hinata (Hinata, Tanaka, Nishinoya, Sugawara).
In the third match, Team Hinata (Hinata, Tanaka, Nishinoya, Sugawara) beat Team Lev (Lev, Yaku, Kuroo, Kenma).

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Hinata ranks in terms of intrinsic athleticism?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Tanaka put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Team Kageyama and Team Lev, who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Hinata and Tsukishima (Team 1) and Kageyama and Tanaka (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
