BACKGROUND  
In this event, the athletes are playing a series of Rugby Sevens matches, with 7 players per team on the field. The winning team in each match is determined by the players’ intrinsic rugby ability (speed, strength, tackling, and decision-making) together with how much effort they put into that match.  
Players compete either individually in sprint-tackle tests or as part of a 7-player team.  
All matches and tests take place on the same day.
