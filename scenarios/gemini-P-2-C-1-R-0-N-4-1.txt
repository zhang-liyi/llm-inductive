BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Ice Hockey scrimmages. In each scrimmage, the team that wins depends on the total goals scored, based on the athletes' intrinsic skating speed and puck handling skills modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that scrimmage.
Athletes compete in teams of 6 (5 skaters + 1 goalie).
All scrimmages take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first scrimmage, the team of Jamie, Ben, Dan, Fred, Greg, and Hal beat the team of Sam, Tom, Vic, Will, Zach, and Ian.
In the second scrimmage, the team of Jamie, Sam, Tom, Vic, Will, and Zach beat the team of Ben, Dan, Fred, Greg, Hal, and Ian.
In the third scrimmage, the team of Jamie, Ben, Sam, Dan, Tom, and Fred lost to the team of Vic, Will, Zach, Greg, Hal, and Ian.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Jamie ranks in terms of intrinsic skating speed and puck handling skills?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Jamie put into the third scrimmage?
Query 3: In a new scrimmage later this same day between the team of Jamie, Ben, Dan, Fred, Greg, and Hal (Team 1) and the team of Sam, Tom, Vic, Will, Zach, and Ian (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new scrimmage later this same day between the team of Jamie, Sam, Tom, Vic, Will, and Zach (Team 1) and the team of Ben, Dan, Fred, Greg, Hal, and Ian (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
