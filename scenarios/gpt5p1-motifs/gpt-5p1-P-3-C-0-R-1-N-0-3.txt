BACKGROUND
In this small-sided soccer event, the players compete in 2-on-2 matches on a reduced-size field. The outcome of each match depends on the players’ intrinsic soccer ability (ball control, passing, shooting, and defending) and how much effort they put into that match.
All matches take place on the same day.
Teams are fixed pairs of two players.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Nadia and Elena lost to Omar and Tariq.
In the second match, Nadia and Elena beat Julia and Sven.
In the third match, Nadia and Elena lost to Julia and Sven.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random soccer players, where do you think Nadia ranks in terms of intrinsic playing ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Julia put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Nadia and Elena (Team 1) and Julia and Sven (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Omar and Tariq (Team 1) and Julia and Sven (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
