BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of small-sided soccer matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the average ball control and tactical awareness of the athletes, based on their intrinsic talent modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that match.
Athletes compete as a team of three.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Rio, Xavi, and Iniesta beat Modric, Kroos, and Case.
In the second match, Rio, Modric, and Kroos beat Xavi, Iniesta, and Case.
In the third match, Rio, Case, and Kroos beat Xavi, Iniesta, and Modric.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Rio ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Xavi put into the third race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Rio and Xavi (Team 1) and Modric and Kroos (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Modric and Case (Team 1) and Xavi and Iniesta (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
