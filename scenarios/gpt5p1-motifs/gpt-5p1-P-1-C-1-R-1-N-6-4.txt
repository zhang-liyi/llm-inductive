BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of ice hockey games. In each game, the winning team is determined by the players’ intrinsic skating, puck‑handling, and shooting ability, modulated by how much effort they put into skating hard and staying active on every shift.
Players compete either individually (in shootouts) or in teams of eight skaters.
All games take place on the same day.
