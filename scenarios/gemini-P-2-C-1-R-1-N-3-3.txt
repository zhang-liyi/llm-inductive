BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Ice Hockey scrimmages. In each game, the winning team is determined by the skating speed and puck handling of the athletes, based on their intrinsic talent modulated by factors including how much effort they put into that game.
Teams consist of 5 skaters.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Skyler and the Bears beat the Lions.
In the second game, Skyler and the Lions beat the Bears.
In the third game, Skyler and the Bears lost to the Lions.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random hockey players, where do you think Skyler ranks in terms of intrinsic talent?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Skyler put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Skyler and the Lions (Team 1) and the Bears (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between the Bears (Team 1) and the Lions (Team 2) without Skyler, who would win and by how much?
