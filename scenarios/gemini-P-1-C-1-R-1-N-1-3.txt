BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of volleyball matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the vertical jump height and spiking power of the athletes, based on their intrinsic explosiveness modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that match.
Athletes compete either individually or as a team of three.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Emma, Mia, and Lily beat Ava, Zoe, and Dakota.
In the second match, Ava, Zoe, and Lily beat Emma, Mia, and Dakota.
In the third match, Dakota lost to Emma.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Dakota ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Ava put into the third race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Dakota and Mia (Team 1) and Lily and Zoe (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Lily and Ava (Team 1) and Mia and Emma (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
