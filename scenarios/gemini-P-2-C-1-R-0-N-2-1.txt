BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of rowing races (Coxless Four). In each race, the team that wins depends on the average speed with which the athletes are able to row, based on their intrinsic power and synchronization modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that race.
Athletes compete as a team of 4.
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, Elena, Zara, Maya, and Chloe beat Tara, Iris, Luna, and Ruby.
In the second race, Elena, Tara, Iris, and Luna beat Zara, Maya, Chloe, and Ruby.
In the third race, Tara, Iris, Luna, and Chloe beat Elena, Ruby, Zara, and Maya.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Elena ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Elena put into the third race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Elena, Zara, Maya, and Chloe (Team 1) and Tara, Iris, Luna, and Ruby (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Tara, Iris, Luna, and Chloe (Team 1) and Elena, Ruby, Zara, and Maya (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
