BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Rugby Sevens matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the points scored, based on the athletes' intrinsic strength and speed modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete as a team of 7.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, the team of Ace, Bax, Cole, Dean, Earl, Finn, and Guy beat the team of Hank, Ike, Jed, Kyle, Liam, Moe, and **Oli**.
In the second match, the team of Hank, Ike, Jed, Kyle, Liam, Moe, and Guy beat the team of Ace, Bax, Cole, Dean, Earl, Finn, and **Oli**.
In the third match, the team of Rex, Sid, Tad, Uri, Van, Walt, and Guy beat the team of Hank, Ike, Jed, Kyle, Liam, Moe, and **Oli**.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think **Oli** ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think **Jed** put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between the team of Ace, Bax, Cole, Dean, Earl, Finn, and Guy (Team 1) and the team of Hank, Ike, Jed, Kyle, Liam, Moe, and **Oli** (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between the team of Rex, Sid, Tad, Uri, Van, Walt, and **Oli** (Team 1) and the team of Hank, Ike, Jed, Kyle, Liam, Moe, and Guy (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
