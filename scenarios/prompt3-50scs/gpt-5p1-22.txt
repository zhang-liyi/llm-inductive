BACKGROUND
In this event, the riders are competing in a series of short mountain biking races on a technical loop. In each race, the winner is decided by how quickly riders complete the course, which depends on their intrinsic power, bike-handling skill, and the level of effort they put into that race.
Riders compete either individually or in small teams.
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, Theo beat Riley.
In the second race, Riley and Uma beat Theo and Victor.
In the third race, Victor beat Uma.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random mountain bikers, where do you think Theo ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Victor put into the third race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Theo and Uma (Team 1) and Riley and Victor (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Theo and Riley (Team 1) and Uma and Will (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
