BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of volleyball matches. Each team consists of 6 players. In each match, the team that wins depends on the collective spiking and blocking ability of the athletes, based on their intrinsic agility modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete as part of a team.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Jamie and Team Alpha lost to Team Beta.
In the second match, Jamie and Team Beta lost to Team Alpha.
In the third match, Jamie and Team Gamma lost to Team Delta.
In the fourth match, Jamie and Team Delta beat Team Gamma.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Jamie ranks in terms of intrinsic agility?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Team Delta put into the fourth match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Team Alpha (Team 1) and Team Gamma (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Team Beta (Team 1) and Team Delta (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
