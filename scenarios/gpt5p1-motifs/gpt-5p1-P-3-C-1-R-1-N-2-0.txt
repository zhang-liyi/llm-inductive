BACKGROUND  
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of half-court basketball games. Outcomes depend on players’ intrinsic shooting, ball-handling, defense, and stamina, modulated by how much effort they put into each game.  
Players compete either in 4-on-4 games or occasionally in 1-on-1 games.  
Most players stay in fixed 4-player squads throughout the day, but Leo is repeatedly moved between different lineups.  
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS  
In the first game, Noah, Ryan, Omar, and Ethan beat Leo, Aaron, Jamie, and Dylan.  
In the second game, Lucas, Ben, Victor, and Mateo beat Leo, Chris, Ivan, and Tyler.  
In a later 1-on-1 game, Leo beat Aaron.  
In the last game, Noah, Ryan, Omar, and Ethan beat Leo, Chris, Victor, and Jamie.

QUERIES  
Query 1: Out of 100 random basketball players, where do you think Leo ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Aaron put into the 1-on-1 game against Leo?  
Query 3: In a new 4-on-4 game later this same day between Leo, Aaron, Jamie, and Dylan (Team 1) and Noah, Ryan, Omar, and Ethan (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new 4-on-4 game later this same day between Leo, Chris, Ivan, and Tyler (Team 1) and Lucas, Ben, Victor, and Mateo (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
