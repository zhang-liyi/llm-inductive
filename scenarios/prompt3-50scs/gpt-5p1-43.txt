BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of golf rounds. In each round, the winner is the golfer or team that completes the course in fewer strokes, determined by intrinsic ball-striking skill, touch, and consistency, modulated by how much physical and mental effort they put into that round.
Athletes compete either individually or as teams of two in a combined-score format.
All rounds take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first round, Dana beat Riley.
In the second round, Dana and Cole beat Riley and Harper.
In the third round, Harper beat Cole.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random golfers, where do you think Dana ranks in terms of intrinsic golfing ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Harper put into the third round?
Query 3: In a new round later this same day between Dana and Harper (Team 1) and Riley and Cole (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new round later this same day between Riley (Team 1) and Dana and Harper (Team 2 using an alternating-shot format), who would win and by how much?
