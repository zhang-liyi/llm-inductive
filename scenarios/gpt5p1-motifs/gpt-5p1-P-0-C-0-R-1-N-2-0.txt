BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes are competing in a series of 4-on-4 half-court basketball games. In each game, the team that wins depends on the players’ intrinsic basketball skill (such as shooting accuracy, ball-handling, and defensive ability) combined with how much effort they put in during that game.
All games are played on the same day.
Each player belongs to a fixed team of four.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Jordan’s team (Jordan, Amir, Leo, and Dante) beat Chris’s team (Chris, Nolan, Victor, and Sam).
In the second game, Jordan’s team beat Tyler’s team (Tyler, Ben, Omar, and Felix).
In the third game, Chris’s team beat Tyler’s team.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random basketball players, where do you think Jordan ranks in terms of intrinsic basketball skill?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Chris put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Jordan’s team and Chris’s team, who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Chris’s team and Tyler’s team, who would win and by how much?
