BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of handball games. The winning team in each game is determined by players’ intrinsic throwing power, agility, and defensive ability, together with how much effort they put in during that game.
Athletes compete either individually in shooting drills or as part of a 7‑player handball team.
All games take place on the same day.
