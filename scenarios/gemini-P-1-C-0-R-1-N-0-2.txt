BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Tennis matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the average precision and speed of their shots, based on their intrinsic technique modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that match.
Athletes compete as a fixed pair.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Novak and Carlos beat Rory and Luca.
In the second match, Rory and Luca lost to Novak and Carlos.
In the third match, Novak and Carlos beat Rory and Luca.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Novak ranks in terms of intrinsic technique?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Rory put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Novak and Carlos (Team 1) and Rory and Luca (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Rory and Carlos (Team 1) and Novak and a new player named Andre (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
