BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of small-sided soccer matches between fixed six-player teams. For each team, the same six athletes always play together. The outcome of each match depends on the players’ intrinsic soccer ability and how much effort they put into that match. All matches take place on the same day.

Team Cobalt: Ethan, Marco, Luis, Daniel, Jerome, Victor.  
Team Amber: Sofia, Carla, Bianca, Laura, Irene, Marta.  
Team Steel: Oliver, Henry, James, Lucas, Ben, Adam.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Team Amber beat Team Cobalt.  
In the second match, Team Steel beat Team Cobalt.  
In the third match, Team Amber beat Team Cobalt again.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random small-sided soccer players, where do you think Ethan ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Sofia put into the first match?  
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Team Amber and Team Steel, who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Team Cobalt and Team Steel, who would win and by how much?
