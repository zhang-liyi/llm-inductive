BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes are playing a series of full‑court basketball scrimmages. Performance in each scrimmage depends on their intrinsic basketball skills (shooting, ball‑handling, decision‑making, athleticism) and how much effort they put in during that game.
Players compete either individually or in 9‑a‑side team scrimmages with rolling substitutions.
There are three club squads—the Panthers, the Thunder, and the Storm—with generally fixed 9‑player rosters. Only one player, Maya, moves between squads and fills in different lineups over the day.
All scrimmages take place on the same day.

QUERIES
