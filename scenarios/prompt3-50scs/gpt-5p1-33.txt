BACKGROUND
In this weightlifting meet, athletes compete in a series of sessions where results are based on the total weight successfully lifted. The winner of each contest is the athlete or team with the higher total, determined by their intrinsic maximal strength and technique, combined with the effort they put into each attempt.
Athletes compete both individually and in two-person team totals.
All sessions take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first session, Iris outlifted Jamal.
In the second session, Jamal and Keiko beat Iris and Leo on combined total.
In the third session, Iris and Mina beat Keiko and Leo on combined total.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random weightlifters, where do you think Jamal ranks in terms of intrinsic maximal strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Iris put into the third session?
Query 3: In a new session later this same day between Iris and Keiko (Team 1) and Jamal and Mina (Team 2), who would win and by how much in total weight?
Query 4: In a new session later this same day between Jamal and Leo (Team 1) and Keiko and Mina (Team 2), who would win and by how much in total weight?
