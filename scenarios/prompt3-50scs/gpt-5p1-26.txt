BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of cross-country skiing races. In each race, the winner is the skier or relay team with the fastest overall pace, which depends on their intrinsic endurance and technique, modulated by how much effort they choose to sustain during the race.
Athletes compete either individually or in teams of three in relay format.
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, Riley beat Jonas.
In the second race, Riley, Tessa, and Uma beat Jonas, Victor, and Wynn.
In the third race, Wynn beat Riley.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random cross-country skiers, where do you think Riley ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Riley put into the third race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Jonas, Victor, and Wynn (Team 1) and Riley, Tessa, and Uma (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Wynn and Jonas (Team 1) and Riley and Victor (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
