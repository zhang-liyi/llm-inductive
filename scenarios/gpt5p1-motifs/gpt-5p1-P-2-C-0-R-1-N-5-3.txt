BACKGROUND
In this event, three team handball squads are playing a set of matches. Standard 7-on-7 lineups are used. The result of a match depends on the players’ intrinsic throwing power, accuracy, agility, and endurance, along with how much effort and focus they invest in that match.
All matches take place on the same day.
There are three fixed teams of seven whose rosters do not change:
- Team North: Gregor, Emil, Lukas, Henrik, Arne, Jonas, Mads.
- Team East: Pavel, Andrej, Milan, Igor, Tomas, Luka, Boris.
- Team West: Sven, Johan, Nils, Erik, Oscar, Kai, Fredrik.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, North beat East.
In the second match, West beat North.
In the third match, North beat West.
In the fourth match, West beat East.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random team handball players, where do you think Gregor ranks in terms of intrinsic playing ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Pavel put into the first match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between North and East, who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between North and West, who would win and by how much?
