BACKGROUND
In this canoe slalom event, athletes complete timed runs through a series of gates on whitewater. The result of each run depends on how quickly and cleanly they navigate the course, which in turn depends on their intrinsic paddling skill and control, modulated by how much effort they put in on that run.
Athletes compete either individually or in pairs.
All runs take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first run, Lina beat Boris.
In the second run, Lina and Maya beat Boris and Daniel.
In the third run, Daniel beat Maya.
In the fourth run, Boris and Maya beat Lina and Daniel.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random slalom paddlers, where do you think Lina ranks in terms of intrinsic skill?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Boris put into the fourth run?
Query 3: In a new run later this same day between Lina and Daniel (Team 1) and Boris and Maya (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new run later this same day between Lina (Team 1) and Maya (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
