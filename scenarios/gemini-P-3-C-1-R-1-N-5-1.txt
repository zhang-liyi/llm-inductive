BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Handball games. In each game, the team that wins depends on the goal difference, based on the athletes' intrinsic throwing power and agility, modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that game.
Athletes compete in teams of 7.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, The Lions and Elias lost to The Tigers.
In the second game, The Bears and Elias lost to The Tigers.
In the third game, The Lions and Elias beat The Bears.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Elias ranks in terms of intrinsic throwing power and agility?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think The Tigers put into the second game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between The Bears and Elias (Team 1) and The Lions (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between The Tigers (Team 1) and The Lions and Elias (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
