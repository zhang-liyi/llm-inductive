BACKGROUND
In this kayaking sprint event, races are held over a flatwater course. The winner of each race is the athlete or team with the greater speed, which depends on their intrinsic power and technique along with the effort they choose to exert in that race.
Athletes compete in single or double kayaks.
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, Diego beat Hana.
In the second race, Hana and Mei beat Diego and Lucas.
In the third race, Lucas and Noor beat Hana and Diego.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random kayaking sprinters, where do you think Diego ranks in terms of intrinsic paddling strength and speed?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Lucas put into the third race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Hana and Noor (Team 1) and Diego and Mei (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Diego (Team 1) and Noor (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
