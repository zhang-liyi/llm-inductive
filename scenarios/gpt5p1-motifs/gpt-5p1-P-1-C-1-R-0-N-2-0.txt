BACKGROUND
In this event, the players are competing in a series of half-court basketball games. In each game, the team that wins depends on the players’ intrinsic shooting, ball-handling, defense, and stamina, together with how much focused effort they put in on that game.
Players compete either individually or as a team.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Mia beat Jonah.
In the second game, Mia, Leo, Priya, and Noah beat Jonah, Zara, Ethan, and Kai.
In the third game, Leo, Zara, Kai, and Mia beat Jonah, Priya, Ethan, and Noah.
In the fourth game, Ethan beat Jonah.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Jonah ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Zara put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Mia, Zara, Kai, and Priya (Team 1) and Jonah, Leo, Ethan, and Noah (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Ethan and Mia (Team 1) and Jonah and Kai (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
