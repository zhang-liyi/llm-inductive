BACKGROUND
In this event, three 9-a-side ultimate frisbee teams compete in a series of games. Each game’s result depends on the players’ intrinsic throwing accuracy, catching reliability, cutting speed, stamina, and how much effort they put into that particular game.
Teams have fixed rosters of 9 players; teammates do not change.
Team Horizon: Lila, Carmen, Zoe, Nina, Paige, Tessa, Brooke, Ivy, Kayla.
Team Orbit: Rowan, Eli, Max, Theo, Ian, Miles, Jamie, Seth, Victor.
Team Drift: Sasha, Mira, Priyanka, Anika, Rhea, Kavya, Leena, Disha, Rania.
All games are played on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Horizon beat Drift.
In the second game, Orbit beat Drift.
In the third game, Horizon beat Drift again.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random ultimate frisbee players, where do you think Sasha ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Mira put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Horizon and Orbit, who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Drift and Orbit, who would win and by how much?
