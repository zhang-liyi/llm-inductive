BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Badminton matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the combined agility and smash power of the athletes, based on their intrinsic reflexes modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that specific match.
Athletes compete as a team of two.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Liam and Noah beat Rohan and Ben.
In the second match, Ben and Noah beat Rohan and Liam.
In the third match, Rohan and Noah beat Ben and Liam.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Rohan ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Rohan put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Rohan and Ben (Team 1) and Liam and Oliver (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Liam and Noah (Team 1) and Ben and Carter (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
