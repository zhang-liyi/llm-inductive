BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of indoor volleyball games. In each game, the team that wins depends on how powerfully and accurately the players can serve, pass, set, and attack, based on their intrinsic volleyball skill and how much focused effort they put into that game.
Athletes compete in small teams.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Ava, Bella, and Cara beat Diego, Eva, and Finn.
In the second game, Ava, Diego, and Gia lost to Bella, Eva, and Hugo.
In the third game, Ava, Finn, and Hugo beat Bella, Cara, and Gia.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random volleyball players, where do you think Ava ranks in terms of intrinsic skill?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Diego put into the second game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Ava, Bella, and Gia (Team 1) and Diego, Eva, and Hugo (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Cara and Finn (Team 1) and Gia and Hugo (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
