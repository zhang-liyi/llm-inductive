BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Ultimate Frisbee matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the speed and throwing accuracy of the athletes, based on their intrinsic agility modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete as a team of 6.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Flynn, Drew, Nash, Tara, Zane, and Cleo beat Milo, Jade, Seth, Rose, Kirk, and Hope.
In the second match, Flynn, Drew, Milo, Jade, Seth, and Rose beat Nash, Tara, Zane, Cleo, Kirk, and Hope.
In the third match, Flynn, Drew, Kirk, Hope, Nash, and Tara lost to Zane, Cleo, Milo, Jade, Seth, and Rose.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Flynn ranks in terms of intrinsic agility?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Flynn put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Team 1 (Flynn, Drew, Nash, Tara, Zane, Cleo) and Team 2 (Milo, Jade, Seth, Rose, Kirk, Hope), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Team 1 (Milo, Jade, Nash, Tara, Kirk, Hope) and Team 2 (Zane, Cleo, Seth, Rose, Flynn, Drew), who would win and by how much?
