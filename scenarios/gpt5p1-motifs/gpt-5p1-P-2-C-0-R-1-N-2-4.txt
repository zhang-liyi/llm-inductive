BACKGROUND
In this table tennis event, players compete in individual singles matches and team ties between 4‑player club teams. The outcome of each match or tie depends on the players’ intrinsic table tennis skill, together with how much effort they put into that contest.
Players compete either individually or as part of a fixed 4‑player team.
Each player belongs to exactly one fixed 4‑player team, and team lineups remain the same for all team ties.
All matches take place on the same day.
Team SpinMasters: Haruto, Kenji, Daichi, Sora.
Team TopSpin: Lucas, Miguel, André, Bruno.
Team SideSpin: Chen, Li, Wei, Zhang.

CONDITIONS
In a singles match, Haruto beat Lucas.
In the first team tie, SpinMasters beat TopSpin.
In the second team tie, SpinMasters lost to SideSpin.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random club table tennis players, where do you think Haruto ranks in terms of intrinsic skill?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Wei put into the second team tie?
Query 3: In a new team tie later this same day between SpinMasters and SideSpin, who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new team tie later this same day between TopSpin and SideSpin, who would win and by how much?
