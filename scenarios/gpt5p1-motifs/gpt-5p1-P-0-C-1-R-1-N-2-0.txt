BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of 4-on-4 half-court basketball games. In each game, the team that wins depends on how effectively the players can shoot, defend, and move, based on their intrinsic basketball ability and fitness, modulated by how much effort they put in to that game.
Athletes compete either individually (1-on-1) or as a team of four.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Maya, Jordan, Leo, and Ben beat Dylan, Omar, Ian, and Felix.
In the second game, Maya, Omar, Hugo, and Marco beat Jordan, Leo, Ben, and Chris.
In the third game, Maya beat Jordan in a 1-on-1 game.
In the fourth game, Dylan, Omar, Ian, and Felix beat Hugo, Marco, Sean, and Victor.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random basketball players, where do you think Maya ranks in terms of intrinsic basketball ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Jordan put into the second game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Maya, Dylan, Ian, and Felix (Team 1) and Jordan, Leo, Ben, and Chris (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Hugo, Marco, Sean, and Victor (Team 1) and Omar, Dylan, Ian, and Felix (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
