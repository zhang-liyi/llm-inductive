BACKGROUND  
In this event, the players are competing in a series of 7-a-side Ultimate Frisbee games. In each game, the winning team is determined by the players’ intrinsic Ultimate skills (throwing accuracy, catching reliability, cutting speed, and field awareness) together with the effort they put into that game.  
Players compete either one-on-one in throwing challenges or as part of a 7-player team.  
All games and challenges take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS  
In the first game, Aisha, Tom, Riley, Kevin, Mia, Jordan, and Blake beat Sarah, Luis, Harper, Neil, Chloe, Ivan, and Jonah.  
In the second game, Aisha, Sarah, Neil, Chloe, Omar, Grace, and Ivan beat Tom, Luis, Harper, Kevin, Mia, Jordan, and Blake.  
In the third game, Aisha beat Luis in a one-on-one long-throw accuracy contest.

QUERIES  
Query 1: Out of 100 random Ultimate Frisbee players, where do you think Aisha ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Luis put into the third contest?  
Query 3: In a new 7-a-side game later this same day between Aisha, Tom, Harper, Grace, Kevin, Mia, and Jonah (Team 1) and Sarah, Luis, Riley, Neil, Chloe, Jordan, and Blake (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new 7-a-side game later this same day between Aisha, Luis, Harper, Ivan, Omar, Riley, and Chloe (Team 1) and Sarah, Tom, Neil, Grace, Kevin, Mia, and Blake (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
