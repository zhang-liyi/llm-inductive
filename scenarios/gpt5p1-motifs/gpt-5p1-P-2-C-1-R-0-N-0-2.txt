BACKGROUND  
In this event, the players are in a series of small-sided 2-on-2 soccer games on a short pitch. The team that wins each game depends on the players’ intrinsic ball control, speed, and decision-making, as well as the intensity of effort they put into running, pressing, and finishing during that game.  
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS  
In the first game, Diego and Amir beat Lucas and Ben.  
In the second game, Diego and Lucas beat Amir and Rafa.  
In the third game, Ben and Rafa beat Diego and Kai.  
In the fourth game, Diego and Ben beat Kai and Lucas.

QUERIES  
Query 1: Out of 100 random soccer players, where do you think Diego ranks in terms of intrinsic playing ability?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Kai put into the third game?  
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Diego and Rafa (Team 1) and Amir and Ben (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Diego and Kai (Team 1) and Lucas and Rafa (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
