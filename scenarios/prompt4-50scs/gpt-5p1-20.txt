BACKGROUND  
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of short road cycling races on the same circuit. In each race, the team that wins depends on the riders’ intrinsic aerobic power and endurance, along with how much effort they choose to put into that race.  
Cyclists compete either individually or in small teams.  
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS  
In the first race, Leo beat Jonah.  
In the second race, Leo and Mara beat Jonah and Priya.  
In the third race, Priya and Jonah beat Leo and Sam.

QUERIES  
Query 1: Out of 100 random road cyclists, where do you think Leo ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Sam put into the third race?  
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Leo and Mara (Team 1) and Jonah and Sam (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Priya and Leo (Team 1) and Jonah and Mara (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
