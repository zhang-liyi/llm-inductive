BACKGROUND
In this event, the players are competing in a series of 4-on-4 ice hockey scrimmages on a reduced-size rink. In each scrimmage, the winning side is determined by players’ intrinsic skating, puck-handling, and shooting abilities, combined with how much effort they put into that scrimmage.
Contests are either 1-on-1 shootout-style games or 4-on-4 scrimmages.
Most players remain with fixed 4-player units (Colin, Derek, Evan, and Frank on one side; Gabe, Harry, Ian, and Jack on the other), while Jonas frequently changes sides.
All contests take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first scrimmage, Colin, Derek, Evan, and Frank beat Gabe, Harry, Ian, and Jack.
In the second scrimmage, Jonas, Gabe, Harry, and Ian lost to Colin, Derek, Evan, and Frank.
In the third scrimmage, Jonas, Colin, Derek, and Evan lost to Gabe, Harry, Ian, and Jack.
In the fourth contest, Jonas lost to Gabe in a 1-on-1 shootout.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random ice hockey players, where do you think Colin ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Jonas put into the third scrimmage?
Query 3: In a new 4-on-4 scrimmage later this same day between Colin, Derek, Evan, and Frank (Team 1) and Jonas, Gabe, Harry, and Ian (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new 1-on-1 shootout later this same day between Jonas (Player 1) and Jack (Player 2), who would win and by how much?
