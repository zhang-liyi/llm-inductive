BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are racing in three-person rowing boats. The outcome of each race depends on the boat’s sustained speed, which is determined by each rower’s intrinsic rowing strength and technique and the effort they put into that race.
Rowers compete as part of fixed three-person crews, and occasionally in individual contests on land.
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, Ben, Oliver, and Tomas beat Diego, Lucas, and Rafael.
In the second race, Ivan, Marek, and Pavel beat Diego, Lucas, and Rafael.
In the third race, Diego, Lucas, and Rafael beat Ivan, Marek, and Pavel.
In the fourth race, Oliver beat Ivan.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random rowers, where do you think Diego ranks in terms of intrinsic rowing ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Diego put into the second race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Diego, Lucas, and Rafael (Crew 1) and Ben, Oliver, and Tomas (Crew 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Ben, Oliver, and Tomas (Crew 1) and Ivan, Marek, and Pavel (Crew 2), who would win and by how much?
