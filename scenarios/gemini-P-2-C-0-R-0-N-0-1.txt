BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Beach Volleyball games. In each game, the team that wins depends on the average defensive coverage and spiking power the athletes are able to generate, based on their intrinsic athleticism modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that game.
Athletes compete either individually or as a team of two.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Elara and Sofia beat Lucas and Mateo.
In the second game, Elara and Sofia beat Mateo and Gabriel.
In the third game, Elara and Sofia lost to Lucas and Gabriel.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Elara ranks in terms of intrinsic athleticism?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Sofia put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Elara and Sofia (Team 1) and Lucas and Noah (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Lucas and Mateo (Team 1) and Sofia and Oliver (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
