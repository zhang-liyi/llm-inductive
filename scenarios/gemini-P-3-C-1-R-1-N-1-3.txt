BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Ultimate Frisbee matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the score reached, based on the athletes' intrinsic speed and catching ability modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete as a team of three.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Morgan, Ben, and Jerry lost to Tom, Ron, and April.
In the second match, Morgan, Tom, and Ron lost to Ben, Jerry, and April.
In the third match, Morgan, April, and Andy beat Ben, Jerry, and Tom.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Morgan ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think April put into the third race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Morgan and Ben (Team 1) and Tom and Jerry (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Tom and Ron (Team 1) and Ben and April (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
