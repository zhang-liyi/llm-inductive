BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of half-court basketball games. In each game, the team that wins depends on the scoring efficiency and defensive capability of the athletes, based on their intrinsic skill modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that game.
Athletes compete as a team of four.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Sarah, Emily, Jessica, and Ashley lost to Megan, Samantha, Lauren, and Kayla.
In the second game, Sarah, Emily, Jessica, and Ashley lost to Megan, Samantha, Rachel, and Nicole.
In the third game, Sarah, Emily, Jessica, and Ashley beat Lauren, Kayla, Rachel, and Nicole.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Sarah ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Megan put into the second game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Sarah, Emily, Jessica, and Ashley (Team 1) and Megan, Samantha, Lauren, and Kayla (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Megan, Samantha, Rachel, and Nicole (Team 1) and Lauren, Kayla, Rachel, and Nicole (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
