BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are playing a sequence of half-rink ice hockey scrimmages. In each scrimmage, the result depends on the skaters’ intrinsic skating speed, puck control, and shooting ability, together with how much effort they put in during that scrimmage.
Athletes compete in small teams without line changes.
All scrimmages take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first scrimmage, Nina beat Oliver.
In the second scrimmage, Nina and Paolo beat Quinn and Oliver.
In the third scrimmage, Quinn beat Nina.
In the fourth scrimmage, Oliver and Quinn beat Nina and Paolo.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random ice hockey players, where do you think Nina ranks in terms of intrinsic skill?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Oliver put into the fourth scrimmage?
Query 3: In a new scrimmage later this same day between Nina and Quinn (Team 1) and Oliver and Riley (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new scrimmage later this same day between Nina, Paolo, and Riley (Team 1) and Oliver, Quinn, and Taylor (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
