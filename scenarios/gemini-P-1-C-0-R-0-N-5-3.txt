BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes compete in 7-a-side Soccer matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the number of goals scored, based on the athletes' intrinsic ball control and shooting accuracy modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete in teams of 7.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, the team of Marco, Sergio, and 5 others beat the team of Paolo, Luca, and 5 others.
In the second match, the team of Diego, Leo, and 5 others beat the team of Paolo, Luca, and 5 different players.
In the third match, the team of Marco, Diego, and 5 others beat the team of Paolo, Luca, and a new set of 5 players.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Paolo ranks in terms of intrinsic ball control and shooting accuracy?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Diego put into the second match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between a team led by Marco and Diego (Team 1) and a team led by Paolo and Luca (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between a team led by Sergio and Leo (Team 1) and a team led by Paolo and a random player (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
