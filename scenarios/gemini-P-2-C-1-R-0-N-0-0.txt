BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Beach Volleyball matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the effectiveness of their blocks and spikes, based on their intrinsic athleticism modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that match.
Athletes compete as a team of two.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Jada and Kai beat Liam and Noah.
In the second match, Jada and Liam beat Kai and Noah.
In the third match, Jada and Noah lost to Kai and Liam.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Jada ranks in terms of intrinsic athleticism?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Jada and Noah put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Jada and Kai (Team 1) and Liam and Noah (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Kai and Noah (Team 1) and Liam and Jada (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
