BACKGROUND  
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of figure skating programs on the same ice rink. The results of each program depend on the skaters’ intrinsic technical and artistic ability, together with how much effort and precision they bring to that performance.  
Skaters compete either as singles, as pairs, or in small nation-style teams where the combined performances are judged together.  
All programs take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS  
In the first program, Ava beat Rowan.  
In the second program, Ava and Luka beat Rowan and Mei.  
In the third program, Mei beat Ava.  
In the fourth program, Rowan, Mei, and Luka beat Ava, Theo, and Sara.

QUERIES  
Query 1: Out of 100 random figure skaters, where do you think Ava ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Rowan put into the fourth program?  
Query 3: In a new program later this same day between Ava and Mei (Team 1) and Rowan and Luka (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new team program later this same day between Ava, Luka, and Theo (Team 1) and Rowan, Mei, and Sara (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
