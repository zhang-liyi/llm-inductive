BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of ice hockey scrimmages. In each game, the winning team is the one whose overall play, determined by skaters’ intrinsic hockey ability and the effort they put in, is stronger.
Each side has six players on the ice.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Marco, Alex, Ben, Carter, Dylan, and Finn lost to Grace, Henry, Isla, Jake, Kara, and Liam.
In the second game, Marco, Grace, Henry, Carter, Isla, and Liam lost to Alex, Ben, Dylan, Jake, Kara, and Finn.
In the third game, Alex, Grace, Ben, Jake, Kara, and Finn beat Henry, Isla, Carter, Dylan, Liam, and Nora.
In the fourth game, Marco, Jake, Kara, Dylan, Liam, and Nora lost to Alex, Grace, Henry, Ben, Isla, and Finn.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random ice hockey players, where do you think Marco ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Grace put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Marco, Alex, Ben, Dylan, Kara, and Liam (Team 1) and Grace, Henry, Isla, Jake, Carter, and Finn (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Marco, Grace, Henry, Carter, Jake, and Nora (Team 1) and Alex, Ben, Isla, Dylan, Kara, and Finn (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
