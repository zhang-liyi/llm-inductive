BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes are competing in a series of Handball matches. Each team consists of 7 players. The winning team is determined by the collective goal-scoring ability and defense of the squad, based on their intrinsic agility and tactical intelligence, modulated by the effort they exert during the match.
One player, Elena, is a "rover" who switches teams, while the other squads (The Vipers and The Cobras) remain with their fixed rosters.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Elena and The Vipers beat The Cobras.
In the second match, Elena and The Cobras beat The Vipers.
In the third match, Elena and The Vipers lost to The Cobras.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random handball players, where do you think Elena ranks in terms of intrinsic agility?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think The Cobras put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Elena and The Vipers (Team 1) and The Cobras (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between The Vipers (Team 1) and Elena and The Cobras (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
