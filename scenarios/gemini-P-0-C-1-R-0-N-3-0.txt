BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Basketball games. In each game, the team that wins depends on the collective scoring and defensive efficiency of the players, based on their intrinsic basketball skill modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that game.
Athletes compete in teams of 5.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Jordan, Scottie, Dennis, Steve, and Luc beat Karl, John, Jeff, Bryon, and Greg.
In the second game, Jordan, Karl, John, Jeff, and Bryon beat Scottie, Dennis, Steve, Luc, and Greg.
In the third game, Jordan, Greg, Toni, Ron, and Bill beat Clyde, Terry, Kevin, Jerome, and Clifford.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Jordan ranks in terms of intrinsic basketball skill?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Karl put into the second game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Jordan, Scottie, Dennis, Steve, and Luc (Team 1) and Karl, John, Jeff, Bryon, and Greg (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Scottie, Dennis, Steve, Luc, and Greg (Team 1) and Karl, John, Jeff, Bryon, and Toni (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
