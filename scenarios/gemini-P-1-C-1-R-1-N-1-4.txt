BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in small-sided soccer matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the ball control and tactical awareness of the athletes, based on their intrinsic technical skill modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that match.
Athletes compete either individually or as a team of three.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Kai, Jay, and Roy beat Max, Ian, and Jamie.
In the second match, Max, Ian, and Roy beat Kai, Jay, and Jamie.
In the third match, Jamie lost to Kai.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Jamie ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Max put into the third race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Jamie and Jay (Team 1) and Roy and Ian (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Roy and Max (Team 1) and Jay and Kai (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
