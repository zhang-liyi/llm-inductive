BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Ultimate Frisbee matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the score reached, determined by the athletes' throwing precision and sprinting speed, based on their intrinsic athleticism modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete as a fixed team of three.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, The Flyers (Alex, Ben, and Chris) beat The Discs (Cody, Tyler, and Austin).
In the second match, The Hucks (Dan, Eric, and Fred) beat The Discs (Cody, Tyler, and Austin).
In the third match, The Zones (Greg, Harry, and Ian) beat The Discs (Cody, Tyler, and Austin).

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Cody ranks in terms of intrinsic athleticism?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Cody put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Alex and Dan (Team 1) and Cody and Tyler (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Ben and Eric (Team 1) and Austin and Greg (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
