BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Ice Hockey scrimmages. In each scrimmage, the team that wins depends on the average skating speed and puck handling of the athletes, based on their intrinsic talent modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that scrimmage.
Athletes compete as a team of 5.
All scrimmages take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first scrimmage, Wayne, Mark, Jari, Paul, and Grant beat Mario, Ron, Francis, Ulf, and Larry.
In the second scrimmage, Wayne, Mark, Jari, Paul, and Grant beat Mario, Ron, Francis, Dale, and Bryan.
In the third scrimmage, Wayne, Mark, Jari, Paul, and Grant beat Dale, Bryan, Ulf, Larry, and Mario.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Wayne ranks in terms of intrinsic talent?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Mario put into the third scrimmage?
Query 3: In a new scrimmage later this same day between Wayne, Mark, Jari, Paul, and Grant (Team 1) and Mario, Ron, Francis, Ulf, and Larry (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new scrimmage later this same day between Wayne, Mario, Dale, Paul, and Larry (Team 1) and Mark, Ron, Jari, Francis, and Grant (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
