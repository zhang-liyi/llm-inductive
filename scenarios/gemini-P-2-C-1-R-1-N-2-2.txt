BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Cycling (Track Team Pursuit) races. In each race, the team that wins depends on the average speed and aerodynamics the athletes are able to maintain, based on their intrinsic power modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that race.
Athletes compete as a team of 4.
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, Marco, Paul, John, and Ringo beat George, Stu, Brian, and Mick.
In the second race, Marco, George, Stu, and Brian beat Paul, John, Ringo, and Mick.
In the third race, Marco, Paul, John, and Ringo lost to George, Stu, Brian, and Mick.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Marco ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Paul put into the third race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Marco, George, Paul, and John (Team 1) and Stu, Brian, Ringo, and Mick (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Paul, John, George, and Stu (Team 1) and Marco, Ringo, Brian, and Mick (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
