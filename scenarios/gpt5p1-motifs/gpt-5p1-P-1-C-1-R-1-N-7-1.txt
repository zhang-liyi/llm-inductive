BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are playing a series of softball games. Each team fields 9 players on defense. Game outcomes depend on the players’ intrinsic batting and fielding ability, combined with how much effort they put in during that game.
Most athletes are on fixed teams: the Comets, the Dragons, and the Falcons. Lila is a guest player who sometimes fills in for different teams. Players can also face off individually in batting duels against a pitcher.
All games take place on the same afternoon.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, the Comets with Lila in the lineup lost to the Dragons.
In the second game, the Falcons with Lila in the lineup lost to the Dragons.
In the third game, the Dragons beat the Comets using their regular lineups without Lila.
In an individual batting duel, Lila lost to Naomi from the Dragons.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random softball players, where do you think Lila ranks in terms of intrinsic softball ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Lila put into the second game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between the Dragons and the Comets with Lila added to the Comets, who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between the Dragons and the Falcons with Lila added to the Falcons, who would win and by how much?
