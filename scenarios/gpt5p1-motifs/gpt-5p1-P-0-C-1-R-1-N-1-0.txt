BACKGROUND
In this event, the players are competing in a series of 3-on-3 half-court basketball games. In each game, the team that wins depends on the players’ intrinsic basketball ability (shooting, ball-handling, defense) combined with how much effort they put in during that game.
Teams are of size three.
Most players stay in fixed trios for the day, but Jordan is a roaming player who joins different teammates in different games.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Jordan, Casey, and Max beat Ness, Willow, and Blake.
In the second game, Jordan, Taylor, and Sam beat Ness, Willow, and Blake.
In the third game, Jordan, Riley, and Dana beat Casey, Max, and Taylor.
In the fourth game, Jordan beat Ness.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random basketball players, where do you think Jordan ranks in terms of intrinsic basketball ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Casey put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Jordan, Willow, and Sam (Team 1) and Ness, Max, and Taylor (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Casey, Max, and Taylor (Team 1) and Ness, Willow, and Blake (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
