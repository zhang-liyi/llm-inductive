BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of 4-player volleyball games. In each game, the team that wins depends on how well the players can serve, pass, set, and spike, based on their intrinsic volleyball ability and jumping power, modulated by how much effort they put in to that game.
Athletes compete either individually in 1-on-1 serving and rally games, or as a team of four.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Nina, Ella, Kate, and Lauren beat Rachel, Sarah, Tessa, and Vivian.
In the second game, Nina, Rachel, Brooke, and Carly beat Ella, Kate, Lauren, and Megan.
In the third game, Nina beat Ella in a 1-on-1 serving and rally game.
In the fourth game, Rachel, Sarah, Tessa, and Vivian beat Brooke, Carly, Dana, and Erin.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random volleyball players, where do you think Nina ranks in terms of intrinsic volleyball ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Ella put into the first game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Nina, Rachel, Sarah, and Tessa (Team 1) and Ella, Kate, Lauren, and Megan (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Brooke, Carly, Dana, and Erin (Team 1) and Rachel, Sarah, Tessa, and Vivian (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
