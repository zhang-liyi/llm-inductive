BACKGROUND
In this event, the rowers are competing in a series of rowing races. In each race, the boat that wins depends on how much power and technical efficiency the rowers generate, determined by their intrinsic rowing strength and technique together with the effort they apply in that race.
Rowers compete either individually in single sculls or in eights.
All races take place on the same day.
