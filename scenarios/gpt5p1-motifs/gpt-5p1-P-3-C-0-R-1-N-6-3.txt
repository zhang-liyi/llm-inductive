BACKGROUND
This event is a small-sided rugby tournament. Each side has 8 fixed players, and the same players line up together in every game.
Game results are determined by the players’ intrinsic rugby ability and the level of effort and physical commitment they bring to that game.
All games are played on the same day.
Caleb is on Team Iron in every game he plays.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Team Storm beat Team Iron.
In the second game, Team Crest beat Team Iron.
In the third game, Team Iron beat Team Storm.
In the fourth game, Team Storm beat Team Crest.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random rugby players, where do you think Caleb ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Jonah from Team Storm put into the first game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Team Iron and Team Crest, who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Team Storm and Team Iron, who would win and by how much?
