BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are playing a series of Ultimate Frisbee games. In each game, the winning team is determined by the average level of Ultimate-specific ability of the players on the field (such as sprint speed, throwing precision, and catching reliability), together with how much effort they put into that game.
Players compete either individually in 1-on-1 throwing contests or in 6-a-side teams.
All games take place on the same day.
