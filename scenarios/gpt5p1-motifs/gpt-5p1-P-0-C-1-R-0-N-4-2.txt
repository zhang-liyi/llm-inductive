BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are playing a series of full-court basketball scrimmages. In each scrimmage, the winning team depends on the players’ intrinsic shooting, ball-handling, and defensive ability, along with how much effort they put in that game.
Athletes compete either individually (in 1-on-1) or in 6-player teams.
All scrimmages take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first scrimmage, Malik, Jonah, Chris, Eli, Andre, and Victor beat Liam, Ben, Carlos, Owen, Derek, and Miles.
In the second scrimmage, Malik, Liam, Carlos, Owen, Derek, and Miles beat Jonah, Chris, Eli, Andre, Victor, and Ben.
In the third scrimmage, Malik beat Ben in a 1-on-1 game.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random basketball players, where do you think Malik ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Ben put into the 1-on-1 game in the third scrimmage?
Query 3: In a new scrimmage later this same day between Malik, Jonah, Ben, Carlos, Andre, and Miles (Team 1) and Liam, Chris, Eli, Owen, Derek, and Victor (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
