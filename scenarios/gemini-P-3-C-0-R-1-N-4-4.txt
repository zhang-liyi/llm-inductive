BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of rugby matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the average power and agility of the athletes, based on their intrinsic strength modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete in fixed teams of 6.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Gareth's team (consisting of Gareth and 5 fixed teammates) beat Owen's team.
In the second match, Gareth's team lost to Alun's team.
In the third match, Gareth's team lost to Finn's team.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Gareth ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Gareth's team put into the first match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Gareth's team (Team 1) and Finn's team (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Owen's team (Team 1) and Alun's team (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
