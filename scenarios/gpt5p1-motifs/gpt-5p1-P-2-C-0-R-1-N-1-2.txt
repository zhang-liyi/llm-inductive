BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are playing a small-sided 3-on-3 soccer tournament. In each match, the team that wins depends on the players’ intrinsic ball control, speed, and finishing ability, combined with how much effort they put into that specific match.
Athletes compete either individually or as part of their fixed 3-player team.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Marco, Enzo, and Luca beat Theo, Hugo, and Leo.
In the second match, Marco, Enzo, and Luca beat Amir, Omar, and Yusuf.
In the third match, Theo, Hugo, and Leo beat Amir, Omar, and Yusuf.
In the fourth match, Amir, Omar, and Yusuf beat Marco, Enzo, and Luca.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random soccer players, where do you think Marco ranks in terms of intrinsic soccer ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Luca put into the fourth match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Marco, Enzo, and Luca (Team 1) and Theo, Hugo, and Leo (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Theo, Hugo, and Leo (Team 1) and Amir, Omar, and Yusuf (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
