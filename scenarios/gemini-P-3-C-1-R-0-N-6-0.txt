BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes compete in a series of rowing races in "Eights" (boats with 8 rowers). The winning boat is determined by the collective power and synchronization of the crew, based on their intrinsic rowing ability and the effort they exert during the race.
Athletes are assigned to different crews for each race.
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, the crew of (Ian, Jon, Ken, Leo, Mat, Ned, Oli, Pat) beat the crew of (Sam, Ben, Cal, Dan, Ed, Fay, Gus, Hal).
In the second race, the crew of (Ian, Jon, Ken, Leo, Mat, Ned, Oli, Pat) beat the crew of (Sam, Ray, Tim, Val, Wes, Xul, Yan, Zed).
In the third race, the crew of (Sam, Ian, Jon, Ken, Leo, Mat, Ned, Oli) beat the crew of (Ben, Cal, Dan, Ed, Fay, Gus, Hal, Pat).
In the fourth race, the crew of (Jon, Ken, Leo, Mat, Ned, Oli, Pat, Ray) beat the crew of (Sam, Tim, Val, Wes, Xul, Yan, Zed, Art).

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Sam ranks in terms of intrinsic rowing ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Ben put into the third race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between the crew of (Sam, Ian, Jon, Ken, Leo, Mat, Ned, Oli) (Team 1) and the crew of (Ben, Cal, Dan, Ed, Fay, Gus, Hal, Pat) (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between the crew of (Sam, Ray, Tim, Val, Wes, Xul, Yan, Zed) (Team 1) and the crew of (Ian, Jon, Ken, Leo, Mat, Ned, Oli, Pat) (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
