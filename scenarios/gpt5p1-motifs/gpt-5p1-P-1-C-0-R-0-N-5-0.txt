BACKGROUND
In this event, the players are competing in a series of small‑sided soccer games. In each game, the team that wins is determined by the players’ intrinsic soccer ability (technique, speed, vision, finishing) combined with how much effort they put into that game.
Games are played either as 1v1 drills or as seven‑a‑side scrimmages.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
Define Team D (Diego’s team) as: Diego, Liam, Noah, Ethan, Mason, Oliver, Lucas.

1. In the first scrimmage, Ava, Emma, Sophia, Mia, Charlotte, Amelia, Harper beat Diego, Liam, Noah, Ethan, Mason, Oliver, Lucas.
2. In the second scrimmage, Jack, Henry, Ava, Ella, Sophia, Harper, Mia beat Diego, Liam, Noah, Ethan, Mason, Oliver, Lucas.
3. In the third scrimmage, Charlotte, Amelia, Ella, Jack, Henry, Carter, Emma beat Diego, Liam, Noah, Ethan, Mason, Oliver, Lucas.
4. In a penalty‑shootout drill, Emma beat Mason.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random soccer players, where do you think Diego ranks in terms of intrinsic soccer ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Ava put into the second scrimmage?
Query 3: In a new seven‑a‑side scrimmage later this same day between Diego, Liam, Noah, Ethan, Mason, Oliver, Lucas (Team 1) and Jack, Henry, Ella, Carter, Ava, Emma, Sophia (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new seven‑a‑side scrimmage later this same day between Ava, Emma, Mia, Charlotte, Amelia, Harper, Ella (Team 1) and Jack, Henry, Sophia, Carter, Mason, Oliver, Lucas (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
