BACKGROUND
In this event, the players are competing in a series of small-sided soccer matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on players’ intrinsic ball control, passing, and finishing abilities, along with the effort they put into that match.
Matches are played either 1-on-1 or 4-on-4.
Most players stay in fixed 4-player squads (Leo, Mateo, Noah, and Oscar on one side; Hana, Iris, Jade, and Kara on the other), while Priya regularly changes teammates.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Leo, Mateo, Noah, and Oscar beat Hana, Iris, Jade, and Kara.
In the second match, Priya, Hana, Iris, and Jade lost to Leo, Mateo, Noah, and Oscar.
In the third match, Priya, Leo, Mateo, and Noah lost to Hana, Iris, Jade, and Kara.
In the fourth match, Priya lost to Leo.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random soccer players, where do you think Leo ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Priya put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new 4-on-4 match later this same day between Hana, Iris, Jade, and Kara (Team 1) and Priya, Leo, Mateo, and Noah (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new 1-on-1 match later this same day between Priya (Player 1) and Oscar (Player 2), who would win and by how much?
