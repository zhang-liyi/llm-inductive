BACKGROUND  
In this event, the athletes are competing in rowing races, both in single sculls and in 4-person boats. In each race, the boat that wins depends on the rowers’ intrinsic rowing power and technique, combined with how much effort they put into that race.  
All races take place on the same day.  

CONDITIONS  
In the first race, Alex beat Derek.  
In the second race, Derek, Ethan, Frank, and George lost to Alex, Ben, Carl, and David.  
In the third race, Derek, Ethan, Frank, and George lost to Ben, Carl, Harry, and Ian.  

QUERIES  
Query 1: Out of 100 random rowers, where do you think Alex ranks in terms of intrinsic rowing ability?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Derek put into the second race?  
Query 3: In a new 4-person boat race later this same day between Alex, Ben, Carl, and Harry (Boat 1) and Derek, Ethan, Frank, and George (Boat 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new 4-person boat race later this same day between David, Harry, Ethan, and Frank (Boat 1) and Ben, Carl, George, and Derek (Boat 2), who would win and by how much?
