BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Ultimate Frisbee matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on their ability to advance the disc and score, based on their intrinsic athleticism and field vision, modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete either individually or as a team of three.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Morgan, Alex, and Sam beat Jordan, Casey, and Drew.
In the second match, Morgan, Jordan, and Casey beat Alex, Sam, and Drew.
In the third match, Morgan beat Jordan.
In the fourth match, Morgan, Drew, and Alex lost to Jordan, Casey, and Sam.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Morgan ranks in terms of intrinsic athleticism?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Morgan put into the fourth match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Morgan, Alex, and Sam (Team 1) and Jordan, Casey, and Drew (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Jordan, Casey, and Alex (Team 1) and Morgan, Sam, and Drew (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
