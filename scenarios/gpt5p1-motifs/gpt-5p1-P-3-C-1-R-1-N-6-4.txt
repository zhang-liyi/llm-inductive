BACKGROUND
This is an 8-a-side cricket event. Match outcomes depend on each player’s intrinsic batting, bowling, and fielding ability, adjusted by the amount of effort they put into that match.
Players compete either in head-to-head batting challenges or as part of 8-player sides.
All matches and challenges are held on the same day.
Most players remain on fixed sides, but Tyrese changes teams between matches.
