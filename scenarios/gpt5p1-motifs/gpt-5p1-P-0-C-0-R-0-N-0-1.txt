In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of badminton matches. The outcome of each match depends on their intrinsic speed, racket control, and tactical awareness, along with how much effort they put into that match.  
Players compete either individually or in doubles teams of two.  
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS  
In the first match, Mina and Sara beat Kenji and Luke.  
In the second match, Mina beat Priya.  
In the third match, Mina and Sara beat Priya and Kenji.  
In the fourth match, Mina and Sara beat Luke and Priya.

QUERIES  
Query 1: Out of 100 random badminton players, where do you think Mina ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Priya put into the third match?  
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Mina and Sara (Team 1) and Luke and Kenji (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Priya and Luke (Team 1) and Sara and Kenji (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
