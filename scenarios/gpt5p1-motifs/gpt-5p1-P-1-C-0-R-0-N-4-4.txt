BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of pool swimming races. In each race, the winner is determined by how fast the swimmers move through the water, based on their intrinsic speed, technique, and endurance, combined with how much effort they put into that race.
Athletes compete either individually or in relay teams.
When competing as a team, each relay team consists of six swimmers.
All races take place on the same day.
