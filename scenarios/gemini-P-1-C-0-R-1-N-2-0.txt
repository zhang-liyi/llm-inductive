BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes are competing in a 4-on-4 basketball tournament. In each game, the team that wins depends on the collective scoring ability and defensive coordination of the players, based on their intrinsic skill modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that specific game.
Athletes compete in fixed teams of four.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, the team of Finn, Luca, Cole, and Nash beat the team of Rory, Kai, Jaden, and Seth.
In the second game, the team of Omar, Zion, Jax, and Reed beat the team of Rory, Kai, Jaden, and Seth.
In the third game, the team of Finn, Luca, Cole, and Nash beat the team of Omar, Zion, Jax, and Reed.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Rory ranks in terms of intrinsic skill?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Finn put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between the team of Rory, Kai, Jaden, and Seth (Team 1) and the team of Finn, Luca, Cole, and Nash (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between the team of Finn, Luca, Cole, and Nash (Team 1) and the team of Omar, Zion, Jax, and Reed (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
