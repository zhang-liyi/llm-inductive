BACKGROUND
In this event, shooters are competing in a series of trap shooting contests. The winner is the one who breaks more targets, which depends on their intrinsic shooting precision and reaction speed, modulated by how much effort and mental focus they invest in that contest.
Shooters compete both individually and in two-person teams, with team scores being the sum of individual performances.
All contests happen on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first contest, Carla and Ben beat Dylan and Riya.
In the second contest, Ben beat Dylan.
In the third contest, Riya beat Carla.
In the fourth contest, Carla and Dylan beat Ben and Riya.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random shooters, where do you think Ben ranks in terms of intrinsic shooting precision?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Riya put into the third contest?
Query 3: In a new contest later this same day between Carla and Ben (Team 1) and Dylan and Riya (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new contest later this same day between Carla and Riya (Team 1) and Ben and Dylan (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
