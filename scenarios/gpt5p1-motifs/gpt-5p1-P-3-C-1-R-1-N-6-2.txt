BACKGROUND
This is a rowing regatta featuring 2000 m races. Boat speed and race outcomes depend on each rower’s intrinsic strength and technique, combined with how much effort they put into that race.
Rowers compete either in single sculls or in 8-person crews.
All races take place on the same day.
Most rowers stay in fixed crews, but Marco switches between different boats.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, Marco rowed with Crew Alpha and Crew Beta beat Crew Alpha.
In the second race, Marco rowed with a composite crew and Crew Gamma beat Marco’s crew.
In the third race, Liam beat Marco in a 1-on-1 single scull race.
In the fourth race, Marco rowed with Crew Beta and Crew Beta beat Crew Gamma.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random rowers, where do you think Marco ranks in terms of intrinsic strength and rowing ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Marco put into the third race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Marco with the rest of Crew Alpha (Boat 1) and the original lineup of Crew Beta without Marco (Boat 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Crew Gamma (Boat 1) and a new composite crew that includes Marco plus seven other rowers (Boat 2), who would win and by how much?
