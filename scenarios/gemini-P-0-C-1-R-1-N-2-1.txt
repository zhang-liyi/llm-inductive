BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of 4x100m swimming relay races. In each race, the team that wins depends on the average speed with which the athletes are able to swim, based on their intrinsic technique and power modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that race.
Athletes compete as a team of four.
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, Morgan, Ash, Lee, and Pat beat Sid, Vic, Joe, and Ken.
In the second race, Morgan, Sid, Vic, and Joe beat Ash, Lee, Pat, and Ken.
In the third race, Morgan, Ash, Lee, and Sid beat Ken, Vic, Joe, and Pat.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Morgan ranks in terms of intrinsic technique and power?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Ken put into the second race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Morgan, Ash, Lee, and Pat (Team 1) and Sid, Vic, Joe, and Ken (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Morgan, Sid, Vic, and Joe (Team 1) and Ash, Lee, Pat, and Ken (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
