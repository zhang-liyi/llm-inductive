BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Field Hockey matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the average stick-handling and running speed of the athletes, based on their intrinsic fitness modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete in teams of 7.
All matches take place on the same day.

In the first match, Morgan, Alex, Sam, Jordan, Casey, Riley, and Taylor beat Drew, Pat, Chris, Jessie, Avery, Quinn, and Cameron.

In the third match, Morgan, Alex, Drew, Sam, Pat, Jordan, and Chris beat Casey, Riley, Taylor, Jessie, Avery, Quinn, and Cameron.

QUERIES
