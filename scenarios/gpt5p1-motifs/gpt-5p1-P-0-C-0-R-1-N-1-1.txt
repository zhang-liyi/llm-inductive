BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of small-sided 3-a-side soccer matches on a reduced-size pitch. The result of each match depends on the players’ intrinsic ball control, speed, endurance, and tactical sense, modulated by how much effort they put into that particular match.
Players compete only in fixed three-player teams.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Mia, Clara, and Zoe beat Hannah, Elise, and Brooke.  
In the second match, Mia, Clara, and Zoe beat Tessa, Lauren, and Paige.  
In the third match, Hannah, Elise, and Brooke beat Tessa, Lauren, and Paige.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random soccer players, where do you think Mia ranks in terms of intrinsic soccer ability?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Paige put into the third match?  
Query 3: In a new 3-a-side match later this same day between Mia, Clara, and Zoe (Team 1) and Hannah, Elise, and Brooke (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new 3-a-side match later this same day between Tessa, Lauren, and Paige (Team 1) and a new team of Ivy, Quinn, and Darcy (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
