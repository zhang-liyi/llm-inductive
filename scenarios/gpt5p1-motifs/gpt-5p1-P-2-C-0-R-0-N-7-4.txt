BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of rowing races in 9-person boats (eight rowers plus a coxswain). Each race is decided by the crew’s intrinsic rowing power and technique, together with how much effort they put into that race.
All races take place on the same day.
Whenever Naomi rows, she is always in the same crew with the same eight teammates: Anna (coxswain), Beth, Claire, Dana, Emma, Flora, Grace, and Hazel.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, Naomi and her usual eight teammates beat Isla, Jade, Kara, Lana, Megan, Nora, Olivia, Paige, and Quinn.
In the second race, Naomi and her usual eight teammates beat Isla, Jade, Kara, Lana, Megan, Nora, Riley, Sara, and Tessa.
In the third race, Naomi and her usual eight teammates lost to Jade, Kara, Lana, Megan, Nora, Olivia, Paige, Quinn, and Riley.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random rowers, where do you think Naomi ranks in terms of intrinsic rowing ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Jade put into the third race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Naomi and her usual eight teammates (Team 1) and Isla, Jade, Kara, Lana, Megan, Nora, Olivia, Paige, and Quinn (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Isla, Jade, Kara, Lana, Megan, Nora, Riley, Sara, and Tessa (Team 1) and Jade, Kara, Lana, Megan, Nora, Olivia, Paige, Quinn, and Riley (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
