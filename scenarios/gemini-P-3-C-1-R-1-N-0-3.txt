BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Table Tennis matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the average reaction time and spin control of the athletes, based on their intrinsic coordination modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete as a team of two.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Daiki and Ren beat Kenji and Hiro.
In the second match, Hiro and Ren beat Kenji and Daiki.
In the third match, Kenji and Ren beat Hiro and Daiki.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Kenji ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Kenji put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Kenji and Hiro (Team 1) and Daiki and Jun (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Daiki and Ren (Team 1) and Hiro and Satoshi (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
