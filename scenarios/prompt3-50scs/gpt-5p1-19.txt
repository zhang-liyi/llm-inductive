BACKGROUND
This event is a distance running meet featuring track and cross-country style races. Outcomes in each race depend on the runners’ intrinsic endurance and pacing ability, combined with how much effort they choose to sustain over the distance.
All races are held on the same day. Some races are purely individual, while others are run in pairs, with each runner covering similar distances.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, a 5km track race, Hannah beat Julia.
In the second race, a 10km track race, Julia beat Hannah.
In the third race, a paired cross-country event, Hannah and Mark lost to Julia and Isaac.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random distance runners, where do you think Hannah ranks in terms of intrinsic endurance ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Hannah put into the second race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day, a 10km road race between Hannah (Bib 1) and Julia (Bib 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day, a paired 5km relay between Hannah and Isaac (Team 1) and Julia and Mark (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
