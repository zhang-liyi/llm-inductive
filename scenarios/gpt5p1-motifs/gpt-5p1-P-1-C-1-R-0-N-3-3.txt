BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are racing in a series of 5-rower sprint rowing races, with occasional individual single-scull races. Each race’s result depends on the rowers’ intrinsic power, endurance, technical stroke efficiency, and how much effort they put into that race.
Athletes compete either individually or as a team of five.
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, Elena, Victor, Hana, Sofia, and Daniel beat Marcos, Lucia, Tomas, Bruno, and Carla.
In the second race, Marcos, Elena, Sofia, Igor, and Rosa lost to Victor, Hana, Daniel, Lucia, and Bruno.
In the third race, Elena, Hana, Sofia, Daniel, and Tomas beat Marcos, Victor, Bruno, Carla, and Rosa.
In a single-scull race, Hana beat Marcos.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random rowers, where do you think Hana ranks in terms of intrinsic rowing ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Marcos put into the third race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Marcos, Elena, Daniel, Lucia, and Rosa (Team 1) and Victor, Hana, Sofia, Tomas, and Bruno (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Marcos, Victor, Sofia, Igor, and Carla (Team 1) and Elena, Hana, Daniel, Lucia, and Bruno (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
