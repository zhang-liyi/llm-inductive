BACKGROUND
In this event, three fixed ice hockey lines of five skaters (without specifying goalies) are scrimmaging in a series of small-area games. The outcome of each game depends on the skaters’ intrinsic skating speed, puck control, shooting precision, and defensive instincts, influenced by how much effort and intensity they bring to that game. The same five skaters always play together on their respective lines.
Line Polar: Andre, Boris, Carl, Denis, Emil.
Line Glacier: Finn, Gregor, Hugo, Ivan, Jakob.
Line Frost: Kamil, Liam, Nico, Oskar, Pavel.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Line Polar beat Line Glacier.
In the second game, Line Polar beat Line Frost.
In the third game, Line Glacier beat Line Frost.
In the fourth game, Line Polar beat Line Glacier again.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random ice hockey skaters, where do you think Andre ranks in terms of intrinsic on-ice ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Nico put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Line Polar (Andre, Boris, Carl, Denis, Emil) and Line Frost (Kamil, Liam, Nico, Oskar, Pavel), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Line Glacier (Finn, Gregor, Hugo, Ivan, Jakob) and Line Frost (Kamil, Liam, Nico, Oskar, Pavel), who would win and by how much?
