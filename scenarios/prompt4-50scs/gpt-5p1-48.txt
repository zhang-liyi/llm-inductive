BACKGROUND  
In this event, the athletes are competing in sport climbing on standardized routes. For each climb, two climbers or teams are put on similar difficulty routes, and the winner is whoever climbs higher or completes the route more quickly. Performance depends on intrinsic strength, technique, and mental focus, combined with how much effort they give on that attempt.  
Climbers compete both individually and in pairs, where team results are based on the combined performance of both climbers.  
All climbs take place on the same day.
