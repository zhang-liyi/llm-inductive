<START_SCENARIO>
BACKGROUND
In this event, three baseball teams with 9 fixed players each play a series of short games. Each game’s result depends on the players’ intrinsic batting, pitching, and fielding ability, combined with how much effort they put in during that game.  
Rosters remain fixed; players do not switch teams at any point.  
Diego plays for the Falcons. The other teams are the Bears and the Lions.  
All games are played on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, the Falcons beat the Bears.  
In the second game, the Falcons beat the Lions.  
In the third game, the Lions beat the Falcons.  
In the fourth game, the Bears beat the Lions.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random baseball players, where do you think Diego ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Owen from the Bears put into the first game?  
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between the Falcons and the Bears, who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between the Falcons and the Lions, who would win and by how much?
