BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of seven-a-side ice hockey games. In each game, the winning team is determined by the players’ intrinsic skating speed, stick-handling skill, and shooting ability, along with how much effort they put in to that game.
Teams consist of fixed squads of seven players, and each player always plays on the same team whenever they appear.
All games take place on the same day.

Team Morgan: Morgan, Alex, Jamie, Riley, Casey, Jordan, Taylor  
Team Nina: Nina, Cole, Harper, Blake, Logan, Drew, Sydney  
Team Pavel: Pavel, Ivan, Mila, Anya, Sergei, Lena, Viktor

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Morgan’s team beat Nina’s team.  
In the second game, Morgan’s team beat Pavel’s team.  
In the third game, Pavel’s team beat Nina’s team.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random ice hockey players, where do you think Morgan ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Nina put into the first game?  
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Morgan’s team (Team 1) and Nina’s team (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Pavel’s team (Team 1) and Nina’s team (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
