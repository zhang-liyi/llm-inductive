BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of basketball games. In each game, the outcome depends on the players’ intrinsic basketball ability, modulated by how much sustained effort they put into running, defending, and staying aggressive on offense.
Players compete either individually (in shooting contests) or in teams of eight.
All games take place on the same day.
