BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Soccer games played on a smaller pitch. Each team fields 8 players. The outcome depends on the average technical skill and stamina of the team members, influenced by their motivation and effort levels in that specific game.
The local clubs (United, City, and Rovers) have fixed lineups, but a visiting professional, Alex, plays as a guest for different clubs throughout the day.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Alex and United beat City.
In the second game, Alex and City beat United.
In the third game, Rovers lost to Alex and City.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Alex ranks in terms of intrinsic technical skill?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Rovers put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Alex and Rovers (Team 1) and United (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between United (Team 1) and City (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
