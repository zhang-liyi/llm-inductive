BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Ultimate Frisbee games. In each game, the team that wins depends on the average speed and throwing accuracy of the athletes, based on their intrinsic strength modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that game.
Athletes compete in fixed teams of 6.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Caleb's team (consisting of Caleb and 5 fixed teammates) beat Noah's team.
In the second game, Caleb's team lost to Liam's team.
In the third game, Caleb's team lost to Mason's team.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Caleb ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Caleb's team put into the first game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Caleb's team (Team 1) and Mason's team (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Noah's team (Team 1) and Liam's team (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
