BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes compete in 5-on-5 Flag American Football games. The winner is determined by the team's total tactical execution and speed, based on the players' intrinsic skills and the effort they put into the game.
There are two main established teams and one player who switches sides.
All games take place on the same field on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, The Pros (Tom, Dick, Harry, Joe, and Bill) beat The Stars (Sam, Mike, Rob, Art, and Len).
In the second game, The Stars beat The Pros (with Sid replacing Tom).
In the third game, The Pros beat The Stars (with Sid replacing Sam).

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Sid ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Tom put into the first game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between The Pros (with Sid replacing Bill) and The Stars, who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between The Stars (with Sid replacing Mike) and The Pros (with Sid replacing Harry), who would win and by how much?
