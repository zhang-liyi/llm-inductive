BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Badminton matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the average reaction time and agility with which the athletes are able to move, based on their intrinsic reflexes modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that match.
Athletes compete either individually or as a fixed pair.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Chen and Lee beat Ollie and Ned.
In the second match, Ollie and Ned lost to Chen and Lee.
In the third match, Chen beat Ollie.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Chen ranks in terms of intrinsic reflexes?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Ollie put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Chen and Lee (Team 1) and Ollie and Ned (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Ollie and Lee (Team 1) and Chen and a new player named Wei (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
