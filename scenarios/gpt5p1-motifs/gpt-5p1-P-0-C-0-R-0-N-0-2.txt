BACKGROUND  
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of beach volleyball games. Each game’s result depends on the players’ intrinsic jumping ability, ball control, and serving accuracy, combined with how much effort they put into that particular game.  
All games are played 2-on-2 on the same day.

CONDITIONS  
In the first game, Diego and Mateo beat Carla and Nina.  
In the second game, Diego and Mateo beat Leo and Carla.  
In the third game, Diego and Mateo beat Nina and Sam.

QUERIES  
Query 1: Out of 100 random beach volleyball players, where do you think Diego ranks in terms of intrinsic skill?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Carla put into the second game?  
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Diego and Mateo (Team 1) and Leo and Sam (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Nina and Carla (Team 1) and Leo and Nina (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
