BACKGROUND

Teams consist of 7 players each.
The games are played back-to-back.

In the third game, the team of Ali, Hal, Bo, Jay, Cy, Kip, and Dax lost to the team of Otis, Eli, Lee, Fox, Mel, Gil, and Ned.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Otis ranks in terms of intrinsic talent?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Gil put into the second game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between the team of Ali, Bo, Cy, Dax, Eli, Fox, and Hal (Team 1) and the team of Otis, Jay, Kip, Lee, Mel, Ned, and Gil (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between the team of Otis, Ali, Hal, Bo, Jay, Cy, and Kip (Team 1) and the team of Dax, Eli, Lee, Fox, Mel, Gil, and Ned (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
