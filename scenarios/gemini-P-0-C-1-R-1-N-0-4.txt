BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Table Tennis matches. In each match, the winner is determined by the spin and speed they can impart on the ball, based on their intrinsic agility modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that match.
Athletes compete either individually or as a team of two.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Haruto beat Kenji.
In the second match, Haruto and Yuto beat Kenji and Ren.
In the third match, Haruto and Ren beat Kenji and Yuto.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Haruto ranks in terms of intrinsic agility?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Kenji put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Haruto and Yuto (Team 1) and Kenji and Ren (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Kenji and Ren (Team 1) and Yuto and a new player, Daiki (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
