BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of track cycling team pursuits. In each race, the team that wins depends on the average speed with which the athletes are able to cycle, based on their intrinsic wattage and aerodynamics modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that race.
Athletes compete as a team of four.
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, Quinn, Bob, Tim, and Art beat Cal, Don, Gil, and Hal.
In the second race, Quinn, Cal, Don, and Gil beat Bob, Tim, Art, and Hal.
In the third race, Quinn, Bob, Tim, and Cal beat Hal, Don, Gil, and Art.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Quinn ranks in terms of intrinsic wattage and aerodynamics?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Hal put into the second race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Quinn, Bob, Tim, and Art (Team 1) and Cal, Don, Gil, and Hal (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Quinn, Cal, Don, and Gil (Team 1) and Bob, Tim, Art, and Hal (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
