BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Ice Hockey mini-matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the number of goals scored, based on the athletes' intrinsic skating speed and puck handling, modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete either individually (shootout drill) or as a fixed team of three.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Sidney, Evgeni, and Kris beat Alex, Nick, and Brooks.
In the second match, Sidney beat Alex.
In the third match, Sidney, Evgeni, and Kris beat Alex, Nick, and Brooks.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Sidney ranks in terms of intrinsic skating speed and puck handling?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Alex put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Sidney, Evgeni, and Kris (Team 1) and Alex, Nick, and Brooks (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Evgeni and Kris (Team 1) and Nick and Brooks (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
