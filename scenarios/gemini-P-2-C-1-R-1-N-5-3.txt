BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes are competing in a series of 7-a-side Soccer matches. The team that wins depends on the number of goals scored, based on the athletes' intrinsic ball control and stamina, modulated by other factors including the effort they put into that match.
Player Leo is a designated "joker" who swaps teams, whereas the two main clubs (Athletic FC and United FC) have fixed rosters of 7 players (when not joined by Leo).
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Leo and Athletic FC beat United FC.
In the second match, Leo and United FC beat Athletic FC.
In the third match, Leo and Athletic FC lost to United FC.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random soccer players, where do you think Leo ranks in terms of intrinsic ball control?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think United FC put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Leo and Athletic FC (Team 1) and United FC (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Athletic FC (Team 1) and Leo and United FC (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
