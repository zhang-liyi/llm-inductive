BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Table Tennis matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the spin control and reflex speed the athletes are able to demonstrate, based on their intrinsic technique modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete either individually or as a team of two.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Wei beat Timo.
In the second match, Wei and Jun beat Timo and Fan.
In the third match, Wei and Jun lost to Fan and Ma.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Wei ranks in terms of intrinsic technique?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Jun put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Wei and Jun (Team 1) and Timo and Xu (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Timo and Fan (Team 1) and Jun and Dimitrij (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
