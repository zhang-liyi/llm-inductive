BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Rowing races (Double Sculls). In each race, the team that wins depends on the power output and synchronization the athletes are able to generate, based on their intrinsic endurance modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that race.
Athletes compete as a team of two.
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, Nick and Dan beat Jordan and Pete.
In the second race, Pete and Rob beat Jordan and Nick.
In the third race, Nick and Dan beat Jordan and Rob.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Jordan ranks in terms of intrinsic endurance?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Jordan put into the third race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Jordan and Pete (Team 1) and Nick and Rob (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Pete and Dan (Team 1) and Nick and Rob (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
