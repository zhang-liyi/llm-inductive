BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Beach Volleyball matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the combined vertical leap and power of the athletes, based on their intrinsic athleticism modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that match.
Athletes compete either individually or as a fixed pair.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Ty beat Koda.
In the second match, Ty and Zane beat Koda and Jax.
In the third match, Koda and Jax lost to Ty and Zane.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Ty ranks in terms of intrinsic athleticism?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Koda put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Ty and Zane (Team 1) and Koda and Jax (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Koda and Zane (Team 1) and Ty and a new player named Leo (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
