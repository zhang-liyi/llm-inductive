BACKGROUND
In this event, three 5‑skater ice hockey lines scrimmage against each other on a full rink. For each scrimmage, the outcome is determined by the skaters’ intrinsic hockey abilities (skating speed, puck control, shooting, positional sense) combined with the intensity of effort they give in that scrimmage. Each player stays on the same line for all scrimmages; teams are fixed.
Line Frost: Connor, Dylan, Erik, Finn, Gabe.
Line Blaze: Andre, Boris, Cody, Derek, Evan.
Line Storm: Harry, Ivan, Jake, Kyle, Liam.
Connor always plays on Line Frost.

CONDITIONS
In the first scrimmage, Blaze beat Frost.
In the second scrimmage, Storm beat Frost.
In the third scrimmage, Blaze beat Storm.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random ice hockey skaters, where do you think Andre ranks in terms of intrinsic hockey ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Connor put into the second scrimmage?
Query 3: In a new scrimmage later this same day between Blaze and Frost, who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new scrimmage later this same day between Storm and Frost, who would win and by how much?
