BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes compete in Lacrosse matches using the "Sixes" format (6 players per side). The winner is decided by the team's aggregate agility and stick-work precision, derived from intrinsic coordination modulated by the intensity of effort during the match.
There are established clubs: The Nets, The Sticks, and The Goals. A standout player, Avery, is a "floater" who plays for whichever team draws the lottery card.
All matches take place during a single tournament day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, The Nets (featuring Avery) beat The Sticks (led by Morgan).
In the second match, The Goals (featuring Avery) beat The Nets (led by Riley).
In the third match, The Sticks (featuring Avery) beat The Goals (led by Jamie).

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random lacrosse players, where do you think Avery ranks in terms of intrinsic coordination?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Jamie put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between The Nets (featuring Avery) and The Goals (led by Jamie), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between The Sticks (led by Morgan) and The Nets (led by Riley), who would win and by how much?
