BACKGROUND
In this volleyball event, three teams of 8 players each compete in short matches. At any time, 6 players per side are on court, with 2 substitutes available on the bench. The outcome of each match depends on the players’ intrinsic serving, passing, and attacking ability, modulated by how much effort they put into that match.
Players stay on the same 8-player roster for all matches.
Keiko plays for Team Wave in every match with the same seven teammates.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Team Wave beat Team Summit.
In the second match, Team Wave beat Team Ember.
In the third match, Team Summit beat Team Wave.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random volleyball players, where do you think Keiko ranks in terms of intrinsic playing ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Lina (on Team Summit) put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Team Wave and Team Ember, who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Team Summit and Team Ember, who would win and by how much?
