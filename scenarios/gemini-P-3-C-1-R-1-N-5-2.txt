BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Rugby Sevens matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the points scored from tries and conversions, based on the athletes' intrinsic speed and tackling ability, modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete in teams of 7.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, The Warriors and Liam lost to The Titans.
In the second match, The Spartans and Liam lost to The Titans.
In the third match, The Warriors and Liam beat The Spartans.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Liam ranks in terms of intrinsic speed and tackling ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think The Titans put into the second match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between The Spartans and Liam (Team 1) and The Warriors (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between The Titans (Team 1) and The Warriors and Liam (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
