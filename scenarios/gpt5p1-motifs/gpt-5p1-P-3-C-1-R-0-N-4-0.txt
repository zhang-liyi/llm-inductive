BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of volleyball games. Outcomes depend on each player’s intrinsic volleyball ability (such as jumping power, ball control, and reaction speed) combined with how much effort they put into each game. 
Athletes compete either individually or in teams of six.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Ben beat Jordan.
In the second game, Ben, Emma, Henry, Iris, Leo, and Maya beat Jordan, Alice, Carla, Diego, Felix, and Grace.
In the third game, Alice, Ben, Carla, Diego, Felix, and Grace beat Jordan, Emma, Henry, Olivia, Paolo, and Noah.
In the fourth game, Jordan, Alice, Leo, Maya, Noah, and Olivia beat Ben, Carla, Diego, Emma, Felix, and Grace.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random volleyball players, where do you think Jordan ranks in terms of intrinsic volleyball ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Ben put into the first game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Jordan, Emma, Henry, Iris, Leo, and Paolo (Team 1) and Ben, Carla, Diego, Felix, Grace, and Maya (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Alice, Noah, Olivia, Carla, Diego, and Felix (Team 1) and Emma, Henry, Iris, Leo, Maya, and Paolo (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
