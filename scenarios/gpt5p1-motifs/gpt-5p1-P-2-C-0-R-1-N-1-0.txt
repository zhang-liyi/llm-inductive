BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are playing a 3-on-3 half-court basketball tournament. In each game, the team that wins depends on the players’ intrinsic basketball ability (including shooting accuracy, quickness, and decision-making), modulated by how much effort they put into that specific game.
Athletes compete either individually or as part of their fixed 3-player team.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Jordan, Malik, and Ethan beat Carlos, Diego, and Luis.
In the second game, Jordan, Malik, and Ethan beat Noah, Ryan, and Jack.
In the third game, Carlos, Diego, and Luis beat Noah, Ryan, and Jack.
In the fourth game, Noah, Ryan, and Jack beat Jordan, Malik, and Ethan.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random basketball players, where do you think Jordan ranks in terms of intrinsic basketball ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Ethan put into the fourth game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Jordan, Malik, and Ethan (Team 1) and Carlos, Diego, and Luis (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Carlos, Diego, and Luis (Team 1) and Noah, Ryan, and Jack (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
