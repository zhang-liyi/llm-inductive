BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of mini-ice hockey games. In each game, the team that wins depends on the combined skating speed and puck handling of the athletes, based on their intrinsic talent modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that game.
Athletes compete as a team of three.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Liam, Noah, and Ethan lost to Mason, Logan, and Lucas.
In the second game, Liam, Mason, and Logan beat Noah, Ethan, and Lucas.
In the third game, Liam, Lucas, and Noah lost to Mason, Logan, and Ethan.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Mason ranks in terms of intrinsic talent?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Liam put into the second game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Mason, Logan, and Lucas (Team 1) and Liam, Noah, and Ethan (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Liam, Mason, and Ethan (Team 1) and Noah, Logan, and Lucas (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
