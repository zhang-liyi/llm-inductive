BACKGROUND
In this event, players are competing in a 6-a-side soccer tournament held over a single day. The outcome of each match is determined by the players’ intrinsic soccer ability and the level of effort they put in during that match. Athletes can compete individually in penalty contests or as part of 6-player teams.
