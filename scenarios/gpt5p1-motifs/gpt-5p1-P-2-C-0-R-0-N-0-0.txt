BACKGROUND  
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of tennis matches, mostly in doubles. In each match, the team that wins depends on the players’ intrinsic racket skills, speed, and coordination, modulated by how much effort they put in during that match.  
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS  
In the first match, Lina and Rosa beat Mark and Jonah.  
In the second match, Lina and Rosa beat Priya and Mark.  
In the third match, Ken and Priya beat Lina and Rosa.

QUERIES  
Query 1: Out of 100 random tennis players, where do you think Lina ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Priya put into the third match?  
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Lina and Rosa (Team 1) and Mark and Ken (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Jonah and Priya (Team 1) and Mark and Ken (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
