BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in 3-on-3 beach volleyball matches. The winning team in each match depends on the players’ intrinsic serving, spiking, setting, and defensive instincts, adjusted by how much effort they put into that particular match.
Players compete only in fixed three-player squads.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Rafael, Diego, and Bruno beat Lucas, Andre, and Marco.  
In the second match, Rafael, Diego, and Bruno beat Stefan, Viktor, and Emil.  
In the third match, Lucas, Andre, and Marco beat Stefan, Viktor, and Emil.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random beach volleyball players, where do you think Rafael ranks in terms of intrinsic volleyball ability?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Marco put into the third match?  
Query 3: In a new 3-on-3 match later this same day between Rafael, Diego, and Bruno (Team 1) and Lucas, Andre, and Marco (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new 3-on-3 match later this same day between Stefan, Viktor, and Emil (Team 1) and a new team of Henrik, Jonas, and Mikel (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
