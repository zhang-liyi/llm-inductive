BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes are competing in a series of Ice Hockey matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the average skating speed and puck handling of the athletes, based on their intrinsic coordination modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete in teams of 5.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, The Bears beat Casey, Jon, Tim, Ben, and Al.
In the second match, The Wolves beat Casey, Ray, Sal, Joe, and Ken.
In the third match, Casey, Max, Ned, Opi, and Gus beat The Eagles.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Casey ranks in terms of intrinsic coordination?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Jon put into the first match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Casey, Jon, Tim, Ben, and Al (Team 1) and Casey, Max, Ned, Opi, and Gus (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between The Bears (Team 1) and The Wolves (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
