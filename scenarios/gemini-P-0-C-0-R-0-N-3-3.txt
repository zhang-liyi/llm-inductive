BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of 5-a-side Soccer matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the average ball control and tactical sense of the athletes, based on their intrinsic ability modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete as a team of 5.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Lionel, Andres, Xavi, Carles, and Sergio beat Cristiano, Luka, Karim, Gareth, and Marcelo.
In the second match, Lionel, Andres, Xavi, Carles, and Sergio beat Cristiano, Luka, Karim, Iker, and Pepe.
In the third match, Lionel, Andres, Xavi, Carles, and Sergio beat Iker, Pepe, Gareth, Marcelo, and Cristiano.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Lionel ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Cristiano put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Lionel, Andres, Xavi, Carles, and Sergio (Team 1) and Cristiano, Luka, Karim, Gareth, and Marcelo (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Lionel, Cristiano, Iker, Carles, and Marcelo (Team 1) and Andres, Luka, Xavi, Karim, and Sergio (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
