BACKGROUND
In this half-court basketball event, the athletes play 2-on-2 games. The result of each game depends on the players’ intrinsic basketball ability (shooting, ball-handling, defense, and decision-making) together with how much effort they put in during that game.
All games take place on the same day.
Teams are fixed pairs of two players.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Leo and Amir lost to Ben and Chris.
In the second game, Leo and Amir beat Diego and Samira.
In the third game, Leo and Amir lost to Ben and Chris.
In the fourth game, Leo and Amir lost to Diego and Samira.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random basketball players, where do you think Leo ranks in terms of intrinsic playing ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Ben put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Leo and Amir (Team 1) and Ben and Chris (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Diego and Samira (Team 1) and Ben and Chris (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
