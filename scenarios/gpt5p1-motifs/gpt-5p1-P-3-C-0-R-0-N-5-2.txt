BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Ultimate Frisbee games. The winning team in each game is determined by players’ intrinsic throwing accuracy, speed, and catching ability, combined with how much effort they put in during that game.
Athletes compete either individually in accuracy drills or as part of a 7‑player Ultimate team.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Ivy, Jack, Kara, Leo, Maya, Owen, and Piper beat Liam, Ava, Ben, Chloe, Ethan, Grace, and Noah.
In the second game, Jack, Kara, Leo, Maya, Owen, Quinn, and Riley beat Liam, Ava, Ben, Chloe, Ethan, Grace, and Noah.
In the third game, Liam, Ava, Ben, Chloe, Ethan, Grace, and Noah beat Kara, Leo, Maya, Owen, Piper, Sara, and Theo.
In the fourth game, Ava, Ben, Grace, Ivy, Jack, Maya, and Owen beat Noah, Piper, Quinn, Riley, Uma, Victor, and Willow.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random Ultimate Frisbee players, where do you think Liam ranks in terms of intrinsic Ultimate ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Jack put into the second game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Liam, Ava, Ben, Chloe, Ethan, Grace, and Noah (Team 1) and Ivy, Jack, Kara, Leo, Maya, Owen, and Piper (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Liam, Ava, Ben, Chloe, Ethan, Grace, and Noah (Team 1) and Noah, Piper, Quinn, Riley, Uma, Victor, and Willow (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
