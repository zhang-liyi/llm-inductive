BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Badminton matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the score achieved through shuttlecock placement, based on the athletes' intrinsic reflexes and stamina, modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete as a team of two.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Hiro and Ken beat Ryu and Jin.
In the second match, Hiro and Ken beat Jin and Taka.
In the third match, Hiro and Ken beat Ryu and Taka.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Hiro ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Ryu put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Hiro and Ken (Team 1) and Ryu and Daiki (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Jin and Taka (Team 1) and Ken and Satoshi (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
