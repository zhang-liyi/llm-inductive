BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Swimming relay races. In each race, the team that wins depends on the aggregate swimming speed of the four athletes, based on their intrinsic technique and power modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that race.
Athletes compete as a team of four.
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, Luca, Enzo, Leo, and Theo beat Ryan, Connor, Dylan, and Ethan.
In the second race, Luca, Enzo, Leo, and Theo beat Ryan, Connor, Owen, and Luke.
In the third race, Luca, Enzo, Leo, and Theo beat Dylan, Ethan, Owen, and Luke.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Luca ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Ryan put into the second race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Luca, Enzo, Leo, and Theo (Team 1) and Ryan, Dylan, Owen, and Luke (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Connor, Ethan, Owen, and Luke (Team 1) and Dylan, Ryan, Jack, and Mason (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
