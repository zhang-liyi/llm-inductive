BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in 8‑a‑side Ultimate Frisbee games and occasional 1‑on‑1 throwing contests. In each game, the winning team is determined by players’ intrinsic Ultimate skills (throwing accuracy, speed, catching) together with how much effort they put into that game.
Players compete either individually or as part of an 8‑player team.
All games take place on the same day.

The Comets usually consist of Adam, Ben, Chloe, Dylan, Emma, Felix, Grace, and Henry.
The Dragons usually consist of Iris, Jack, Katie, Liam, Maya, Noah, Olivia, and Parker.
The Phoenix usually consist of Quinn, Riley, Sara, Theo, Una, Violet, Will, and Zoe.
Luca is the only player who moves between teams.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Luca joined the Comets (replacing Henry), and the Comets beat the Dragons.
In the second game, Luca joined the Dragons (replacing Parker), and the Dragons beat the Phoenix.
In a later 1‑on‑1 throwing accuracy contest, Parker beat Luca.
