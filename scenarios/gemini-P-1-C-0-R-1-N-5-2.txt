BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Rugby Sevens matches. Each team consists of 7 athletes. In each match, the team that wins depends on the average speed and tackling strength of the athletes, based on their intrinsic athleticism modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete in fixed teams.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Team Alpha (containing Silas) lost to Team Bravo.
In the second match, Team Charlie beat Team Alpha (containing Silas).
In the third match, Team Bravo beat Team Charlie.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Silas ranks in terms of intrinsic athleticism?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Team Bravo put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Team Alpha (Team 1) and Team Charlie (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Team Bravo (Team 1) and Team Charlie (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
