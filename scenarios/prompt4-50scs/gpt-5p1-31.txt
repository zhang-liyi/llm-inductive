BACKGROUND
In this kayaking sprint regatta, athletes race over a straight course on flat water. Each race is decided by the speed of the kayak, which depends on the paddler’s intrinsic power and technique, shaped by how much effort they put into that race.
Athletes compete in single kayaks or in pairs.
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, Hugo beat Tessa.
In the second race, Omar and Tessa beat Hugo and Riley.
In the third race, Riley beat Omar.
In the fourth race, Hugo and Omar beat Tessa and Riley.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random kayak sprinters, where do you think Hugo ranks in terms of intrinsic power?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Tessa put into the second race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Hugo and Riley (Team 1) and Omar and Tessa (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Omar (Team 1) and Tessa (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
