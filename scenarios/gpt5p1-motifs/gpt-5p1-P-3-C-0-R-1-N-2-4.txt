BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are riding team pursuit races on an indoor track, with four cyclists per team. Each race is decided by the team’s overall pace, which depends on the riders’ intrinsic cycling power and aerodynamics, along with how much effort they put into that race. All pursuits take place on the same day. Teams are fixed and always use the same four riders.

CONDITIONS
Team Velocity consists of Aaron, Connor, Blake, and Jared.
Team Apex consists of Damian, Victor, Sean, and Kyle.
Team Horizon consists of Julian, Owen, Patrick, and Stuart.

In the first pursuit, Team Apex beat Team Velocity.
In the second pursuit, Team Horizon beat Team Velocity.
In the third pursuit, Team Velocity beat Team Horizon.
In the fourth pursuit, Team Apex beat Team Horizon.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random track cyclists, where do you think Aaron ranks in terms of intrinsic cycling ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Julian put into the third pursuit?
Query 3: In a new pursuit later this same day between Aaron, Connor, Blake, and Jared (Team 1) and Damian, Victor, Sean, and Kyle (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new pursuit later this same day between Julian, Owen, Patrick, and Stuart (Team 1) and a new team of four riders, Nate, Ellis, Morgan, and Grant (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
