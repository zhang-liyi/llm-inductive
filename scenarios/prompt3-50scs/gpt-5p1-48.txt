BACKGROUND  
In this event, the athletes are competing in lead sport climbing rounds on indoor routes. In each round, the climber or team that does better is the one whose underlying climbing ability—technique, strength, and efficiency—combined with how much effort they put into that round, allows them to climb higher or faster.  
Athletes compete individually and in teams where their performances are combined.  
All rounds take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS  
In the first round, Lina beat Noah.  
In the second round, Lina and Maya lost to Noah and Omar.  
In the third round, Maya beat Omar.

QUERIES  
Query 1: Out of 100 random sport climbers, where do you think Lina ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Lina put into the second round?  
Query 3: In a new round later this same day between Lina and Omar (Team 1) and Noah and Maya (Team 2), whose team would do better overall and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new individual round later this same day between Noah (Climber 1) and Maya (Climber 2), who would do better and by how much?
