BACKGROUND
In this event, three 5‑player volleyball teams play a series of rally‑scoring matches on an indoor court. In each match, the winner is determined by the players’ intrinsic volleyball ability (serving, passing, setting, attacking, blocking) together with how much sustained effort they put in during that match. Each player remains on the same team for all matches; teams are fixed.

CONDITIONS

QUERIES
