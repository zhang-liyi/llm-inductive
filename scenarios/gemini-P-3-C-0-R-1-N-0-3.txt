BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Table Tennis matches. In each match, the winning side is determined by the reaction time and spin control the athletes generate, based on their intrinsic coordination modulated by how much focus and effort they put into that specific match.
Athletes compete in fixed teams.
All matches take place in the same session.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Kenji and Ryu beat Daiki and Hiro.
In the second match, Kenji and Ryu lost to Daiki and Hiro.
In the third match, Kenji and Ryu lost to Daiki and Hiro.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Kenji ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Daiki put into the first match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Kenji and Daiki (Team 1) and Ryu and Hiro (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Ryu and Hiro (Team 1) and Kenji and Daiki (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
