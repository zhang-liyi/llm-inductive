BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of 7‑a‑side soccer matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the combined soccer ability of the seven players, based on their intrinsic skill (speed, control, awareness, etc.) and how much effort they put into that match.
All matches take place on the same day.
Some athletes appear on different teams across matches, but Diego always plays with the same six teammates.

QUERIES
