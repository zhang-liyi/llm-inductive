BACKGROUND
In this event, the players are competing in a series of small-sided 3v3 soccer matches on a reduced-size pitch. In each match, the winning team is determined by how effectively the players can control the ball, move, and finish chances, based on their intrinsic soccer ability combined with the effort they put into that match.
Players compete on three-player teams.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Maya, Liam, and Emma beat Noah, Chloe, and Ethan.  
In the second match, Maya, Noah, and Ava beat Liam, Chloe, and Emma.  
In the third match, Maya, Chloe, and Ethan lost to Liam, Noah, and Ava.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random soccer players, where do you think Maya ranks in terms of intrinsic soccer ability?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Liam put into the third match?  
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Maya, Liam, and Ava (Team 1) and Noah, Chloe, and Emma (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Maya, Noah, and Chloe (Team 1) and Liam, Ethan, and Ava (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
