BACKGROUND  
In this event, athletes compete in doubles badminton matches. Each match is decided by which pair can control rallies more effectively, based on their intrinsic speed, coordination, and shot quality along with the effort they put into that particular match.  
There is also the possibility of a singles match.  
Whenever players enter a doubles match, they always play with the same partner for this event.  
All matches take place on the same day.
