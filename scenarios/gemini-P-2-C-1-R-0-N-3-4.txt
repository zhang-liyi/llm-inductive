BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of American Football 5-on-5 drills. In each drill, the team that wins depends on their ability to execute plays and score touchdowns, based on their intrinsic strength and agility modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that drill.
Athletes compete in teams of 5.
All drills take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first drill, Tyrell, Marcus, Deon, Jamal, and Trey beat Chris, Kevin, Brian, David, and Eric.
In the second drill, Tyrell, Chris, Kevin, Brian, and David beat Marcus, Deon, Jamal, Trey, and Eric.
In the third drill, Tyrell, Marcus, Chris, Deon, and Kevin lost to Jamal, Trey, Brian, David, and Eric.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Tyrell ranks in terms of intrinsic strength and agility?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Tyrell put into the third drill?
Query 3: In a new drill later this same day between Tyrell, Marcus, Deon, Chris, and Kevin (Team 1) and Jamal, Trey, Brian, David, and Eric (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new drill later this same day between Marcus, Deon, Jamal, Trey, and Eric (Team 1) and Tyrell, Chris, Kevin, Brian, and David (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
