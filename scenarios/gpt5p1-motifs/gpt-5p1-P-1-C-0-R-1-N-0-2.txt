BACKGROUND
This event is a local tennis meet featuring both doubles and singles matches. Each result depends on the players’ intrinsic tennis ability (such as technique, coordination, and court movement) together with the effort they choose to exert in that particular match. Higher combined intrinsic ability and match effort produces better performance.
All matches occur on the same day.
Players always have fixed doubles partners: Jason always plays doubles with Nina, Marcus always plays doubles with Ella, and Victor always plays doubles with Chloe. Singles matches involve players competing alone.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Jason and Nina beat Marcus and Ella.
In the second match, Victor and Chloe beat Marcus and Ella.
In the third match, Jason and Nina beat Victor and Chloe.
In a singles match later that day, Jason beat Victor.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random tennis players, where do you think Jason ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Marcus put into the second match?
Query 3: In a new doubles match later this same day between Jason and Nina versus Marcus and Ella, who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new doubles match later this same day between Victor and Chloe versus Marcus and Ella, who would win and by how much?
