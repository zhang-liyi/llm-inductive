BACKGROUND
In this event, the players are competing in a series of half-court basketball scrimmages and 1-on-1 games. In each game, the result depends on each player's intrinsic basketball ability (shooting, ball-handling, defense) combined with how much effort they put in on that play. 
Players compete either in 1-on-1 games or in 5-player teams.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Jordan, Riley, Sam, Devon, and Mia beat Alex, Casey, Taylor, Morgan, and Blake.
In the second game, Jordan, Riley, Sam, Devon, and Mia beat Casey, Taylor, Morgan, Blake, and Quinn.
In the third game, Alex beat Taylor.
In the fourth game, Casey, Morgan, Quinn, Alex, and Blake lost to Taylor, Riley, Sam, Devon, and Mia.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random basketball players, where do you think Jordan ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Taylor put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Jordan, Riley, Sam, Devon, and Mia (Team 1) and Alex, Casey, Taylor, Morgan, and Blake (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Casey, Quinn, Blake, Morgan, and Taylor (Team 1) and Alex, Riley, Sam, Devon, and Mia (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
