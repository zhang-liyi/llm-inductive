CONDITIONS

In the third match, Liam beat Alex.  

QUERIES  
Query 1: Out of 100 random tennis players, where do you think Serena ranks in terms of intrinsic tennis ability?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Liam put into the third match?  
Query 3: In a new doubles match later this same day between Serena and Liam (Team 1) and Nora and Alex (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new singles match later this same day between Alex and Nora, who would win and by how much?
