BACKGROUND  
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of small‑sided Ultimate Frisbee games. In each game, the team that wins depends on how well the athletes can cut, throw, and catch, based on their intrinsic ability combined with how much effort they put into that game.  
Athletes compete either individually or in small teams.  
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS  
In the first game, Alex, Blake, and Casey beat Dana, Eli, and Fran.  
In the second game, Alex and Dana beat Blake and Eli.  
In the third game, Alex, Eli, and Fran beat Blake, Casey, and Dana.

QUERIES  
Query 1: Out of 100 random Ultimate players, where do you think Alex ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Blake put into the third game?  
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Alex and Fran (Team 1) and Blake and Casey (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Dana and Eli (Team 1) and Alex and Casey (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
