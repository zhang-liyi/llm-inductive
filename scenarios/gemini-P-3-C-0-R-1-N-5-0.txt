BACKGROUND

The winning team is determined by the collective speed, throwing accuracy, and defensive agility of the players, modulated by the effort they exert during the match. The teams are fixed throughout the day.
Liam plays for the "Falcons," and they are playing a series against the "Vipers."
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS

In the second match, the Vipers beat the Falcons.
In the third match, the Falcons beat the Vipers.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Liam ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?

Query 3: In a new match later this same day between the Falcons (Team 1) and the Vipers (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
