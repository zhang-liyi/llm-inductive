BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of ultimate frisbee games. In each game, the outcome depends on players’ intrinsic ultimate frisbee ability together with how much effort they put into that game. Athletes sometimes play individually and sometimes as part of 8‑player teams.

Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Hugo put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game under the same conditions between Tariq, Alice, Raya, Sam, Tia, Dev, Finn, and Gia (Team 1) and Hugo, Iris, Jay, Kim, Leo, Mira, Nate, and Opal (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
