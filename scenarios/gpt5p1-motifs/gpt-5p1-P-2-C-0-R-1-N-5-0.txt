BACKGROUND
In this event, three mixed-gender teams are playing a short ultimate frisbee round-robin. Each game is 7-on-7. The outcome of a game depends on the players’ intrinsic speed, throwing accuracy, catching consistency, and endurance, combined with how much effort they decide to put in for that game.
All games take place on the same day.
There are three fixed teams of seven whose rosters do not change:
- Team Aurora: Lena, Priya, Jonah, Felix, Carmen, Omar, Tessa.
- Team Borealis: Riley, Noah, Sasha, Devin, Marco, Haley, Quinn.
- Team Comet: Iris, Leo, Mateo, Nina, Victor, Zoe, Colin.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Aurora beat Borealis.
In the second game, Aurora beat Comet.
In the third game, Comet beat Borealis.
In the fourth game, Comet beat Aurora.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random ultimate frisbee players, where do you think Lena ranks in terms of intrinsic playing ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Quinn put into the first game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Aurora and Borealis, who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Borealis and Comet, who would win and by how much?
