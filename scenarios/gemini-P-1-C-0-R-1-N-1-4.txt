BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Rugby Sevens training drills (3v3). In each drill, the team that wins depends on the ground gained and tries scored, based on the athletes' physical strength and tackling ability, modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that drill.
Athletes compete either individually (1-on-1 tackling) or as a fixed team of three.
All drills take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first drill, The Warriors (Richie, Dan, and Beauden) beat The Knights (Ewan, Gareth, and Rhys).
In the second drill, The Crusaders (Jonny, Owen, and Maro) beat The Knights (Ewan, Gareth, and Rhys).
In the third drill, Richie beat Ewan.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Ewan ranks in terms of intrinsic physical strength and tackling ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Ewan put into the third drill?
Query 3: In a new drill later this same day between Richie and Jonny (Team 1) and Ewan and Gareth (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new drill later this same day between Dan and Owen (Team 1) and Rhys and Maro (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
