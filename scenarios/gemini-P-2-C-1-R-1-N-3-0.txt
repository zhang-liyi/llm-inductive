BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes compete in full-court Basketball games. The winning team is determined by the collective shooting accuracy and defensive capabilities of the 5 players on the court, based on their intrinsic skill levels modulated by the effort they exert during the specific game.
Teams consist of 5 players.
All games take place during a single tournament weekend.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Jordan and the Red Team beat the Blue Team.
In the second game, Jordan and the Blue Team beat the Red Team.
In the third game, Jordan and the Red Team lost to the Blue Team.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random basketball players, where do you think Jordan ranks in terms of intrinsic skill?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Jordan put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Jordan and the Blue Team (Team 1) and the Red Team (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between the Red Team (Team 1) and the Blue Team (Team 2) without Jordan playing, who would win and by how much?
