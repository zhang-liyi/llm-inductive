BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes are competing in a series of Field Hockey matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the average stick-work and speed of the players, based on their intrinsic coordination modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete in teams of 7.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, the team of Sarah, Tina, Una, Val, Wendy, Xena, and Yara lost to the team of Abby, Bea, Cara, Deb, Elle, Fran, and Gwen.
In the second match, the team of Sarah, Tina, Una, Val, Wendy, Xena, and Yara lost to the team of Abby, Bea, Cara, Hope, Ivy, Jen, and Kim.
In the third match, the team of Sarah, Tina, Una, Val, Wendy, Xena, and Yara beat the team of Deb, Elle, Fran, Hope, Ivy, Jen, and Kim.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Sarah ranks in terms of intrinsic coordination?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Sarah put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between the team of Sarah, Tina, Una, Val, Wendy, Xena, and Yara (Team 1) and the team of Abby, Bea, Cara, Deb, Elle, Fran, and Gwen (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between the team of Deb, Elle, Fran, Hope, Ivy, Jen, and Kim (Team 1) and the team of Sarah, Tina, Una, Val, Wendy, Xena, and Yara (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
