BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are playing a series of full-court basketball scrimmages. In each scrimmage, the team that wins depends on the players’ intrinsic basketball ability (shooting, ball-handling, speed, decision-making) combined with how much effort they put in that game.
Players compete either individually or in teams of 6.
All games take place on the same day.

Later that day, Maya beat Noah.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random basketball players, where do you think Maya ranks in terms of intrinsic basketball ability?

Query 3: In a new scrimmage later this same day between Maya, Leo, Priya, Sam, Jordan, and Elena (Team 1) and Noah, Chris, Victor, Kim, Diego, and Riley (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
