BACKGROUND
In this triathlon, athletes complete a swim, bike, and run in sequence. Their overall placing in each event is determined by total time, which depends on their intrinsic endurance and speed, influenced by how much effort they choose to expend across the disciplines.
All athletes compete individually.
All triathlons take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the morning triathlon, Priya beat Jonah.
In the second triathlon, held a few hours later, Jonah beat Leah.
In the third triathlon, Leah beat Priya.
In the fourth triathlon, Marcus beat Jonah, Priya, and Leah.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random triathletes, where do you think Priya ranks in terms of intrinsic endurance?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Jonah put into the second triathlon?
Query 3: In a new triathlon later this same day between Priya (Team 1) and Jonah (Team 2), who would finish ahead and by how much?
Query 4: In a new triathlon later this same day between Leah (Team 1) and Marcus (Team 2), who would finish ahead and by how much?
