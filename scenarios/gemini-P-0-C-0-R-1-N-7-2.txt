BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes are competing in a series of cricket matches. Each side fields 9 players for this specific format. The winner of each match depends on the total runs scored and wickets taken, which is a function of the athletes' intrinsic cricketing ability modulated by factors including the effort they put into that match.
The team rosters are fixed.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, The Titans (captained by Ravi) beat The Warriors.
In the second match, The Titans (captained by Ravi) beat The Knights.
In the third match, The Titans (captained by Ravi) beat The Royals.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random cricket players, where do you think Ravi ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think The Royals put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between The Titans (captained by Ravi) and The Warriors, who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between The Warriors and The Knights, who would win and by how much?
