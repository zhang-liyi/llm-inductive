BACKGROUND  
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of lacrosse games. In each game, the outcome depends on the players’ intrinsic speed, coordination, and shooting accuracy, combined with the level of effort they put in during that game.  
Athletes compete in 1-on-1 situations and in small-sided teams.  
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS  
In the first game, Casey beat Rowan.  
In the second game, Casey and Drew beat Rowan and Sky.  
In the third game, Drew and Sky beat Casey and Rowan.

QUERIES  
Query 1: Out of 100 random lacrosse athletes, where do you think Casey ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Rowan put into the third game?  
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Casey and Sky (Team 1) and Rowan and Drew (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Casey and Rowan (Team 1) and Drew and Taylor (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
