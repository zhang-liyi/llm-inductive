BACKGROUND
In this event, the swimmers are competing in individual races and four-person relays. Each race is decided by the swimmers’ intrinsic speed and endurance in the water, together with how much effort they put into that race.
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, Chloe lost to Hannah.
In the second race, Chloe, Maya, Lila, and Erin lost to Hannah, Zoe, Nina, and Kate.
In the third race, Chloe, Zoe, Lila, and Tom lost to Hannah, Maya, Nina, and Erin.
In the fourth race, Chloe, Nina, Kate, and Tom beat Hannah, Zoe, Maya, and Lila.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random swimmers, where do you think Chloe ranks in terms of intrinsic swimming ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Hannah put into the fourth race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Chloe, Hannah, Maya, and Tom (Team 1) and Zoe, Nina, Lila, and Erin (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Chloe, Zoe, Nina, and Erin (Team 1) and Hannah, Maya, Lila, and Kate (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
