BACKGROUND

The outcome of each contest is determined by players’ intrinsic Ultimate skills (such as throwing precision, sprint speed, and catching ability) and the amount of effort they choose to invest in that contest.
Athletes compete either individually or in teams of 6.
All contests take place on the same day.
