BACKGROUND
In this event, the skaters are competing in a series of figure skating programs. In each program, the winner is decided by overall quality, which depends on each skater’s intrinsic technical and artistic ability, along with how much effort and focus they invest in that specific performance.
Skaters perform either individually or in pairs/teams.
All programs take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first program, Julia beat Ben.
In the second program, Julia and Claire beat Ben and David.
In the third program, Ben and Claire beat Julia and Emma.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random figure skaters, where do you think Claire ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Julia put into the third program?
Query 3: In a new program later this same day between Julia and Ben (Team 1) and Claire and David (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new program later this same day between Ben and Emma (Team 1) and Julia and Liam (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
