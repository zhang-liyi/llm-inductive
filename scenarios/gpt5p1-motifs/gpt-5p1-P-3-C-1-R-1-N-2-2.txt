BACKGROUND  
In this small-sided soccer event, players are competing in short matches. Match outcomes depend on intrinsic skills like ball control, shooting, and speed, combined with the effort they put into each match.  
Players compete either in 4-a-side games or occasionally in 1-on-1 shootout contests.  
Most players remain in fixed 4-player squads, but Diego is rotated through different lineups.  
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS  
In the first match, Marco, Luis, Bruno, and Rafa beat Diego, Carlo, Enzo, and Felix.  
In the second match, Omar, Nico, Pavel, and Tariq beat Diego, Felix, Hugo, and Jonas.  
In a 1-on-1 penalty shootout later, Diego beat Felix.  
In the last match, Marco, Luis, Bruno, and Rafa beat Diego, Carlo, Hugo, and Jonas.

QUERIES  
Query 1: Out of 100 random soccer players, where do you think Diego ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Felix put into the 1-on-1 penalty shootout against Diego?  
Query 3: In a new 4-a-side match later this same day between Diego, Carlo, Enzo, and Felix (Team 1) and Marco, Luis, Bruno, and Rafa (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new 4-a-side match later this same day between Diego, Felix, Hugo, and Jonas (Team 1) and Omar, Nico, Pavel, and Tariq (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
