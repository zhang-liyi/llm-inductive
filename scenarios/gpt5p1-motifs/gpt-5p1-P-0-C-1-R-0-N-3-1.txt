BACKGROUND
In this event, the players are competing in a series of 5-a-side indoor soccer matches. In each match, the winning team is determined by the players’ intrinsic soccer ability (ball control, speed, shooting, positioning) combined with the level of effort they put into that match.  
Players compete either individually (in penalty shootout duels) or as part of a 5-player team.  
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Mia, Carla, Dani, Rosa, and Ivy beat Lara, Noor, Priya, Tessa, and Val.  
In the second match, Mia, Lara, Dani, Noor, and Quinn beat Carla, Ivy, Priya, Rosa, and Sam.  
In the third match, Mia beat Priya in a penalty shootout.  
In the fourth match, Mia, Sam, Tessa, Val, and Quinn beat Dani, Carla, Ivy, Lara, and Noor.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random soccer players, where do you think Mia ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Priya put into the third match?  
Query 3: In a new 5-a-side match later this same day between Mia, Carla, Noor, Tessa, and Quinn (Team 1) and Dani, Ivy, Priya, Rosa, and Val (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Lara and Rosa (a penalty shootout duel), who would win and by how much?
