BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a local ultimate frisbee tournament. Each team plays with 9 athletes on the field. The outcome of each game depends on the players’ intrinsic throwing, catching, and running ability, as well as the effort they put in.
Most players are on stable squads: Aurora, Blitz, and Cyclones. Marcus is a flexible player who sometimes joins different teams. Athletes can also compete in individual one‑on‑one cutting and marking drills.
All contests occur on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Aurora with Marcus in the lineup lost to Blitz.
In the second game, Cyclones with Marcus in the lineup lost to Aurora’s regular lineup.
In the third game, Blitz beat Cyclones using their regular squads without Marcus.
In a one‑on‑one cutting drill, Marcus lost to Jonah from Aurora.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random ultimate frisbee players, where do you think Marcus ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Marcus put into the first game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Blitz and Aurora with Marcus added to Aurora, who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Blitz and Cyclones with Marcus added to Cyclones, who would win and by how much?
