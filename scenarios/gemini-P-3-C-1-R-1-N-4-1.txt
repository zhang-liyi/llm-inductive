BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of ice hockey games. Each team consists of 6 players on the ice. In each game, the team that wins depends on the average skating speed and puck handling with which the athletes are able to play, based on their intrinsic coordination modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that game.
Athletes compete as part of a team.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Morgan and the Ice Breakers lost to the Puck Masters.
In the second game, Morgan and the Puck Masters lost to the Ice Breakers.
In the third game, Morgan and the Winter Wolves beat the Arctic Foxes.
In the fourth game, Morgan and the Arctic Foxes lost to the Winter Wolves.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Morgan ranks in terms of intrinsic coordination?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think the Winter Wolves put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between the Ice Breakers (Team 1) and the Arctic Foxes (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between the Puck Masters (Team 1) and the Winter Wolves (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
