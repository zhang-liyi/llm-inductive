BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of American Football games (5v5 Flag). In each game, the team that wins depends on the average speed and route-running ability of the athletes, based on their intrinsic agility modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that game.
Athletes compete as a team of 5.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Isaac, Gabriel, Anthony, Dylan, and Luke beat Connor, Wyatt, Carter, Jayden, and Julian.
In the second game, Isaac, Gabriel, Anthony, Connor, and Wyatt beat Dylan, Luke, Carter, Jayden, and Julian.
In the third game, Julian, Isaac, Gabriel, Anthony, and Dylan beat Luke, Connor, Wyatt, Carter, and Jayden.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Julian ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Julian put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Julian, Isaac, Gabriel, Anthony, and Connor (Team 1) and Dylan, Luke, Wyatt, Carter, and Jayden (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Julian, Dylan, Luke, Carter, and Jayden (Team 1) and Isaac, Gabriel, Anthony, Connor, and Wyatt (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
