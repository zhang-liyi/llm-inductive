BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of volleyball matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the average spike power and reaction time of the athletes, based on their intrinsic ability modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete as a team of three.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Elena, Mia, and Zoe lost to Ava, Harper, and Lily.
In the second match, Elena, Ava, and Harper lost to Mia, Zoe, and Lily.
In the third match, Elena, Lily, and Mia beat Ava, Harper, and Zoe.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Lily ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Elena put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Lily, Mia, and Zoe (Team 1) and Elena, Ava, and Harper (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Elena, Lily, and Ava (Team 1) and Mia, Zoe, and Harper (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
