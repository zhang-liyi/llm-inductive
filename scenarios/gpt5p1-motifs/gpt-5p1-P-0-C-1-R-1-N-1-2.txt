BACKGROUND
In this event, the players are competing in a series of 3-on-3 small-sided soccer games on a short field. The winning team in each game is determined by the players’ intrinsic soccer abilities (control, passing, shooting, speed) combined with how much effort they put into that game.
Teams are of size three.
Most players stay in the same trios for the day, but Leo moves between different teammates in different games.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Leo, Omar, and Priya beat Riley, Sara, and Tom.
In the second game, Leo, Quinn, and Uma beat Riley, Sara, and Tom.
In the third game, Leo, Vince, and Will beat Omar, Priya, and Quinn.
In the fourth game, Leo beat Tom.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random soccer players, where do you think Leo ranks in terms of intrinsic soccer ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Omar put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Leo, Priya, and Uma (Team 1) and Riley, Sara, and Vince (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Omar, Priya, and Quinn (Team 1) and Riley, Sara, and Tom (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
