BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of 3-on-3 half-court basketball games. In each game, the team that wins depends on the players’ intrinsic skills such as shooting accuracy, quickness, and ball-handling, combined with how much effort they put in on offense and defense in that particular game.
All games are played on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Alex, Ben, and Chris beat Riley, Dana, and Eva.  
In the second game, Dana, Eva, and Finn beat Riley, Alex, and Ben.  
In the third game, Alex, Ben, and Chris beat Riley, Finn, and Taylor.  
In a later one-on-one game, Chris beat Riley.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random basketball players, where do you think Riley ranks in terms of intrinsic basketball ability?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Chris put into the third game?  
Query 3: In a new 3-on-3 game later this same day between Alex, Ben, and Chris (Team 1) and Dana, Eva, and Finn (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new 3-on-3 game later this same day between Riley, Taylor, and Max (Team 1) and Alex, Ben, and Chris (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
