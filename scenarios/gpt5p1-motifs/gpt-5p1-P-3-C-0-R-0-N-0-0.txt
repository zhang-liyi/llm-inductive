BACKGROUND

In each match, the team that wins depends on how consistently the players can execute serves, returns, and smashes, based on their intrinsic reflexes, coordination, and tactical sense, modulated by how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete either individually or as a doubles team.
All matches take place on the same day.
