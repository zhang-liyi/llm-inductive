BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a 5-on-5 flag American Football tournament. In each game, the team that wins depends on the points scored, based on the athletes' intrinsic agility and route-running ability, modulated by other factors including the physical effort they put into that game.
Athletes compete in fixed teams of 5.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, the Eagles (Jalen, DeVonta, AJ, Fletcher, and Jason) beat the Ducks (Chester, Bill, Bob, Ben, and Barry).

In the third game, the Niners (Brock, Christian, Deebo, George, and Nick) beat the Ducks (Chester, Bill, Bob, Ben, and Barry).

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Chester ranks in terms of intrinsic agility and route-running ability?

Query 3: In a new game later this same day between the Ducks (Team 1) and the Eagles (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between the Ducks (Team 1) and the Chiefs (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
