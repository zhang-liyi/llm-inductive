BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are playing 3-on-3 ultimate frisbee games on a short field. The outcome of each game depends on the teams’ offensive and defensive effectiveness, determined by each player’s intrinsic ultimate skills (throwing, cutting, marking) and the effort they put into that game.
Players compete as part of fixed three-player lines, and occasionally in individual contests.
All games take place on the same day.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random ultimate frisbee players, where do you think Noah ranks in terms of intrinsic ultimate ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Noah put into the second game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Noah, Caleb, and Jonah (Line 1) and Blake, Casey, and Morgan (Line 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Avery, Dylan, and Reese (Line 1) and Blake, Casey, and Morgan (Line 2), who would win and by how much?
