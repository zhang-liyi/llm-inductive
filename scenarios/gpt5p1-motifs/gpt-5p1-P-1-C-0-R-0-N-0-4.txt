BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in tennis doubles and singles matches. In each match, the winning side depends on the players’ intrinsic shot precision, movement, and stamina, together with how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete either individually or in doubles teams.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first doubles match, Nora and Pavel beat Theo and Isla.
In the second doubles match, Theo and Isla lost to Rina and Omar.
In the third doubles match, Pavel and Rina beat Theo and Isla.
In a singles match, Nora beat Omar.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random tennis players, where do you think Rina ranks in terms of intrinsic skill?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Theo put into the third doubles match?
Query 3: In a new doubles match later this same day between Nora and Rina (Team 1) and Pavel and Omar (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new doubles match later this same day between Theo and Isla (Team 1) and Nora and Pavel (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
