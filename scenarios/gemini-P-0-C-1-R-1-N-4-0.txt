BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes compete in a series of Volleyball matches. Each team consists of 6 players. The team that wins depends on the collective jumping power and hitting accuracy of the players, based on their intrinsic athleticism modulated by the effort they exert during the set.
There are three fixed squads: The Spikers, The Blockers, and The Diggers. One star athlete, Rowan, rotates between these squads.
All matches occur on the same weekend.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, The Spikers (featuring Rowan) beat The Blockers (led by Sam).
In the second match, The Diggers (featuring Rowan) beat The Spikers (led by Taylor).
In the third match, The Blockers (featuring Rowan) beat The Diggers (led by Chris).

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random volleyball players, where do you think Rowan ranks in terms of intrinsic athleticism?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Chris put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between The Spikers (featuring Rowan) and The Diggers (led by Chris), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between The Blockers (led by Sam) and The Spikers (led by Taylor), who would win and by how much?
