BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Tennis matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the combined serve speed and forehand accuracy of the athletes, based on their intrinsic technique modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete either individually or as a team of two.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Roger beat Felix.
In the second match, Roger and Novak beat Felix and Rafa.
In the third match, Felix and Novak beat Roger and Rafa.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Felix ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Felix put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Felix and Rafa (Team 1) and Roger and Andy (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Roger and Novak (Team 1) and Rafa and Stan (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
