BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Beach Volleyball matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the combined defensive coverage and attacking power of the athletes, based on their intrinsic athleticism modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that match.
Athletes compete as a team of two.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Jordan and Sam beat Alex and Chris.
In the second match, Jordan and Alex beat Sam and Chris.
In the third match, Jordan and Chris beat Sam and Alex.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Jordan ranks in terms of intrinsic athleticism?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Sam put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Jordan and Sam (Team 1) and Alex and Chris (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Sam and Alex (Team 1) and Chris and a new player, Taylor (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
