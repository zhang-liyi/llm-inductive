BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes are competing in Ice Hockey games. Each team fields 6 skaters (including the goalie) at a time. The winning team is determined by the average skating speed and puck handling of the lineup, based on intrinsic skill modulated by the physical effort put into the periods.
The league has fixed teams: The Frost, The Blades, and The Glaciers. An elite player, Quinn, plays as a substitute for different teams throughout the day.
All games are played back-to-back.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, The Frost (featuring Quinn) beat The Blades (led by Alex).
In the second game, The Glaciers (featuring Quinn) beat The Frost (led by Jordan).
In the third game, The Blades (featuring Quinn) beat The Glaciers (led by Casey).

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random hockey players, where do you think Quinn ranks in terms of intrinsic skill?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Casey put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between The Frost (featuring Quinn) and The Blades (led by Alex), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between The Glaciers (led by Casey) and The Frost (led by Jordan), who would win and by how much?
