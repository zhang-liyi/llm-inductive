BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of beach volleyball matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the combined agility and spiking power of the athletes, based on their intrinsic athletic ability modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete as a team of two.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Leo and Sam beat Tom and Ray.
In the second match, Leo and Tom lost to Sam and Ray.
In the third match, Leo and Ray lost to Sam and Tom.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Leo ranks in terms of intrinsic athletic ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Tom put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Leo and Sam (Team 1) and Tom and Ray (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Leo and Ray (Team 1) and Sam and Tom (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
