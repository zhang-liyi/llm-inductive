BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes are competing in Lacrosse scrimmages played 8-on-8. The winning team is the one that scores more points, based on the players' intrinsic coordination and athleticism, modulated by the effort they put into the scrimmage.
There are two core units of players, with two designated "floaters" who switch teams.
All scrimmages take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first scrimmage, Unit 1 (Ash, Baz, Cid, Dax, Emo, Fox, Gil) and Kit beat Unit 2 (Huc, Ian, Jax, Kip, Lex, Max, Nix) and **Reese**.
In the second scrimmage, Unit 2 and Kit beat Unit 1 and **Reese**.
In the third scrimmage, Unit 2 and **Reese** beat Unit 1 and Kit.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think **Reese** ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Kit put into the third scrimmage?
Query 3: In a new scrimmage later this same day between Unit 1 and Kit (Team 1) and Unit 2 and **Reese** (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new scrimmage later this same day between a team of Ash, Baz, Cid, Huc, Ian, Jax, Kit, and **Reese** (Team 1) and a team of Dax, Emo, Fox, Gil, Kip, Lex, Max, and Nix (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
