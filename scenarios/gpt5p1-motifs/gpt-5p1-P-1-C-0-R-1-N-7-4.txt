BACKGROUND
In this event, three 9-a-side field hockey teams play several matches. Each match outcome depends on the players’ intrinsic stick skills, speed, balance, decision-making, and the level of effort they exert in that match.
Teams have fixed rosters of 9 players that remain constant throughout the day.
Team Glacier: Erik, Jonas, Lukas, Henrik, Emil, Tobias, Simon, Oskar, Martin.
Team Ember: Clara, Sofia, Lina, Johanna, Freja, Elsa, Maja, Karin, Tilde.
Team Storm: Nadia, Farah, Yasmin, Layla, Samira, Huda, Amal, Rania, Salma.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Ember beat Storm.
In the second match, Glacier beat Storm.
In the third match, Ember beat Storm again.
In the fourth match, Glacier beat Storm again.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random field hockey players, where do you think Nadia ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Layla put into the fourth match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Ember and Glacier, who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Storm and Glacier, who would win and by how much?
