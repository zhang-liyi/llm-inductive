BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of small-sided field hockey games. In each game, the team that wins depends on the stick-handling and tactical movement of the athletes, based on their intrinsic talent modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that game.
Athletes compete as a team of four.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Ravi, Amit, Singh, and Raj beat Rohan, Vikram, Arjun, and Karan.
In the second game, Ravi, Amit, Singh, and Raj lost to Rohan, Vikram, Kabir, and Dev.
In the third game, Ravi, Amit, Singh, and Raj lost to Arjun, Karan, Kabir, and Dev.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Ravi ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Kabir put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Ravi, Amit, Singh, and Raj (Team 1) and Rohan, Vikram, Kabir, and Dev (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Rohan, Vikram, Arjun, and Karan (Team 1) and Arjun, Karan, Kabir, and Dev (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
