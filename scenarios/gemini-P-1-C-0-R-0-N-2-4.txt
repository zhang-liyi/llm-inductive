BACKGROUND
In this competition, athletes are swimming in 4x100m freestyle relays. The winning team is determined by the total time taken, which relies on the intrinsic swimming speed of the athletes modulated by their form and the effort they put into the race.
Athletes compete in teams of 4.
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, the team of Greg, Hank, Ian, and Jon beat the team of Cody, Drew, Eli, and Finn.
In the second race, the team of Greg, Hank, Kyle, and Leo beat the team of Cody, Drew, Eli, and Finn.
In the third race, the team of Ian, Jon, Kyle, and Leo beat the team of Cody, Drew, Eli, and Finn.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Cody ranks in terms of intrinsic swimming speed?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Kyle put into the third race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Cody, Drew, Eli, and Finn (Team 1) and Greg, Ian, Kyle, and Matt (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Greg, Hank, Ian, and Jon (Team 1) and Kyle, Leo, Matt, and Nick (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
