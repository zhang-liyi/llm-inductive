BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes compete in 5-on-5 Ice Hockey scrimmages. The winning team depends on the aggregate skating speed and puck handling of the squad, based on intrinsic ability and the effort exerted during the scrimmage.
The athletes are divided into two main squads, with one floater player.
All scrimmages occur in one afternoon.

CONDITIONS
In the first scrimmage, The Blades (Jack, Ken, Leo, Mike, and Nick) beat The Pucks (Oscar, Pete, Ray, Stan, and Ted).
In the second scrimmage, The Pucks beat The Blades (with Val replacing Jack).
In the third scrimmage, The Blades beat The Pucks (with Val replacing Oscar).

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Val ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Jack put into the first scrimmage?
Query 3: In a new scrimmage later this same day between The Blades (with Val replacing Mike) and The Pucks, who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new scrimmage later this same day between The Pucks (with Val replacing Ray) and The Blades (with Val replacing Ken), who would win and by how much?
