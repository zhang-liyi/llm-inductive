BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Tennis matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the quality of their returns and serves, based on their intrinsic power and stamina modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that match.
Athletes compete as a team of two.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Serena and Roger beat Arthur and Gwen.
In the second match, Roger and Novak beat Arthur and Gwen.
In the third match, Serena and Novak beat Arthur and Gwen.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Serena ranks in terms of intrinsic power and stamina?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Arthur put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Serena and Roger (Team 1) and Arthur and a new player named Tim (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Arthur and Gwen (Team 1) and Novak and a new player named Mary (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
