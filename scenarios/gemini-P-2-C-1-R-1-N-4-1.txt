BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Ice Hockey scrimmages. Each team consists of 6 players (5 skaters and 1 goalie). In each game, the team that wins depends on the average skating speed and puck handling of the athletes, based on their intrinsic skill modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that game.
There are three established squads: The Jets, The Sharks, and The Bears. A star player, Sergei, plays for different squads throughout the day.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, The Jets (with Sergei) beat The Sharks.
In the second game, The Bears (with Sergei) beat The Jets.
In the third game, The Sharks (with Sergei) lost to The Bears.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Sergei ranks in terms of intrinsic skill?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Sergei put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between The Jets (with Sergei) and The Bears, who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between The Sharks (without Sergei) and The Jets (without Sergei), who would win and by how much?
