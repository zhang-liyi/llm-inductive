BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of 5-on-5 basketball games, with occasional individual shooting contests. In each game, the team that wins depends on the players’ intrinsic shooting accuracy, ball-handling, defensive ability, and stamina, combined with how much effort they put into that game.
Athletes compete either individually or as a team of five.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Liam, Noah, Ava, Mia, and Zoe beat Jonah, Caleb, Emma, Lucas, and Ryan.

In the third game, Liam, Ava, Mia, Emma, and Mason beat Jonah, Noah, Zoe, Caleb, and Harper.
In a later shooting contest, Liam beat Jonah.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random basketball players, where do you think Noah ranks in terms of intrinsic playing ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Jonah put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Jonah, Ava, Zoe, Harper, and Mason (Team 1) and Liam, Noah, Mia, Emma, and Lucas (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Noah, Emma, Ryan, Harper, and Lucas (Team 1) and Liam, Ava, Mia, Zoe, and Caleb (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
