BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of small‑sided soccer games. Each team fields 9 players. The result of each game depends on the combined intrinsic soccer ability of the players on the field and how much effort they put into that particular game.
Players usually stay with their regular team squads: the Hawks, the Tigers, and the Wolves. One player, Diego, is a floating substitute who sometimes joins different teams. Players can also compete in individual penalty shootout duels.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, the Hawks with Diego in the lineup lost to the Tigers.
In the second game, the Wolves with Diego in the lineup lost to the Hawks.
In the third game, the Tigers beat the Wolves using their regular squads without Diego.
In a penalty shootout later that day, Diego lost to Maya from the Hawks.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random soccer players, where do you think Diego ranks in terms of intrinsic soccer ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Diego put into the second game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between the Tigers and the Hawks with Diego in the Hawks’ lineup again, who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between the Wolves with Diego in their lineup and the Tigers, who would win and by how much?
