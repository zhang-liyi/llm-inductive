BACKGROUND

Teams consist of fixed squads of seven players, and each player always plays on the same team whenever they appear.
All matches take place on the same day.

In the first match, Leah’s team beat Omar’s team.  
In the second match, Leah’s team beat Priya’s team.  
In the third match, Priya’s team beat Omar’s team.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random seven-a-side soccer players, where do you think Leah ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Omar put into the first match?  
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Leah’s team (Team 1) and Omar’s team (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Priya’s team (Team 1) and Omar’s team (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
