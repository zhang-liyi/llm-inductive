BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Ultimate Frisbee matches played on a smaller field. In each match, the team that wins depends on the collective speed, agility, and throwing accuracy of the players, based on their intrinsic athleticism modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete as a team of four.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Zara, Mila, Aria, and Luna beat Hazel, Violet, Aurora, and Savannah.
In the second match, Zara, Mila, Aria, and Luna beat Hazel, Violet, Stella, and Bella.
In the third match, Zara, Mila, Aria, and Luna beat Aurora, Savannah, Stella, and Bella.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Zara ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Hazel put into the second match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Zara, Mila, Aria, and Luna (Team 1) and Hazel, Aurora, Stella, and Bella (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Violet, Savannah, Stella, and Bella (Team 1) and Aurora, Hazel, Ivy, and Willow (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
