BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes are competing in a series of Ultimate Frisbee matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the collective scoring ability and defensive coordination of the players, based on their intrinsic athleticism modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete in fixed teams of four.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Liam, Noah, Oliver, and Elijah beat James, William, Ben, and Lucas.
In the second match, Liam, Noah, Oliver, and Elijah beat James, William, Ben, and Lucas.
In the third match, James, William, Ben, and Lucas lost to Liam, Noah, Oliver, and Elijah.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Liam ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think James put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Liam, Noah, Oliver, and Elijah (Team 1) and James, William, Ben, and Lucas (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Liam, Noah, James, and William (Team 1) and Oliver, Elijah, Ben, and Lucas (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
