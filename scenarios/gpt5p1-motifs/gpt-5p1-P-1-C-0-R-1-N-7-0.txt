BACKGROUND
In this event, three 9-a-side soccer teams play a series of short matches. Each match outcome depends on the players’ intrinsic soccer skill (touch, vision, speed, endurance) combined with how much effort they put into that specific match.
Teams have fixed rosters of 9 players that never change throughout the day.
Team Aurora: Diego, Liam, Noah, Ethan, Mason, Logan, Aiden, Lucas, Caleb.
Team Blaze: Harper, Ella, Chloe, Grace, Lily, Zoey, Riley, Aria, Nora.
Team Comet: Jack, Henry, Samuel, Owen, Wyatt, Carter, Jayden, Matthew, Daniel.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Blaze beat Aurora.
In the second match, Comet beat Aurora.
In the third match, Blaze beat Aurora again.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random soccer players, where do you think Diego ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Liam put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Blaze and Comet, who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Aurora and Comet, who would win and by how much?
