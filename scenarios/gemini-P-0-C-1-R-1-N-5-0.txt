BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes compete in an Ultimate Frisbee tournament. Teams consist of 7 players. The winning team is determined by the collective speed and throwing accuracy of the players, based on their intrinsic agility modulated by the effort they exert during the match.
Most players belong to fixed squads (The Flyers, The Divers, The Huckers, The Hammers), but one player, Jordan, rotates between teams.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, The Flyers (with Jordan) beat The Divers.
In the second match, The Huckers (with Jordan) beat The Hammers.
In the third match, The Divers (with Jordan) beat The Flyers.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Jordan ranks in terms of intrinsic agility?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think The Flyers put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between The Huckers (with Jordan) and The Divers, who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between The Flyers and The Hammers, who would win and by how much?
