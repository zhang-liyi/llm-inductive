BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes are competing in a Rugby Sevens tournament. In each match, the team that wins depends on the combined speed and physicality of the players, based on their intrinsic strength modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that specific match.
Athletes compete in teams of 7.
All matches take place on the same day.
