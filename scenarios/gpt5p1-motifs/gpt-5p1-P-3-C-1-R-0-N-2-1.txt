BACKGROUND
In this event, the rowers are competing in a series of races in single sculls and in four-person boats. In each race, the result depends on the rowers’ intrinsic power and technique, combined with how much effort they put into that particular race.
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, Ethan lost to Colin.
In the second race, Ethan, Mia, Luca, and Zoe lost to Colin, Tara, Ben, and Nadia.
In the third race, Ethan, Tara, Ben, and Mia beat Colin, Luca, Zoe, and Nadia.
In the fourth race, Ethan, Nadia, Zoe, and Leo lost to Colin, Mia, Ben, and Tara.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random rowers, where do you think Ethan ranks in terms of intrinsic rowing strength and technique?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Colin put into the third race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Ethan, Colin, Mia, and Leo (Team 1) and Tara, Luca, Zoe, and Ben (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Ethan, Nadia, Tara, and Ben (Team 1) and Colin, Mia, Luca, and Zoe (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
