BACKGROUND
In this event, the players are competing in a series of small-sided soccer games. The result of each game depends on players’ intrinsic ball control, speed, decision-making, and endurance, together with the intensity of effort they apply in that game.
Players compete either individually against a goalkeeper or as a team.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Nia beat Felix.
In the second game, Nia, Oliver, Samir, and Tessa beat Felix, Cara, Julian, and Ben.
In the third game, Oliver, Cara, Ben, and Nia beat Felix, Samir, Julian, and Tessa.
In the fourth game, Julian beat Felix.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Felix ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Cara put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Nia, Cara, Julian, and Samir (Team 1) and Felix, Oliver, Ben, and Tessa (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Julian and Nia (Team 1) and Felix and Ben (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
