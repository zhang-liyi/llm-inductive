BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of field hockey matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the running endurance and ball control of the six players, based on their intrinsic strength modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete in teams of six.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, the team of Jim, Kev, Len, Mat, Ned, and Ole beat the team of Pam, Sue, Val, Wyn, Zoe, and Ann.
In the second match, the team of Pam, Sue, Val, Wyn, Zoe, and Ann beat the team of Jim, Kev, Len, Mat, Ned, and Skyler.
In the third match, the team of Jim, Kev, Len, Mat, Ned, and Ole beat the team of Pam, Sue, Val, Wyn, Zoe, and Skyler.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Jim ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Skyler put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between the team of Jim, Kev, Len, Mat, Ned, and Ole (Team 1) and the team of Pam, Sue, Val, Wyn, Zoe, and Skyler (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between a team of Jim, Kev, Pam, Sue, Len, and Mat (Team 1) and a team of Ned, Skyler, Val, Wyn, Zoe, and Ann (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
