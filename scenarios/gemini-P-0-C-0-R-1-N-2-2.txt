BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of 4x100m Swimming relays. In each race, the team that wins depends on the cumulative speed of the swimmers, based on their intrinsic technique and power modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that race.
Athletes compete in fixed teams of four.
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, Ethan, Alexander, Henry, and Jackson beat Sebastian, Jack, Owen, and Theodore.
In the second race, Ethan, Alexander, Henry, and Jackson beat Sebastian, Jack, Owen, and Theodore.
In the third race, Sebastian, Jack, Owen, and Theodore lost to Ethan, Alexander, Henry, and Jackson.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Ethan ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Sebastian put into the third race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Ethan, Alexander, Henry, and Jackson (Team 1) and Sebastian, Jack, Owen, and Theodore (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Ethan, Alexander, Sebastian, and Jack (Team 1) and Henry, Jackson, Owen, and Theodore (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
