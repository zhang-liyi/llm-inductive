BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes compete in a baseball tournament. Each team consists of 9 players. The team that wins a game is determined by the collective batting and fielding average of the players, based on their intrinsic baseball skills modulated by factors such as the effort they put into that specific game.
The teams are fixed throughout the tournament.
All games are played on the same weekend.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, The Rockets (featuring Elias) beat The Giants.
In the second game, The Rockets (featuring Elias) beat The Marlins.
In the third game, The Rockets (featuring Elias) beat The Dodgers.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random baseball players, where do you think Elias ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think The Dodgers put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same weekend between The Rockets (featuring Elias) and The Giants, who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same weekend between The Giants and The Marlins, who would win and by how much?
