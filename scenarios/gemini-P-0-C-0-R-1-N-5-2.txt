BACKGROUND
In this competition, athletes participate in Rugby Sevens matches. As the name implies, there are 7 players per team. The outcome of each match depends on the speed and physical power of the squad (intrinsic capability), influenced by their stamina and the effort they apply during the halves.
The rosters are fixed, and athletes do not switch teams.
All matches are played consecutively on the same field.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Jonah's squad beat the Highlanders.
In the second match, Jonah's squad beat the Islanders.
In the third match, Jonah's squad beat the Coastal team.

QUERIES

Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Jonah's squad and the City team, who would win and by how much?
