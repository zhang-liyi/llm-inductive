BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of sprint track relays (4x100m). In each race, the team that wins depends on the speed with which the athletes complete the track, based on their intrinsic sprinting ability modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that race.
Athletes compete as a team of 4.
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, Lila, Rose, Ivy, and Fern beat Hazel, Jade, Amber, and Opal.
In the second race, Lila, Hazel, Jade, and Amber beat Rose, Ivy, Fern, and Opal.
In the third race, Hazel, Jade, Amber, and Fern beat Lila, Opal, Rose, and Ivy.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Lila ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Lila put into the third race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Lila, Rose, Ivy, and Fern (Team 1) and Hazel, Jade, Amber, and Opal (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Hazel, Jade, Amber, and Fern (Team 1) and Lila, Opal, Rose, and Ivy (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
