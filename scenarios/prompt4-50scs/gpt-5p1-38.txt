BACKGROUND
In this event, judoka are competing in a series of judo matches. In each match, the winner depends on throwing technique, gripping skill, balance, and timing, along with how much concentrated effort and persistence they put into that match.  
Athletes compete individually or in teams of three, where the overall team performance decides the winner.  
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Sora beat Hana.  
In the second match, Sora and Ken beat Hana and Riku.  
In the third match, Ken, Riku, and Mei beat Sora, Hana, and Taro.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random judo athletes, where do you think Sora ranks in terms of intrinsic technical skill?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Ken put into the third match?  
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Sora, Ken, and Mei (Team 1) and Hana, Riku, and Taro (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Mei (Team 1) and Riku (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
