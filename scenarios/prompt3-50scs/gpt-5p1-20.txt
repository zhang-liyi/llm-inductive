BACKGROUND
In this event, the cyclists are competing in a series of road cycling races on a closed circuit. In each race, the rider or team that wins is determined by their average speed, which depends on each cyclist’s intrinsic endurance and power, along with how much effort they choose to put into that race.
Cyclists compete either individually or in teams.
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, Iris beat Jonah.
In the second race, Iris and Kyla beat Jonah and Leo.
In the third race, Leo and Iris lost to Jonah and Kyla.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random road cyclists, where do you think Iris ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Leo put into the third race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Iris and Jonah (Team 1) and Leo and Mila (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Leo and Jonah (Team 1) and Kyla and Noah (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
