BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in beach volleyball matches in teams of two. In each match, the winning team is determined by the players’ intrinsic ball control, jumping ability, and reaction speed, along with how much effort they put into that match.
All matches are played as 2-on-2.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Lucas and Mia beat Fatima and Hana.
In the second match, Jonah and Lucas beat Fatima and Hana.
In the third match, Mia and Zoe beat Fatima and Hana.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random beach volleyball players, where do you think Lucas ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Hana put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Lucas and Zoe (Team 1) and Jonah and Mia (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Fatima and Hana (Team 1) and Jonah and Zoe (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
