BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of team cycling time trials. In each race, the team that wins depends on the average speed the athletes can maintain, based on their intrinsic leg strength and stamina modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that race.
Athletes compete in fixed teams of three.
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, Mason, Logan, and Lucas beat Liam, Noah, and Ethan.
In the second race, Liam, Noah, and Ethan beat Mason, Logan, and Lucas.
In the third race, Mason, Logan, and Lucas beat Liam, Noah, and Ethan.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Liam ranks in terms of intrinsic leg strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Liam put into the second race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Liam and Logan (Team 1) and Mason and Noah (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Ethan and Lucas (Team 1) and Noah and Mason (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
