BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Tennis matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on their court coverage and shot precision, based on their intrinsic speed and strength modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete as a team of two.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Emma and Olivia lost to Ava and Sophia.
In the second match, Emma and Olivia lost to Ava and Mia.
In the third match, Emma and Olivia beat Sophia and Mia.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Emma ranks in terms of intrinsic speed and strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Emma put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Emma and Olivia (Team 1) and Ava and Harper (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Ava and Sophia (Team 1) and Olivia and Mia (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
