BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are playing a series of small-sided soccer games. In each game, the team that wins depends on the players’ intrinsic ball control, speed, and decision-making, combined with how much effort they put in during that game.
Athletes compete either individually (in penalty shootout duels) or in 6-player teams.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Priya, Lucas, Emma, Noah, Sofia, and Amir beat Chloe, Diego, Hana, Isaac, Lila, and Marcus.
In the second game, Priya, Chloe, Diego, Noah, Lila, and Marcus beat Lucas, Emma, Sofia, Amir, Hana, and Isaac.
In the third game, Priya, Hana, Isaac, Emma, Sofia, and Lila beat Lucas, Diego, Chloe, Noah, Amir, and Marcus.
In the fourth game, Priya beat Diego in a penalty shootout.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random soccer players, where do you think Priya ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Diego put into the penalty shootout in the fourth game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Priya, Lucas, Diego, Hana, Isaac, and Zoe (Team 1) and Emma, Sofia, Amir, Chloe, Lila, and Marcus (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Lucas, Emma, Hana, Isaac, Lila, and Marcus (Team 1) and Priya, Noah, Sofia, Amir, Chloe, and Diego (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
