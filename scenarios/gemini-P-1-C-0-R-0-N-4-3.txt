BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes are competing in Lacrosse Sixes matches. The winning team is decided by the collective goal-scoring ability and defensive maneuvering of the six players, based on their intrinsic athleticism modulated by the effort they commit to the match.
Athletes compete in teams of six.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, the North Squad beat the South Squad (consisting of Arthur, Luke, Han, Chewy, Lando, and Wedge).
In the second match, the East Squad beat the South Squad (consisting of Arthur, Luke, Han, Chewy, Lando, and Wedge).
In the third match, the South Squad (consisting of Arthur, Luke, Han, Chewy, Lando, and Wedge) lost to the West Squad.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Arthur ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think the West Squad put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between the North Squad (Team 1) and the South Squad (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between the East Squad (Team 1) and the West Squad (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
