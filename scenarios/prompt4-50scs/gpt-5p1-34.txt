BACKGROUND
In this powerlifting meet, athletes compete in the squat, bench press, and deadlift, and their result in each round is based on the combined total of their best lifts. Outcomes depend on their intrinsic maximal strength and control, along with how much effort they choose to exert and how aggressively they attempt heavier lifts.
Athletes lift individually and in small team totals.
All rounds take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first round, Carla beat Ethan.
In the second round, Ethan beat Jamie.
In the third round, Carla and Jamie beat Ethan and Victor.
In the fourth round, Victor beat Carla.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random powerlifters, where do you think Carla ranks in terms of intrinsic maximal strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Victor put into the fourth round?
Query 3: In a new round later this same day between Carla and Ethan (Team 1) and Jamie and Victor (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new round later this same day between Ethan (Team 1) and Victor (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
