BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of 3-on-3 basketball games. In each game, the team that wins depends on the average scoring ability and defensive skill of the athletes, based on their intrinsic talent modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that game.
Athletes compete as fixed teams of three, with one optional 1-on-1 matchup.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Kobe, Shaq, and Pau beat Jordan, Mike, and Scott.
In the second game, Kobe, Shaq, and Pau beat Jordan, Mike, and Scott.
In the third game, Jordan, Mike, and Scott beat Kobe, Shaq, and Pau.
In the fourth game, Kobe beat Jordan.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Jordan ranks in terms of intrinsic talent?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Jordan put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Jordan and Shaq (Team 1) and Kobe and Mike (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Scott and Pau (Team 1) and Mike and Shaq (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
