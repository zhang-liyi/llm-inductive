BACKGROUND  
In this event, the players are playing small-sided soccer games on a reduced field, 2 versus 2. Each game’s outcome depends on the players’ intrinsic ball control, speed, and finishing ability, modulated by how much effort and work rate they bring into that game.  
All games are played on the same day.

CONDITIONS  
In the first game, Nadia and Chloe lost to Riley and Sam.  
In the second game, Nadia and Emma lost to Riley and Sam.  
In the third game, Nadia and Brooke lost to Leo and Max.

QUERIES  
Query 1: Out of 100 random small-sided soccer players, where do you think Riley ranks in terms of intrinsic soccer ability?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Nadia put into the second game?  
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Nadia and Sam (Team 1) and Riley and Emma (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Chloe and Brooke (Team 1) and Leo and Max (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
