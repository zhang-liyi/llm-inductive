BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Lacrosse (Sixes format) matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the number of goals scored, based on the athletes' intrinsic stick skills and speed modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete in teams of 6.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, the team of Parker, Ash, Bob, Cal, Dom, and Eli beat the team of Fay, Gus, Hal, Ian, Jon, and Ken.
In the second match, the team of Parker, Fay, Gus, Hal, Ian, and Jon beat the team of Ash, Bob, Cal, Dom, Eli, and Ken.
In the third match, the team of Parker, Ash, Fay, Bob, Gus, and Cal lost to the team of Dom, Eli, Hal, Ian, Jon, and Ken.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Parker ranks in terms of intrinsic stick skills and speed?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Parker put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between the team of Parker, Ash, Bob, Cal, Dom, and Eli (Team 1) and the team of Fay, Gus, Hal, Ian, Jon, and Ken (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between the team of Parker, Fay, Gus, Hal, Ian, and Jon (Team 1) and the team of Ash, Bob, Cal, Dom, Eli, and Ken (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
