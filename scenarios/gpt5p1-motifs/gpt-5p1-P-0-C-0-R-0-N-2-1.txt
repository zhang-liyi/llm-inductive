BACKGROUND

All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Priya beat Marco.
In the second match, Priya and Lena beat Marco and Zoe.
In the third match, Priya and Lena beat Ben and Dan.
In the fourth match, Zoe and Dan beat Marco and Ben.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random players, where do you think Priya ranks in terms of intrinsic tennis ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Marco put into the second match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Priya and Lena (Team 1) and Zoe and Ben (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Marco and Dan (Team 1) and Zoe and Ben (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
