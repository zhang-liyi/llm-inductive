BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of small-sided soccer matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the number of goals scored, which is a result of the ball control and tactical awareness of the athletes, based on their intrinsic talent modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete either individually or as a team of three.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Liam beat Cristiano.
In the second match, Liam, Noah, and Oliver beat Cristiano, Gareth, and Karim.
In the third match, Liam, Noah, and Oliver beat Cristiano, Luka, and Sergio.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Liam ranks in terms of intrinsic talent?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Cristiano put into the second match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Liam, Noah, and Oliver (Team 1) and Gareth, Luka, and Karim (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Cristiano, Noah, and Gareth (Team 1) and Liam, Karim, and Sergio (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
