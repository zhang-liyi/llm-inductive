BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes compete in Ultimate Frisbee matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the collective cutting speed and throwing accuracy of the players, based on their intrinsic athleticism modulated by factors such as how much effort they put into that specific match.
Athletes compete in teams of 7.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Jace, Cole, Finn, Wes, Ian, Ray, and Leo beat Seth, Ben, Dan, Rob, Art, Gil, and Mat.
In the second match, Jace, Cole, Finn, Wes, Ian, Ray, and Leo beat Seth, Ben, Dan, Kip, Ned, Oly, and Pat.
In the third match, Jace, Cole, Finn, Wes, Ian, Ray, and Leo beat Rob, Art, Gil, Kip, Ned, Oly, and Pat.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Jace ranks in terms of intrinsic athleticism?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Pat put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Jace, Cole, Finn, Wes, Ian, Ray, and Leo (Team 1) and Seth, Ben, Dan, Rob, Art, Gil, and Kip (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Seth, Ben, Dan, Rob, Art, Gil, and Mat (Team 1) and Kip, Ned, Oly, Pat, and three new average players (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
