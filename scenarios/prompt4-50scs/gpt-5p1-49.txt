BACKGROUND  
In this event, the athletes are competing in bouldering rounds. On each set of problems, climbers try to complete as many boulders as possible in limited attempts. A climber or team “beats” another if they complete more problems or the same number with fewer attempts. Performance depends on intrinsic power, technique, and problem-solving, along with how much effort they spend in that round.  
Climbers compete both individually and in small teams of 3, where the team score is based on the combined performance of all members.  
All rounds take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS  
In the first round, Kai beat Rowan.  
In the second round, Rowan beat Tessa.  
In the third round, Kai, Tessa, and Uma beat Rowan, Vince, and a substitute.  
In the fourth round, Vince beat Uma.

QUERIES  
Query 1: Out of 100 random boulderers, where do you think Kai ranks in terms of intrinsic climbing ability?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Rowan put into the second round?  
Query 3: In a new round later this same day between Kai, Uma, and a substitute (Team 1) and Rowan, Tessa, and Vince (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new round later this same day between Tessa and Uma (Team 1) and Rowan and a strong substitute (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
