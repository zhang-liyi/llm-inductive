BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Tennis matches. In each match, the winner is determined by the average precision and speed of the players' shots, based on their intrinsic skill level modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete either individually or as a fixed doubles team.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Roger beat Novak.
In the second match, Roger and Rafa beat Novak and Andy.
In the third match, Roger and Rafa lost to Novak and Andy.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Roger ranks in terms of intrinsic skill level?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Roger put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Roger and Rafa (Team 1) and Novak and a new partner, Stan (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Novak and Andy (Team 1) and Rafa and Stan (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
