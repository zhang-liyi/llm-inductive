BACKGROUND  
In this event, the players are competing in a series of 3-on-3 half-court basketball games. In each game, the team that wins depends on the players’ intrinsic basketball skill (shooting, ball-handling, decision-making, defense) combined with how much effort they put in during that game.  
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS  
In the first game, Liam, Noah, and Ethan lost to Marco, Ryan, and Jake.  
In the second game, Liam, Marco, and Tyler lost to Noah, Ava, and Ethan.  
In the third game, Liam, Ryan, and Chloe lost to Jake, Tyler, and Ava.

QUERIES  
Query 1: Out of 100 random basketball players, where do you think Marco ranks in terms of intrinsic skill?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Liam put into the second game?  
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Noah, Jake, and Tyler (Team 1) and Marco, Ethan, and Ava (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Liam, Marco, and Ryan (Team 1) and Ethan, Jake, and Chloe (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
