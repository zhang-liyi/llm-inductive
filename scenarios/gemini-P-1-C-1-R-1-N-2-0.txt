BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Ultimate Frisbee matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the collective scoring ability of the athletes, based on their intrinsic speed and throwing accuracy modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete in teams of 4.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, The Hawks (Tim, Sal, Dan, and Ben) beat The Eagles (Jon, Ray, Mike) and Rory.
In the second match, The Crows (Ned, Art, Cal, and Vin) beat The Owls (Pat, Gil, Wes) and Rory.
In the third match, The Eagles (Jon, Ray, Mike, and Ben) beat The Hawks (Tim, Sal, Dan) and Rory.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Rory ranks in terms of intrinsic speed and throwing accuracy?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Rory put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between The Eagles (Jon, Ray, Mike) and Ben (Team 1) and The Hawks (Tim, Sal, Dan) and Rory (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between The Owls (Pat, Gil, Wes) and Vin (Team 1) and The Crows (Ned, Art, Cal) and Rory (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
