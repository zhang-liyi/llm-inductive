BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes are competing in small-sided soccer games, played 8-versus-8. The winner is the team that scores the most goals, determined by the players' intrinsic technical skill and tactical awareness, modulated by their current effort levels.
The players are divided into two main training groups, with two specific players swapping sides for assessment.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, the "Reds" (Jon, Ken, Len, Mat, Nat, Oli, Pat) and Sam beat the "Blues" (Ray, Tim, Uli, Vic, Wes, Xav, Yan) and **Dakota**.
In the second game, the "Blues" and Sam beat the "Reds" and **Dakota**.
In the third game, the "Blues" and **Dakota** beat the "Reds" and Sam.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think **Dakota** ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Sam put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between the "Reds" and Sam (Team 1) and the "Blues" and **Dakota** (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between a team of Jon, Ken, Len, Ray, Tim, Uli, Sam, and **Dakota** (Team 1) and a team of Mat, Nat, Oli, Pat, Vic, Wes, Xav, and Yan (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
