BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Ultimate Frisbee matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the average speed and throwing precision of the athletes, based on their intrinsic athleticism modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete either individually or as a team of three.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Sarah, Ben, and Chloe lost to Dave, Earl, and Fiona.
In the second match, Sarah, Dave, and Earl lost to Ben, Chloe, and Fiona.
In the third match, Sarah beat Fiona.
In the fourth match, Sarah, Fiona, and Ben lost to Dave, Earl, and Chloe.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Chloe ranks in terms of intrinsic athleticism?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Sarah put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Chloe, Ben, and Fiona (Team 1) and Sarah, Dave, and Earl (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Sarah, Chloe, and Dave (Team 1) and Ben, Earl, and Fiona (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
