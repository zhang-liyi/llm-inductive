BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of beach volleyball matches between fixed six-player teams. For each team, the same six athletes always play together. The team that wins a match depends on the players’ intrinsic beach volleyball ability and how much effort they put into that match. All matches take place on the same day.

Team Tides: Maya, Zoe, Lily, Hannah, Clara, Beth.  
Team Blaze: Ryan, Leo, Jack, Omar, Felix, Noah.  
Team Drift: Ava, Chloe, Emma, Sarah, Nina, Paige.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Team Blaze beat Team Tides.  
In the second match, Team Drift beat Team Tides.  
In the third match, Team Blaze beat Team Tides again.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random beach volleyball players, where do you think Maya ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Ryan put into the first match?  
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Team Blaze and Team Drift, who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Team Tides and Team Drift, who would win and by how much?
