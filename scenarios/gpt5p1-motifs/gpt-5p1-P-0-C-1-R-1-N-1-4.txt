BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of 3-on-3 volleyball games. The winning team in each game is determined by the players’ intrinsic volleyball abilities (serving, passing, setting, attacking) combined with how much effort they put into that game.
Teams are of size three.
Most players remain in fixed trios for the day, but Carlos moves between different lineups across games.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Carlos, Liam, and Noah beat Peter, Quinn, and Ryan.
In the second game, Carlos, Oliver, and Sean beat Peter, Quinn, and Ryan.
In the third game, Carlos, Tara, and Uma beat Liam, Noah, and Oliver.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random volleyball players, where do you think Carlos ranks in terms of intrinsic volleyball ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Liam put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Carlos, Noah, and Sean (Team 1) and Peter, Quinn, and Oliver (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Liam, Noah, and Oliver (Team 1) and Peter, Quinn, and Ryan (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
