BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Badminton matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the combined agility and shot precision of the athletes, based on their intrinsic skill level modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that specific match.
Athletes compete either individually or as a team of two.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Kaito beat Haruto.
In the second match, Kaito and Ren beat Haruto and Yuto.
In the third match, Kaito and Ren lost to Yuto and Riku.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Kaito ranks in terms of intrinsic skill level?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Ren put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Kaito and Ren (Team 1) and Haruto and Daiki (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Haruto and Yuto (Team 1) and Ren and Sora (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
