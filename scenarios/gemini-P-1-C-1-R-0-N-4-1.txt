BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Volleyball sets. In each set, the team that wins depends on the average vertical leap and reaction time of the athletes, based on their intrinsic athleticism modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that set.
Athletes compete in teams of 6.
All sets take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first set, the team of Emma, Ava, Sophia, Isabella, Mia, and Charlotte beat the team of Amelia, Harper, Evelyn, Abigail, Emily, and Jamie.
In the second set, the team of Amelia, Harper, Evelyn, Abigail, Emily, and Charlotte beat the team of Emma, Ava, Sophia, Isabella, Mia, and Jamie.
In the third set, the team of Emma, Amelia, Ava, Harper, Sophia, and Evelyn beat the team of Isabella, Abigail, Mia, Emily, Charlotte, and Jamie.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Jamie ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Charlotte put into the third race?
Query 3: In a new set later this same day between Team 1 (Emma, Ava, Sophia, Isabella, Mia, Charlotte) and Team 2 (Amelia, Harper, Evelyn, Abigail, Emily, Jamie), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new set later this same day between Team 1 (Emma, Amelia, Sophia, Evelyn, Mia, Emily) and Team 2 (Ava, Harper, Isabella, Abigail, Charlotte, Jamie), who would win and by how much?
