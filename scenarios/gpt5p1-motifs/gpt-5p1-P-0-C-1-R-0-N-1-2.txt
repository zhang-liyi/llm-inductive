BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are playing a series of 3-on-3 small-sided soccer matches on a short field. The team that wins each match depends on the players’ intrinsic soccer ability (ball control, passing, finishing, and agility), modulated by how much effort they put in during that match.
Players compete either individually or on 3-player teams.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Diego, Sara, and Lukas beat Mei, Aaron, and Fatima.
In the second match, Diego, Mei, and Joel beat Sara, Aaron, and Lukas.
In the third match, Diego, Fatima, and Aaron beat Mei, Joel, and Sara.
In the fourth match, Diego beat Lukas.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random soccer players, where do you think Diego ranks in terms of intrinsic soccer ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Sara put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Diego, Sara, and Mei (Team 1) and Lukas, Aaron, and Joel (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Fatima, Mei, and Aaron (Team 1) and Diego, Lukas, and Casey (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
