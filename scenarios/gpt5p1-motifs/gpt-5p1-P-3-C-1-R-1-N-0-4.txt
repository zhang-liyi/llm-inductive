BACKGROUND
In this rowing event, the athletes are racing in pairs. For each race, the winning boat is determined by the rowers’ intrinsic rowing strength and technique, together with how much effort they put into that particular race.

Most rowers stay in fixed pairs, while Tomas changes partners between races.
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, Tomas and Igor lost to Ben and Carl.
In the second race, Tomas and Viktor lost to Elias and Jonah.
In the third race, Tomas and Luka lost to Ben and Carl.
In the fourth race, Tomas and Igor beat Elias and Jonah.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random rowers, where do you think Tomas ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Igor put into the fourth race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Tomas and Viktor (Team 1) and Ben and Carl (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Luka and Igor (Team 1) and Elias and Jonah (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
