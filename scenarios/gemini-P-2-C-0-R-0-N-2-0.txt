BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Ultimate Frisbee matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the collective speed and throwing accuracy of the athletes, based on their intrinsic athleticism modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete in teams of four.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Jordan, Sam, Riley, and Casey beat Alex, Jamie, Drew, and Taylor.
In the second match, Jordan, Sam, Riley, and Casey beat Alex, Jamie, Morgan, and Quinn.
In the third match, Jordan, Sam, Riley, and Casey lost to Drew, Taylor, Morgan, and Quinn.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Jordan ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Jordan put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Jordan, Sam, Riley, and Casey (Team 1) and Alex, Jamie, Drew, and Taylor (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Drew, Taylor, Morgan, and Quinn (Team 1) and Alex, Jamie, Riley, and Casey (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
