BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Badminton matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the reaction time and court coverage the athletes are able to maintain, based on their intrinsic reflexes modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that match.
Athletes compete as a team of two.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Chen and Lee beat Alex and Lin.
In the second match, Lin and Wei beat Alex and Chen.
In the third match, Chen and Lee beat Alex and Wei.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Alex ranks in terms of intrinsic reflexes?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Alex put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Alex and Lin (Team 1) and Chen and Wei (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Lin and Lee (Team 1) and Chen and Wei (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
