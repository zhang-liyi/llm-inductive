BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Pickleball matches. In each match, the winner is determined by the placement accuracy and hand speed of the athletes, based on their intrinsic coordination modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete either individually or as a fixed doubles team.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Ben beat Tyson.
In the second match, Ben and Anna beat Tyson and Catherine.
In the third match, Ben and Anna lost to Tyson and Catherine.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Ben ranks in terms of intrinsic coordination?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Ben put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Ben and Anna (Team 1) and Tyson and a new partner, Lucy (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Tyson and Catherine (Team 1) and Anna and Lucy (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
