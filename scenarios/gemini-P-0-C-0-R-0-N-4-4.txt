BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of American Football (6-man variant) games. In each game, the team that wins depends on the tactical execution and physical strength of the athletes, based on their intrinsic ability modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that game.
Athletes compete as a team of 6.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Jamal, Deon, Andre, Brian, Chris, and David beat Eddie, Frank, Gary, Harry, Isaac, and Jack.
In the second game, Jamal, Deon, Eddie, Frank, Gary, and Harry beat Andre, Brian, Chris, David, Isaac, and Jack.
In the third game, Jamal, Deon, Isaac, Jack, Andre, and Brian beat Eddie, Frank, Gary, Harry, Chris, and David.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Jamal ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Eddie put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Jamal, Deon, Chris, David, Isaac, and Jack (Team 1) and Andre, Brian, Eddie, Frank, Gary, and Harry (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Andre, Brian, Eddie, Frank, Gary, and Harry (Team 1) and Chris, David, Isaac, Jack, Jamal, and Deon (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
