BACKGROUND
In this event, players are taking part in a one-day 6-on-6 ultimate frisbee tournament. The outcome of each game depends on the players’ intrinsic ultimate frisbee ability and the effort they put in during that game. Athletes can compete either individually in throwing contests or as part of 6-player teams.

The Skyhawks are a fixed team consisting of Aaron, Bella, Chloe, Daniel, Eva, and Felix. The Thunder are a fixed team consisting of Gina, Henry, Iris, Jason, Karen, and Leo. Mateo appears in several games but does not have a fixed team, instead joining different groups of players.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, the Skyhawks beat the Thunder.  
In the second game, Mateo, Nora, Olivia, Paige, Quinn, and Rosa lost to the Skyhawks.  
In the third game, Mateo, Sam, Tara, Victor, Wyatt, and Zoe lost to the Thunder.  
In an individual distance-throw accuracy contest, Mateo beat Jason.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random ultimate frisbee players, where do you think Mateo ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Eva put into the first game?  
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Mateo, Nora, Olivia, Paige, Quinn, and Rosa (Team 1) and Gina, Henry, Iris, Jason, Karen, and Leo (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Aaron, Bella, Chloe, Daniel, Eva, and Felix (Team 1) and Mateo, Sam, Tara, Victor, Wyatt, and Gina (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
