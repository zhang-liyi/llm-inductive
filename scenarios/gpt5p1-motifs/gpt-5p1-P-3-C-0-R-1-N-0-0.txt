BACKGROUND
In this beach volleyball event, athletes compete in pairs. The outcome of each match depends on the players’ intrinsic beach volleyball ability (serving, passing, setting, attacking, and defense) combined with how much effort they put into that particular match.
All matches take place on the same day.
Teams are fixed pairs of two players.

CONDITIONS

In the second match, Maya and Jordan lost to Nia and Rui.
In the third match, Maya and Jordan beat Lina and Carlos.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random beach volleyball players, where do you think Jordan ranks in terms of intrinsic playing ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Carlos put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Maya and Jordan (Team 1) and Nia and Rui (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Lina and Carlos (Team 1) and Nia and Rui (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
