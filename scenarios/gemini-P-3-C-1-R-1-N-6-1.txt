BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes are competing in Ultimate Frisbee matches played 8-on-8. The winning team is decided by the score limit, reached faster by the team with higher average agility and throwing skill, modulated by the effort put into the match.
Most players remain on fixed lines (Line A and Line B), while two players float between them.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Line A (composed of Art, Ben, Cal, Dan, Eli, Fay, Gus) and Val beat Line B (composed of Hal, Ivy, Jay, Kay, Leo, Mia, Ned) and **Rory**.
In the second match, Line B and Val beat Line A and **Rory**.
In the third match, Line A and **Rory** beat Line B and Val.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think **Rory** ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Val put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Line A and Val (Team 1) and Line B and **Rory** (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between a team of Art, Ben, Cal, Hal, Ivy, Jay, Val, and **Rory** (Team 1) and a team of Dan, Eli, Fay, Gus, Kay, Leo, Mia, and Ned (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
