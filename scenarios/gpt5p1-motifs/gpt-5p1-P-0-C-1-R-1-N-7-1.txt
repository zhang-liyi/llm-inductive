BACKGROUND

Each match is decided by how well the players execute their skills, determined by their intrinsic volleyball ability (such as jumping, coordination, and ball control) combined with how much effort they put into that match.
Matches are either 1 on 1 skill duels (such as serve‑receive drills) or full matches between two squads of 9 players rotating on and off the court.
All matches take place on the same day.
Most players stay with one squad, but Ravi is invited to play for different squads.
