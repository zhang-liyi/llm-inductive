BACKGROUND
In this event, the players are competing in a series of five-a-side soccer matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the players’ intrinsic ball control, speed, and finishing ability, combined with how much effort and intensity they bring to that match. Players compete either individually in short duels or in teams of five. All matches take place on the same day.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random soccer players, where do you think Lucia ranks in terms of intrinsic soccer ability?
