BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes are competing in a 7-a-side Soccer tournament. The team that wins depends on the number of goals scored, based on the intrinsic dribbling skills and vision of the athletes, modulated by the running effort they put into the match.
Most players stay with their designated clubs (City FC, United FC, Rovers FC, Athletic FC), but Reese is a wildcard player who joins different teams.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, City FC (with Reese) beat United FC.
In the second match, Rovers FC (with Reese) beat Athletic FC.
In the third match, United FC (with Reese) beat City FC.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Reese ranks in terms of intrinsic dribbling skills?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think City FC put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Rovers FC (with Reese) and United FC, who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between City FC and Athletic FC, who would win and by how much?
