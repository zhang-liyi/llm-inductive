BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Ultimate Frisbee matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the average agility and throwing accuracy of the athletes, based on their intrinsic athleticism modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete in fixed teams of three.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Chris, Pat, and Taylor beat Riley, Sam, and Alex.
In the second match, Riley, Sam, and Alex beat Chris, Pat, and Taylor.
In the third match, Chris, Pat, and Taylor beat Riley, Sam, and Alex.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Riley ranks in terms of intrinsic athleticism?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Riley put into the second match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Riley and Pat (Team 1) and Chris and Sam (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Alex and Taylor (Team 1) and Sam and Pat (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
