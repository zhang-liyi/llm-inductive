BACKGROUND
In this event, players are competing in a series of ultimate frisbee games. In each game, the outcome depends on their intrinsic ultimate skills (throwing accuracy, cutting, speed, catching) and how much effort they put into that game.
Players compete either individually in skill contests or in 9‑a‑side ultimate games.
There are three teams—the Comets, the Horizon, and the Pulse—with generally fixed 9‑player rosters. Only one player, Lucas, occasionally plays for different teams in different games.
All contests occur on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first contest, a throwing accuracy duel, Lucas beat Adrian.
In the second game, Lucas and the Comets beat the Horizon.
In the third game, Lucas and the Horizon beat the Pulse.
In the fourth game, Lucas and the Pulse lost to the Comets.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random ultimate frisbee players, where do you think Lucas ranks in terms of intrinsic ultimate ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Adrian put into the first contest?
Query 3: In a new 9‑a‑side game later this same day between Lucas and the Comets (Team 1) and Adrian and the Horizon (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new 9‑a‑side game later this same day between Lucas and the Horizon (Team 1) and Serena and the Pulse (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
