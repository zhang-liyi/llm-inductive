BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Basketball games. In each game, the team that wins depends on the total points scored, which is a function of the athletes' intrinsic shooting and defensive abilities modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that specific game.
Athletes compete in teams of 5.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Marcus, Jay, Dan, Phil, and Rob beat Sam, Tom, Ben, Ken, and Ray.
In the second game, Marcus, Jay, Dan, Phil, and Rob beat Sam, Tom, Lou, Vin, and Cal.
In the third game, Marcus, Jay, Dan, Phil, and Rob lost to Ben, Ken, Lou, Vin, and Cal.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Marcus ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Marcus put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Marcus, Jay, Dan, Phil, and Rob (Team 1) and Sam, Tom, Ben, Ken, and Lou (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Ben, Ken, Lou, Vin, and Cal (Team 1) and Ray, Sam, Tom, Jay, and Dan (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
