BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of beach volleyball sets. In each set, the team that wins depends on the average power and agility of the athletes, based on their intrinsic physical ability modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that set.
Athletes compete as a team of three.
All sets take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first set, Karch, Misty, and Kerri beat Logan, Zhu, and Giba.
In the second set, Karch, Misty, and Kerri lost to Logan, Giba, and Lang.
In the third set, Karch, Misty, and Kerri beat Zhu, Lang, and Kim.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Karch ranks in terms of intrinsic physical ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Misty put into the second set?
Query 3: In a new set later this same day between Karch, Misty, and Kerri (Team 1) and Logan, Lang, and April (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new set later this same day between Logan, Zhu, and Kim (Team 1) and Giba, Lang, and April (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
