BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes are competing in a series of Basketball games. In each game, the team that wins depends on the collective scoring and defensive efficiency of the players, based on their intrinsic athleticism and skill modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that specific game.
Athletes compete in teams of 5.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, The Vipers beat Jordan, Mike, Dan, Sam, and Rob.
In the second game, The Cobras beat Jordan, Eric, Tom, Wes, and Ian.
In the third game, Jordan, Greg, Leo, Nick, and Pat beat The Pythons.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Jordan ranks in terms of intrinsic athleticism?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Mike put into the first game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Jordan, Mike, Dan, Sam, and Rob (Team 1) and Jordan, Greg, Leo, Nick, and Pat (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between The Vipers (Team 1) and The Cobras (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
