BACKGROUND
This event is a day-long Ultimate Frisbee showcase featuring 7-on-7 games and occasional 1v1 throwing challenges. Performance in each game depends on the athletes’ intrinsic abilities in running, cutting, catching, and throwing, along with the effort they choose to exert in that specific game. Most players stay with their regular squads (the Comets, Rockets, and Meteors), but one versatile handler, Sofia, is allowed to switch between teams. All games are played on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Sofia and the Comets beat the Rockets.
In the second game, Sofia and the Rockets beat the Meteors.
In the third contest, Sofia lost to Dana in a 1v1 long-throw accuracy challenge.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random Ultimate Frisbee players, where do you think Sofia ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Dana put into the third contest?
Query 3: In a new 7-on-7 game later this same day between Sofia and the Comets (Team 1) and the Meteors (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new 7-on-7 game later this same day between Sofia and the Rockets (Team 1) and the Comets (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
