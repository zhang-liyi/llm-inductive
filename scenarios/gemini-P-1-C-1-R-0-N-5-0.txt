BACKGROUND
In this tournament, athletes compete in Ultimate Frisbee matches. The winning team is determined by the collective speed and handling skills of the seven players on the field, based on their intrinsic athleticism modulated by the effort they exert during the specific match.
Matches are played 7-versus-7.
All matches occur on the same weekend.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, the team of Ben, Tom, Dan, Sam, Ian, Joe, and Art beat the team of Rory, Max, Leo, Ken, Ray, Guy, and Wes.
In the second match, the team of Max, Leo, Ken, Ray, Guy, Wes, and Art beat the team of Rory, Ben, Tom, Dan, Sam, Ian, and Joe.
In the third match, the team of Ben, Max, Tom, Leo, Dan, Ken, and Sam lost to the team of Rory, Ian, Ray, Joe, Guy, Art, and Wes.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Rory ranks in terms of intrinsic athleticism?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Art put into the second match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between the team of Ben, Tom, Dan, Sam, Ian, Joe, and Max (Team 1) and the team of Rory, Leo, Ken, Ray, Guy, Wes, and Art (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between the team of Rory, Ben, Max, Tom, Leo, Dan, and Ken (Team 1) and the team of Sam, Ian, Ray, Joe, Guy, Art, and Wes (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
