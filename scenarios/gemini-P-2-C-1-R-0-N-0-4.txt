BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Pickleball games. In each game, the winner is determined by their ability to control the kitchen line and place shots, based on their intrinsic hand-eye coordination modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that game.
Athletes compete either individually or as a team.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Luna beat Ivy.
In the second game, Luna and Milo beat Ivy and Otis.
In the third game, Luna and Otis lost to Milo and Ivy.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Luna ranks in terms of intrinsic hand-eye coordination?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Luna put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Luna and Milo (Team 1) and Ivy and Otis (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Milo and Ivy (Team 1) and Luna and Otis (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
