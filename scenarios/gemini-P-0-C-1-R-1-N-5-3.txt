BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes are competing in a 7-on-7 American Football passing tournament. The winning team is determined by successful completions and touchdowns, based on the intrinsic route-running and catching ability of the players, modulated by the effort they put into the game.
Players are attached to fixed teams (The Jets, The Sharks, The Wolves, The Bears), except for Avery, who plays quarterback for different teams.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, The Jets (with Avery) beat The Sharks.
In the second game, The Wolves (with Avery) beat The Bears.
In the third game, The Sharks (with Avery) beat The Jets.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Avery ranks in terms of intrinsic route-running ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think The Jets put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between The Wolves (with Avery) and The Sharks, who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between The Jets and The Bears, who would win and by how much?
