BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Lacrosse matches (Sixes format). In each match, the team that wins depends on the coordination and stick-handling skills of the athletes, based on their intrinsic talent modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete in teams of 6.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, the team of Luna, Mila, Gianna, Aria, Ella, and Avery beat the team of Scarlett, Grace, Chloe, Penelope, Layla, and Morgan.
In the second match, the team of Scarlett, Grace, Chloe, Penelope, Layla, and Avery beat the team of Luna, Mila, Gianna, Aria, Ella, and Morgan.
In the third match, the team of Luna, Scarlett, Mila, Grace, Gianna, and Chloe beat the team of Aria, Penelope, Ella, Layla, Avery, and Morgan.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Morgan ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Avery put into the third race?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Team 1 (Luna, Mila, Gianna, Aria, Ella, Avery) and Team 2 (Scarlett, Grace, Chloe, Penelope, Layla, Morgan), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Team 1 (Luna, Scarlett, Gianna, Chloe, Ella, Layla) and Team 2 (Mila, Grace, Aria, Penelope, Avery, Morgan), who would win and by how much?
