BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of 4x100m track relays. In each race, the squad that wins depends on the cumulative speed of the athletes, based on their intrinsic sprinting ability modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that race.
Athletes compete in fixed squads of four.
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, the squad of Rick, Dan, Mike, and Joe beat the squad of Jared, Bill, Tom, and Sam.
In the second race, the squad of Bob, Art, Ken, and Len beat the squad of Jared, Bill, Tom, and Sam.
In the third race, the squad of Rick, Dan, Mike, and Joe beat the squad of Bob, Art, Ken, and Len.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Jared ranks in terms of intrinsic sprinting ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Rick put into the third race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between the squad of Jared, Bill, Tom, and Sam (Team 1) and the squad of Rick, Dan, Mike, and Joe (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between the squad of Rick, Dan, Mike, and Joe (Team 1) and the squad of Bob, Art, Ken, and Len (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
