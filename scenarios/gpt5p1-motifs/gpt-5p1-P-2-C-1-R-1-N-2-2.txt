BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a mix of individual sprints and 4‑runner relay races on the track (100–400 m). In each race, the result depends on intrinsic sprinting ability (speed and acceleration) modulated by how much effort they put into that race.
Athletes compete either individually or in 4‑runner relay teams.
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, Riley beat Noah.
