BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes compete in Rowing using "The Eight" configuration (8 rowers per boat). The winning boat is determined by the collective speed generated by the crew, which depends on the rowers' intrinsic power and synchronization, modulated by the effort they exert in that specific race.
While the boat crews (teams) are generally fixed rosters, one athlete, Lucas, acts as a substitute moving between boats for this regatta.
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, the Red Crew (with Lucas) beat the Blue Crew.
In the second race, the Green Crew (with Lucas) beat the Blue Crew.
In the third race, the Green Crew (with Lucas) lost to the Red Crew.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Lucas ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think the Red Crew put into the third race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between the Red Crew (with Lucas) and the Green Crew (without Lucas), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between the Blue Crew (with Lucas) and the Green Crew (without Lucas), who would win and by how much?
