BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are playing a series of 6-a-side soccer matches on a small-sided pitch. In each match, the team that wins depends on the players’ intrinsic soccer ability (first touch, passing, speed, finishing, defending) together with how much effort they put in that match.
Players compete either individually (for example in penalty contests) or in teams of 6.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Rafael, Luca, Amir, Jonas, Malik, and Sergio beat Owen, Theo, Jay, Liam, Isaac, and Bruno.
In the second match, Rafael, Luca, Amir, Jonas, Malik, and Sergio beat Carlos, Devon, Felix, Hugo, Niko, and Pablo.
In the third match, Owen, Theo, Carlos, Devon, Felix, and Hugo beat Jay, Liam, Isaac, Bruno, Niko, and Pablo.
Later that day, Rafael beat Owen.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random soccer players, where do you think Rafael ranks in terms of intrinsic soccer ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Owen put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Rafael, Luca, Amir, Jonas, Malik, and Sergio (Team 1) and Owen, Theo, Carlos, Devon, Felix, and Hugo (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Luca, Owen, Jay, Liam, Isaac, and Niko (Team 1) and Theo, Carlos, Bruno, Devon, Felix, and Pablo (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
