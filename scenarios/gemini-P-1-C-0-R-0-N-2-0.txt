BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes compete in Ultimate Frisbee matches. The winning team is determined by the collective athleticism and tactical intelligence of the players, based on their intrinsic skill levels modulated by the effort they exert during the match.
Athletes compete in teams of 4.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, the team of Adam, Ben, Carl, and Dan beat the team of Rory, Sam, Tom, and Vic.
In the second match, the team of Adam, Ben, Earl, and Fred beat the team of Rory, Sam, Tom, and Vic.
In the third match, the team of Carl, Dan, Earl, and Fred beat the team of Rory, Sam, Tom, and Vic.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Rory ranks in terms of intrinsic skill?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Carl put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Rory, Sam, Tom, and Vic (Team 1) and Adam, Carl, Earl, and Greg (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Adam, Ben, Carl, and Dan (Team 1) and Earl, Fred, Greg, and Hank (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
