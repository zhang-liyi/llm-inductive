BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of recreational volleyball matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the points scored through successful spikes and blocks, based on the vertical leap and coordination of the athletes, derived from their intrinsic physical prowess modulated by how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete as a team of three.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Karch, Kent, and Steve beat Phil, Todd, and Sinjin.
In the second match, Karch, Kent, and Steve beat Phil, Randy, and Tim.
In the third match, Karch, Kent, and Steve beat Todd, Randy, and Sinjin.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Karch ranks in terms of intrinsic physical prowess?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Phil put into the second match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Karch, Kent, and Steve (Team 1) and Tim, Todd, and Phil (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Phil, Kent, and Randy (Team 1) and Karch, Tim, and Sinjin (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
