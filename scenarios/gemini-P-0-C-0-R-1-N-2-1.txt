BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Rowing races (Coxless Four). In each race, the team that wins depends on the average power and synchronization with which the athletes are able to row, based on their intrinsic endurance modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that race.
Athletes compete in fixed teams of four.
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, Emma, Olivia, Ava, and Isabella beat Sophia, Mia, Charlotte, and Amelia.
In the second race, Emma, Olivia, Ava, and Isabella beat Sophia, Mia, Charlotte, and Amelia.
In the third race, Sophia, Mia, Charlotte, and Amelia lost to Emma, Olivia, Ava, and Isabella.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Emma ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Sophia put into the third race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Emma, Olivia, Ava, and Isabella (Team 1) and Sophia, Mia, Charlotte, and Amelia (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Emma, Olivia, Sophia, and Mia (Team 1) and Ava, Isabella, Charlotte, and Amelia (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
