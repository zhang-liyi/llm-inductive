BACKGROUND
In this event, players compete in a half-court 4-on-4 basketball tournament. The winning team is determined by the total points scored, which depends on shooting accuracy and defensive capability, based on the players' intrinsic skill levels and the effort they put into the game.
Athletes compete in teams of four.
All games take place on the same day.
