BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Volleyball matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the collective offensive and defensive effectiveness of the players, based on their intrinsic jumping and hitting ability modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete in teams of 6.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, the team of Sasha, Liam, Noah, Olivia, Emma, and Ava beat the team of Mason, Ethan, Logan, Lucas, Mia, and Harper.
In the second match, the team of Sasha, Mason, Ethan, Logan, Lucas, and Mia beat the team of Liam, Noah, Olivia, Emma, Ava, and Harper.
In the third match, the team of Sasha, Liam, Mason, Noah, Ethan, and Logan lost to the team of Olivia, Emma, Ava, Lucas, Mia, and Harper.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Sasha ranks in terms of intrinsic jumping and hitting ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Sasha put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between the team of Sasha, Liam, Noah, Olivia, Emma, and Ava (Team 1) and the team of Mason, Ethan, Logan, Lucas, Mia, and Harper (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between the team of Sasha, Mason, Ethan, Logan, Lucas, and Mia (Team 1) and the team of Liam, Noah, Olivia, Emma, Ava, and Harper (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
