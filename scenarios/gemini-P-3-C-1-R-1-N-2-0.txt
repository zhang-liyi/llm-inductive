BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes compete in a series of 4x100m relay races on a sprint track. The winning team is determined by the collective speed of the four runners, which depends on their intrinsic sprinting speed and baton-passing technique, modulated by the effort they exert during the specific race.
Athletes compete in teams of four.
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, Elias, Ben, Chris, and David lost to Frank, Greg, Hank, and Ian.
In the second race, Elias, Jack, Ken, and Leo lost to Mike, Nick, Oscar, and Paul.
In the third race, Elias, Quinn, Ray, and Sam beat Tom, Uri, Vic, and Will.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Elias ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Frank put into the first race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Elias, Ben, Chris, and David (Team 1) and Mike, Nick, Oscar, and Paul (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Frank, Greg, Hank, and Ian (Team 1) and Tom, Uri, Vic, and Will (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
