BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of 9-a-side basketball scrimmages. In each game, the team that wins depends on the players’ intrinsic basketball skill, modulated by how much effort they put into that game.
All games take place on the same day.
Whenever Lauren plays, she is always on the same side with the same eight teammates: Brianna, Chloe, Dana, Elise, Fiona, Grace, Hannah, and Ivy.

In the fourth game, Lauren and her usual eight teammates beat Jenna, Lila, Maya, Nadia, Olivia, Paige, Quinn, Tessa, and Vanessa.

QUERIES

Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Lauren and her usual eight teammates (Team 1) and Jenna, Kara, Lila, Maya, Nadia, Olivia, Paige, Quinn, and Riley (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
