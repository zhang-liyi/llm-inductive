BACKGROUND

In each match, the team that wins depends on the number of goals scored, based on the athletes' intrinsic ball control and tactical awareness, modulated by other factors including the running intensity and effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete in fixed teams of 5.
All matches take place on the same day.

QUERIES
