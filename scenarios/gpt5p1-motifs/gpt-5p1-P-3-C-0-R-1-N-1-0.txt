BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are playing 3-on-3 half-court basketball games. The outcome of each game depends on the teams’ average on-court performance, which is determined by each player’s intrinsic basketball ability (shooting, ball-handling, defense) and how much effort they put into that game.
Players compete as part of fixed three-player teams, and occasionally in individual contests.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Eric, Samir, and Kyle beat Malik, Jordan, and Theo.
In the second game, Dante, Omar, and Felix beat Malik, Jordan, and Theo.
In the third game, Malik, Jordan, and Theo beat Eric, Samir, and Kyle.
In the fourth game, Dante beat Eric.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random basketball players, where do you think Malik ranks in terms of intrinsic basketball ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Malik put into the second game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Malik, Jordan, and Theo (Team 1) and Dante, Omar, and Felix (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Eric, Samir, and Kyle (Team 1) and Dante, Omar, and Felix (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
