BACKGROUND
In this triathlon event, athletes compete individually over a swim, bike, and run course. The winner is the athlete with the fastest total time, which depends on their intrinsic endurance and speed, together with how much effort they put in across the three disciplines in each race.
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, Ava finished ahead of Bruno.
In the second race, Bruno finished ahead of Chloe.
In the third race, Chloe finished ahead of Ava, and Dan finished behind both of them.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random triathletes, where do you think Ava ranks in terms of intrinsic endurance and overall ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Bruno put into the second race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Ava (Team 1) and Chloe (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Bruno (Team 1) and Dan (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
