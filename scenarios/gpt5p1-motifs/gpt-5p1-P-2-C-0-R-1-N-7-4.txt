BACKGROUND
In this regatta, three rowing crews of 9 athletes each (eight rowers plus a cox) race against one another multiple times. A crew’s speed in any race depends on the rowers’ intrinsic rowing power and coordination, together with the effort they put into that race.  
Crews have fixed lineups of 9 athletes that do not change during the day.  
Arjun rows for the Blue crew. The other crews are the Red crew and the Green crew.  
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, the Blue crew beat the Red crew.  
In the second race, the Blue crew beat the Green crew.  
In the third race, the Green crew beat the Blue crew.  
In the fourth race, the Red crew beat the Green crew.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random rowers, where do you think Arjun ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Mira from the Green crew put into the third race?  
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between the Blue crew and the Red crew, who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between the Blue crew and the Green crew, who would win and by how much?
