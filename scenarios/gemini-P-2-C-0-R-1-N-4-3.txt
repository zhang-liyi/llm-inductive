BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes are competing in a Lacrosse (Sixes format) tournament. Each team fields 6 players at a time. The winning team is determined by the running endurance and stick-handling capability of the group, based on their intrinsic coordination modulated by the intensity of effort they put into the game.
Teams are fixed and play together consistently.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Sophie and her 5 teammates beat The Nets.
In the second game, Sophie and her 5 teammates lost to The Sticks.
In the third game, Sophie and her 5 teammates beat The Goals.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Sophie ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think the defender from The Sticks put into the second game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Sophie’s team (Team 1) and The Nets (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between The Sticks (Team 1) and The Goals (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
