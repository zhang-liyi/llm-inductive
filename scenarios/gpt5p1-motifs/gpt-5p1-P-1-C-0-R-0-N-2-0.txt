BACKGROUND  
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of 4-on-4 half-court basketball games and occasional 1-on-1 games. In each game, the team that wins depends on the players’ intrinsic basketball ability (shooting, speed, decision-making), modulated by how much effort they put in on that day.  
All games take place on the same day.  

CONDITIONS  
In the first game, Ava beat Lucas.  
In the second game, Lucas, Marco, Nina, and Olivia lost to Ava, Ben, Cara, and Diego.  
In the third game, Lucas, Marco, Nina, and Olivia lost to Ben, Cara, Diego, and Emma.  

QUERIES  
Query 1: Out of 100 random basketball players, where do you think Ava ranks in terms of intrinsic basketball ability?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Olivia put into the third game?  
Query 3: In a new 4-on-4 game later this same day between Ava, Ben, Cara, and Emma (Team 1) and Lucas, Marco, Nina, and Olivia (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new 4-on-4 game later this same day between Diego, Emma, Nina, and Olivia (Team 1) and Ben, Cara, Marco, and Lucas (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
