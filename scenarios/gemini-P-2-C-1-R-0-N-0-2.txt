BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Badminton matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on their court coverage and smash power, based on their intrinsic reflexes modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that match.
Athletes compete as a team of two.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Wei and Bo beat Lin and Chen.
In the second match, Wei and Lin beat Bo and Chen.
In the third match, Wei and Chen lost to Bo and Lin.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Wei ranks in terms of intrinsic reflexes?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Wei and Chen put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Wei and Bo (Team 1) and Lin and Chen (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Bo and Lin (Team 1) and Wei and Chen (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
