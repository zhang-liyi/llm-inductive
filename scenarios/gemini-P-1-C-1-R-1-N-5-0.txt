BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Ultimate Frisbee matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the collective scoring ability of the athletes, based on their intrinsic speed and throwing accuracy modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete as a team of 7.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, the team of Ash, Bo, Cy, Dax, Ev, Fox, and Gus beat the team of Hal, Ian, Jay, Kay, Leo, Max, and **Nico**.
In the second match, the team of Hal, Ian, Jay, Kay, Leo, Max, and Gus beat the team of Ash, Bo, Cy, Dax, Ev, Fox, and **Nico**.
In the third match, the team of Roy, Sam, Tim, Uma, Val, Wes, and Gus beat the team of Hal, Ian, Jay, Kay, Leo, Max, and **Nico**.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think **Nico** ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think **Max** put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between the team of Ash, Bo, Cy, Dax, Ev, Fox, and Gus (Team 1) and the team of Hal, Ian, Jay, Kay, Leo, Max, and **Nico** (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between the team of Roy, Sam, Tim, Uma, Val, Wes, and **Nico** (Team 1) and the team of Hal, Ian, Jay, Kay, Leo, Max, and Gus (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
