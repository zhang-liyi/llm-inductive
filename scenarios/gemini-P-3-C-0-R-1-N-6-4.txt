BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a short-format Cricket tournament where teams consist of 8 players. In each match, the team that wins depends on the run-scoring and wicket-taking abilities of the athletes, based on their intrinsic talent modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Players compete in fixed teams.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Arjun's XI lost to the Royals.
In the second match, Arjun's XI lost to the Titans.
In the third match, Arjun's XI beat the Challengers.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random cricketers, where do you think Arjun ranks in terms of intrinsic talent?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think the Royals put into the first match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Arjun's XI (Team 1) and the Titans (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between the Royals (Team 1) and the Challengers (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
