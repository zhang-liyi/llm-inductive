BACKGROUND
In this event, the players are competing in small-sided 4-on-4 soccer games and occasional 1-on-1 contests. The outcome of each game depends on the players’ intrinsic soccer abilities (ball control, speed, finishing, defending) and on how much effort they put forth in that game.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Diego lost to Malik.
In the second game, Diego, Omar, Leo, and Finn lost to Malik, Aaron, Hugo, and Kai.
In the third game, Diego, Aaron, Kai, and Noah beat Malik, Omar, Hugo, and Leo.
In the fourth game, Diego, Hugo, Noah, and Eli lost to Malik, Aaron, Finn, and Kai.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random soccer players, where do you think Diego ranks in terms of intrinsic soccer ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Malik put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Diego, Malik, Kai, and Eli (Team 1) and Aaron, Omar, Hugo, and Noah (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Diego, Aaron, Leo, and Finn (Team 1) and Malik, Kai, Hugo, and Noah (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
