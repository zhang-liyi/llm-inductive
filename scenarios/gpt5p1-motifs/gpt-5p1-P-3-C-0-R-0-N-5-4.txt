BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of small‑sided field hockey games. The winning team in each game depends on players’ intrinsic stick skills, speed, and defensive ability, together with how much effort they put in for that game.
Athletes compete either individually in basic stick‑handling drills or as part of a 7‑player field hockey team.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Daichi, Erika, Fumi, Gen, Hanae, Isamu, and Jun beat Kazuo, Akira, Emi, Haru, Naoko, Rin, and Sora.
In the second game, Daichi, Fumi, Gen, Hanae, Isamu, Kana, and Makoto beat Kazuo, Akira, Emi, Haru, Naoko, Rin, and Sora.
In the third game, Kazuo, Akira, Emi, Haru, Naoko, Rin, and Sora beat Erika, Fumi, Gen, Hanae, Nori, Osamu, and Rika.
In the fourth game, Akira, Emi, Haru, Daichi, Erika, Fumi, and Gen beat Naoko, Rin, Sora, Kana, Makoto, Shun, and Takumi.

QUERIES
