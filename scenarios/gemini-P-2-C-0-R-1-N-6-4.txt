BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes compete in lacrosse matches played with a modified squad size. Each team fields exactly 8 players. The outcome of the match depends on the speed and coordination of the squad, based on their intrinsic stick skills and physical conditioning, modulated by the effort they exert during play.
Players have fixed teams and compete with the same 7 teammates in every match.
All matches take place on the same day.
