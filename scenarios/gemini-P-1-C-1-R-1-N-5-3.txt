BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of 7-a-side Soccer matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the number of goals scored, based on the athletes' intrinsic ball control and speed modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete as a team of 7.
All matches take place on the same day.

In the third match, the team of Paul, Quinn, Ross, Sean, Tony, Vince, and Greg beat the team of Hans, Ivan, Jack, Karl, Luis, Mark, and **Noah**.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think **Noah** ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?

Query 3: In a new match later this same day between the team of Alex, Brad, Chad, Dave, Eric, Fred, and Greg (Team 1) and the team of Hans, Ivan, Jack, Karl, Luis, Mark, and **Noah** (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
