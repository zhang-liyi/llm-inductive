BACKGROUND
In this session, the players are taking part in a series of small-sided 3-on-3 soccer games on a reduced field. Each game’s outcome depends on the players’ intrinsic ball control, vision, and finishing ability, combined with how much effort they put into pressing, moving, and staying focused in that game.
The club usually uses fixed 3-player mini-squads, but today Diego is being tried in different combinations while the other players mostly remain with their standard partners.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Diego, Bruno, Carlos beat Elias, Mateo, Tomas.
In the second game, Diego, Elias, Mateo beat Bruno, Carlos, Tomas.
In the third game, Bruno, Carlos, Tomas beat Diego, Elias, Mateo.
In the fourth game, Diego beat Bruno.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random soccer players, where do you think Diego ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Tomas put into the second game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Diego, Bruno, Tomas (Team 1) and Carlos, Elias, Mateo (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Diego, Carlos, Elias (Team 1) and Bruno, Mateo, Tomas (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
