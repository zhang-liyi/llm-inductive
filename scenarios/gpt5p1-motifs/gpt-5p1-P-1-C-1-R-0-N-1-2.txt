BACKGROUND  
In this event, the rowers are competing in a series of crew races using three-person boats. In each race, the boat that wins depends on the rowers’ intrinsic rowing strength and technique, combined with how much effort they put into that race.  
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS  
In the first race, Tom, Alex, and Ben lost to Chris, David, and Emma.  
In the second race, Tom, Chris, and Fiona lost to Alex, Emma, and Henry.  
In the third race, Tom, David, and Grace lost to Ben, Fiona, and Henry.

QUERIES  
Query 1: Out of 100 random rowers, where do you think Alex ranks in terms of intrinsic strength and technique?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Tom put into the third race?  
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Alex, Ben, and Emma (Boat 1) and Chris, David, and Henry (Boat 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Tom, Emma, and Grace (Boat 1) and Ben, Fiona, and David (Boat 2), who would win and by how much?
