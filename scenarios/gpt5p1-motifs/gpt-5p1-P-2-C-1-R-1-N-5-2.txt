BACKGROUND
In this rugby sevens event, teams of 7 players compete in short, high-intensity matches, and some players also take part in 1v1 contact-and-speed drills. The result of each match depends on the players’ intrinsic strength, speed, and tackling ability, modulated by how much effort they put in for that particular match. Most players stay with their regular teams (the Hawks, Bulls, and Sharks), but one standout runner, Malik, is rotated among the teams. All matches and drills occur on the same day.
