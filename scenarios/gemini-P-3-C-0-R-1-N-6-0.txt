BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes compete in a Rowing regatta. Each boat contains a crew of 8 rowers. The winning crew is determined by the average power output and synchronization of the rowers, based on their intrinsic endurance modulated by how much effort they put into that specific race.
The crews are fixed for the duration of the event.
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, Jonas's Crew beat the River City Club.
In the second race, Jonas's Crew lost to the Lakeside Club.
In the third race, Jonas's Crew lost to the Harbor Club.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random rowers, where do you think Jonas ranks in terms of intrinsic endurance?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Jonas's Crew put into the third race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Jonas's Crew (Team 1) and the Harbor Club (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between the Lakeside Club (Team 1) and the River City Club (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
