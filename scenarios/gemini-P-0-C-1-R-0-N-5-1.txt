BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Handball games. In each game, the team that wins depends on the average power and agility with which the athletes play, based on their intrinsic skill modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that game.
Athletes compete in teams of 7.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Reese, Noah, Liam, Mason, Jacob, William, and Ethan beat Daniel, Matthew, Henry, Joseph, Samuel, David, and Carter.
In the second game, Reese, Daniel, Matthew, Henry, Joseph, Samuel, and David beat Noah, Liam, Mason, Jacob, William, Ethan, and Carter.
In the third game, Reese, Noah, Daniel, Liam, Matthew, Mason, and Henry beat Jacob, William, Ethan, Joseph, Samuel, David, and Carter.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Reese ranks in terms of intrinsic skill?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Carter put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Reese, Noah, Liam, Mason, Jacob, William, and Ethan (Team 1) and Daniel, Matthew, Henry, Joseph, Samuel, David, and Carter (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Noah, Liam, Mason, Jacob, William, Ethan, and Carter (Team 1) and Reese, Daniel, Matthew, Henry, Joseph, Samuel, and David (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
