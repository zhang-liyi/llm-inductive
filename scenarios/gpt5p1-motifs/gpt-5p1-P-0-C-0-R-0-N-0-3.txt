BACKGROUND  
In this event, the players are competing in a series of table tennis matches. The winner of each match is determined by the players’ intrinsic reaction speed, spin control, and shot precision, together with the level of effort they commit during that match.  
Players compete either in singles or in doubles teams of two.  
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS  
In the first match, Hana and Iris beat Colin and Mei.  
In the second match, Hana beat Victor.  
In the third match, Hana and Iris beat Victor and Mei.  
In the fourth match, Hana and Iris beat Colin and Victor.

QUERIES  
Query 1: Out of 100 random table tennis players, where do you think Hana ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Victor put into the fourth match?  
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Hana and Iris (Team 1) and Colin and Mei (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Victor and Mei (Team 1) and Iris and Colin (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
