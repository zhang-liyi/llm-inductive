They play doubles (teams of two), with all matches occurring on the same day.

In the second match, Omar and Liam lost to Victor and Hugo.

Query 4: In a new doubles match later this same day between Liam and Noah (Team 1) and Eric and Jason (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
