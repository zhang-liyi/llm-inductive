BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of table tennis matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the average hand-eye coordination and spin technique of the athletes, based on their intrinsic talent modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete as a team of two.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Elara and Jin lost to Kian and Rose.
In the second match, Elara and Kian lost to Jin and Rose.
In the third match, Elara and Rose beat Jin and Kian.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Elara ranks in terms of intrinsic talent?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Kian put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Elara and Jin (Team 1) and Kian and Rose (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Elara and Kian (Team 1) and Jin and Rose (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
