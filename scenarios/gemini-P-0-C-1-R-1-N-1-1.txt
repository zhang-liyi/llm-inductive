BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Ultimate Frisbee matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the average speed and throwing precision of the athletes, based on their intrinsic athleticism modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that match.
Athletes compete either individually or as a team of three.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Flynn beat Disc.
In the second match, Flynn, Cutter, and Handler beat Disc, Deep, and Mid.
In the third match, Flynn, Disc, and Deep beat Cutter, Handler, and Mid.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Flynn ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Cutter put into the third race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Flynn and Cutter (Team 1) and Disc and Deep (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Disc and Mid (Team 1) and Cutter and Handler (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
