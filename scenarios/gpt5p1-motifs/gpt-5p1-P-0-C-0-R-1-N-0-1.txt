BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are playing a series of half-court 2-on-2 basketball games. The outcome of each game depends on the players’ intrinsic basketball ability (shooting accuracy, ball-handling, decision-making) and how much effort they put in on offense and defense in that game.  
All games are 2-on-2.  
All games are played on the same day, and intrinsic ability stays constant while effort can vary by game.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Devon and Marcus beat Tyler and Chris.  
In the second game, Devon and Marcus beat Jamal and Andre.  
In the third game, Jamal and Andre beat Tyler and Chris.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random basketball players, where do you think Devon ranks in terms of intrinsic skill?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Tyler put into the third game?  
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Devon and Marcus (Team 1) and Jamal and Andre (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Tyler and Chris (Team 1) and Jamal and Andre (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
