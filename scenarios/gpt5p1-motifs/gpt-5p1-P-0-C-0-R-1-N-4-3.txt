BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are playing competitive tennis in both individual and team formats. Match outcomes depend on each player’s intrinsic tennis ability (stroke quality, movement, anticipation) together with how much effort they put in on this day.
Athletes belong to fixed 6-player teams, and team results are based on the combined outcomes of their individual matches; team rosters do not change.
All matches take place on the same day.
