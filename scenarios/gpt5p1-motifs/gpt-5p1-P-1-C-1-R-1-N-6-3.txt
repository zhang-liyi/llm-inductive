BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of rowing races. In each race, the winning boat is determined by the rowers’ intrinsic rowing power and technique, modulated by how much effort they sustain over the course.
Rowers compete either individually in single sculls or in crews of eight.
All races take place on the same day.
