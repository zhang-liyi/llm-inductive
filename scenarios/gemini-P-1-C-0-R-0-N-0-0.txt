BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Badminton matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the combined court coverage and shot precision of the athletes, based on their intrinsic agility and technique modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that match.
Athletes compete either individually or as a team of two.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Sarah beat Rohan.
In the second match, Sarah and Mike beat Rohan and Liam.
In the third match, Mike and Jenny beat Rohan and Liam.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Sarah ranks in terms of intrinsic agility and technique?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Rohan put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Sarah and Mike (Team 1) and Rohan and a new player named Dave (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Rohan and Liam (Team 1) and Jenny and a new player named Zoe (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
