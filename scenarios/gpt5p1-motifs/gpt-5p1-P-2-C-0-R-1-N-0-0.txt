BACKGROUND  
In this event, pairs compete in a series of beach volleyball matches. In each match, the team that wins is the one whose players can generate more effective plays overall, based on their intrinsic volleyball skill combined with how much effort they put into that match.  
Matches are either doubles (teams of two) or, in rare cases, singles challenges.  
Whenever athletes play doubles, they always use the same fixed partner for this event.  
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS

In the second match, Maya and Lina beat Priya and Noor.  
In the third match, Zoe and Carla beat Maya and Lina.

QUERIES  
Query 1: Out of 100 random beach volleyball players, where do you think Maya ranks in terms of intrinsic skill?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Zoe put into the third match?  
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Maya and Lina (Team 1) and Zoe and Carla (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Priya and Noor (Team 1) and Zoe and Carla (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
