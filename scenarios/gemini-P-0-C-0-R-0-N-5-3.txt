BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes compete in 7-a-side Soccer matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the average ball control and tactical sense of the athletes, based on their intrinsic talent modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete in teams of 7.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Nico, Rio, Sal, Tom, Val, Zed, and Mac beat Ash, Bil, Cal, Dom, Ed, Fox, and Guy.
In the second match, Nico, Rio, Sal, Tom, Val, Zed, and Mac beat Ash, Bil, Cal, Hal, Ian, Jay, and Ken.
In the third match, Nico, Rio, Sal, Tom, Val, Zed, and Mac beat Dom, Ed, Fox, Hal, Ian, Jay, and Ken.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Nico ranks in terms of intrinsic talent?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Ken put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Nico, Rio, Sal, Tom, Val, Zed, and Mac (Team 1) and Ash, Bil, Cal, Dom, Ed, Fox, and Hal (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Ash, Bil, Cal, Dom, Ed, Fox, and Guy (Team 1) and Hal, Ian, Jay, Ken, and three new average players (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
