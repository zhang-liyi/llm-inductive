BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Sprint Track relays (4x400m). In each race, the team that wins depends on the total time taken, based on the athletes' intrinsic top speed and stamina modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that race.
Athletes compete in squads of 4.
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, Squad 1 (Eva, Mia, Zoe, and Ava) beat Squad 2 (Lia, Nia, Pia) and Taylor.
In the second race, Squad 3 (Ana, Bea, Cat, and Dot) beat Squad 4 (Fay, Gay, Ivy) and Taylor.
In the third race, Squad 2 (Lia, Nia, Pia, and Ava) beat Squad 1 (Eva, Mia, Zoe) and Taylor.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Taylor ranks in terms of intrinsic top speed and stamina?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Taylor put into the third race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Squad 2 (Lia, Nia, Pia) and Ava (Team 1) and Squad 1 (Eva, Mia, Zoe) and Taylor (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Squad 4 (Fay, Gay, Ivy) and Dot (Team 1) and Squad 3 (Ana, Bea, Cat) and Taylor (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
