BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Badminton matches. In each match, the team that wins depends on the court coverage and reaction time of the athletes, based on their intrinsic reflexes modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete as a fixed pair.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Lin and Lee beat Chen and Viktor.
In the second match, Lin and Lee beat Chen and Viktor.
In the third match, Lin and Lee lost to Chen and Viktor.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Lin ranks in terms of intrinsic reflexes?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Lin put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Lin and Lee (Team 1) and Chen and a new partner, Hans (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Chen and Viktor (Team 1) and Lee and Hans (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
