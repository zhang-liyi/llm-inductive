BACKGROUND
In this tennis event, players compete primarily in doubles, with occasional singles matches. In every point, the outcome depends on the athletes’ intrinsic tennis ability (serves, groundstrokes, volleys, and movement) and how much effort they put into that match.
All matches take place on the same day.
Doubles teams are fixed pairs of two players.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Priya and Hannah lost to Zoe and Marco.
In the second match, Priya and Hannah beat Dan and Luis.
In the third match, Priya and Hannah lost to Zoe and Marco.
In the fourth match, Zoe beat Dan.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random tennis players, where do you think Priya ranks in terms of intrinsic playing ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Zoe put into the fourth match?
Query 3: In a new doubles match later this same day between Priya and Hannah (Team 1) and Zoe and Marco (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new doubles match later this same day between Dan and Luis (Team 1) and Zoe and Marco (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
