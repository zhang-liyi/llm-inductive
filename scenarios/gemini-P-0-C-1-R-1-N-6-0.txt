BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes compete in Ultimate Frisbee matches. Each team consists of 8 players. The winning team is determined by the collective speed, agility, and tactical awareness of the players, modulated by the effort they exert during the match.
There are established squads (Red Squad, Blue Squad, and Green Squad) that have fixed rosters, but one star athlete, Jordan, rotates between them for different matches.
All matches occur on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Jordan and the Red Squad beat the Blue Squad.
In the second match, Jordan and the Blue Squad beat the Red Squad.
In the third match, the Green Squad lost to Jordan and the Blue Squad.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Jordan ranks in terms of intrinsic agility and skill?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think the Green Squad put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Jordan and the Green Squad (Team 1) and the Red Squad (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between the Red Squad (Team 1) and the Blue Squad (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
