BACKGROUND  
In this event, three 9‑a‑side soccer teams play a round of short matches. Each team has 9 fixed players.  
The team that wins a match is the one whose players show higher intrinsic soccer ability (speed, ball control, positioning, finishing, goalkeeping reflexes) combined with the effort they put into that specific match.  
All matches and individual duels occur on the same day.  
Teams are fixed, and players always stay with their own 9-person team.

Team Aurora: Diego, Malik, Jonas, Pavel, Hugo, Amir, Liam, Kai, Stefan  
Team Eclipse: Bruno, Carlos, Enzo, Ravi, Omar, Felix, Theo, Milan, Serge  
Team Comet: Leo, Ivan, Andre, Miguel, Rafa, Jonas B., Victor, Yann, Toby  

CONDITIONS  
In the first match, Aurora beat Eclipse.  
In the second match, Aurora beat Comet.  
Later that day, in a 1‑on‑1 dribbling and finishing challenge, Diego beat Bruno.

QUERIES  
Query 1: Out of 100 random soccer players, where do you think Diego ranks in terms of intrinsic soccer ability?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Bruno put into the 1‑on‑1 challenge?  
Query 3: In a new 9‑a‑side match later this same day between Aurora and Comet, who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new 9‑a‑side match later this same day between Eclipse and Comet, who would win and by how much?
