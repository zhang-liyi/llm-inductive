BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are playing a series of 3-on-3 half-court basketball games. In each game, the team that wins depends on the players’ intrinsic shooting, ball-handling, and defensive ability, modulated by how much effort they put into that particular game.  
All games take place on the same day.  
Teams are always of size 3 in these games.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, Jordan, Riya, and Malik beat Devon, Casey, and Leo.  
In the second game, Jordan, Riya, and Malik beat Talia, Noor, and Devon.  
In the third game, Jordan, Riya, and Malik beat Casey, Hayden, and Noor.  
In the fourth game, Jordan, Riya, and Malik beat Leo, Talia, and Hayden.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random half-court basketball players, where do you think Jordan ranks in terms of intrinsic basketball ability?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Noor put into the third game?  
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Jordan, Riya, and Malik (Team 1) and Devon, Casey, and Talia (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Devon, Noor, and Hayden (Team 1) and Casey, Leo, and Talia (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
