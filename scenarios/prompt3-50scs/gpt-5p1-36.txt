BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of boxing matches. In each match, the boxer or team that wins depends on their effective striking ability, which comes from intrinsic power and speed combined with how much effort they put into that match.
Athletes compete either individually or as a team.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Carlos beat Ethan.
In the second match, Ethan and Dana beat Carlos and Fiona.
In the third match, Carlos and Grace beat Dana and Ethan.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random boxers, where do you think Carlos ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Ethan put into the second match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Carlos and Dana (Team 1) and Ethan and Grace (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Ethan and Fiona (Team 1) and Carlos and Dana (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
