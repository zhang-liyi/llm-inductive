BACKGROUND
In this regatta, the athletes are competing in a series of rowing races. In each race, the boat that wins depends on the rowers’ intrinsic power, endurance, and technical efficiency, together with how much effort they put into that race.
Rowers may compete individually in single sculls or as part of a 4‑person crew.
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS

In the third race, Sonia, Ben, Clara, and Hugo beat Lucas, Victor, Paige, and Iris.
In the fourth race, Sonia, Ben, Clara, and Hugo lost to Theo, Victor, Omar, and Paige.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random competitive rowers, where do you think Sonia ranks in terms of intrinsic rowing ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Nina put into the second race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Sonia, Ben, Clara, and Hugo (Boat 1) and Theo, Nina, Omar, and Paige (Boat 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Lucas, Victor, Omar, and Iris (Boat 1) and Ben, Clara, Paige, and Theo (Boat 2), who would win and by how much?
