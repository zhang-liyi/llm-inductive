BACKGROUND
This event is a small table tennis meet with doubles matches. In each match, the outcome depends on each player's intrinsic table tennis ability (such as reflexes, control, and coordination) and how much effort they put into that particular match. Higher combined intrinsic ability and effort from a team leads to better performance.
All matches take place on the same day.
Players always compete in fixed pairs: Leo and Sara form one team, Dylan and Maya form another, and Noah and Erin form a third.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Leo and Sara beat Dylan and Maya.
In the second match, Noah and Erin beat Dylan and Maya.
In the third match, Leo and Sara beat Noah and Erin.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random table tennis players, where do you think Leo ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Dylan put into the second match?
Query 3: In a new doubles match later this same day between Leo and Sara versus Dylan and Maya, who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new doubles match later this same day between Noah and Erin versus Leo and Sara, who would win and by how much?
