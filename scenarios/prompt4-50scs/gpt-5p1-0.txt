BACKGROUND  
In this event, the players are competing in a series of half-court basketball games. The team that wins a game depends on the players’ intrinsic basketball ability (shooting, defense, decision-making) together with how much effort they put into that game.  
Players sometimes play 3-on-3 and sometimes play individually in quick 1-on-1 games.  
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS  
In the first game, Jordan, Riley, and Sam beat Taylor, Casey, and Devon.  
In the second game, Jordan, Taylor, and Riley beat Sam, Casey, and Devon.  
In the third game, Devon beat Riley.  

QUERIES  
Query 1: Out of 100 random basketball players, where do you think Jordan ranks in terms of intrinsic basketball ability?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Taylor put into the second game?  
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Jordan, Sam, and Casey (Team 1) and Taylor, Riley, and Devon (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new 1-on-1 game later this same day between Jordan (Player 1) and Devon (Player 2), who would win and by how much?
