BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes compete in Handball games. In each game, the team that wins depends on the total goals scored, based on the athletes' intrinsic throwing power and tactical intelligence modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that game.
Athletes compete in teams of 7.
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first game, the team of Jonas, Torbjorn, and 5 others beat the team of Lars, Erik, and 5 others.
In the second game, the team of Magnus, Henrik, and 5 others beat the team of Lars, Erik, and 5 different players.
In the third game, the team of Jonas, Magnus, and 5 others beat the team of Lars, Erik, and a new mix of 5 players.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Lars ranks in terms of intrinsic throwing power and tactical intelligence?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Jonas put into the third game?
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between a team led by Jonas and Magnus (Team 1) and a team led by Lars and Erik (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between a team led by Torbjorn and Henrik (Team 1) and a team led by Lars and a random player (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
