BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Tennis matches. In each match, the winner is determined by the quality of shot placement and court coverage, based on the athletes' intrinsic technique modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that match.
Athletes compete either individually or as a team of two.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Riley beat Morgan.
In the second match, Riley and Jamie beat Morgan and Taylor.
In the third match, Riley and Taylor beat Morgan and Jamie.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Riley ranks in terms of intrinsic technique?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Morgan put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Riley and Jamie (Team 1) and Morgan and Taylor (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Morgan and Taylor (Team 1) and Jamie and a new player, Casey (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
