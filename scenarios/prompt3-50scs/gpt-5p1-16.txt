BACKGROUND
This event is a swimming meet featuring individual races and relays in a short-course pool. Results in each race depend on the swimmers’ intrinsic speed and technique, combined with how much effort they choose to exert in that particular race.
All races take place on the same day. Swimmers sometimes race individually and sometimes in relays.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, a 100m freestyle, Chloe beat Daniel.
In the second race, a 200m freestyle, Felix beat Chloe.
In the third race, a 4×100m freestyle relay, Chloe and Emma beat Daniel and Felix.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random swimmers, where do you think Chloe ranks in terms of intrinsic swimming ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Felix put into the third race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day, a 200m freestyle between Chloe (Lane 1) and Felix (Lane 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day, a 4×100m freestyle relay between Chloe and Daniel (Team 1) and Felix and Emma (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
