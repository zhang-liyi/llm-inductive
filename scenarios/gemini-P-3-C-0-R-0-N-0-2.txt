BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of Rowing races (double sculls). In each race, the team that wins depends on the boat velocity generated, based on the athletes' intrinsic muscular endurance and stroke technique modulated by other factors including how much effort they put into that race.
Athletes compete as a team of two.
All races take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, Henry and Jack lost to Finn and George.
In the second race, Henry and Jack beat George and Arthur.
In the third race, Henry and Jack lost to Finn and Arthur.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Henry ranks in terms of intrinsic muscular endurance and stroke technique?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Henry put into the second race?
Query 3: In a new race later this same day between Henry and Jack (Team 1) and Finn and Ian (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new race later this same day between Finn and George (Team 1) and Jack and Arthur (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
