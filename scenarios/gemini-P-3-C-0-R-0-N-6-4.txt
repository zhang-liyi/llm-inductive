BACKGROUND
In this event, athletes compete in 8-v-8 small-sided Soccer matches. The winner is determined by the team's collective speed and ball control, which relies on intrinsic talent modulated by the amount of effort the players put into the match.
One player, Omar, refuses to change his lineup and plays with the same seven teammates all day, while the opposing teams shuffle their rosters.
All matches are played consecutively.

In the third match, Omar, Ali, Bas, Cid, Dan, Emo, Fox, and Gus beat Pat, Quin, Roy, Sam, Ted, Ugo, Vin, and Wes.
