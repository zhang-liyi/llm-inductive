BACKGROUND  
In this event, the athletes are playing a series of 2-on-2 half-court basketball games. In each game, the winning team is determined by the players’ intrinsic shooting, ball-handling, and defensive ability, combined with how much effort they put into that particular game.  
All games take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS  
In the first game, Diego and Malik beat Tara and Zoe.  
In the second game, Diego and Malik beat Ben and Owen.  
In the third game, Tara and Ben beat Diego and Malik.

QUERIES  
Query 1: Out of 100 random basketball players, where do you think Diego ranks in terms of intrinsic ability?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Tara put into the third game?  
Query 3: In a new game later this same day between Diego and Malik (Team 1) and Ben and Zoe (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new game later this same day between Tara and Owen (Team 1) and Ben and Zoe (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
