BACKGROUND
In this tournament, teams are playing 4-a-side soccer on a small pitch. In each match, the winning team is determined by the players’ intrinsic soccer ability (such as ball control, finishing, and defensive skill) together with how much effort they put in during that match.
All matches are played on the same day.
Each player belongs to a fixed team of four.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Rafael’s team (Rafael, Hugo, Marco, and Diego) beat Owen’s team (Owen, Liam, Carter, and Blake).
In the second match, Rafael’s team beat Isaac’s team (Isaac, Noah, Julian, and Elias).
In the third match, Owen’s team beat Isaac’s team.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random soccer players, where do you think Rafael ranks in terms of intrinsic soccer ability?
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Owen put into the third match?
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Rafael’s team and Owen’s team, who would win and by how much?
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Owen’s team and Isaac’s team, who would win and by how much?
