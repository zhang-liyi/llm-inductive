BACKGROUND  
In this event, the players are competing in a series of table tennis doubles matches. Outcomes depend on each player’s intrinsic table tennis ability (speed, spin control, reflexes) and how much effort and focus they bring to each match.  
Matches are played as 2-on-2 doubles. All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS  
In the first match, Omar and Li lost to Sara and Ben.  
In the second match, Omar and Sara beat Li and Priya.  
In the third match, Omar and Ben lost to Priya and Li.

QUERIES  
Query 1: Out of 100 random table tennis players, where do you think Omar ranks in terms of intrinsic skill?  
Query 2: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Priya put into the third match?  
Query 3: In a new match later this same day between Omar and Li (Team 1) and Sara and Priya (Team 2), who would win and by how much?  
Query 4: In a new match later this same day between Omar and Ben (Team 1) and Li and Sara (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
